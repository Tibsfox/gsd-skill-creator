# Welcome to tibsfox

Technology education built on first principles. Every concept explained from the ground up, every abstraction earned through understanding.

## What You'll Find Here

This site is a living collection of educational packs, essays, and technical resources designed for people who want to understand how things actually work. Not just the API surface, not just the tutorial steps, but the fundamental principles that make everything click into place.

Whether you're learning electronics from Ohm's law to microcontroller design, exploring the cultural history of bulletin board systems, or understanding the biomechanics behind martial arts cinema, every resource here follows the same philosophy: **start with the foundations, build toward mastery, never skip the why**.

## Educational Packs

Our packs are structured learning experiences that take you from zero to competent in a specific domain. Each pack includes theory, hands-on exercises, and real-world projects. They're designed to be worked through sequentially, but every section stands on its own as a reference you'll return to.

- [Electronics](/packs/electronics/) -- From resistors to radio, the complete path
- [BBS Culture](/packs/bbs-culture/) -- The networks that built the internet generation
- [Kung Fu Cinema](/packs/kung-fu-cinema/) -- Art, history, and technique in motion

## Essays

Longer-form thinking about technology, learning, and the spaces between disciplines where the most interesting ideas live. These aren't tutorials; they're explorations of how things connect and why certain design decisions echo across decades.

- [The Space Between](/essays/the-space-between/) -- On the fertile ground between disciplines
- [The Amiga Principle](/essays/amiga-principle/) -- What a 1985 computer teaches us about design

## Open Knowledge

Everything on this site is built with openness in mind. Content is available in plain markdown alongside the rendered HTML. AI agents and research tools can discover and navigate the full corpus through standardized protocols. The [bibliography](/bibliography/) provides full citations for every source referenced across the site.

This is education without walls. Take what's useful, build on what you find, share what you learn.

# About This Site

This site exists because the best educational resources are the ones that respect your intelligence while meeting you exactly where you are.

## Philosophy

Too much technical education falls into one of two traps. The first is the "just follow these steps" tutorial that gives you a working result but no understanding. The second is the academic paper that assumes you already know everything except the one novel contribution being presented. Both leave you stranded: either you can copy but not create, or you can read but not apply.

The approach here is different. Every topic starts with the most fundamental concept and builds upward, one layer at a time. Each layer is explained not just in terms of what it does, but **why it exists** -- what problem it solves, what alternatives were considered, and what tradeoffs were made. This is the difference between knowing how to use a tool and understanding how to choose the right one.

## The Packs

Educational packs are the core offering. Each pack is a complete curriculum for a specific domain, structured as a progressive journey from absolute beginner to confident practitioner. They include:

- **Theory sections** that explain the underlying principles
- **Hands-on exercises** that build practical skills incrementally
- **Real-world projects** that synthesize everything into applied knowledge
- **Reference cards** you'll return to long after completing the pack

The packs span wildly different domains -- electronics, BBS culture, martial arts cinema -- because the learning method itself is the through-line. The same pedagogical principles that help you understand Kirchhoff's voltage law also help you appreciate the choreographic grammar of a Shaw Brothers fight scene.

## Open by Design

All content is published in dual format: rendered HTML for reading and plain markdown for reuse. The site exposes machine-readable indexes through `llms.txt`, `agents.md`, and Schema.org structured data so that AI assistants, research tools, and aggregators can discover and navigate the full corpus without scraping.

This isn't an afterthought. It's a core architectural decision. Knowledge that can only be consumed through a single interface is knowledge at risk of being lost. By publishing in open, standardized formats, the content outlives any particular rendering technology.

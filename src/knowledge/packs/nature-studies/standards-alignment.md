# NATURE-101: Nature Studies Standards Alignment

## NAAEE Environmental Education Standards

**North American Association for Environmental Education (NAAEE)** Guidelines for Excellence alignment:

| Strand | NATURE-101 Modules | Connection |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Strand 1:** Questioning & Analysis | M1, M4 | Systematic observation, asking ecological questions |
| **Strand 2:** Systems & Scale | M4, M5 | Ecosystems, interconnections, spatial scales |
| **Strand 3:** Decision-Making | M5 | Conservation choices, citizen science participation |
| **Strand 4:** Individual & Social Responsibility | M5 | Stewardship, habitat protection, ethical action |

## NGSS Life Science Standards

**Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)** ecology & life science alignment:

| Standard | NATURE-101 Modules | Coverage |
|----------|-------------------|----------|
| **LS1:** Structure & Function | M2, M3 | Plant/animal adaptations to environment |
| **LS2:** Ecosystems (Core) | M4, M5 | Food webs, energy flow, biodiversity, succession |
| **LS2-1:** Interdependent Relationships | M4 | Predation, mutualism, parasitism, competition |
| **LS2-5:** Matter Cycling | M4 | Water, carbon, nitrogen cycles at ecosystem level |
| **LS4:** Evolution & Biodiversity | M2, M3, M4 | Speciation, adaptation, fossil record |
| **ESS3:** Earth & Human Activity | M5 | Conservation, sustainable practices |

# THEO-101: Theology & Religion Studies

**Classification:** Specialized & Deepening
**Domain:** Theological Studies
**Grades:** K through College
**Approach:** Academic, Comparative, Non-Proselytizing

## What This Pack Covers

THEO-101 explores humanity's deepest questions about the sacred, the divine, and the meaning of existence -- studied academically and comparatively. Every tradition is treated with equal scholarly respect. The goal is religious literacy, not religious instruction.

### Modules

1. **Stories & Traditions** -- Sacred narratives, creation stories, moral tales across cultures
2. **World Religions Survey** -- Major traditions: origins, beliefs, practices, global presence
3. **Sacred Texts & Interpretation** -- Reading religious texts as literature and theology
4. **Religion, Ethics & Society** -- How religious ideas shape law, art, ethics, and culture
5. **Comparative Theology & Interfaith Dialogue** -- Common ground and genuine differences

## Who This Pack Is For

- Learners seeking to understand the world's religious traditions
- Students of history, literature, art, or philosophy who need religious context
- Anyone preparing for informed, respectful engagement in a pluralistic world
- Families who want their children to understand neighbors of different faiths
- Educators building religious literacy curricula

## Academic Approach

This pack is **not** religious instruction. It does not teach any religion as "true." It does not mock, dismiss, or undermine any tradition. It presents what religions teach, how they developed, why they matter, and how they continue to shape the world.

- Every tradition treated with equal depth and respect
- Internal diversity within traditions always acknowledged
- Comparative method used throughout
- Primary sources (sacred texts) wherever possible
- Non-religious worldviews included with equal respect

## Recommended Prior Knowledge

- **PHILO-101** -- Philosophy provides tools of argument and ethical reasoning
- **HIST-101** -- Historical thinking is essential for religious history
- **READ-101** -- Reading comprehension for engaging with sacred texts

## Content Sensitivity

Religion touches deeply held convictions. This pack follows a strict sensitivity protocol:
- No proselytizing or denigration of any tradition
- Equal treatment across all traditions
- Age-appropriate handling of sensitive topics
- Multiple perspectives on contested questions
- Language that respects each tradition's self-understanding

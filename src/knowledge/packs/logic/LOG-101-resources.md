# LOG-101 Resources

## Foundational Texts & References

### Classic Logic Books
- [An Introduction to Logic](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Logic-Irving-M-Copi/dp/0134499565) — Copi & Cohen. Industry standard college textbook; rigorous but clear
- [Thinking, Fast and Slow](https://www.amazon.com/Thinking-Fast-Slow-Daniel-Kahneman/dp/0374533555) — Daniel Kahneman. How humans reason (and fail to reason) psychologically grounded
- [Language, Proof and Logic](https://www.amazon.com/Language-Proof-Logic-Jon-Barwise/dp/157586374X) — Barwise & Etchemendy. Excellent introduction to formal logic with interactive software
- [The Art of the Argument](https://www.amazon.com/Art-Argument-Rhetorical-Persuasion-Exposition/dp/1890626414) — Stefan Behnke. Practical guide to constructing and evaluating arguments
- [Logically Fallacious: The Ultimate Collection of Over 300 Logical Fallacies](https://www.logicallyfallacious.com/) — Bo Bennett. Comprehensive resource with examples and explanations

### For Younger Learners
- [What is Logic?](https://www.amazon.com/What-Logic-Christopher-Davis-Ortiz/dp/1493229753) — Christopher Davis-Ortiz. Picture book introducing logic to children
- [Logicomix: An Epic Search for Truth](https://www.amazon.com/Logicomix-Epic-Search-Truth-Apostolos/dp/0747597200) — Doxiadis & Papadimitriou. Graphic novel about Kurt Gödel; accessible and engaging
- [Sophie's World](https://www.amazon.com/Sophies-World-History-Philosophy-Novel/dp/0553573403) — Jostein Gaarder. Novel presenting history of philosophy and logic for teens

## For Deeper Study: Philosophy & Logic

### Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (Free Online)
The Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy provides peer-reviewed, authoritative entries on logic topics:

- [Logic](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/logic/) — Overview of logic as a discipline
- [Propositional Logic](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/logic-propositional/) — In-depth treatment of propositional calculus
- [Predicate Logic](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/logic-firstorder/) — First-order logic and quantifiers
- [Relevance Logic](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/logic-relevance/) — Alternative logical systems
- [Modal Logic](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/logic-modal/) — Logic of possibility and necessity
- [Fallacies](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/fallacies/) — Comprehensive overview of logical fallacies
- [Proof Theory](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/proof-theory/) — Foundations of mathematical proof

### Philosophy Books for Advanced Learners
- [Introduction to Formal Logic](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Formal-Logic-Peter-Smith/dp/1107539162) — Peter Smith. Online (free) and in print; excellent progression
- [Deductive Logic](https://www.amazon.com/Deductive-Logic-Nicholas-JJ-Smith/dp/0748642919) — Smith. Clear, modern introduction
- [A Concise Introduction to Logic](https://www.amazon.com/Concise-Introduction-Logic-Patrick-Hurley/dp/0357447131) — Hurley. Popular college textbook

## Interactive Tools & Online Platforms

### Logic Simulators & Generators
- [Truth Table Generator](https://www.truth-tables.com/) — Interactive truth table construction and exploration
- [Propositional Logic Evaluator](https://www.umsu.de/logik/evaluate.html) — Enter formulas and evaluate for different truth assignments
- [Logic Circuit Simulator](https://logic.ly/) — Design digital logic circuits with AND, OR, NOT gates
- [Logitech](https://github.com/mhucka/logitech) — Free logic simulation software

### Proof Assistants & Verification Tools
- [Lean Theorem Prover](https://leanprover.github.io/) — Modern proof assistant used in mathematics and CS
- [Coq Proof Assistant](https://coq.inria.fr/) — Interactive theorem proving
- [Isabelle/HOL](https://isabelle.in.tum.de/) — Powerful proof verification system

## Games & Puzzles

### Classic Logic Games
- [Sudoku](https://www.sudoku.org/) — Logic puzzle requiring deduction and constraint satisfaction
- [KenKen](https://www.kenken.com/) — Math-based logic puzzle combining arithmetic with logical reasoning
- [The Puzzle Zapper](https://www.thepuzzles.com/) — Collection of logic puzzles online
- [Mastermind](https://www.logicgames.org/mastermind.html) — Code-breaking game using logical deduction
- [Blokus](https://www.boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/2453/blokus) — Spatial logic and pattern recognition
- [Set Card Game](https://www.setgame.com/) — Visual logic and pattern recognition game

### Interactive Logic Games
- [Zachtronics Games](https://www.zachtronics.com/) — Programming/logic puzzle games: TIS-100, Exapunks, Shenzhen I/O
- [Portal](https://www.thinkwithportals.com/) — Physics + logic puzzle video game
- [The Witness](https://www.pcgamer.com/the-witness-release-date/) — Logic puzzle game exploring patterns and deduction

### Chess
- [Chess.com](https://www.chess.com/) — Play, learn, and analyze chess (pure logic and strategy)

# READ-101: Reading & Literacy

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

Reading as the master skill that unlocks all other learning -- phonemic awareness, decoding, vocabulary, comprehension strategies, critical analysis, and discipline-specific reading.

## Quick Start

This pack teaches the **full arc of literacy**: from phonemic awareness and decoding in the earliest years, through vocabulary and comprehension strategies, to critical reading and discipline-specific text analysis. READ-101 is the prerequisite for all other knowledge packs that require reading.

### Modules

| Module | Name | Focus |
|--------|------|-------|
| M1 | Foundations of Reading | Phonics, decoding, fluency, sight words |
| M2 | Vocabulary & Language | Context clues, morphology, academic vocabulary |
| M3 | Comprehension & Meaning-Making | Predicting, questioning, summarizing, connecting |
| M4 | Critical Reading & Analysis | Bias detection, evidence evaluation, source comparison |
| M5 | Reading Across the Curriculum | Science, history, math, technical, and digital reading |

### Prerequisites

- None required (this is the foundational literacy pack)
- No prior reading skill assumed for Module 1

### What This Pack Enables

- WRIT-101 (Writing)
- CRIT-101 (Critical Thinking)
- HIST-101 (History)
- COMM-101 (Communication)

# PHYS-101: Physics --- Foundational Knowledge Pack

Physics as the study of how the universe works at its most fundamental level:
motion, forces, energy, waves, and matter. Building intuition about physical
phenomena before formalism, then connecting intuition to mathematical models.

## Classification

- **Pack ID:** PHYS-101
- **Tier:** Core Academic
- **Domain:** Physical Science
- **Status:** Alpha
- **Version:** 1.0.0

# MUSIC-101: Music Standards Alignment

## NCAS (National Core Arts Standards) Music

**MUSIC-101** fully implements **NCAS music standards**:

| Standard | MUSIC-101 Modules | Content |
|----------|-------------------|---------|
| **MU:Cr1.1** Creating (Generate ideas) | M1, M2, M5 | Composition, improvisation, songwriting |
| **MU:Cr2.1** Creating (Plan and make) | M5 | Recording, arrangement, production |
| **MU:Pr4.1** Performing (Sing/play accurately) | M1, M2, M3 | Vocal performance, instrumental technique |
| **MU:Re7.1** Responding (Analyze music) | M4 | Listening analysis, form recognition |
| **MU:Cn10.1** Connecting (Connect to context) | M4 | Music in culture, history, society |

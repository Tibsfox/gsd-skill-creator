## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- MFAB-101 emphasizes hands-on material testing with everyday materials
- Sustainability and lifecycle thinking integrated throughout
- Spans K-12 with progression from exploration to design application

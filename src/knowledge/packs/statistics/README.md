# STAT-101: Accounting & Statistics

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

Numbers quantify the world -- from tracking your allowance to predicting election outcomes. Accounting provides the language of business (where money goes, what it does). Statistics provides the language of uncertainty (what data says, how confident we should be). Together they form the quantitative backbone of informed decision-making.

## Quick Start

This pack teaches **accounting and statistics through real-world application**: running classroom stores, flipping coins, surveying classmates, and managing budgets before formulas and formal notation. It combines financial literacy with statistical reasoning.

### Modules

| Module | Name | Focus |
|--------|------|-------|
| M1 | Accounting Principles & Bookkeeping | Transactions, double-entry, the accounting equation |
| M2 | Financial Statements & Analysis | Income statements, balance sheets, cash flow, ratios |
| M3 | Probability & Randomness | Chance, expected value, conditional probability, Bayes |
| M4 | Statistical Analysis & Inference | Descriptive stats, sampling, hypothesis testing, correlation |
| M5 | Financial Literacy & Personal Finance | Budgeting, compound interest, investing, debt management |

### Prerequisites

- None required (Module 3 starts from coin flips; Module 1 starts from tracking transactions)
- MATH-101 recommended for arithmetic fluency and algebraic foundations

### What This Pack Enables

- DATA-101 (Data Science)
- ECON-101 (Economics)

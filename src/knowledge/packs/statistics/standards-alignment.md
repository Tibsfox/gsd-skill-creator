- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- STAT-101 combines statistical reasoning with personal finance applications
- Dual emphasis: analytical (statistical thinking) and practical (financial literacy)
- Spans K-12 with concrete experiences before abstract concepts

# TECH-101: Technology & Tools

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Overview

Technology as understanding how humans extend their capabilities through tools, systems, and design. This pack covers simple machines, systems thinking, the engineering design process, digital technology foundations, and technology's relationship with society.

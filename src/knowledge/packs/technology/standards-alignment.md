## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- TECH-101 spans K-12 with practical, hands-on approach to tools and systems
- Emphasizes design thinking, problem-solving, and technology's role in society
- Standards alignment covers both foundational tool use and systems thinking

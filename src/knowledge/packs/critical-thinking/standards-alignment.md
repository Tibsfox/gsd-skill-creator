## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- CRIT-101 emphasizes HOW to think, not WHAT to think
- Spans K-12 with discussion-based inquiry throughout
- Metacognition and intellectual humility integrated across all modules

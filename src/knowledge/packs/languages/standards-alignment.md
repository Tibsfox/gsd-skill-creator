# LANG-101: Standards Alignment

## ACTFL World-Readiness Standards

**Standard 1.1** (Communication - Interpersonal): Learners interact and negotiate meaning in spoken, signed, or written conversations to share information, reactions, feelings, and opinions.

**Standard 1.2** (Communication - Interpretive): Learners understand, interpret, and analyze what is heard, read, or viewed on a variety of topics.

**Standard 1.3** (Communication - Presentational): Learners present information, concepts, and ideas to an audience of listeners, readers, or viewers on a variety of topics.

**Standard 2.1** (Cultures - Practices & Perspectives): Learners use the language to investigate, explain, and reflect on the relationship between the practices and perspectives of the cultures studied.

**Standard 2.2** (Cultures - Products & Perspectives): Learners use the language to investigate, explain, and reflect on the relationship between the products and perspectives of the cultures studied.

## CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference) Proficiency Levels

- **A1** (Breakthrough): Can understand and use very basic phrases for immediate needs
- **A2** (Elementary): Can understand sentences relating to familiar areas
- **B1** (Intermediate): Can understand main points of clear standard input on familiar matters
- **B2** (Upper Intermediate): Can understand extended speech and implicit meaning
- **C1** (Advanced): Can understand demanding, longer texts and implicit meaning
- **C2** (Mastery): Can understand with ease virtually everything heard or read

## IB MYP Language Acquisition Standards

**AoI.1**: Language and identity — How learners use language to express self and understand others

**AoI.2**: Communication and culture — How language enables communication within cultural contexts

**AoI.3**: Multilingualism and global engagement — How multiple languages enable global participation

## Curriculum Connection Notes

- **K-2 Emphasis**: Sound awareness, basic classroom language, cultural exposure
- **3-5 Emphasis**: Basic vocabulary, simple patterns, listening comprehension
- **6-8 Emphasis**: Conversational phrases, grammatical structures, cultural understanding
- **9-12 Emphasis**: Advanced proficiency, cultural nuance, real-world communication

## Language-Specific Applications

LANG-101 is a **meta-skill pack** teaching principles applicable to any target language:
- Spanish-101, French-101, Mandarin-101 future packs will build on LANG-101 foundations
- IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet) principles from M1 apply to all languages
- Grammar pattern recognition frameworks from M2 transfer across language families

# ECON-101: Standards Alignment

## CEE (Council for Economic Education) Standards

**Standard 1**: Scarcity — Every society must answer what, how, and for whom to produce
**Standard 2**: Opportunity Cost & Trade-offs — Making choices involves giving up alternatives
**Standard 3**: Production — Understanding how goods and services are created
**Standard 4**: Exchange — Markets enable trade and resource allocation
**Standard 5**: Wages & Earnings — Labor markets determine income
**Standard 15**: Financial Markets — Saving and investing

## Common Core Mathematics (Financial Literacy & Ratios)

**CCSS.MATH.7.RP**: Ratios and Proportional Relationships (prices, interest rates)
**CCSS.MATH.8.EE**: Expressions and Equations (algebra in financial planning)

## C3 Framework for Social Studies

**Dimension 3**: Evaluating sources and using evidence in decision-making
**Dimension 4**: Constructing and defending claims using evidence

## IB MYP Individuals & Societies (Economics Module)

- Economic systems and resource allocation
- Markets and trade
- Personal financial decision-making
- Sustainable economic practices

## AP Microeconomics Alignment

Units 1-5: Supply/Demand, Production, Consumer Choice, Firm Behavior, Market Efficiency

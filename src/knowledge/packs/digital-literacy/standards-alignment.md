# DIGLIT-101: Standards Alignment

## ISTE Standards for Students

**Standard 1** (Empowered Learner): Use technology to develop computational thinking and design thinking
**Standard 2** (Digital Citizen): Recognize rights, responsibilities, opportunities in digital world
**Standard 3** (Knowledge Constructor): Curate information from digital sources
**Standard 4** (Innovative Designer): Design solutions using technology
**Standard 5** (Computational Thinker): Understand computing and algorithmic thinking
**Standard 6** (Creative Communicator): Communicate ideas using digital tools

## Common Sense Media Digital Citizenship Curriculum

- Balance online time with offline activities
- Privacy and security practices
- Creating positive digital footprints
- Critical thinking about online content
- Cyberbullying and online harassment prevention
- Digital communication and etiquette

## AASL (American Association of School Librarians) Standards Framework

**Inquire**: Research and information evaluation
**Collaborate**: Digital collaboration practices
**Curate**: Managing and sharing information
**Engage**: Creating content responsibly
**Explore**: Understanding information systems

## UK Computing Curriculum

**Digital Literacy** (KS1-4): Understanding technology, online safety, responsible use, information evaluation

## Curriculum Connections

- **K-2 Emphasis**: Basic technology use, online safety basics, creative digital exploration
- **3-5 Emphasis**: Search skills, source evaluation, digital communication, citizenship
- **6-8 Emphasis**: Information literacy, online safety deepening, data awareness
- **9-12 Emphasis**: Algorithmic thinking, privacy strategies, ethical AI, informed citizenship

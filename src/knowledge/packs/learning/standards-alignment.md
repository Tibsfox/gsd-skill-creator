# LEARN-101: Learning & Metacognition Standards Alignment

## IB Approaches to Learning (ATL)

**LEARN-101** fully implements **IB ATL** framework—the meta-competencies that enable learning in all subjects:

| ATL Skill Category | LEARN-101 Modules | Examples |
|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Thinking Skills** | M1, M2, M3, M4, M5 | Critical thinking, metacognition, creativity |
| **Communication Skills** | M2, M5 | Explaining thinking, teaching others, articulation |
| **Social Skills** | M4, M5 | Collaboration, peer learning, growth mindset |
| **Self-Management Skills** | M2, M3, M4 | Goal-setting, time management, self-regulation |
| **Research Skills** | M5 | Finding information, evaluating sources, synthesis |

## Metacognitive Learning Standards

**LEARN-101** teaches the science of learning itself:**

| Learning Dimension | Content | Modules |
|-------------------|---------|---------|
| **Memory Systems** | Encoding, storage, retrieval | M1 |
| **Attention & Focus** | Concentration, distraction management | M1, M2 |
| **Sleep & Learning** | Brain consolidation during sleep | M1 |
| **Effective Strategies** | Retrieval practice, spaced repetition, interleaving | M2 |
| **Transfer of Learning** | Applying knowledge to new contexts | M5 |
| **Growth Mindset** | Intelligence and ability as learnable | M4 |
| **Productive Struggle** | Difficulty as opportunity for growth | M4 |

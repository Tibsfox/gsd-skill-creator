# PE-101: Physical Education Standards Alignment

## SHAPE America National Standards for K-12 Physical Education

**PE-101** directly implements **SHAPE America's 5 National Standards**:

| Standard | PE-101 Modules | Content |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **S1:** Motor Skills & Movement Patterns | M1, M3, M4 | Locomotor, non-locomotor, manipulative skills progression |
| **S2:** Concepts, Principles, Strategies, Tactics | M2, M3, M5 | Game strategy, fitness principles, biomechanics |
| **S3:** Health-Enhancing Fitness | M2, M5 | Aerobic, strength, flexibility, balance fitness components |
| **S4:** Responsible Personal & Social Behavior | M3, M4, M5 | Respect, cooperation, sportsmanship, safety |
| **S5:** Value Physical Activity | M2, M4, M5 | Health, enjoyment, challenge, self-expression, social interaction |

## IB MYP Physical & Health Education

| Key Concept | Modules | Examples |

## UK National Curriculum PE

**PE-101** aligns with **UK Key Stages**:

| Stage | Grade Equivalent | PE-101 Modules |
|-------|------------------|-----------------|
| Key Stage 2 | 3-5 | M1, M2, M3 (foundation skills) |
| Key Stage 3 | 6-8 | M3, M4, M5 (application & depth) |
| Key Stage 4 (GCSE) | 9-10 | M5 (specialist focus) |

## Physical Literacy Framework

**PE-101** develops all four pillars of **Physical Literacy**:

| Pillar | Content | Modules |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Physical Competence | Motor skills, fitness, coordination | M1, M2, M3, M4 |
| Daily Behavior | Active living habits, lifetime wellness | M2, M5 |
| Motivation & Confidence | Intrinsic motivation, self-efficacy, enjoyment | M4, M5 |
| Knowledge & Understanding | Exercise science, biomechanics, wellness concepts | M2, M5 |

## Adapted Physical Education Alignment

**PE-101** fully incorporates **Universal Design for Learning (UDL)** and **Adapted PE Standards**:

- Multiple means of representation
- Multiple means of engagement
- Multiple means of action and expression
- Inclusive language throughout
- Adaptations for every activity

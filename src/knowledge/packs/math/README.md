## Overview

Mathematics as a way of thinking: pattern recognition, precise reasoning, and building complexity from simple rules. This pack covers number sense, algebraic thinking, geometry, and data/statistics from pre-K through college level.

## Modules

1. **M1 — Number & Operations:** Quantity, place value, combining/separating (K-12)
2. **M2 — Patterns & Algebraic Thinking:** Patterns, variables, equations, functions (K-12)
3. **M3 — Geometry & Spatial Thinking:** Shapes, transformations, measurement (K-12)
4. **M4 — Data, Probability & Statistics:** Data literacy, probability, inference (K-12)

# BUS-101: Accessibility Audit

**Pack:** Business — Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Audit Date:** 2026-02-20
**Overall Status:** Mostly Accessible

## Feature Status

| Feature | Status | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Screen Reader Compatible | Implemented | Business concepts and scenarios in text |
| Large Text Support | Implemented | Financial tables and text scale well |
| High Contrast Mode | Implemented | High-contrast interface available |
| Keyboard Navigation | Implemented | Business simulation games support keyboard input |
| Alt Text for Images | Partial | Business organization diagrams included; flowcharts need descriptions |
| Media Captions | Partial | Some business case study videos; captions needed for all |

## Content-Specific Considerations

- **Business Simulations:** Trading games and business simulations include text-based decision points and outcomes. Audio feedback and verbal descriptions of game state can support all learners.
- **Financial Information:** Charts, tables, and financial statements are text-friendly; high-contrast options provided for visual clarity.
- **Case Studies:** Scenario-based learning uses narrative description; text-based case studies are naturally accessible.
- **Discussion Activities:** Business ethics and governance topics are discussion-based; written alternatives available.

## Remediation Path

### Priority 1 (Critical)
1. Ensure business simulation games have clear text descriptions of state/options
2. Provide audio feedback for game state changes and outcomes
3. Add detailed descriptions of business organization charts
4. Create text-based alternatives for any business flowcharts
5. Add captions to all business case study videos

### Priority 2 (Important)
1. Develop accessible financial planning spreadsheet templates
2. Create keyboard shortcuts guide for business simulation tools
3. Build collaborative business case discussion guides
4. Develop accessible business plan templates
5. Create text-based assessment alternatives for simulations

### Priority 3 (Enhancement)
1. Develop audio descriptions of business scenarios
2. Create multi-sensory business simulation feedback
3. Build customizable simulation interface options
4. Develop audio versions of business case studies

## WCAG 2.1 Compliance Notes

- **Target:** WCAG 2.1 Level AA
- **Current Status:** Level AA for text content; Level A for some video demonstrations
- **Major Strengths:**
  - Business concepts are text-expressible
  - Simulation games can be keyboard-controlled
  - Good contrast in financial interfaces
  - Discussion format is inclusive
- **Minor Gaps:**
  - Simulation game state descriptions could be more detailed
  - Some instructional videos lack captions
  - Business visualization flowcharts need descriptions

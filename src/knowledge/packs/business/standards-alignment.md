## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- BUS-101 integrates business operations with ethical and legal considerations
- Spans K-12 with trading games and simulations before formal concepts
- Module 5 (Ethics & Governance) ensures business thinking includes responsibility

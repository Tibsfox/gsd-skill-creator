## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- SCI-101 emphasizes scientific practices and inquiry over content
- Standards alignment spans K-12 and early college
- Focus on observation, hypothesis, experimentation, and communication across all frameworks

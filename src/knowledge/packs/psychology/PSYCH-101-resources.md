## How to Evaluate Psychology Information

Not all psychology resources are equally reliable. When evaluating sources:

**Good Signs:**
- Cites research studies or professional organizations
- Written by someone with psychology credentials
- Distinguishes between research findings and opinion
- Updated regularly with current information
- Acknowledges limitations and nuance

**Red Flags:**
- Makes dramatic claims without evidence
- Promises quick fixes or miracle solutions
- Written by someone selling something related to the claim
- Relies on testimonials instead of research
- Oversimplifies complex psychology

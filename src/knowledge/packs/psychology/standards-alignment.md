# PSYCH-101: Standards Alignment

## APA National Standards for High School Psychology

**Standard 1.1**: Understanding behavior at biological, cognitive, and social levels
**Standard 1.2**: Understanding development across the lifespan
**Standard 2.1**: Mental processes and consciousness
**Standard 3.1**: Social influences on behavior
**Standard 4.1**: Research methods and statistical reasoning

## IB MYP Sciences (Human Science Strand)

- Understand human body systems and homeostasis
- Investigate human development and behavior
- Apply scientific method to psychology questions

## SEL (Social-Emotional Learning) Competencies

- **Self-Awareness**: Understanding emotions, strengths, limitations
- **Self-Management**: Emotion regulation, motivation, resilience
- **Social Awareness**: Empathy, perspective-taking, social understanding
- **Relationship Skills**: Communication, collaboration, conflict resolution
- **Responsible Decision-Making**: Using evidence to make sound choices

## AP Psychology Alignment

Covers Units 1, 3, 4, 5, 6: Scientific Foundations, Sensation & Perception, Learning & Memory, Motivation & Emotion, Personality

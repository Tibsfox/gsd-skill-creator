## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- GEO-101 emphasizes spatial thinking and observation over place memorization
- Astronomy integrated as cosmic context (M4)
- Spans K-12 with developmentally appropriate spatial complexity

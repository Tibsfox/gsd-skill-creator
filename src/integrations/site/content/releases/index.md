The release notes chronicle the evolution of gsd-skill-creator from its earliest foundations through the current milestone. Each entry documents the features introduced, improvements made, and the overarching goals accomplished during that development cycle. Starting from version 1.0, the project has grown through more than fifty milestones, each building on the patterns and discoveries of its predecessors.

Release notes cover new skill definitions, agent configurations, validation improvements, pattern discovery enhancements, and the various educational packs that have been integrated over time. Whether you are looking for details on a specific version or want to understand the trajectory of the project as a whole, this collection provides a comprehensive record.

Navigate individual release pages to see per-milestone breakdowns, including the number of phases executed, plans completed, and the key artifacts delivered. The notes also capture important architectural decisions and lessons learned that shaped subsequent development.

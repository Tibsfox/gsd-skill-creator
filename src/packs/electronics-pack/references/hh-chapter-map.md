## Coverage Notes

The following H&H chapters are **not covered** in the v1 scope of this pack:

- **Chapter 7** (Oscillators and Timers) -- planned for future expansion
- **Chapter 8** (except Section 8.11 on Noise) -- low-noise/precision techniques deferred
- **Chapter 11** (Programmable Logic / FPGAs) -- deferred to future digital expansion

These chapters may be added as supplementary modules in a later version.

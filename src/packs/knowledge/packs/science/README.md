# SCI-101: Science Method & Nature

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

Science as a way of knowing -- observation, hypothesis testing, experimental design, evidence evaluation, and the nature of science itself.

## Quick Start

This pack teaches the **process** of science, not the content of any particular science. It is the inquiry engine for all STEM packs (PHYS-101, CHEM-101, ENVR-101, NATURE-101).

### Modules

| Module | Name | Focus |
|--------|------|-------|
| M1 | Observation & Inquiry | Noticing, recording, questioning |
| M2 | Hypothesis & Experimental Design | Testable ideas, variables, controls |
| M3 | Data Collection & Analysis | Measurement, patterns, conclusions |
| M4 | Scientific Communication & Argumentation | Writing, presenting, peer review |
| M5 | History & Nature of Science | How science evolves, ethics, pseudoscience |

### Prerequisites

- None required (this is a foundational pack)
- MATH-101 recommended for quantitative data analysis skills

### What This Pack Enables

- PHYS-101 (Physics)
- CHEM-101 (Chemistry)
- ENVR-101 (Environmental Science)
- NATURE-101 (Nature Study)

# ART-101: Visual Arts Standards Alignment

## NCAS (National Core Arts Standards) Visual Arts

**ART-101** fully implements **NCAS visual arts standards**:

| Standard | ART-101 Modules | Content |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **VA:Cr1.1** Creating (Make informed choices) | M1, M2, M3, M5 | Observational decisions, material selection |
| **VA:Cr2.1** Creating (Combine materials and processes) | M2, M3, M5 | Hands-on making with diverse media |
| **VA:Pr4.1** Presenting (Present artwork) | M5, M4 | Portfolio, artist statement, critique |
| **VA:Re7.1** Responding (Analyze artworks) | M4, M5 | Historical context, artistic intent |
| **VA:Cn10.1** Connecting (Connect to other disciplines) | M4, M5 | Art in history, culture, communication |

## IB MYP Arts (Visual Arts)

| Key Concept | Modules | Connection |

| **Communication** | M4, M5 | How art conveys meaning and emotion |

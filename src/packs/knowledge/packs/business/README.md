# BUS-101: Business & Law

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

Business and law as interconnected systems of human organization. Understanding how humans organize to cooperate, trade, and resolve conflicts -- from running a lemonade stand to understanding constitutional rights.

## Modules

| ID | Module | Focus |
|----|--------|-------|
| M1 | Economic Organization & Markets | Supply and demand, trade, money, market structures, specialization |
| M2 | Business Structures & Entrepreneurship | Sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation, nonprofit, starting ventures |
| M3 | Finance & Accounting Fundamentals | Revenue, expenses, profit, budgeting, financial statements, personal finance |
| M4 | Contracts, Rights & Legal Systems | Agreements, rights, obligations, courts, dispute resolution |
| M5 | Ethics, Governance & Social Responsibility | Corporate responsibility, regulation, transparency, stakeholder interests |

## Grade Levels

- **Foundation (PreK-2):** 20-40 hours -- Trading, fairness, needs vs. wants, play money
- **Elementary (3-5):** 40-60 hours -- Budgets, simple business plans, advertising, rules
- **Middle School (6-8):** 60-100 hours -- Market structures, financial statements, contracts, consumer rights
- **High School (9-12):** 100-160 hours -- Corporate governance, financial analysis, employment law, ethics cases
- **College+ (13+):** 160-220 hours -- Corporate finance, securities law, international business, ESG

## Prerequisites

- **Recommended:** MATH-101 (Mathematics), COMM-101 (Communication)
- **Required:** None -- business and legal thinking start with trading and fairness

## Core Philosophy

Business and law are not subjects reserved for professionals -- they are the operating systems of society. Every person trades, signs agreements, manages money, and exercises legal rights. This pack teaches understanding of these systems at every level, emphasizing reasoning and judgment over memorization of terminology. The goal is informed citizens who can navigate economic and legal systems effectively and ethically.

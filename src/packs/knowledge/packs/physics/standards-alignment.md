## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- PHYS-101 emphasizes intuition and everyday phenomena before formal mathematics
- Spans K-12 with hands-on experiments using common materials
- Modern Physics & Cosmology (M5) provides contemporary physics context

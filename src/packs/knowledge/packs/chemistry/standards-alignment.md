## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- CHEM-101 emphasizes kitchen chemistry and hands-on experimentation
- Periodic table introduced as organizing principle, not memorization focus
- Spans K-12 with macroscopic phenomena before atomic/molecular models

# ENVR-101: Standards Alignment

## NGSS Earth & Life Science Standards

**LS1.A** (Grades 3-5): Structures and Functions of Living Organisms
**LS2.A** (Grades 3-5): Interdependent Relationships in Ecosystems
**LS2.B** (Grades 6-8): Cycles of Matter and Energy Transfer
**ESS2.A** (Grades 6-8): Earth's Systems
**ESS3.A** (Grades 6-12): Natural Resources; Human Impacts on Earth Systems
**ESS3.B** (Grades 6-12): Climate Change
**ESS3.C** (Grades 9-12): Human Dependence on Natural Systems
**ESS3.D** (Grades 9-12): Global Climate Change

## AP Environmental Science Standards

Units 1-8 covering: Earth Systems & Resources, Ecosystems, Populations, Interactions, Energy, Biogeochemical Cycles, Aquatic Systems, Global Change

## IB MYP Sciences (Ecosystems & Sustainability)

- Ecosystem structure and function
- Human impacts and sustainability
- Evidence-based environmental reasoning

## AAAS Benchmarks for Science Literacy

- Life processes and ecosystems
- Earth systems
- Human interactions with environment
- Sustainability and resources

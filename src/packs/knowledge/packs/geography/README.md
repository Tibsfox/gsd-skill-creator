# GEO-101: Geography & Space Science

**Classification:** Core Academic
**Status:** Alpha
**Version:** 1.0.0

Geography as spatial thinking and Earth systems understanding -- not memorizing capitals, but learning to think spatially about how physical and human worlds interact, from neighborhood to cosmos.

## Quick Start

This pack teaches **spatial reasoning** as a foundational cognitive skill. It integrates physical Earth science, human geography, cartography, introductory astronomy, and climate science into a unified framework for understanding place, space, and scale.

### Modules

| Module | Name | Focus |
|--------|------|-------|
| M1 | Earth Systems & Physical Geography | Tectonics, rock cycle, water cycle, biomes, hazards |
| M2 | Human Geography & Cultural Landscapes | Population, migration, urbanization, culture, borders |
| M3 | Maps, Spatial Analysis & GIS | Cartography, coordinates, projections, spatial reasoning |
| M4 | Astronomy & Our Place in Space | Solar system, stars, galaxies, cosmic scale |
| M5 | Climate, Weather & Environmental Change | Atmosphere, climate zones, greenhouse effect, stewardship |

### Prerequisites

- None required (this is a foundational pack)
- SCI-101 recommended for scientific inquiry skills
- MATH-101 recommended for quantitative spatial analysis

### What This Pack Enables

- ENVR-101 (Environmental Science)
- ASTRO-101 (Astronomy)
- NATURE-101 (Nature Study)

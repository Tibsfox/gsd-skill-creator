## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- HIST-101 emphasizes inquiry, evidence analysis, and perspective-taking
- Primary source work central to all modules
- Spans K-12 with age-appropriate historical complexity

## Notes

- Coverage levels: Full (comprehensive), Partial (some aspects), Introductory (foundational)
- ENGR-101 emphasizes design process and real-world problem-solving
- Spans K-12 with materials and practical constraints as central learning tools
- Engineering ethics integrated throughout all modules

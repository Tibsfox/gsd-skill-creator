# NUTR-101: Standards Alignment

## NHES (National Health Education Standards)

**Standard 1**: Students comprehend concepts related to health promotion and disease prevention
**Standard 5**: Students demonstrate the ability to use decision-making skills to enhance health
**Standard 6**: Students demonstrate the ability to use goal-setting and decision-making skills
**Standard 7**: Students demonstrate the ability to practice health-enhancing behaviors

## SHAPE America National Standards

- Nutrition and food systems understanding
- Making evidence-based food choices
- Critical evaluation of health claims
- Systems thinking about food security and sustainability

## Common Core Mathematics (Statistics)

**CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.6.SP**: Data interpretation in nutrition contexts

## NGSS Science Practices

**SEP-4**: Analyzing and Interpreting Data (nutrition labels, food research)
**SEP-6**: Constructing Explanations (how nutrients function in body)
**SEP-7**: Engaging in Argument from Evidence (evaluating nutrition claims)

## IB MYP Sciences & Individuals & Societies

- Human body systems and nutrition
- Food systems sustainability
- Evidence-based decision-making

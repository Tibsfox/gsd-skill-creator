## Notes on Naming

**ACE naming note:** The SYS cluster (Cluster 3) contains a member with code ACE. In the Adaptive Systems department (Panel A), ACE refers to the Adaptive Critic Element from Barto, Sutton & Anderson (1983). Whether the SYS cluster's ACE is the same concept is an open question (S-2 in the Living Sensoria research thread). Companion proposal `TC-rosetta-translations.md` addresses the disambiguation. Until resolved, treat the two as distinct unless documentation indicates otherwise.

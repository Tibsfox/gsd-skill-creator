# Music & Sound

**Domain:** music
**Source:** MUSIC-101 Music & Sound Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of music education spanning rhythm and movement, melody and voice, harmony and structure, instruments and ensemble, and music history and culture -- from first beat to music theory and cultural context

## Wings

- Rhythm & Movement -- Steady beat, meter, rhythmic notation, syncopation, polyrhythm, body percussion
- Melody & Voice -- Singing in tune, scales, intervals, melodic contour, solfege, sight-singing
- Harmony & Structure -- Chords, progressions, tonality, key signatures, formal structure
- Instruments & Ensemble -- Instrument families, physics of sound, ensemble skills, blending and balance
- Music History & Culture -- Classical periods, jazz and blues roots, world music, music as social force

## Entry Point

music-rhythm

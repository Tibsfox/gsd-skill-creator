# Physical Education & Movement

**Domain:** physical-education
**Source:** PE-101 Physical Education Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of physical education spanning movement foundations, fitness science, sports and games, lifetime wellness, and outdoor adventure -- building physical competence and lifelong activity habits

## Wings

- Movement Foundations -- Locomotor, non-locomotor, manipulative skills; spatial and body awareness
- Fitness & Body Science -- Cardiovascular system, FITT principle, training adaptation, warm-up and cool-down
- Sports & Games -- Individual and team sports, rules, strategy, sportsmanship, transferable skills
- Wellness & Lifetime Fitness -- Goal setting, stress management, sleep, nutrition, injury prevention
- Outdoor & Adventure -- Hiking, climbing, orienteering, Leave No Trace, outdoor leadership

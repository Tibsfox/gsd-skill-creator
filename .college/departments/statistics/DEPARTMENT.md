# Accounting & Statistics

**Domain:** statistics
**Source:** STAT-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational quantitative reasoning spanning accounting and bookkeeping, financial statement analysis, probability, statistical analysis, and personal financial literacy -- developing the numeracy skills essential for informed decision-making in business and life

## Wings

- Accounting Principles & Bookkeeping -- Accounting equation, double-entry bookkeeping, journal entries, ledgers, cash versus accrual
- Financial Statements & Analysis -- Income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, financial ratios
- Probability & Randomness -- Experimental versus theoretical probability, expected value, conditional probability, Bayes' theorem
- Statistical Analysis & Inference -- Measures of center and spread, data visualization, sampling, hypothesis testing, correlation
- Financial Literacy & Personal Finance -- Budgeting, compound interest, investing, debt management, credit, taxes

## Entry Point

stat-accounting-equation

## Concepts

### Accounting Principles & Bookkeeping (4 concepts)
- stat-accounting-equation -- Assets = Liabilities + Equity: the fundamental equation that underlies all accounting
- stat-double-entry-bookkeeping -- Every transaction has two sides: debit and credit in equal amounts
- stat-journal-ledgers -- Recording transactions in journals and organizing them in ledger accounts
- stat-cash-vs-accrual -- Revenue and expense recognition: when cash changes hands versus when earned or incurred

### Financial Statements & Analysis (4 concepts)
- stat-income-statement -- Revenue, expenses, and profit over a period of time
- stat-balance-sheet -- Assets, liabilities, and equity at a single point in time
- stat-cash-flow-statement -- Cash in and out from operations, investing, and financing activities
- stat-financial-ratios -- Liquidity, profitability, and leverage ratios for evaluating financial health

### Probability & Randomness (4 concepts)
- stat-probability-foundations -- Probability as a measure from 0 to 1; sample spaces and events
- stat-experimental-theoretical -- Comparing results from actual trials with mathematically expected outcomes
- stat-expected-value -- The long-run average outcome; the basis for rational decision-making under uncertainty
- stat-conditional-probability -- How knowing one event changes the probability of another; Bayes' theorem

### Statistical Analysis & Inference (4 concepts)
- stat-descriptive-statistics -- Mean, median, mode, range, IQR, and standard deviation for summarizing data
- stat-data-visualization -- Choosing and interpreting histograms, box plots, scatter plots, and other displays
- stat-sampling-bias -- Survey design, random sampling, and sources of bias in data collection
- stat-hypothesis-testing -- Null and alternative hypotheses, p-values, and distinguishing signal from noise

### Financial Literacy & Personal Finance (4 concepts)
- stat-personal-budgeting -- Income, expenses, savings goals, and maintaining a balanced budget
- stat-compound-interest -- How interest grows exponentially over time and its implications for saving and debt
- stat-investing-basics -- Stocks, bonds, index funds, risk, return, and diversification
- stat-debt-credit -- Credit scores, interest rates, the true cost of debt, and avoiding predatory products

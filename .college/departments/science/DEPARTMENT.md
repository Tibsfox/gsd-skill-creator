# Science

**Domain:** science
**Source:** SCI-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational scientific inquiry skills spanning observation, hypothesis formation, experimental design, data analysis, scientific communication, and the history and nature of science -- developing the habits of mind that define scientific thinking

## Wings

- Observation & Inquiry -- Careful observation, asking scientific questions, variables, and the spirit of scientific investigation
- Hypothesis & Experimental Design -- Forming testable predictions, designing controlled experiments, and understanding fair tests
- Data Collection & Analysis -- Gathering, recording, visualizing, and drawing conclusions from scientific evidence
- Scientific Communication & Argumentation -- Claims-evidence-reasoning, lab reports, peer review, and scientific vocabulary
- History & Nature of Science -- Scientific paradigms, landmark discoveries, creativity in science, and science in society

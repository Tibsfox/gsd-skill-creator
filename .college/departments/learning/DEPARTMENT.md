# Learning to Learn

**Domain:** learning
**Source:** LEARN-101 Learning to Learn Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of learning science spanning neuroscience, evidence-based study strategies, mindset and motivation, metacognition, and learning environments -- the meta-skill that improves all other learning

## Wings

- Your Learning Brain -- Memory encoding and retrieval, working memory, sleep consolidation, brain myths
- Study Strategies That Work -- Retrieval practice, spaced repetition, interleaving, elaboration, concept mapping
- Mindset & Motivation -- Growth mindset, intrinsic motivation, procrastination, resilience
- Metacognition -- Calibration, self-monitoring, error analysis, Dunning-Kruger, expert learning
- Learning Environments -- Distraction management, Pomodoro, note-taking systems, digital vs physical

# Chemistry

**Domain:** chemistry
**Source:** CHEM-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational chemistry spanning matter and properties, atomic theory, chemical bonding, reactions and energy, and applied chemistry -- building molecular understanding from observable phenomena through industrial and biochemical applications

## Wings

- Matter & Properties -- States of matter, physical and chemical properties, mixtures, density, changes of state
- Atoms & the Periodic Table -- Atomic structure, electron configuration, periodic trends, isotopes
- Bonds & Molecular Structure -- Ionic, covalent, and metallic bonding; VSEPR theory; polarity; intermolecular forces
- Chemical Reactions & Energy -- Balancing equations, reaction types, stoichiometry, acids and bases, thermochemistry
- Applied Chemistry -- Polymers, pharmaceuticals, atmospheric chemistry, green chemistry, food science applications

## Concepts

### Matter & Properties (4 concepts)
- chem-states-of-matter -- Solid, liquid, gas, and plasma: particle arrangement, energy, and transitions between states
- chem-physical-chemical-properties -- Physical properties (density, melting point) versus chemical properties (reactivity, flammability)
- chem-mixtures-pure-substances -- Elements, compounds, homogeneous and heterogeneous mixtures, and separation techniques
- chem-density -- Density as a ratio of mass to volume and its applications in identifying substances

### Atoms & the Periodic Table (4 concepts)
- chem-atomic-structure -- Protons, neutrons, and electrons; atomic number, mass number, and the nuclear model
- chem-periodic-table-organization -- How elements are organized by atomic number, periods, and groups
- chem-periodic-trends -- Atomic radius, electronegativity, ionization energy, and reactivity trends
- chem-isotopes-radioactivity -- Isotopes, radioisotopes, half-life, and types of radioactive decay

### Bonds & Molecular Structure (4 concepts)
- chem-ionic-bonding -- Electron transfer, ionic bond formation, lattice energy, and properties of ionic compounds
- chem-covalent-bonding -- Electron sharing, Lewis structures, single, double, and triple bonds
- chem-molecular-geometry -- VSEPR theory, bond angles, molecular shapes, and polarity
- chem-intermolecular-forces -- Van der Waals forces, dipole-dipole interactions, and hydrogen bonding

### Chemical Reactions & Energy (5 concepts)
- chem-balancing-equations -- Conservation of matter, balancing chemical equations, and reaction stoichiometry
- chem-reaction-types -- Synthesis, decomposition, single and double displacement, combustion reactions
- chem-acids-bases -- pH, acid-base definitions (Arrhenius, Bronsted-Lowry), neutralization, and buffers
- chem-oxidation-reduction -- Oxidation states, redox reactions, electrochemistry, and batteries
- chem-thermochemistry -- Enthalpy, endothermic and exothermic reactions, Hess's law, and bond energies

### Applied Chemistry (4 concepts)
- chem-polymers -- Monomers, polymer structure, natural and synthetic polymers, and plastics
- chem-biochemistry-basics -- Carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, and nucleic acids as chemical structures
- chem-atmospheric-chemistry -- Ozone, greenhouse gases, air pollution chemistry, and climate connections
- chem-green-chemistry -- Atom economy, waste prevention, safer solvents, and sustainable synthesis

# Reading

**Domain:** reading
**Source:** READ-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational reading skills from phonological awareness and decoding through vocabulary development, comprehension, critical analysis, and reading across subject-area disciplines

## Wings

- Foundations of Reading -- Phonological awareness, phonics, decoding, sight words, and reading fluency
- Vocabulary & Language -- Word learning strategies, morphology, context clues, academic vocabulary, figurative language
- Comprehension & Meaning-Making -- Main idea, inferencing, text structure, summarizing, and monitoring understanding
- Critical Reading & Analysis -- Author purpose, argument analysis, bias identification, literary analysis, multi-text synthesis
- Reading Across the Curriculum -- Informational texts, discipline-specific vocabulary, primary sources, digital reading

## Entry Point

read-phonological-awareness

## Concepts

### Foundations of Reading (4 concepts)
- read-phonological-awareness -- Recognizing and manipulating sounds in spoken language: phonemes, syllables, rhyme
- read-phonics-decoding -- Letter-sound relationships and strategies for decoding unfamiliar words
- read-sight-words -- High-frequency words recognized instantly without decoding
- read-reading-fluency -- Reading accurately, at appropriate pace, and with expression

### Vocabulary & Language (4 concepts)
- read-word-learning-strategies -- Context clues, morphology, and dictionary use for building vocabulary
- read-morphology -- Prefixes, suffixes, and root words as tools for understanding new words
- read-academic-vocabulary -- Tier 2 and Tier 3 words essential for academic success
- read-figurative-language -- Metaphor, simile, idiom, hyperbole, and other non-literal language

### Comprehension & Meaning-Making (5 concepts)
- read-main-idea-details -- Identifying central ideas and distinguishing them from supporting details
- read-inferencing -- Drawing conclusions not directly stated using text evidence and prior knowledge
- read-text-structure -- Recognizing organizational patterns: cause-effect, compare-contrast, sequence, problem-solution
- read-summarizing -- Condensing text to its most essential ideas in one's own words
- read-monitoring-comprehension -- Self-checking understanding and applying fix-up strategies when meaning breaks down

### Critical Reading & Analysis (4 concepts)
- read-author-purpose-perspective -- Identifying why an author writes and how their viewpoint shapes the text
- read-argument-analysis -- Identifying claims, evidence, and reasoning in persuasive texts
- read-evaluating-bias -- Recognizing slant, selective use of evidence, and loaded language
- read-literary-analysis -- Analyzing character, plot, theme, symbolism, and narrative craft

### Reading Across the Curriculum (4 concepts)
- read-informational-text -- Reading nonfiction for information: features, structures, and strategies
- read-primary-sources -- Interpreting historical documents, speeches, and original records
- read-digital-reading -- Navigating hypertext, evaluating online sources, and reading multimodal texts
- read-technical-documents -- Reading manuals, instructions, scientific reports, and procedural texts

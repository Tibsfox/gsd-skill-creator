# Trades & Applied Skills

**Domain:** trades
**Source:** TRADE-101 Trades & Applied Skills Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of applied trades spanning tools and workshop safety, woodworking and fabrication, electrical basics, plumbing and HVAC, and project management -- building real-world capability through hands-on skill development

## Wings

- Tools, Safety & Workshop -- Tool identification, PPE, safety culture, workshop organization, first aid
- Woodworking & Fabrication -- Wood types, measurement, cutting, joining methods, finishing
- Electrical Basics -- Circuit concepts, Ohm's law, safe repairs, reading electrical diagrams
- Plumbing & HVAC -- Water systems, common repairs, HVAC basics, energy efficiency
- Project Management -- Planning, materials estimation, sequencing, quality control, troubleshooting

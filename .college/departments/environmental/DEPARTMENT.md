# Environmental Department

**Domain:** environmental
**Source:** ENVR-101 Environmental Science & Sustainability Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Ecosystems, Earth systems, human impacts, climate science, and sustainability across K-College -- evidence-based reasoning over activism, systems thinking over alarmism

## Wings

- Ecosystems & Biodiversity -- Life organization, food webs, energy flow, succession, biodiversity, species interactions, carrying capacity
- Earth Systems -- Atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere, biogeochemical cycles (carbon, nitrogen, water), plate tectonics
- Human Impacts -- Pollution, habitat destruction, deforestation, overfishing, invasive species, land use change
- Climate Science -- Greenhouse effect, climate forcing, feedback loops, evidence for change, regional impacts, attribution
- Sustainability & Solutions -- Renewable energy, conservation strategies, sustainable agriculture, policy tools, lifecycle analysis

## Entry Point

envr-ecosystem-organization

## Concepts

### Ecosystems & Biodiversity (4 concepts)
- envr-ecosystem-organization -- Organization of life from cells through ecosystems; energy flow through trophic levels
- envr-food-webs -- Producers, consumers, decomposers and the transfer of energy through feeding relationships
- envr-biodiversity-resilience -- Species richness as ecosystem insurance against disturbance and change
- envr-succession -- Primary and secondary succession: how ecosystems recover and change after disturbance

### Earth Systems (4 concepts)
- envr-carbon-cycle -- Carbon pools (atmosphere, ocean, biosphere, lithosphere) and fluxes between them
- envr-water-cycle -- Precipitation, evaporation, transpiration, infiltration, runoff -- global water distribution
- envr-nitrogen-cycle -- Fixation, nitrification, denitrification -- how nitrogen moves between living and non-living systems
- envr-atmosphere-layers -- Troposphere, stratosphere, ozone layer, greenhouse gas distribution and function

### Human Impacts (4 concepts)
- envr-pollution-types -- Air, water, soil, light, noise pollution -- sources, pathways, and effects
- envr-habitat-destruction -- Fragmentation, edge effects, minimum viable populations, extinction debt
- envr-invasive-species -- Pathways, impacts on native biodiversity, management strategies
- envr-climate-forcing -- Anthropogenic greenhouse gas emissions and their measured atmospheric effects

### Climate Science (4 concepts)
- envr-greenhouse-effect -- How infrared-absorbing gases trap heat: the radiative forcing mechanism
- envr-climate-feedbacks -- Ice-albedo, water vapor, cloud feedbacks -- amplifiers and dampeners of warming
- envr-climate-evidence -- Ice cores, temperature records, sea level rise, phenological shifts -- converging evidence
- envr-attribution-science -- How scientists determine that specific extreme events are made more likely by warming

### Sustainability & Solutions (4 concepts)
- envr-renewable-energy -- Solar, wind, hydro, geothermal -- capacity factors, intermittency, storage
- envr-conservation-strategies -- Protected areas, corridors, keystone species management, rewilding
- envr-sustainable-agriculture -- Permaculture, no-till, integrated pest management, food system sustainability
- envr-lifecycle-analysis -- Measuring environmental impact from raw material through disposal

### Atmospheric Radiation & Clouds (2 concepts)
- environmental-aerosol-indirect-erf -- IPCC AR6 effective radiative forcing from aerosol-cloud interactions (Twomey + Albrecht)

# Economics Department

**Domain:** economics
**Source:** ECON-101 Economics & Personal Finance Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Economic thinking, personal finance, and systems literacy across K-College -- from lemonade stands to global trade, emphasizing reasoning over terminology and real-world relevance over abstraction

## Wings

- Scarcity & Choice -- Scarcity as fundamental economic fact, wants vs. needs, opportunity cost, trade-offs, resource allocation
- Markets & Exchange -- Supply and demand, price mechanisms, competition, market structures, market failures, externalities
- Money & Banking -- Currency functions, banking systems, credit, interest rates, inflation, monetary policy
- Personal Finance -- Budgeting, saving, investing, compound interest, insurance, taxes, credit scores, financial planning
- Economic Systems -- Macroeconomics, GDP, unemployment, fiscal policy, global trade, comparative advantage

## Entry Point

econ-scarcity-choice

## Concepts

### Scarcity & Choice (4 concepts)
- econ-scarcity-choice -- Resources are finite; every choice forecloses alternatives -- the central economic fact
- econ-opportunity-cost -- The value of the next-best alternative forgone when making any decision
- econ-trade-offs -- Every allocation decision involves winners and losers -- no free lunch
- econ-marginal-thinking -- Decisions are made at the margin: one more unit, one more hour

### Markets & Exchange (4 concepts)
- econ-supply-demand -- How prices emerge from the intersection of buyer willingness and seller willingness
- econ-price-mechanism -- Prices as signals that coordinate economic activity without central planning
- econ-market-structures -- Perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition
- econ-market-failures -- Externalities, public goods, information asymmetry, when markets produce bad outcomes

### Money & Banking (4 concepts)
- econ-money-functions -- Medium of exchange, store of value, unit of account -- why money beats barter
- econ-banking-system -- Fractional reserve banking, money creation, the role of central banks
- econ-interest-rates -- Price of money: how rates affect saving, borrowing, and investment decisions
- econ-inflation -- Rising price levels, purchasing power erosion, the real vs. nominal distinction

### Personal Finance (4 concepts)
- econ-budgeting -- Income, expenses, tracking spending, living within means across income levels
- econ-compound-interest -- Time value of money: how interest compounds and why starting early matters
- econ-investing-basics -- Stocks, bonds, diversification, risk-return tradeoff, index investing
- econ-credit-scores -- How credit is built, what FICO measures, the cost of poor credit

### Economic Systems (4 concepts)
- econ-gdp-growth -- Gross domestic product as economic output measure, real vs. nominal, limitations
- econ-unemployment -- Types (frictional, structural, cyclical), measurement, the natural rate concept
- econ-fiscal-policy -- Government spending and taxation as tools for economic stabilization
- econ-comparative-advantage -- Why specialization and trade create gains even when one party is better at everything

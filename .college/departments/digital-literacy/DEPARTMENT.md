# Digital Literacy Department

**Domain:** digital-literacy
**Source:** DIGLIT-101 Digital Literacy & Information Science Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Digital systems, information evaluation, online safety, and algorithmic awareness across K-College -- not how to use apps, but how to think critically about information systems

## Wings

- Digital Foundations -- Hardware components, operating systems, networks, digital information representation, troubleshooting
- Information Literacy -- Search strategies, source credibility, fact-checking, misinformation tactics, media literacy, bias recognition
- Digital Communication -- Professional communication, email etiquette, collaboration tools, digital media creation, copyright
- Online Safety -- Password security, privacy settings, cyberbullying, digital footprint, scams and phishing, digital rights
- Algorithmic Awareness -- Data collection, algorithmic decision-making, personalization systems, AI/ML, algorithmic bias, data ethics

## Entry Point

diglit-hardware-components

## Concepts

### Digital Foundations (4 concepts)
- diglit-hardware-components -- CPU, RAM, storage, GPU, peripherals and their functions
- diglit-networks-internet -- How packets travel, TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP/HTTPS -- the internet as infrastructure
- diglit-operating-systems -- What OS does: process management, file systems, device drivers
- diglit-information-representation -- Binary, ASCII, pixels -- how computers represent everything as numbers

### Information Literacy (4 concepts)
- diglit-search-strategies -- Keywords, operators, filters -- getting better results from search engines
- diglit-source-credibility -- SIFT method (Stop, Investigate, Find better coverage, Trace claims)
- diglit-fact-checking -- Lateral reading, reverse image search, primary source verification
- diglit-misinformation-tactics -- Emotional manipulation, false context, impersonation, bot amplification

### Digital Communication (4 concepts)
- diglit-professional-communication -- Email etiquette, appropriate tone, audience awareness in digital contexts
- diglit-collaborative-tools -- Shared documents, version history, simultaneous editing, commenting workflows
- diglit-digital-media-creation -- Presentation design, multimedia, copyright-safe asset sourcing
- diglit-copyright-attribution -- Creative Commons, fair use, how to cite digital sources correctly

### Online Safety (4 concepts)
- diglit-password-security -- Entropy, password managers, multi-factor authentication, phishing resistance
- diglit-privacy-management -- Privacy settings, data minimization, what platforms collect and why
- diglit-digital-footprint -- Permanence of digital actions, search results, reputation management
- diglit-cyberbullying-response -- Recognition, documentation, reporting, and bystander intervention

### Algorithmic Awareness (4 concepts)
- diglit-data-collection -- What apps collect, cookies, trackers, advertising ecosystems
- diglit-recommendation-systems -- How Netflix, YouTube, social feeds are personalized and why
- diglit-algorithmic-bias -- Facial recognition failures, credit score disparities, hiring algorithm problems
- diglit-ai-limitations -- What AI can and cannot do, hallucination, the human in the loop

# Philosophy

**Domain:** philosophy
**Source:** PHILO-101 Philosophy Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of philosophical inquiry spanning wonder and questioning, logic and argumentation, ethics, epistemology, and aesthetics -- the art of thinking well across K-College

## Wings

- Wonder & Questioning -- Philosophical inquiry, Socratic method, thought experiments, intellectual humility
- Logic & Reasoning -- Argument structure, deductive/inductive reasoning, fallacies, validity and soundness
- Ethics -- Consequentialism, deontology, virtue ethics, applied ethics, moral dilemmas
- Epistemology -- Justified true belief, skepticism, induction, scientific knowledge, social epistemology
- Aesthetics -- Beauty, taste, artistic value, aesthetic experience, philosophy of art

## Entry Point

philo-socratic-method

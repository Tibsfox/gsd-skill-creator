# Problem Solving

**Domain:** problem-solving
**Source:** PROB-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational problem-solving skills spanning problem comprehension, strategic toolkits, creative and lateral thinking, collaborative approaches, and navigating complex wicked problems -- building the capacity to tackle challenges across every domain

## Wings

- Understanding Problems -- Problem representation, identifying knowns and unknowns, constraints versus goals, problem type recognition
- Problem-Solving Strategies -- Working backwards, drawing diagrams, pattern recognition, simplification, systematic listing
- Creative & Lateral Thinking -- Brainstorming, lateral thinking, analogical reasoning, reframing, challenging assumptions
- Collaborative Problem Solving -- Group processes, dividing cognitive work, building on ideas, achieving collective breakthroughs
- Complex & Wicked Problems -- Systems thinking, adaptive management, uncertainty, incomplete information, ethical dimensions

## Entry Point

prob-problem-representation

## Concepts

### Understanding Problems (4 concepts)
- prob-problem-representation -- Restating a problem in your own words, drawing diagrams, and building an accurate mental model
- prob-knowns-unknowns -- Systematically identifying what is given, what is needed, and what constraints apply
- prob-problem-types -- Recognizing categories of problems: well-defined, ill-defined, optimization, decision, puzzle
- prob-goal-decomposition -- Breaking a large problem into smaller sub-problems that can be solved independently

### Problem-Solving Strategies (5 concepts)
- prob-working-backwards -- Starting from the goal and working backwards to find the steps needed
- prob-pattern-recognition -- Finding analogous problems already solved and applying the same structure
- prob-simplification -- Solving a simpler version of the problem first to gain insight
- prob-systematic-listing -- Organizing all possibilities methodically to ensure nothing is missed
- prob-trial-error-iteration -- Structured experimentation: try, evaluate, adjust, and repeat

### Creative & Lateral Thinking (4 concepts)
- prob-brainstorming -- Generating large quantities of ideas without judgment before evaluating
- prob-lateral-thinking -- Using indirect and unconventional approaches to bypass mental blocks
- prob-analogical-reasoning -- Drawing solutions from distant domains by recognizing structural similarities
- prob-assumption-challenging -- Questioning taken-for-granted constraints to open up solution space

### Collaborative Problem Solving (4 concepts)
- prob-group-problem-process -- Shared understanding, role allocation, and structured group problem-solving
- prob-cognitive-division -- Distributing problem components to leverage different expertise
- prob-idea-building -- Building on rather than dismissing teammates' suggestions
- prob-conflict-in-groups -- Productive disagreement, resolving impasses, and reaching workable consensus

### Complex & Wicked Problems (4 concepts)
- prob-systems-thinking -- Seeing interdependencies, feedback loops, and emergent behavior in complex systems
- prob-adaptive-management -- Learning and adjusting strategies as a complex situation evolves
- prob-uncertainty-management -- Making decisions with incomplete information and managing risk
- prob-ethics-in-problems -- Recognizing stakeholder interests, trade-offs, and ethical dimensions of complex solutions

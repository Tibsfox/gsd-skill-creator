# Home Economics & Life Skills

**Domain:** home-economics
**Source:** DOMESTIC-101 Home Economics Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of practical life skills spanning kitchen and cooking, textiles and clothing care, home management, financial literacy, and childcare and community -- the indispensable skills for independent adult life

## Wings

- Kitchen Skills & Cooking -- Kitchen safety, cooking techniques, meal planning, food safety, cultural foodways
- Textiles, Clothing & Care -- Sewing, mending, laundry, care labels, sustainable fashion
- Home Management -- Cleaning systems, basic maintenance, organization, energy efficiency
- Financial Literacy -- Budgeting, banking, saving, investing, avoiding debt traps
- Childcare & Community -- Child development, first aid, elder care, mutual aid

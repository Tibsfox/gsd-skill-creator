# Theology & Religion Studies

**Domain:** theology
**Source:** THEO-101 Theology & Religion Studies Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of religion and theological inquiry spanning sacred narratives, world religions, ritual practices, ethics and justice, and ultimate questions -- approached with scholarly respect and comparative methodology

## Wings

- Stories & Traditions -- Sacred narratives, creation stories, parables, flood traditions, oral transmission
- World Religions Survey -- Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Hinduism, Buddhism, Sikhism, indigenous traditions
- Sacred Practices -- Ritual, prayer, meditation, pilgrimage, fasting, communal worship
- Ethics & Justice -- Religious approaches to justice, dignity, care for the poor, environmental responsibility
- Ultimate Questions -- Theodicy, afterlife traditions, mysticism, meaning, existence of God

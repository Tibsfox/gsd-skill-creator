# Languages Department

**Domain:** languages
**Source:** LANG-101 World Languages Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** How to learn any language: sound, grammar, vocabulary, communication, and culture -- language learning as meta-skill applicable to any target language

## Wings

- Sound Systems -- Phoneme inventories, IPA notation, ear training, pronunciation, suprasegmental features (tone, stress, intonation)
- Grammar Patterns -- Word order typology, morphology, agreement systems, syntactic structures, patterns across language families
- Vocabulary & Reading -- High-frequency vocabulary, spaced repetition, cognates, word families, reading progression from controlled to authentic
- Speaking & Listening -- Listening comprehension, conversation strategies, intelligible speech, register and formality, repair strategies
- Language & Culture -- Language as cultural reflection, linguistic relativity, multilingual identity, code-switching, language diversity and power

## Entry Point

lang-phoneme-inventory

## Concepts

### Sound Systems (4 concepts)
- lang-phoneme-inventory -- The distinct sounds of a language and how they contrast to create meaning
- lang-ipa-notation -- International Phonetic Alphabet: universal system for transcribing any language's sounds
- lang-ear-training -- Perceiving non-native phonemes through focused listening and discrimination practice
- lang-suprasegmentals -- Tone, stress, and intonation patterns that carry meaning above the phoneme level

### Grammar Patterns (4 concepts)
- lang-word-order-typology -- SOV, SVO, VSO orders and what they reveal about language structure
- lang-morphology -- How words are built from roots, prefixes, suffixes, and inflections
- lang-agreement-systems -- How verbs, nouns, and adjectives agree in gender, number, case
- lang-syntactic-structures -- Phrase structure, embedded clauses, and how sentences nest

### Vocabulary & Reading (4 concepts)
- lang-high-frequency-words -- The 1000-2000 most common words that unlock 85-95% of everyday text
- lang-spaced-repetition -- The forgetting curve and optimal review intervals for vocabulary retention
- lang-cognates-word-families -- Recognizing related words across languages to accelerate vocabulary growth
- lang-reading-progression -- Moving from controlled readers to authentic texts through graded exposure

### Speaking & Listening (4 concepts)
- lang-listening-comprehension -- Parsing natural speech rate, connected speech phenomena, discourse markers
- lang-conversation-strategies -- Turn-taking, clarification requests, topic management in real conversation
- lang-intelligible-speech -- Pronunciation accuracy vs. fluency -- what actually matters for communication
- lang-formality-register -- Formal vs. informal, written vs. spoken register differences across languages

### Language & Culture (4 concepts)
- lang-language-culture-link -- How vocabulary, grammar, and pragmatics encode cultural values
- lang-linguistic-relativity -- How language shapes thought: color terms, spatial reference, evidentiality
- lang-multilingual-identity -- Code-switching as sophisticated behavior, heritage languages, identity and language choice
- lang-language-diversity -- 7000 living languages, endangered languages, language rights and power dynamics

# Materials & Fabrication

**Domain:** materials
**Source:** MFAB-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational materials science and fabrication knowledge spanning material properties and testing, the four material families, fabrication processes, design for manufacturing, and sustainability and lifecycle thinking -- connecting the physical world to engineering decisions

## Wings

- Material Properties & Testing -- Mechanical, thermal, electrical, and optical properties; standardized testing methods
- Materials Families -- Metals and alloys, polymers, ceramics and glasses, composites: structures and characteristics
- Fabrication & Manufacturing Processes -- Casting, forging, machining, joining, additive manufacturing, surface finishing
- Design for Manufacturing -- Tolerances, design for assembly, cost considerations, reliability, design-manufacturing trade-offs
- Sustainability & Lifecycle -- Lifecycle assessment, embodied energy, recycling, material scarcity, circular economy

- mfab-polymers -- Thermoplastics, thermosets, and elastomers: chain structure, processing, and applications

- mfab-casting -- Pouring liquid material into molds: sand casting, die casting, investment casting

- mfab-machining -- Removing material by cutting: turning, milling, drilling, and grinding
- mfab-joining -- Welding, brazing, soldering, adhesive bonding, and mechanical fastening
- mfab-additive-manufacturing -- 3D printing processes (FDM, SLA, SLS) and their material and geometric capabilities

- mfab-design-for-reliability -- Designing against failure modes: fatigue, corrosion, wear, and thermal cycling

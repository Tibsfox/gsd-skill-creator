# Communication

**Domain:** communication
**Source:** COMM-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational oral and interpersonal communication skills spanning speaking, listening, nonverbal presence, structured presentation, collaborative discussion, and adapting communication across contexts and audiences

## Wings

- Foundations of Speaking & Listening -- Active listening, clear speaking, articulation, pace, tone, and respectful dialogue
- Nonverbal Communication & Presence -- Body language, facial expression, gesture, eye contact, proxemics, vocal paralanguage
- Structured Presentation -- Speech organization, introductions, transitions, visual aids, audience adaptation, managing nerves
- Discussion, Debate & Collaboration -- Facilitated discussion, structured debate, consensus-building, respectful disagreement
- Communication Across Contexts -- Formal versus informal registers, intercultural communication, digital communication, professional settings

## Entry Point

comm-active-listening

## Concepts

### Foundations of Speaking & Listening (4 concepts)
- comm-active-listening -- Attending fully, paraphrasing, asking clarifying questions, and responding thoughtfully
- comm-voice-articulation -- Volume, pace, clarity, and pronunciation for effective spoken delivery
- comm-conversation-skills -- Turn-taking, interruption norms, topic maintenance, and conversational repair
- comm-listening-comprehension -- Understanding spoken messages, identifying main ideas, and retaining key information

### Nonverbal Communication & Presence (4 concepts)
- comm-body-language -- Posture, gesture, and movement as communication signals
- comm-eye-contact-expression -- Facial expression and eye contact in building connection and conveying meaning
- comm-proxemics -- Personal space, physical distance, and cultural variation in spatial norms
- comm-vocal-paralanguage -- Tone, pitch, rate, volume, and pauses as meaning-makers beyond words

### Structured Presentation (4 concepts)
- comm-presentation-structure -- Organizing a speech with clear introduction, body, and conclusion
- comm-audience-adaptation -- Tailoring content, language, and delivery to a specific audience and purpose
- comm-visual-aids -- Using slides, objects, and multimedia to support (not replace) the spoken message
- comm-managing-presentation-anxiety -- Strategies for turning nerves into effective energy

### Discussion, Debate & Collaboration (4 concepts)
- comm-facilitated-discussion -- Techniques for leading and participating in productive group discussions
- comm-structured-debate -- Argumentation, rebuttal, and the rules of formal debate formats
- comm-consensus-building -- Finding common ground, synthesizing views, and reaching group decisions
- comm-respectful-disagreement -- Disagreeing with ideas while respecting people

### Communication Across Contexts (4 concepts)
- comm-register-formality -- Adjusting language formality to match social and professional contexts
- comm-intercultural-communication -- Navigating cultural differences in communication style, norms, and expectations
- comm-digital-communication -- Clarity, tone, and professionalism in email, messaging, and online communication
- comm-professional-communication -- Job interviews, workplace communication, and professional relationship-building

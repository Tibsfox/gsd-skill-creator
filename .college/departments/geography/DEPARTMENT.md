# Geography

**Domain:** geography
**Source:** GEO-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational geography spanning Earth's physical systems, human cultural landscapes, spatial analysis and GIS, astronomy, and climate science -- developing spatial thinking and understanding of Earth as a complex interconnected system

## Wings

- Earth Systems & Physical Geography -- Plate tectonics, landforms, biomes, hydrosphere, and interactions among Earth's systems
- Human Geography & Cultural Landscapes -- Population, migration, cultural diffusion, urbanization, economic geography, geopolitics
- Maps, Spatial Analysis & GIS -- Map projections, coordinates, thematic mapping, remote sensing, Geographic Information Systems
- Astronomy & Our Place in Space -- Solar system, seasons, lunar cycles, stellar evolution, galaxies, space exploration
- Climate, Weather & Environmental Change -- Weather systems, atmospheric circulation, climate zones, climate change science

## Entry Point

geo-plate-tectonics

## Concepts

### Earth Systems & Physical Geography (4 concepts)
- geo-plate-tectonics -- Continental drift, plate boundaries, earthquakes, volcanoes, and mountain building
- geo-landforms-erosion -- Formation of mountains, valleys, rivers, coastlines, and the role of erosion
- geo-biomes -- Major terrestrial biomes (tropical, desert, tundra, etc.) and their climate-vegetation relationships
- geo-hydrosphere -- Water cycle, freshwater systems, ocean currents, and groundwater

### Human Geography & Cultural Landscapes (4 concepts)
- geo-population-migration -- Population distribution, demographic transition, push-pull migration factors
- geo-cultural-diffusion -- How ideas, practices, and innovations spread across space and cultures
- geo-urbanization -- City growth, urban systems, land use patterns, and urban challenges
- geo-economic-geography -- Resource distribution, global trade, development patterns, and economic regions

### Maps, Spatial Analysis & GIS (4 concepts)
- geo-map-projections -- How spherical Earth is represented on flat surfaces; distortion and trade-offs
- geo-coordinates-scale -- Latitude, longitude, map scale, and understanding spatial relationships
- geo-thematic-mapping -- Choropleth, dot, and proportional symbol maps for displaying geographic data
- geo-gis-remote-sensing -- Geographic Information Systems, satellite imagery, and spatial data analysis

### Astronomy & Our Place in Space (4 concepts)
- geo-solar-system -- Planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and the structure of our solar system
- geo-seasons-tides -- Earth's axial tilt, the cause of seasons, and tidal forces from Moon and Sun
- geo-stellar-evolution -- Life cycles of stars from nebula to white dwarf, neutron star, or black hole
- geo-galaxies-universe -- The Milky Way, galaxy types, the expanding universe, and the cosmic scale

### Climate, Weather & Environmental Change (5 concepts)
- geo-weather-systems -- Air masses, fronts, pressure systems, storms, and day-to-day weather
- geo-atmospheric-circulation -- Hadley cells, trade winds, jet streams, and global wind patterns
- geo-climate-zones -- Koppen classification, factors determining regional climate
- geo-climate-change-science -- Greenhouse effect, feedback loops, observed changes, and projections
- geo-environmental-impact -- Deforestation, desertification, habitat loss, and human modification of landscapes

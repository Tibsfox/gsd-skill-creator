# Spatial Computing

**Domain:** spatial-computing
**Source:** Domain knowledge -- Minecraft as spatial computing and engineering education platform
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Spatial reasoning, engineering design, and systems thinking through block-based world-building -- five wings progressing from 3D navigation to large-scale collaborative engineering projects

## Wings

- Spatial Foundations -- Coordinate systems, 3D navigation, spatial reasoning, geometric structures in block space
- Building & Architecture -- Structural principles, material selection, blueprint-to-build translation, aesthetic design
- Redstone Engineering -- Boolean logic via redstone dust, combinational circuits, timing, repeaters, comparators, pistons
- Systems & Automation -- Automated farms, item sorting networks, transportation systems, production chains, resource flows
- Collaborative Design -- Server project planning, role specialization, iterative build cycles, community construction

### Spatial Foundations (3 concepts)

- spatial-reasoning-3d -- Mentally rotating structures, visualizing cross-sections, planning multi-floor builds
- spatial-geometric-structures -- Circles and spheres in block space, symmetry, pixel art scaling, voxel geometry

### Building & Architecture (3 concepts)
- spatial-structural-principles -- Load-bearing patterns, pillar spacing, arch construction, spanning gaps without supports
- spatial-material-properties -- Block hardness, blast resistance, aesthetic textures, biome-appropriate materials
- spatial-blueprint-design -- Schematic planning on paper before building, layer-by-layer construction approach

### Redstone Engineering (3 concepts)
- spatial-logic-gates-redstone -- AND/OR/NOT/NAND gates built with redstone dust, torches, and repeaters
- spatial-circuit-timing -- Repeater delay, comparator subtraction mode, clock circuits, monostable circuits
- spatial-signal-propagation -- Redstone signal strength (0-15), power loss over distance, repeater amplification

### Systems & Automation (3 concepts)
- spatial-automated-farms -- Crop farms with water flow, mob farms with fall damage, tree farms with pistons
- spatial-item-transport-sorting -- Hopper chains, dropper arrays, item sorters using comparators and named items
- spatial-resource-production-chains -- Smelting arrays, auto-crafting setups, bulk storage with barrel systems

### Collaborative Design (3 concepts)
- spatial-server-project-planning -- Town planning, district zoning, infrastructure before aesthetics
- spatial-role-specialization -- Architect, engineer, resource gatherer, decorator -- dividing large builds by skill
- spatial-iterative-build-process -- Build basic version, get feedback, tear down and rebuild better -- engineering mindset

# Technology

**Domain:** technology
**Source:** TECH-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational technology literacy spanning tools and simple machines, systems and processes, the technology design cycle, digital foundations, and technology's relationship with society -- developing the understanding and agency to use, evaluate, and create technology responsibly

## Wings

- Tools & Simple Machines -- Hand and power tools, the six simple machines, mechanical advantage, history of tool use
- Systems & Processes -- System components (input, process, output, feedback), system modeling, process flow, optimization
- Design & Engineering Process -- Needs assessment, design brief, concept development, modeling, prototyping, testing
- Digital Technology Foundations -- Binary and data representation, how computers work, networking, software, security basics
- Technology & Society -- History of transformative technologies, digital rights, privacy, environmental impact, responsible innovation

## Entry Point

tech-simple-machines

## Concepts

### Tools & Simple Machines (4 concepts)
- tech-simple-machines -- The six simple machines (lever, wheel, pulley, inclined plane, wedge, screw) and mechanical advantage
- tech-hand-power-tools -- Safe and effective use of hand tools and power tools for making and repairing
- tech-mechanical-advantage -- How tools multiply force or distance and the trade-offs involved
- tech-history-of-tools -- How tool development has shaped human history from stone tools to industrial machinery

### Systems & Processes (4 concepts)
- tech-system-components -- Inputs, processes, outputs, and feedback loops as the anatomy of any system
- tech-system-modeling -- Using diagrams, flowcharts, and models to represent and understand systems
- tech-process-flow -- Sequential and parallel processes, bottlenecks, and optimizing process efficiency
- tech-system-optimization -- Improving system performance by adjusting inputs, processes, or feedback

### Design & Engineering Process (4 concepts)
- tech-design-brief -- Defining the problem, constraints, criteria, and user needs before designing
- tech-concept-development -- Generating multiple design alternatives before committing to one solution
- tech-prototyping-testing-tech -- Building and testing models to evaluate design alternatives
- tech-design-iteration -- Using test results to improve designs through multiple cycles of refinement

### Digital Technology Foundations (5 concepts)
- tech-binary-data -- How computers represent all information as binary (0s and 1s) patterns
- tech-computer-architecture -- CPU, memory, storage, and how they work together to process information
- tech-networking-basics -- How devices connect and communicate via the internet and other networks
- tech-software-concepts -- Programs, algorithms, operating systems, and the software layer of technology
- tech-information-security -- Threats, encryption, authentication, and basic practices for staying secure

### Technology & Society (4 concepts)
- tech-transformative-technologies -- Case studies of technologies that changed how people live and work
- tech-digital-rights-privacy -- Data privacy, surveillance, digital rights, and who controls personal information
- tech-environmental-impact-tech -- Energy consumption, e-waste, and the ecological footprint of digital technology
- tech-responsible-innovation -- Ethical frameworks for creating technology that benefits people without causing harm

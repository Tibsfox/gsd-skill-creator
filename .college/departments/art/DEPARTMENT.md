# Visual Arts

**Domain:** art
**Source:** ART-101 Visual Arts Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of visual arts education spanning observation, color theory, creative expression across media, art history and cultural context, and creative process -- explorable as College code with progressive skill development

## Wings

- Seeing & Drawing -- Observation skills, contour and gesture drawing, proportion, shading, spatial reasoning
- Color, Value & Composition -- Color theory, hue, saturation, value scales, balance, emphasis, compositional principles
- Materials & Making -- Pencil, charcoal, watercolor, acrylic, clay, printmaking, collage, digital art
- Art in Context -- Art history movements, cultural traditions, artists' biographies, art as social commentary
- Creative Process & Portfolio -- Brainstorming, sketchbooks, critique circles, artist statements, portfolio curation

## Entry Point

art-observational-drawing

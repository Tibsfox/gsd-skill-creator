# Business

**Domain:** business
**Source:** BUS-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational business knowledge spanning economic organization and markets, business structures and entrepreneurship, finance and accounting, legal systems and contracts, and business ethics and governance -- building commercial literacy for creators, employees, and citizens

## Wings

- Economic Organization & Markets -- Supply and demand, price signals, market structures, market failures, government role
- Business Structures & Entrepreneurship -- Sole proprietorships, partnerships, corporations, entrepreneurial process, business planning
- Finance & Accounting Fundamentals -- Business financing, cost-benefit analysis, break-even, investment appraisal, financial planning
- Contracts, Rights & Legal Systems -- Contract formation, property rights, consumer protection, employment law, intellectual property
- Ethics, Governance & Social Responsibility -- Stakeholder theory, corporate governance, CSR, sustainability, business and society

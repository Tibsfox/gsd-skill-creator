# Nature Studies & Naturalism

**Domain:** nature-studies
**Source:** NATURE-101 Nature Studies Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of field naturalism spanning outdoor observation, plant and fungi identification, animal and bird studies, ecology and habitats, and citizen science contribution -- building deep connection to the natural world through patient observation

## Wings

- Outdoor Observation -- Field skills, nature journaling, sit spot practice, weather awareness, seasonal phenology
- Plants & Fungi -- Tree and wildflower identification, fungal ecology, life cycles, mycorrhizal networks
- Animals & Birds -- Birding by ear and sight, mammal tracking, reptile and insect natural history
- Ecology & Habitats -- Food webs, energy flow, succession, keystone species, habitat types
- Citizen Science -- iNaturalist, eBird, phenology monitoring, contributing to real research

**Domain:** mind-body
**Status:** Active

- Meditation -- Mindfulness, concentration, zazen, body scan, walking meditation, metta
- Yoga -- Poses, sequences, breath-movement linking, styles from Hatha to Ashtanga
- Pilates -- Powerhouse, principles, mat work, alignment, rehabilitation applications
- Martial Arts -- History, stances, strikes, forms, style overviews across cultures
- Tai Chi -- Principles, qigong, Beijing 24 form, push hands concepts
- Relaxation -- PMR, yoga nidra, myofascial release, stretching, sleep hygiene, nervous system
- Philosophy -- Zen, Taoism, Yoga Sutras, Bushido/wude, mindfulness in daily life, shoshin

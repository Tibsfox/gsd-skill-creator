# Nutrition Department

**Domain:** nutrition
**Source:** NUTR-101 Nutrition & Health Sciences Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Nutrition science, food systems, and nutritional literacy from K-College -- evidence-based understanding of how food sustains the body, without diet culture messaging

**Note:** This department covers nutritional science comprehensively (digestion, food systems, critical literacy). The culinary-arts department has a distinct Nutrition wing focused on how cooking methods affect nutrient content. Cross-references between these two are established in Phase 25.

## Wings

- Nutrients & Functions -- Macronutrients, micronutrients, water, nutrient functions in the body, reading nutrition labels
- Digestion & Body Systems -- Digestive system anatomy, nutrient absorption, gut microbiome, metabolism, energy balance
- Food Sources & Systems -- Where food comes from: agriculture, food processing, global food systems, food access and equity
- Nutritional Literacy -- Evaluating dietary claims, understanding research design, diet culture, food marketing, evidence-based guidance
- Health Integration -- Nutrition across the lifespan, sports nutrition, chronic disease prevention, food as medicine principles

## Entry Point

nutr-macronutrients

## Concepts

### Nutrients & Functions (4 concepts)
- nutr-macronutrients -- Proteins (4 cal/g, building blocks), carbohydrates (4 cal/g, primary fuel), fats (9 cal/g, hormones/insulation)
- nutr-micronutrients -- Vitamins (fat-soluble A/D/E/K vs. water-soluble B/C) and minerals (macro vs. trace) -- small amounts, essential functions
- nutr-water-electrolytes -- Water as universal solvent, electrolyte balance (sodium, potassium, magnesium), hydration assessment
- nutr-nutrient-interactions -- Vitamin C enhancing iron absorption, fat-soluble vitamin absorption with fat, calcium-vitamin D synergy

### Digestion & Body Systems (4 concepts)
- nutr-digestive-system -- Mouth through large intestine: mechanical and chemical digestion of each macronutrient
- nutr-nutrient-absorption -- How nutrients cross intestinal epithelia: passive diffusion, active transport, enterohepatic circulation
- nutr-gut-microbiome -- 38 trillion bacteria in the colon, fiber fermentation, short-chain fatty acids, gut-brain axis
- nutr-energy-metabolism -- ATP production pathways: glycolysis, Krebs cycle, oxidative phosphorylation -- how cells extract energy from food

### Food Sources & Systems (4 concepts)
- nutr-food-sources -- Whole foods vs. processed: nutrient density, fortification, bioavailability across food categories
- nutr-food-processing -- Mechanical, thermal, chemical processing effects on nutrient content and food safety
- nutr-food-systems -- Farm to fork: agriculture, distribution, retail, waste -- environmental and nutritional implications
- nutr-food-access -- Food deserts, food insecurity, economic determinants of diet, equity in food systems

### Nutritional Literacy (4 concepts)
- nutr-evaluating-claims -- Correlation vs. causation in nutrition studies, study design hierarchy, single-study limitations
- nutr-diet-culture -- Weight-centric vs. health-centric frameworks, intuitive eating, anti-diet perspectives, body weight science
- nutr-food-marketing -- Health halos, front-of-package claims, supplement industry, how marketing shapes food choices
- nutr-evidence-based-guidance -- Dietary guidelines, Mediterranean and DASH patterns, what the evidence actually supports

### Health Integration (4 concepts)
- nutr-lifespan-nutrition -- Nutrient needs from infancy through aging: growth, bone density, muscle mass, cognitive health
- nutr-sports-nutrition -- Fueling for performance: carbohydrate loading, protein timing, hydration for exercise
- nutr-chronic-disease -- Diet-disease relationships: cardiovascular disease, type 2 diabetes, metabolic syndrome
- nutr-food-as-medicine -- Anti-inflammatory foods, phytonutrients, therapeutic diets -- evidence and limitations

# Mathematics

**Domain:** math
**Source:** MATH-101 Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Foundational mathematics spanning number sense, algebraic thinking, geometry, data analysis, probability, and statistical inference -- taught through exploration, pattern recognition, and real-world problem solving across all grade levels

## Wings

- Number & Operations -- Quantity, place value, operations, computational fluency from subitizing through multi-digit arithmetic
- Patterns & Algebraic Thinking -- Pattern recognition, variables, equations, functional relationships, and algebraic reasoning
- Geometry & Spatial Thinking -- Shapes, transformations, measurement, coordinate geometry, trigonometry, and 3D visualization
- Data & Probability -- Data collection, graphical representation, probability foundations, experimental versus theoretical probability
- Statistics & Inference -- Measures of center and spread, sampling, hypothesis testing, correlation versus causation, statistical modeling

## Entry Point

math-number-cardinality

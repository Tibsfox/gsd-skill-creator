# Astronomy & Cosmology

**Domain:** astronomy
**Source:** ASTRO-101 Astronomy Foundational Knowledge Pack
**Status:** Active
**Purpose:** Five wings of astronomy spanning naked-eye sky observation, Earth-Moon-Sun geometry, stellar physics, solar system science, and cosmology -- from first stargazing to deep space and cosmic origins

## Wings

- Observing the Sky -- Constellations, celestial coordinates, planisphere, stellar magnitude, planet tracking
- Earth-Moon-Sun System -- Seasons, Moon phases, eclipses, tides, orbital mechanics
- Stellar Physics -- HR diagram, nuclear fusion, stellar evolution, spectroscopy, distance ladder
- Solar System -- Planetary science, Kepler's laws, comets, space exploration, formation
- Cosmology -- Big Bang, cosmic microwave background, dark matter, dark energy, expansion

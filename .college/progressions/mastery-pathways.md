## Domain-Specific Pathways

### Mathematical Thinking Progression

#### Level 1: Pattern Recognition (2-4 weeks)
```yaml
entry_requirements: "Basic counting and comparison skills"

core_competencies:
  - identifies_numerical_patterns (2, 4, 6, 8... arithmetic sequences)
  - recognizes_geometric_patterns (circles, squares, triangles in environment)
  - notices_measurement_relationships (taller/shorter, heavier/lighter)

validation_criteria:
  pattern_extension: "Correctly continues 3 different pattern types"
  pattern_creation: "Designs original pattern following given rule"
  pattern_explanation: "Describes pattern rule in own words"

assessment_methods:
  hands_on_demonstration: "Using physical objects to show pattern understanding"
  verbal_explanation: "Explaining pattern thinking to peer or teacher"
  creative_application: "Finding patterns in art, music, or nature"

sustainable_pace_indicators:
  - enjoys_pattern_activities (engagement maintained throughout period)
  - shows_confidence_with_basic_patterns (self-initiated pattern exploration)
  - applies_pattern_thinking_naturally (transfers to other activities)

exit_gateway: "Can identify, extend, and create patterns across visual, numerical, and natural domains"
```

#### Level 2: Rule Discovery (4-8 weeks)
```yaml
entry_requirements: "Demonstrated Level 1 competence"

core_competencies:
  - articulates_pattern_rules (states underlying principle behind patterns)
  - predicts_pattern_behavior (forecasts what comes next in complex sequences)
  - connects_rules_across_contexts (sees same rule in different situations)

validation_criteria:
  rule_articulation: "States pattern rules precisely in 2+ different notations"
  predictive_accuracy: ">90% accuracy in pattern predictions"
  cross_context_recognition: "Identifies same rule in 3+ different domains"

assessment_methods:
  rule_explanation_challenge: "Teach pattern rule to someone else"
  prediction_verification: "Make predictions and test them systematically"
  connection_mapping: "Find examples of same rule in art, nature, and numbers"

grove_integration:
  quantitative_grove: "Mathematical pattern rules in data and equations"
  creative_expression: "Pattern rules in music rhythm and visual composition"
  living_systems: "Pattern rules in biological growth and reproduction"

sustainable_pace_indicators:
  - builds_on_level_1_naturally (doesn't lose pattern recognition skills)
  - shows_curiosity_about_why_patterns_work (intrinsic motivation developing)
  - applies_rule_thinking_spontaneously (transfers without prompting)

exit_gateway: "Can discover, articulate, and apply pattern rules across multiple domains"
```

#### Level 3: System Building (8-16 weeks)
```yaml
entry_requirements: "Demonstrated Level 2 competence"

core_competencies:
  - combines_multiple_rules (creates complex systems from simple principles)
  - designs_functional_systems (builds systems that accomplish specific goals)
  - troubleshoots_system_failures (diagnoses and fixes system problems)

validation_criteria:
  system_construction: "Builds working system using 3+ different rules"
  goal_achievement: "System reliably produces intended outcomes"
  problem_diagnosis: "Identifies and corrects system malfunctions"

assessment_methods:
  design_challenge: "Create system to solve specific real-world problem"
  peer_collaboration: "Work with others to build complex system"
  iteration_improvement: "Refine system based on testing and feedback"

grove_integration:
  built_environment: "Design structures using mathematical principles"
  information_technology: "Create algorithms combining logical rules"
  living_systems: "Model ecosystem interactions using mathematical relationships"

sustainable_pace_indicators:
  - maintains_rule_discovery_skills (can still work with individual rules)
  - shows_persistence_with_complex_problems (doesn't give up when systems fail)
  - demonstrates_collaborative_system_building (works well with others)

exit_gateway: "Can design, build, and troubleshoot complex systems using mathematical principles"
```

#### Level 4: Cross-Domain Connection (1-2 years)
```yaml
entry_requirements: "Demonstrated Level 3 competence in math + competence in 2+ other domains"

core_competencies:
  - recognizes_mathematical_structures_in_other_domains (sees algebra in physics, geometry in art)
  - translates_between_domain_languages (explains physics using math, math using art)
  - solves_interdisciplinary_problems (applies math to biology, economics, psychology)

validation_criteria:
  cross_domain_recognition: "Identifies mathematical structures in 4+ non-math contexts"
  translation_fluency: "Explains same concept using vocabulary from 3+ domains"
  interdisciplinary_problem_solving: "Successfully completes projects requiring 2+ domains"

assessment_methods:
  domain_translation_portfolio: "Collection of explanations showing cross-domain connections"
  collaborative_grove_projects: "Substantial contributions to inter-grove initiatives"
  teaching_demonstration: "Teaches mathematical concepts using non-mathematical analogies"

grove_integration:
  - leads_cross_grove_collaborations (serves as mathematical consultant to other groves)
  - mentors_earlier_level_learners (helps others see mathematical connections)
  - contributes_to_real_world_applications (applies math to community problems)

sustainable_pace_indicators:
  - retains_system_building_capabilities (can still construct mathematical systems)
  - shows_enthusiasm_for_connection_making (actively seeks cross-domain relationships)
  - demonstrates_teaching_and_mentoring_skills (helps others advance)

exit_gateway: "Can serve as mathematical consultant across multiple domains and teach others to see mathematical connections"
```

#### Level 5: Innovation Synthesis (2+ years)
```yaml
entry_requirements: "Demonstrated Level 4 competence + significant experience in real-world applications"

core_competencies:
  - creates_new_mathematical_approaches (develops novel methods for existing problems)
  - identifies_new_application_domains (finds unexplored connections between math and other fields)
  - contributes_to_mathematical_knowledge (adds to the body of mathematical understanding)

validation_criteria:
  novel_method_creation: "Develops new approach that improves on existing methods"
  pioneering_application: "Successfully applies math in previously unexplored domain"
  knowledge_contribution: "Creates new understanding that others can build upon"

assessment_methods:
  research_project: "Extended investigation culminating in new insights"
  peer_review: "Work evaluated and validated by other Level 4-5 practitioners"
  community_impact: "Demonstrated improvement in real-world outcomes"

grove_integration:
  - initiates_new_grove_focuses (identifies emerging challenges requiring mathematical insight)
  - establishes_external_partnerships (connects college work with research institutions)
  - shapes_curriculum_evolution (influences how mathematical thinking is taught)

sustainable_pace_indicators:
  - maintains_cross_domain_connection_abilities (can still work effectively across domains)
  - shows_commitment_to_knowledge_sharing (actively contributes to community learning)
  - demonstrates_long_term_project_persistence (sustains work over years)

exit_gateway: "Recognized as mathematical innovator contributing new knowledge and approaches to the field"
```

## Grove-Specific Pathways

### Living Systems Grove Progression

#### Level 1: Observer (Natural History Foundation)
```yaml
timeline: "6-10 weeks"
core_focus: "Developing acute observation skills and natural curiosity"

competencies:
  - systematic_observation (notices details, changes, patterns in living systems)
  - respectful_interaction (approaches living systems without harm)
  - question_formation (asks investigable questions about natural phenomena)

validation_through:
  nature_journal: "Daily observations recorded with drawings and questions"
  species_identification: "Correctly identifies 20+ local species across 3+ taxonomic groups"
  investigation_design: "Proposes testable questions about observed phenomena"

grove_integration:
  built_environment: "Observes how living systems interact with human structures"
  quantitative: "Measures and quantifies natural phenomena"
  creative_expression: "Creates art inspired by natural observations"

mentorship_role: "Guides newcomers in observation techniques and nature appreciation"
```

#### Level 2: Investigator (Scientific Method Application)
```yaml
timeline: "3-6 months"
prerequisite: "Observer competence + basic measurement and data skills"

competencies:
  - experimental_design (creates controlled investigations of biological questions)
  - data_collection_analysis (gathers and interprets biological data)
  - hypothesis_formation_testing (makes predictions and tests them systematically)

validation_through:
  original_investigation: "Designs and conducts investigation answering biological question"
  peer_collaboration: "Works effectively with others on complex investigations"
  result_communication: "Shares findings clearly with multiple audiences"

grove_integration:
  quantitative: "Uses statistical analysis to interpret biological data"
  human_systems: "Investigates connections between human behavior and environmental health"
  information_technology: "Uses digital tools for data collection and analysis"

mentorship_role: "Teaches scientific method and helps observers develop investigation skills"
```

#### Level 3: Systems Analyst (Ecological Understanding)
```yaml
timeline: "6-12 months"
prerequisite: "Investigator competence + systems thinking foundation"

competencies:
  - ecosystem_analysis (understands complex interactions within ecological communities)
  - impact_assessment (evaluates how changes affect living system health)
  - restoration_planning (designs interventions to support ecosystem recovery)

validation_through:
  ecosystem_assessment: "Comprehensive analysis of local ecosystem health and recommendations"
  restoration_project: "Leads or significantly contributes to ecosystem restoration effort"
  impact_prediction: "Accurately predicts consequences of proposed environmental changes"

grove_integration:
  built_environment: "Designs human infrastructure that supports ecosystem health"
  human_systems: "Understands human social systems as part of larger ecological systems"
  quantitative: "Models ecosystem dynamics using mathematical and computational tools"

mentorship_role: "Guides investigators in systems thinking and helps observers understand ecological connections"
```

#### Level 4: Regenerative Practitioner (Applied Ecology)
```yaml
timeline: "1-3 years"
prerequisite: "Systems Analyst competence + demonstrated community engagement"

competencies:
  - regenerative_design (creates human systems that enhance rather than degrade ecosystems)
  - community_education (teaches ecological principles to diverse audiences)
  - adaptive_management (adjusts practices based on ecological feedback)

validation_through:
  community_project: "Leads regenerative project with measurable ecological and social benefits"
  curriculum_development: "Creates educational materials helping others develop ecological understanding"
  long_term_stewardship: "Maintains regenerative practices over multiple years"

grove_integration:
  - coordinates_multi_grove_initiatives (leads projects requiring diverse expertise)
  - establishes_external_partnerships (connects college work with community organizations)
  - influences_grove_evolution (shapes focus areas based on ecological needs)

mentorship_role: "Mentors all lower levels and helps shape grove direction and community connections"
```

#### Level 5: Ecological Innovator (Cutting-Edge Practice)
```yaml
timeline: "3+ years"
prerequisite: "Regenerative Practitioner competence + significant innovation demonstrated"

competencies:
  - paradigm_development (creates new frameworks for understanding human-nature relationships)
  - technology_integration (develops new tools and methods for ecological practice)
  - movement_leadership (influences broader cultural shifts toward ecological thinking)

validation_through:
  paradigm_contribution: "Develops new approaches that are adopted by other practitioners"
  technology_innovation: "Creates tools that significantly advance ecological practice"
  cultural_impact: "Influences policy, education, or community practices at regional+ scale"

grove_integration:
  - shapes_college_evolution (influences overall college direction based on ecological insights)
  - establishes_research_partnerships (connects with universities and research institutions)
  - creates_new_knowledge_domains (pioneering work that spawns new areas of study)

mentorship_role: "Shapes the development of future ecological leaders and influences grove evolution"
```

## Pace and Sustainability Guidelines

### Sustainable Advancement Principles
```yaml
no_skipping: "Each level must be mastered before advancing to next"
foundation_maintenance: "Advancing learners must retain competencies from previous levels"
individual_pacing: "Timeline suggestions are guidelines; individual variation is expected and honored"
holistic_development: "Advancement considers emotional and social development alongside cognitive"
```

### Support Systems
```yaml
peer_mentoring: "Each level provides guidance and support to previous levels"
flexible_pathways: "Multiple routes through progression accommodate different learning styles"
remediation_support: "Additional support available for learners needing more time at any level"
acceleration_options: "Advanced learners can pursue deeper exploration within current level"
```

### Health Monitoring
```yaml
stress_indicators:
  - frustration_with_current_level (may indicate need for additional support)
  - anxiety_about_advancement (may indicate rushed pacing)
  - loss_of_foundational_skills (indicates advancement too rapid)
  - disengagement_from_grove_activities (may indicate mismatched pathway)

intervention_strategies:
  - individual_consultation_and_pathway_adjustment
  - peer_mentoring_and_collaborative_support
  - alternative_pathway_exploration
  - temporary_focus_shift_to_strength_areas
```

## Concepts

### Wing 1 — Foundations (5 concepts)

- polsci-system-dynamics -- What is a political system; systems theory as the appropriate analytical frame for politics
- polsci-power-theory -- Definitions and dimensions of power; structural, relational, and discursive power
- polsci-complexity-foundations -- Why complexity theory rather than linear analysis for political systems
- polsci-attractor-basins -- Stable and unstable attractors in political phase space; regime type as basin of attraction
- polsci-westphalian-unit -- The Westphalian state as the primary unit of analysis and its structural limitations

### Wing 2 — Historical Arc (6 concepts)

- polsci-westphalian-system -- The 1648 Westphalian system: state sovereignty, the imaginary axis problem of legitimacy, proto-nationalism
- polsci-phase-transitions -- The six major phase transitions from 1648 to 2026 as movements between attractor basins
- polsci-interwar-collapse -- 1919-1945: simultaneous destabilization of both axes; fascism and communism as imaginary-axis capture
- polsci-1945-reconstruction -- UN Charter, Bretton Woods, Marshall Plan, UDHR as attempt to re-establish stable attractors on both axes
- polsci-decolonization -- 1945-1975: approximately 80 new nation-states; imaginary axis shaped by colonial histories the real axis could not overwrite
- polsci-third-wave -- The third wave of autocratization (2016-2026): mechanism, distinguishing characteristics, historical placement

### Wing 3 — Current Global State (6 concepts)

- polsci-vdem-methodology -- V-Dem measurement philosophy: five democracy principles, expert survey methodology, Bayesian aggregation
- polsci-eiu-idea-indices -- EIU Democracy Index and IDEA GSoD as complementary measurement systems with distinct methodological assumptions
- polsci-erosion-mechanisms -- The Kettering Foundation mechanism: imaginary-axis capture preceding real-axis collapse; the four stages
- polsci-regional-analysis -- US competitive authoritarianism debate; Europe's paradox; Global South recovery trajectories; Sahel coup cluster
- polsci-resilience-signals -- Judicial independence, civil society continuity, international anchors, democratic solidarity as resilience factors
- polsci-recovery-patterns -- V-Dem's 76 completed episodes: 37% recovery rate; structural predictors of recovery vs. consolidation

### Wing 4 — Complex Plane Framework (6 concepts)

- polsci-complex-plane-political -- z(t) = R(t) + i·X(t) formal definition; R as institutional democracy; X as experiential legitimacy
- polsci-modulus-stability -- |z(t)| = √(R² + X²) as overall system stability; interpretation across regime types
- polsci-phase-angle-interpretation -- arg(z(t)) = arctan(X/R) as the gap between experience and institution; increasing angle as erosion signal
- polsci-attractor-typology -- Five attractor types: consolidated democracy, flawed democracy, competitive authoritarianism, closed autocracy, revolutionary state
- polsci-euler-political -- Euler's formula e^(iθ) = cos θ + i sin θ; unit circle as democratic-autocratic cycle structure; connection to The Space Between
- polsci-lyapunov-political -- Lyapunov stability applied to political attractors; saddle point interpretation of flawed democracy basin

### Wing 5 — Integration (4 concepts)

- polsci-integration-mapping -- How the five research modules map onto the College's cross-departmental architecture
- polsci-rosetta-architecture -- The four-language Rosetta panel system: Python, R, Julia, SQL and their ecological roles
- polsci-cross-links -- Five cross-departmental bridges: Mathematics, Literature, Philosophy, Economics, Computer Science
- polsci-gsd-cross-refs -- The Space Between connection; The Hundred Voices narrative bridge; Artemis II mission tracking philosophy

## Canopy Governance

### Leadership Structure
```yaml
canopy_council:
  composition: "2 representatives from each grove + 3 synthesis domain coordinators"
  responsibilities: ["Project approval", "Resource allocation", "Conflict resolution", "Forest evolution guidance"]
  term_limits: "3 years maximum, staggered rotation"

synthesis_coordinators:
  qualifications: "Level 5 competence in primary domain + demonstrated synthesis leadership"
  responsibilities: ["Cross-domain project facilitation", "New synthesis opportunity identification", "Canopy-grove communication"]
  selection: "Nominated by canopy council, confirmed by forest community"

forest_stewards:
  composition: "Long-term canopy citizens with 5+ years experience"
  responsibilities: ["Long-term forest health", "Knowledge preservation", "Mentoring synthesis coordinators"]
  selection: "Peer recognition of sustained contribution to forest development"
```

### Project Development Process
```yaml
proposal_phase:
  - synthesis_opportunity_identification (emerges from grove work or external needs)
  - initial_feasibility_assessment (resource requirements, timeline, participant availability)
  - domain_expert_consultation (relevant grove leaders and canopy citizens)

approval_phase:
  - canopy_council_review (alignment with forest values and long-term health)
  - community_input_period (opportunity for all forest members to provide input)
  - resource_allocation_planning (ensuring project doesn't compromise grove health)

execution_phase:
  - cross_grove_team_formation (participants from multiple groves and synthesis domains)
  - regular_progress_assessment (monthly check-ins with synthesis coordinators)
  - adaptive_planning_adjustment (modifications based on emerging insights)

completion_phase:
  - knowledge_integration (insights incorporated into grove curricula and forest resources)
  - community_sharing (results shared with all forest members and external partners)
  - impact_assessment (evaluation of project outcomes and forest development contribution)
```

### Quality Assurance
```yaml
synthesis_standards:
  genuine_integration: "Projects must create knowledge that couldn't emerge from single grove work"
  practical_application: "All synthesis work must connect to real-world challenges and opportunities"
  knowledge_advancement: "Projects must contribute new understanding that advances human knowledge"
  sustainable_impact: "Outcomes must support long-term forest and community health"

review_mechanisms:
  peer_evaluation: "Work reviewed by canopy citizens from all relevant domains"
  external_validation: "Key insights validated by experts outside the forest community"
  community_applicability: "Practical applications tested in real community contexts"
  long_term_follow_up: "Multi-year assessment of project impact and knowledge durability"
```

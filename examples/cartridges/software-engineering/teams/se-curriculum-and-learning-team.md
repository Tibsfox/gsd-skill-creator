# se-curriculum-and-learning-team

Produce a learning pathway, reading list, or course sequence for a CS-theory goal. Curriculum advisor drives selection, professor validates rigor and ordering, algorithms analyst sanity-checks the exercise set.

**Roster:** `curriculum-advisor`, `cs-professor`, `algorithms-analyst`.

**Use when:** building a study plan, choosing textbooks, or mapping a learner's current knowledge to a target curriculum

# se-proof-and-verification-team

Produce a machine-checkable proof or a formal verification artifact. Proof theorist drives the proof, professor approves the specification being proved, language theorist confirms the semantic model is faithful to the language under study.

**Roster:** `proof-theorist`, `cs-professor`, `language-theorist`.

**Use when:** proving correctness of an algorithm, verifying a language feature, or building a proof-assistant artifact

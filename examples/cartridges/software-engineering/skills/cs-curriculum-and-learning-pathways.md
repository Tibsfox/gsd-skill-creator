# cs-curriculum-and-learning-pathways

The canonical textbooks, course sequencing, and open courseware for a rigorous CS education — SICP, CLRS, TAPL, PFPL, HoTT Book, Sipser, Arora-Barak, and the standard undergraduate / graduate paths through them. Designs a personal study plan that matches a learner's starting point and goal.

**Triggers:** `cs curriculum`, `where to start`, `textbook recommendation`, `sicp`, `clrs`, `tapl`, `self study plan`, `course sequence`

**Affinity:** `curriculum-advisor`, `cs-professor`

# lambda-calculus-and-functional-foundations

Untyped and typed lambda calculus, alpha / beta / eta conversion, reduction strategies (normal order, call by value, call by need), Church encodings, combinators (S, K, I), fixed-point operators, and the bridge from pure calculus to practical functional languages.

**Triggers:** `lambda calculus`, `beta reduction`, `church encoding`, `combinator`, `y combinator`, `call by value`, `normal form`

**Affinity:** `language-theorist`

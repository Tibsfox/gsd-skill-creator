# sm-mapping-team

Ramp up on an unfamiliar or inherited codebase. Lead frames the questions to answer, cartographer builds the inventory, architecture extraction, and dependency graph, reviewer spot-checks static analysis findings against the map.

**Roster:** `sm-lead`, `sm-codebase-cartographer`, `sm-reviewer`.

**Use when:** inheriting a repo, onboarding onto an unfamiliar service, or preparing for a port / translation / deep review

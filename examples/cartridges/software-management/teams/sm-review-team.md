# sm-review-team

Review a large or unfamiliar changeset at scale. Reviewer drives, cartographer provides the map that makes blast radius legible, lead arbitrates blocking vs. nit and signs off on the final verdict.

**Roster:** `sm-reviewer`, `sm-codebase-cartographer`, `sm-lead`.

**Use when:** a PR or vendored drop is too large or too unfamiliar for a single reviewer to evaluate in one pass

# change-management

Operate the change-management loop — RFC intake, risk classification, CAB review, approval gates, change windows, and post-change verification. Enforces the rule that every production touch has an approver, a window, and a rollback plan on record. Triggers on `submit rfc`, `change request`, `cab review`, `change window`, `rollback plan`. Affinity: `sysops-change-manager`, `sysops-commander`.

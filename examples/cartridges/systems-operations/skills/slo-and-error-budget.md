# slo-and-error-budget

Define SLIs and SLOs, track error-budget burn, and drive release-freeze decisions off budget state. Owns the quarterly SLO review and the product conversation when the budget is exhausted. Keeps targets honest. Triggers on `define sli`, `set slo`, `error budget`, `burn rate`, `freeze releases`. Affinity: `sysops-reliability-engineer`, `sysops-commander`.

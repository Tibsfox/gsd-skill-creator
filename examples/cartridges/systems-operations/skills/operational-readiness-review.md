# operational-readiness-review

Run ORR for new services or major changes before they reach production — monitoring coverage, runbook existence, SLO proposal, capacity headroom, rollback rehearsal, on-call rota staffing. No service crosses the line without a signed checklist. Triggers on `operational readiness`, `orr review`, `launch checklist`, `go live gate`, `pre launch`. Affinity: `sysops-commander`, `sysops-reliability-engineer`.

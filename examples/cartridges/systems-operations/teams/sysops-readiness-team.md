# sysops-readiness-team

Run an Operational Readiness Review for a new service or major change. Commander chairs, reliability-engineer reviews SLOs and capacity, observability-engineer verifies monitoring coverage, change-manager books the launch window.

**Roster:** `sysops-commander`, `sysops-reliability-engineer`, `sysops-observability-engineer`, `sysops-change-manager`.
**Use when:** a new service or high-risk change is requesting go-live.

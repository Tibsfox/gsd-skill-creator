# sysops-incident-team

Live incident response from detection through stabilization. Commander runs the room, runner executes stabilization steps, observability-engineer surfaces signal from the pipeline, reliability-engineer captures the timeline for the postmortem.

**Roster:** `sysops-commander`, `sysops-runner`, `sysops-observability-engineer`, `sysops-reliability-engineer`.
**Use when:** a production incident has been declared at any severity.

# sysops-postmortem-team

Conduct a blameless postmortem for an incident or near-miss. Reliability-engineer drafts, commander validates the timeline from the incident, observability-engineer contributes signal evidence, change-manager tracks action items to closure.

**Roster:** `sysops-reliability-engineer`, `sysops-commander`, `sysops-observability-engineer`, `sysops-change-manager`.
**Use when:** within 5 business days of an incident at sev2 or above, or any near-miss.

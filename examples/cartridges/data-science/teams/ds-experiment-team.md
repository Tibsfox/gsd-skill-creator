# ds-experiment-team

Design and analyze an experiment or quasi-experiment. Statistician owns design, power, and causal inference, lead aligns with stakeholders on what decision the experiment will unlock, data-wrangler builds the instrumentation and analysis pipeline.

**Roster:** `statistician`, `ds-lead`, `data-wrangler`.

**Use when:** an A/B test, quasi-experiment, or causal-inference study is being designed or analyzed

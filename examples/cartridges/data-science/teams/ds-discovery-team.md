# ds-discovery-team

Scope a new data-science question and decide whether it is answerable. Lead frames the problem and the success metric, data-wrangler pulls a sample and runs EDA, statistician sanity-checks that the question is statistically meaningful before resources are committed.

**Roster:** `ds-lead`, `data-wrangler`, `statistician`.

**Use when:** a new project is proposed and the team needs to decide "can we actually answer this with the data we have"

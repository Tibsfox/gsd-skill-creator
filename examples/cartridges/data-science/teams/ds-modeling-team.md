# ds-modeling-team

Build a predictive or explanatory model end-to-end. Data-wrangler prepares the feature set, ml-modeler trains and evaluates, statistician reviews the validation design and any fairness audit.

**Roster:** `data-wrangler`, `ml-modeler`, `statistician`.

**Use when:** the problem has been framed and the data is ready, and the team needs a trained-and-evaluated model

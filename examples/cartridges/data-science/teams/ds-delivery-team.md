# ds-delivery-team

Package findings for decision-makers. Insights communicator drafts the visualizations and narrative, lead approves the framing against the original success metric, ml-modeler or statistician provides any technical caveats that must appear with the result.

**Roster:** `insights-communicator`, `ds-lead`, `ml-modeler`.

**Use when:** a project has reached conclusions and needs to be delivered as a report, dashboard, or decision memo

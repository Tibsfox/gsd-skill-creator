# exploratory-data-analysis

Look at the data before modeling it. Covers summary statistics, univariate and bivariate distributions, correlation structure, segment comparisons, and the quick visualizations that surface surprises before any model is trained. The discipline of looking before leaping.

**Triggers:** `eda`, `explore the data`, `summary statistics`, `distributions`, `correlation matrix`, `look at the data`, `first look`

**Affinity:** `data-wrangler`, `statistician`

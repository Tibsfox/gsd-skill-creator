# experimentation-and-ab-testing

Design and analyze experiments. Covers hypothesis formulation, randomization, power analysis, sample size, A/B and multi-arm designs, sequential testing, novelty and primacy effects, guardrail metrics, and causal inference from observational data when experiments are impossible.

**Triggers:** `ab test`, `experiment design`, `power analysis`, `sample size`, `causal inference`, `randomized trial`, `treatment effect`, `guardrail metric`

**Affinity:** `statistician`, `ds-lead`

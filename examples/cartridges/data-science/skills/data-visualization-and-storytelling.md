# data-visualization-and-storytelling

Turn analysis into something a decision-maker can act on. Covers chart-type selection (grammar of graphics), annotation, color and accessibility, dashboard layout, narrative structure, and the written summary that tells the story in three sentences before the reader sees the first chart.

**Triggers:** `visualize`, `chart type`, `dashboard`, `data story`, `executive summary`, `grammar of graphics`, `figure caption`

**Affinity:** `insights-communicator`, `ds-lead`

# problem-framing-and-success-metrics

Turn a fuzzy business or research question into a concrete data problem. Defines the unit of analysis, the outcome variable, the success metric, the decision the answer will drive, and the counterfactual — what "doing nothing" looks like. Stops projects that cannot be measured before any data is loaded.

**Triggers:** `frame the problem`, `success metric`, `decision criteria`, `what question are we answering`, `unit of analysis`, `counterfactual`, `project scoping`

**Affinity:** `ds-lead`, `statistician`

# pipeline-and-reproducibility

Turn a notebook experiment into a reproducible pipeline. Covers environment pinning, deterministic seeds, DAG orchestration (Airflow / Prefect / Dagster), artifact and experiment tracking (MLflow / W&B / DVC), and the rule that every published result must be rerunnable from its recorded inputs.

**Triggers:** `reproducible pipeline`, `airflow`, `prefect`, `dagster`, `mlflow`, `dvc`, `experiment tracking`, `deterministic seed`

**Affinity:** `data-wrangler`, `ml-modeler`

# se-requirements-team

Capture stakeholder needs and produce a baselined requirements set. Chief engineer frames scope and allocation, requirements analyst writes and traces each item, configuration manager freezes the baseline and opens the change-control channel.

**Roster:** `se-chief-engineer`, `se-requirements-analyst`, `se-configuration-manager`.

**Use when:** a new program, subsystem, or major change needs a baselined requirements set before design work begins

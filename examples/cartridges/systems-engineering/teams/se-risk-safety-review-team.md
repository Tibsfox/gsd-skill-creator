# se-risk-safety-review-team

Review and disposition technical risks and safety items. Risk and safety officer drives the review, chief engineer approves mitigations that change the architecture, verification lead confirms the mitigation is testable.

**Roster:** `se-risk-and-safety-officer`, `se-chief-engineer`, `se-verification-lead`.

**Use when:** risk review board, hazard review, or change that touches a safety-critical function

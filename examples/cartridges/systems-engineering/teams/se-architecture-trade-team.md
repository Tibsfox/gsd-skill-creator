# se-architecture-trade-team

Design the system architecture and run the trade studies that justify it. Chief engineer drives the architecture and decisions, requirements analyst keeps the trade criteria tied to real stakeholder needs, risk-and-safety officer surfaces hazards that should move the trade.

**Roster:** `se-chief-engineer`, `se-requirements-analyst`, `se-risk-and-safety-officer`.

**Use when:** choosing between architectural alternatives or evaluating a technology insertion that reshapes the system

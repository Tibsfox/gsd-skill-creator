# configuration-and-change-control

Control the evolving configuration of the system — baselines, change requests, impact analysis, change control board, and the audited history of what was built, when, and why. Keeps the as-designed, as-built, and as-operated views consistent.

**Triggers:** `configuration management`, `baseline`, `change request`, `ccb`, `change control board`, `impact analysis`, `as-built`

**Affinity:** `se-configuration-manager`

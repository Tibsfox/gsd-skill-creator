# human-factors-and-usability

Account for the human in the loop — operator workload, error modes, controls and displays, training needs, and accessibility. Produces human-system interface requirements and validates them against representative users before acceptance.

**Triggers:** `human factors`, `hsi`, `operator workload`, `usability test`, `human in the loop`, `controls and displays`, `accessibility`

# safety-and-assurance

Build and maintain the safety case — hazard analysis, fault trees, safety requirements, assurance arguments, and independent review of changes that touch safety-critical functions. Produces evidence that satisfies applicable safety standards.

**Triggers:** `hazard analysis`, `fault tree`, `safety case`, `safety requirement`, `functional hazard assessment`, `fha`, `assurance argument`

**Affinity:** `se-risk-and-safety-officer`

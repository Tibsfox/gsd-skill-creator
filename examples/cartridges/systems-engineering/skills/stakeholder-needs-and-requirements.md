# stakeholder-needs-and-requirements

Elicit, analyze, and baseline stakeholder needs and translate them into well-formed system and subsystem requirements. Covers needs interviews, concept-of-operations authoring, requirements decomposition, allocation, traceability, and quality checks (unambiguous, verifiable, feasible, singular).

**Triggers:** `capture requirements`, `elicit needs`, `write requirement`, `concept of operations`, `conops`, `requirement traceability`, `decompose requirements`, `allocate requirement`

**Affinity:** `se-requirements-analyst`, `se-chief-engineer`

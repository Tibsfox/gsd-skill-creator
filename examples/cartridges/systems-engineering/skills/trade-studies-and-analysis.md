# trade-studies-and-analysis

Run structured trade studies to choose between architectural, technology, or sourcing alternatives. Covers criteria weighting, scoring, sensitivity analysis, cost / schedule / risk trades, and the written rationale that makes the decision reviewable and reversible if new evidence arrives.

**Triggers:** `trade study`, `trade space`, `decision analysis`, `alternatives analysis`, `weighted criteria`, `pugh matrix`, `aoa`

**Affinity:** `se-chief-engineer`

# housekeeping-sweep-team

Run a scheduled housekeeping sweep — repo hygiene, stale files, gitignore drift. Steward drives the scan, curator reviews the findings, capcom approves actions. Writes a HousekeepingSweep record and any StaleFileReport that comes out of it.

**Roster:** `housekeeping-capcom`, `steward`, `curator`.

**Use when:** running a periodic housekeeping sweep on the repo

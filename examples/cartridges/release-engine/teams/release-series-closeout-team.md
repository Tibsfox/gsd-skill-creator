# release-series-closeout-team

Close out a completed release series. Verifier confirms every catalog item shipped, retrospective-analyst writes the series-level retrospective, capcom authors the closeout announcement. Writes a SeriesRetrospective into grove.

**Roster:** `release-capcom`, `release-verifier`, `release-retrospective-analyst`.

**Use when:** finishing a release series (degree 360, mission 720, final book chapter)

# series-closeout

Close out a completed release series. Verifies every catalog item shipped, authors the series-level retrospective, rolls up the flywheel deltas into a single "what we learned across N releases" document, and writes a SeriesRetrospective record into grove. Used at degree 360, mission 720, or after the final chapter of a book ships.

**Triggers:** `close series`, `series retrospective`, `end of series`, `series complete`, `wrap series`

**Affinity:** `release-capcom`, `release-retrospective-analyst`, `release-verifier`

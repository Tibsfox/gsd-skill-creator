# problem-framing-and-symptom-capture

Turn a vague "it's broken" into a precise, falsifiable problem statement. Capture what is observed vs. what is expected, when and how often, environmental conditions, and any recent change. Domain-agnostic — works for a leaking pipe, a failing server, a misbehaving engine, or a team dynamic.

**Triggers:** `frame the problem`, `what's actually broken`, `capture symptoms`, `define the issue`, `expected vs observed`, `problem statement`, `it's broken`

**Affinity:** `troubleshoot-lead`, `symptom-interviewer`

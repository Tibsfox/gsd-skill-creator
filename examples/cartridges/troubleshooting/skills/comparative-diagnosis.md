# comparative-diagnosis

Diagnose by comparing the suspect system to a known-good reference. Side-by-side readings, diff of configs, twin machines, A/B swaps, or a peer unit in the same fleet. Surfaces differences that are invisible when only the broken unit is in view.

**Triggers:** `compare to a working one`, `known good reference`, `diff the configs`, `side by side`, `swap and see`, `ab test the fix`

**Affinity:** `comparative-analyst`

# post-incident-analysis

After stabilization, reconstruct the timeline, identify contributing factors, distinguish symptom from cause, and produce a blameless post-incident report with concrete preventive actions and owners. The feedback loop that turns an incident into institutional learning.

**Triggers:** `post incident`, `postmortem`, `blameless review`, `incident report`, `timeline reconstruction`, `lessons learned`, `preventive actions`

**Affinity:** `incident-historian`, `troubleshoot-lead`

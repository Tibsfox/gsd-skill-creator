# intermittent-and-transient-faults

Hunt the worst category of fault — the one that won't repeat on demand. Covers heisenbugs, thermal-dependent faults, flaky tests, loose connections, environmental triggers, and the discipline of long-duration soak testing to force a transient into the open.

**Triggers:** `intermittent`, `only sometimes`, `flaky`, `heisenbug`, `can't reproduce`, `thermal fault`, `loose connection`, `soak test`

**Affinity:** `hypothesis-tester`, `comparative-analyst`

# observation-and-measurement

Choose the right instrument and read it correctly. Covers multimeters, oscilloscopes, pressure gauges, thermal cameras, log tails, profilers, stethoscopes, feeler gauges, and direct sensory observation (sight, sound, smell, touch). The mantra: measure, don't guess.

**Triggers:** `measure it`, `what does the gauge say`, `read the logs`, `instrumentation`, `multimeter`, `oscilloscope`, `profiler`, `what do you see`

**Affinity:** `symptom-interviewer`, `hypothesis-tester`

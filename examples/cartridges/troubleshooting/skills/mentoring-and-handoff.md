# mentoring-and-handoff

Teach another troubleshooter to finish the job. Explain the current hypothesis, what has been ruled out, what measurements were taken, and why. Writes handoff notes that let a fresh pair of eyes continue without re-running the ruled-out branches. Critical for long-running or shift-based diagnosis.

**Triggers:** `hand off`, `brief the next shift`, `what have you tried`, `teach me to debug this`, `troubleshooting notes`, `walk me through it`

**Affinity:** `incident-historian`, `troubleshoot-lead`

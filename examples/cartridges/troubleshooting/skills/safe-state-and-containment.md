# safe-state-and-containment

Stop the bleeding before diagnosing. Put the system in a safe state, contain collateral damage, preserve forensic evidence, and communicate the impact radius. Diagnosis and repair are always secondary to preventing harm and data loss.

**Triggers:** `safe state`, `contain the damage`, `preserve evidence`, `stop the bleeding`, `shut it down`, `safe mode`, `isolate from production`

**Affinity:** `troubleshoot-lead`

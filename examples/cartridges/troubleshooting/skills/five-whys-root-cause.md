# five-whys-root-cause

Walk backward from a symptom through the causal chain until a fixable upstream cause is reached — typically five levels deep. Prevents "fix the symptom, ship the bug" by forcing the question one more level down, and stops at the first cause the team actually controls.

**Triggers:** `five whys`, `root cause`, `why did that happen`, `underlying cause`, `causal chain`, `ask why again`

**Affinity:** `troubleshoot-lead`, `incident-historian`

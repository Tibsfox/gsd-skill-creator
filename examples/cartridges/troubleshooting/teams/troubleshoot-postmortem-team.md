# troubleshoot-postmortem-team

Blameless post-incident review after stabilization. Historian drives the timeline, lead ensures cause vs. symptom is kept straight, hypothesis-tester confirms proposed preventive actions would actually catch a recurrence.

**Roster:** `incident-historian`, `troubleshoot-lead`, `hypothesis-tester`.

**Use when:** the system is stable and the team is ready to extract institutional learning from the incident

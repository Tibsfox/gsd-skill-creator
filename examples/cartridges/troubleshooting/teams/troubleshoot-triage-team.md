# troubleshoot-triage-team

First-response triage on a live fault. Lead frames the problem and makes the containment call, symptom-interviewer captures what is actually happening, historian opens the incident record so nothing is lost during the rush.

**Roster:** `troubleshoot-lead`, `symptom-interviewer`, `incident-historian`.

**Use when:** a failure has just been reported and the scope, severity, and containment need are still unknown

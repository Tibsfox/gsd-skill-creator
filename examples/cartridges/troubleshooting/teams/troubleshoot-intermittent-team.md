# troubleshoot-intermittent-team

Hunt an intermittent or environmentally triggered fault. Hypothesis-tester designs the soak / stress test, comparative-analyst runs same-fleet comparisons, historian logs every occurrence so the pattern can be seen across time.

**Roster:** `hypothesis-tester`, `comparative-analyst`, `incident-historian`.

**Use when:** the fault will not reproduce on demand and the investigation needs long-duration or environmental evidence

# troubleshoot-deep-dive-team

Structured deep dive on a hard fault. Lead picks the strategy, hypothesis-tester runs experiments, comparative-analyst handles the bisection and known-good comparisons. Moves methodically when intuition has failed.

**Roster:** `troubleshoot-lead`, `hypothesis-tester`, `comparative-analyst`.

**Use when:** the fault has been reproduced but the root cause is not yet localized and guessing has stopped working

# circuit-and-carrier-management

Manage physical and virtual circuits — transit, IP transit, wavelength, MPLS, ethernet transport, cross-connects. Owns circuit IDs, LOAs, A/Z ends, carrier escalation contacts, and the paper trail from order through turn-up to retirement. Triggers on `circuit down`, `carrier ticket`, `cross connect`, `loa cfa`, `ip transit`, `turn up`. Affinity: `netops-engineer`, `netops-architect`.

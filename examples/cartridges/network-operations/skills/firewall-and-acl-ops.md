# firewall-and-acl-ops

Operate network-edge firewalls and ACLs — zone policy, stateful inspection, NAT, and deny/permit lists on routers and switches. Distinct from host firewalls (sysadmin territory) — this is the fabric-level policy that sits in front of hosts. Triggers on `firewall rule`, `acl`, `zone policy`, `nat rule`, `edge firewall`. Affinity: `netops-engineer`, `netops-security-officer`.

# netops-ddos-response-team

Mitigate an in-progress DDoS attack. Security-officer owns the mitigation playbook, incident-responder localizes the attack vector, engineer pushes flowspec or null-route changes, monitoring-engineer validates the mitigation reduces signal.

**Roster:** `netops-security-officer`, `netops-incident-responder`, `netops-engineer`, `netops-monitoring-engineer`.
**Use when:** a volumetric or application-layer DDoS attack is underway.

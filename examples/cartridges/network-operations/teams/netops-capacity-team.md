# netops-capacity-team

Review network capacity and plan augments. Architect owns the long-horizon topology, monitoring-engineer provides traffic trend data, engineer scopes the physical and logical work, security-officer reviews any zone or policy implications.

**Roster:** `netops-architect`, `netops-monitoring-engineer`, `netops-engineer`, `netops-security-officer`.
**Use when:** quarterly capacity review or when a link approaches 80% sustained utilization.

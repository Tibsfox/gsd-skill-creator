# netops-peering-team

Bring up a new BGP peering session or transit circuit. Architect owns the policy decisions (prefix filters, communities, local-preference), engineer configures and turns up, security-officer validates RPKI and max-prefix safeties.

**Roster:** `netops-architect`, `netops-engineer`, `netops-security-officer`.
**Use when:** establishing a new BGP peer, transit provider, or IX connection.

# forge-migration-team

**Members:** forge-librarian, forge-validator, forge-scribe.

Use when migrating a legacy cartridge or forking an existing one.

1. **forge-librarian** does the mechanical fork/migrate work.
2. **forge-validator** runs the gates against the result.
3. **forge-scribe** updates the migration notes and user-facing docs.

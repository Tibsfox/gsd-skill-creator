# forge-design-team

**Members:** forge-architect, forge-distiller, forge-scribe.

Use when creating a brand-new cartridge from an idea or research package.

1. **forge-distiller** extracts concepts from any source material.
2. **forge-architect** decides the shape — chipsets, topology, category.
3. **forge-scribe** writes the user-facing docs.
4. Hand off to **forge-review-team** before shipping.

# skill-authoring-team

Author a new skill, agent, or team from a blank slate. Capcom decides shape and scope, skill-forger writes the files, cartridge- smith runs the forge gates to confirm the new artifact round-trips through validate/eval/dedup.

**Roster:** `skill-creator-capcom`, `skill-forger`, `cartridge-smith`.

**Use when:** creating a new skill, agent, or team from scratch

# chipset-assembly

Bundle skills, agents, and teams into a chipset matching one of the eight kinds in the cartridge spec (department, grove, college, coprocessor, content, muse, evaluation, forge). Covers the discriminator field, src: refs vs. inline, JSON-pointer fragments, and cross-chipset field rules.

**Triggers:** `build a chipset`, `chipset yaml`, `department chipset`, `coprocessor chipset`, `content chipset`, `chipset kind`, `discriminated chipset`

**Affinity:** `cartridge-smith`, `skill-forger`

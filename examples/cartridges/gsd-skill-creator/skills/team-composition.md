# team-composition

Compose agents into a team — roster, use_when clause, routing topology, and the workflow shape the team is built around. Teams are shaped by recurring situations (author, review, incident, drill), not by role buckets. Covers the difference between a team and a multi-agent invocation.

**Triggers:** `compose a team`, `new team`, `team roster`, `team use_when`, `multi-agent team`, `team workflow`

**Affinity:** `skill-forger`

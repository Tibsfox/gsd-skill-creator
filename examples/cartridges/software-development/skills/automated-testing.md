# automated-testing

Write and maintain automated tests — unit, integration, end-to-end, property-based, and snapshot. Pick the right level for the risk, keep tests fast and deterministic, and treat a failing test as a first-class bug. The safety net that makes refactoring possible.

**Triggers:** `write a test`, `unit test`, `integration test`, `e2e test`, `property based`, `test coverage`, `flaky test`

**Affinity:** `sd-test-engineer`, `sd-developer`

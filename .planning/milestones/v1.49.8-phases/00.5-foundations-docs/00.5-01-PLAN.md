# Foundations — Layer 1

The foundations layer is the mathematical bedrock of the tibsfox.com ecosystem. Every
principle, framework, and application in the system traces back to formal structures
introduced here. This is not mathematics for its own sake — it is mathematics as
architecture, providing the precise language that makes everything above it possible.

## What This Layer Contains

Three gateway documents provide entry points to the mathematical foundations:

### [Mathematical Foundations — The Space Between](mathematical-foundations.md)

Gateway to *The Space Between*, a 923-page mathematical textbook tracing the path from
the unit circle through quantum mechanics and AI/ML. The full text is available at
[tibsfox.com/media/The_Space_Between.pdf](https://tibsfox.com/media/The_Space_Between.pdf).

### [The Complex Plane of Experience](complex-plane.md)

The organizing framework that maps logical reasoning (real axis) and creative intuition
(imaginary axis) as dimensions of a complex plane. Every resource in the ecosystem
occupies a position on this plane. If you read one foundations document, read this one.

### [Eight-Layer Progression](eight-layer-progression.md)

A reading guide for the eight mathematical layers in *The Space Between*: unit circle,
Pythagorean theorem, trigonometry, vector calculus, set theory, category theory,
information theory, and L-systems. Each layer builds on the previous and connects to
practical applications in the ecosystem.

## Related Resources

- [The Quark and the Compiler](https://tibsfox.com/the-quark-and-the-compiler.pdf) — explores the connection between physics and computation
- [Progressive Disclosure](../principles/progressive-disclosure.md) — the design principle demonstrated by the eight-layer progression
- [AMIGA Principle](../principles/amiga-principle.md) — architectural leverage grounded in category theory from Layer 6

## How It Connects

The foundations layer (Layer 1) feeds into the principles layer (Layer 2), where design
philosophy draws on the formal structures introduced here. Category theory provides the
mathematical basis for the [AMIGA Principle](../principles/amiga-principle.md).
Information theory explains why [progressive disclosure](../principles/progressive-disclosure.md)
works. The eight-layer progression is itself a demonstration of progressive disclosure in
mathematical form.

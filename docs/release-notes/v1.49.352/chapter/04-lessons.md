   The Iska Dhaaf entry at degree 211 documents structural fusion (multiple traditions contributing co-equal load-bearing elements simultaneously) as a first-class genre category distinct from single-tradition (single-origin) and tradition-plus-influence (primary-anchor-plus-surface-borrowing) models. Future engine documentation of cross-cultural music projects should explicitly distinguish structural fusion from textural borrowing, treating the distinction as a first-class genre-theory analytical frame rather than an implicit interpretive assumption, and future entries should be tracked as instances of either register for cross-degree comparative analysis.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #7982_

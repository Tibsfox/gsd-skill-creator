## Wolf recovery ecology


- Washington state's gray wolf population has grown from near-zero in 2008 to 26+ packs by the early 2020s; the recovery was natural dispersal, not reintroduction; wolves came through the Selkirk Mountains from Idaho and through the Cascade corridor from British Columbia; the North Cascades ecosystem is now wolf-occupied territory; the howl documented at degree 303 is the howl that the North Cascades have not heard at regular intervals since the early twentieth century

## Multi-voice harmonic structure


- the wolf pack howl's frequency structure is not a unison call but a chord: each animal contributes a different fundamental, and the harmonics interact across individuals; the result is a sustained, shifting harmonic texture that has no analog in any other North American predator vocalization; the pack is not howling together but harmonizing, in the technical acoustic sense, across individuals with different pitch ranges and different harmonic series; E=9 reflects both the carry distance and the harmonic complexity

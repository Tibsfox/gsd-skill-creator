# v1.49.9 Retrospective — Learn Kung Fu

**Shipped:** 2026-03-01
**Phases:** 11 | **Plans:** 11 | **Sessions:** 1

## What Was Built
- Mind-body department with 8 wings (breath, meditation, yoga, pilates, martial arts, tai chi, relaxation, philosophy)
- Training Hall entry point with 5 navigation paths
- 8 Try Sessions for immediate hands-on practice (no equipment, no prerequisites)
- Practice Builder with 4 time templates and 9-week progressive structure
- Practice Journal with no-guilt UX and 5-pattern calibration integration
- 3-mode Physical Safety Warden with absolute partner-technique boundary
- Chipset configuration with 10 skills, 3 agents, and cross-discipline connection map

## What Worked
- **College Structure scales**: Second non-technical domain (after culinary-arts) confirmed identical patterns work — the architecture genuinely scales to any domain
- **Research skip (pattern reuse)**: Same as v1.49.8 — teaching reference IS the research, saved ~2 hours again
- **Parallel content modules**: 4 pairs ran in parallel (Breath/Meditation, Yoga/Pilates, Martial Arts/Tai Chi, Relaxation/Philosophy) with zero coordination overhead
- **Safety Warden extensions**: Adding medical condition modifications and partner-technique boundaries to the existing warden framework was straightforward
- **Practice Journal pattern reuse**: Filesystem-as-data pattern from v1.49.8 transferred directly with no-guilt UX additions

## What Was Inefficient
- **Cultural sensitivity overhead**: Each wing required careful cultural attribution research — correct, but time-intensive
- **Try Session format discovery**: Finding the right balance between "immediately useful" and "not dangerous for beginners" required multiple iterations for physical practices
- **Cross-discipline connections**: Mapping relationships between all 8 wings produced a combinatorial explosion of potential connections; needed heuristics to scope

## Patterns Established
- **Solo practice boundary**: Partner/sparring/self-defense redirected to in-person instruction — safety warden enforces automatically
- **Text builds proprioception**: No video content for movement instruction — deliberate philosophical choice
- **No-guilt Journal UX**: Streak tracking without shame mechanics — pattern solidified across two departments
- **Cultural sensitivity framework**: Credit traditions with original terminology, no mystification or trivialization
- **Try Sessions**: Immediate hands-on experience before commitment — zero prerequisites

## Key Lessons
- Two departments proving the same architecture is significantly more convincing than one — the second instance was both faster and higher quality
- Physical safety requires different thinking than food safety — partner techniques are an absolute boundary, not a temperature threshold
- Cultural sensitivity is not optional overhead; it's integral to educational quality
- No-guilt UX in journaling reduces abandonment — shame mechanics are anti-patterns for learning
- Text-based movement instruction forces precision that video can hide

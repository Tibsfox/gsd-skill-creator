Naked Giants' degree-205 entry formalizes the power-trio-as-efficiency-peak band-format register and opens a band-format-vocabulary entry that future degrees can reference when documenting additional power-trio configurations (in Seattle and elsewhere in the engine's geographic reach).
- **The first-impact-generated-sound-in-the-engine structural observation surfaces correctly at Bighorn Sheep clash.** The engine has documented vocalization-based calls, cries, howls, huffs, and bleats across 204 prior SPS degrees, but the impact-generated-sound category had not received its own acoustic-mechanism documentation despite the category's centrality to several large-mammal combat behaviors. The Bighorn Sheep ram clash's degree-205 entry formalizes the impact-generated-sound-category acoustic-mechanism register and opens an acoustic-mechanism-vocabulary entry that future SPS degrees can reference when documenting additional impact-produced acoustic events (Pileated Woodpecker drumming, Ruffed Grouse wing-beat display, Rattlesnake rattle).
- **The E=6/E=6 pair energy match produces the highest recent-Q3 paired-energy total and delivers a structural cross-sync at matched amplitude.** Naked Giants at E=6 paired with Bighorn Sheep clash at E=6 produces combined E=12 paired-energy total, the highest recent-Q3 match and a deliberate amplitude-oscillation from the E=9 floor at degree 204 (Childbirth E=6 + Mountain Goat kid E=3). The matched-amplitude cross-sync at degree 205 demonstrates that equivalent amplitudes can emerge from mechanically unrelated processes (electric-instrument continuous-amplitude versus mechanical-impact single-event-amplitude) whose underlying kinetic-energy-to-acoustic-energy conversion principle organizes them around a common structural register.
- **The Bovidae family-scale amplitude arc at 4-3-6 establishes the widest family-scale range documented so far in SPS.** The three-consecutive-Bovidae-entry arc across degrees 203 (Mountain Goat adult bleat E=4), 204 (Mountain Goat kid contact bleat E=3), and 205 (Bighorn Sheep ram clash E=6) spans quiet adult-contact through quieter infant-contact to maximum-impact-combat amplitude, producing a family-scale amplitude-range documentation that single-species or single-genus arcs cannot match. The arc's structural observation about Bovidae-internal amplitude spread surfaces only through the three-entry accumulation.
- **The cross-sync parallel structure continues to force productive structural observations at degree 205.** The Naked-Giants-power-trio-electric-instrument-continuous-amplitude paired with the Bighorn-Sheep-ram-clash-mechanical-impact-single-event-amplitude at degree 205 produces a kinetic-energy-made-audible structural principle that parallels but differs from the convergence-as-closure principle from degree 204, the sustained-extreme-output principle from degree 199, the perception-versus-physics principle from degree 194, and the sustained-commitment-at-amplitude principle from degree 189. ## What Could Be Better

- **The pre-uplift release notes were extremely thin and contained a factual error.** The original v1.49.346 README was 55 lines (a template-driven ship bundle) and incorrectly identified Naked Giants' label as "New West Records" when the authoritative research documentation (S36 research.md, HTML research artifacts, knowledge-nodes.json) all specify **Sub Pop Records** as the correct label. The uplift expanded from 55 lines to A-grade density while correcting the label-identification error, but the discrepancy between the original README and the accompanying research artifacts suggests future degrees should ship with cross-checked metadata before tagging rather than requiring retroactive correction during uplift.
- **The Washington Bighorn Sheep transplant-and-reintroduction program deserves its own dedicated research artifact.** The multi-decade Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife program that restored the statewide population to 1,500-2,000 animals following near-extirpation is a substantial wildlife-management intervention with implications for Pacific Northwest conservation history, cross-state coordination (Washington, Oregon, Idaho), disease-transmission management (*Mycoplasma ovipneumoniae* from domestic sheep), and the Hells Canyon herd complex. A dedicated research artifact documenting the transplant program would contextualize the engine's Bighorn Sheep entries within the broader conservation-and-management history that shapes species persistence, parallel to how the Roosevelt Elk entry at degree 199 treated Theodore Roosevelt's Olympic Peninsula conservation legacy.
- **The Seattle garage-rock lineage could be developed as its own dedicated research artifact.** The six-decade transmission chain from The Sonics through Jimi Hendrix through the grunge-era economy-of-form bands to Naked Giants represents a specific Pacific Northwest genre-history tradition that the engine documents piecemeal across individual degree entries (Naked Giants at 205 and future Seattle garage-rock entries) but does not yet surface as its own structural documentation. A dedicated research artifact documenting the lineage's transmission mechanisms (touring, recording, personnel overlap, scene-and-label relationships) would surface the six-decade tradition more effectively than individual-degree documentation alone.
- **The cranial-engineering biomechanics register could be developed further across mammalian species documentation.** The Bighorn ram's double-layered-skull-and-frontal-sinus-shock-absorption impact-absorption chain is one of several sophisticated mammalian biomechanical systems that the engine could document as its own research artifact series. Parallel systems include the Muskox's cranial boss, the Giraffe's neck-and-skull fighting biomechanics, and the Sperm Whale's spermaceti-organ head-butting adaptation. A dedicated research-artifact series on mammalian impact-absorption biomechanics would provide comparative context that individual-degree documentation alone cannot generate.
- **The power-trio format documentation could be extended across the engine's broader band-format vocabulary.** The first-power-trio-in-the-engine structural observation at Naked Giants opens a band-format-vocabulary entry, but the engine has not yet documented where other power-trio configurations exist in the Seattle 360 and adjacent catalogs. A dedicated research artifact documenting power-trio configurations across Pacific Northwest rock history (Screaming Trees' trio periods, ZZ Top's non-PNW but comparably formalized trio, Dinosaur Jr.'s three-piece configuration, Nirvana's three-piece configuration before and after Dave Grohl joined) would contextualize Naked Giants' entry within the broader band-format-vocabulary history.

## Lessons Learned

1. **First-in-engine category introductions generate structural observations that individual-entry documentation cannot match.** The degree-205 dual first-in-engine introduction (first power trio on the S36 side; first impact-generated sound on the SPS side) surfaces structural observations about band-format-vocabulary expansion and acoustic-mechanism-vocabulary expansion that any single-entry documentation could not generate. Future engine documentation should explicitly track first-in-engine category introductions as a first-class structural observation, and the simultaneous dual-category-introduction at a single paired degree should be treated as a rare and productive structural event rather than as an accidental coincidence.

2. **Matched-amplitude paired-energy events at E=6/E=6 produce the kinetic-energy-to-acoustic-energy cross-axis structural principle.** The degree-205 E=6/E=6 pair energy match produces a combined E=12 paired-energy total that is the highest recent-Q3 match and that delivers a structural cross-sync at matched amplitude through mechanically unrelated processes (electric-instrument continuous-amplitude versus mechanical-impact single-event-amplitude). The matched-amplitude cross-sync demonstrates that the engine's cross-axis structural-principle vocabulary should track matched-amplitude events as a first-class observation, parallel to the sustained-commitment-at-amplitude, perception-versus-physics, sustained-extreme-output, and convergence-as-closure cross-axes from prior paired degrees.

3. **Family-scale amplitude arcs generate structural observations that single-species or single-genus arcs cannot.** The Bovidae family's three-consecutive-entry arc across degrees 203-205 at 4-3-6 amplitude progression spans the widest family-scale range so far documented in SPS and surfaces structural observations about Bovidae-internal amplitude spread (fragility-competing-against-alpine-wind extreme versus canyon-propagating-impact-percussion extreme within a single family) that single-species or single-genus accumulations cannot match. Future SPS documentation should consider whether additional family-scale amplitude arcs (Cervidae across Roosevelt Elk, Black-tailed Deer, and future Mule Deer or White-tailed Deer entries; Canidae across Coyote, Gray Wolf, and future Red Fox entries) should be structurally formalized as family-scale amplitude-documentation strategies.

4. **Acoustic-mechanism category expansion beyond vocalization opens new SPS documentation territory.** The Bighorn Sheep ram clash's first-impact-generated-sound status extends the engine's acoustic-mechanism vocabulary beyond the vocalization-based categories (calls, cries, howls, huffs, bleats) that constitute every prior SPS entry. Future SPS degrees should consider whether additional non-vocalization acoustic events (Pileated Woodpecker drumming, Ruffed Grouse wing-beat display, Rattlesnake rattle, beaver tail-slap, Sperm Whale head-butting) merit documentation within the now-established impact-generated-sound category, and the engine's acoustic-mechanism vocabulary should be treated as an open-ended classificatory infrastructure rather than as a closed vocalization-only vocabulary.

5. **Volume-per-capita efficiency is a band-configuration-to-amplitude structural register distinct from absolute amplitude alone.** Naked Giants' power-trio E=6 output with three musicians matches or exceeds the output-to-personnel ratio of larger-headcount bands in the engine's band-format vocabulary, and the efficiency emerges from the forced-full-role-occupation constraint rather than from brute-force input-scaling. Future engine documentation should consider whether volume-per-capita efficiency should be tracked as its own structural register alongside absolute amplitude, and the band-configuration-to-amplitude relationship should be treated as a first-class structural observation rather than as an implicit variable.

6. **Six-decade genre-lineage transmission chains document principle-transmission rather than sound-reproduction.** The Seattle garage-rock lineage from The Sonics through Jimi Hendrix through grunge-era economy-of-form bands to Naked Giants transmits the principle of small-bands-making-enormous-noise across sonic-register changes spanning proto-punk, psychedelic rock, grunge, and contemporary indie-garage without requiring any intermediate band to imitate its predecessors. Future genre-history documentation should explicitly distinguish principle-transmission from sound-reproduction, and the multi-decade lineage-documentation strategy should be treated as a legitimate engine strategy rather than as genealogical listing alone.

7. **Cranial-engineering biomechanics registers document physiology-to-behavior relationships that behavior-alone documentation cannot capture.** The Bighorn ram's double-layered cancellous-strut skull, frontal-sinus pneumatic shock absorbers, horn-curl force redirection, and cervical-vertebrae reinforcement together constitute an impact-absorption chain without which the ritual-combat behavior could not exist. The engineering is not incidental context; it is the evolved precondition of the behavior. Future SPS documentation should consider whether physiology-to-behavior biomechanics registers should be explicitly developed for species whose behavioral repertoire depends on specific anatomical adaptations, and the biomechanics-as-behavior-precondition framing should be treated as a first-class documentation structure rather than as supplementary detail.

8. **Post-four-degree-structural-event continuity produces post-event structural observations that the engine's documentation can track across the transition boundary.** Degree 205 documents two simultaneous post-event continuities: the post-Hardly-Art-streak Sub Pop direct re-entry on the S36 side and the post-Mountain-Goat-pair Bovidae continuation on the SPS side. The post-event continuity register enters the engine's structural-observation vocabulary as a legitimate documentation strategy for degrees that immediately follow multi-degree structural events, and future engine documentation should explicitly track post-event continuities as first-class structural observations parallel to the pre-event-accumulation and during-event-accumulation observations that prior paired degrees have already documented.

9. **Label re-entry after subsidiary-streak closure produces label-vocabulary observations that prior documentation could not surface.** Sub Pop's direct re-entry at degree 205 after the four-consecutive-degree Hardly Art subsidiary streak closed at degree 204 documents a label-vocabulary transition that the engine's documentation can track as a first-class structural observation. The parent-label-to-subsidiary-streak-to-parent-label-re-entry pattern reveals the engine's label-vocabulary as a dynamic rather than static classification infrastructure, and future label-cluster documentation should consider whether parent-subsidiary relationships (Sub Pop / Hardly Art, Epitaph / Anti-, Merge / Thrill Jockey subsidiary structures) should be explicitly tracked as label-vocabulary structural observations rather than collapsed into unified label references.

10. **Matched-amplitude cross-syncs at equivalent-amplitude different-mechanism pairs produce structural observations that mechanism-alone or amplitude-alone documentation cannot generate.** The degree-205 E=6/E=6 match between Naked Giants' power-trio electric-instrument continuous-amplitude and Bighorn Sheep ram clash's mechanical-impact single-event-amplitude produces the kinetic-energy-to-acoustic-energy-at-matched-amplitude cross-axis structural principle, a structural observation that neither mechanism-alone documentation (power-trio efficiency documentation without the Bighorn ram pairing) nor amplitude-alone documentation (E=6 accumulation across consecutive degrees without the paired cross-sync) could generate on its own. Future engine documentation should explicitly track matched-amplitude different-mechanism cross-syncs as a first-class structural observation whose productive output emerges from the paired-observation structure itself rather than from either half alone.

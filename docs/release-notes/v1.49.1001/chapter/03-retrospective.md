## Surprises

- **The space between two spacecraft is itself the measurement.** A single spacecraft records only a profile in time, but the gap between two spacecraft a known distance apart is what reveals the boundary's motion, so the catalog's first variable-separation pair turned the empty space between two spacecraft into the key instrument.
- **A coordinated cetacean pod mirrors a coordinated spacecraft pair.** The Orca, whose matrilineal pods travel as a coordinated mother-and-offspring group that adjusts its spacing as it moves, enters as the coordinated-pair mirror to the mission that flew a mother and daughter spacecraft together with an adjustable separation, so the catalog pairs a matrilineal cetacean with a mother-daughter spacecraft pair.
- **A forest flower grows in a matched pair.** The Twinflower's two pink bells hang side by side a short distance apart on one forked stalk, so the catalog's mother-daughter-pair mission is paired with a plant that bears its flowers as a matched pair held a short distance apart, the same form repeated twice on one stem.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Two spacecraft a known distance apart separate structure from motion.** A single spacecraft crossing a moving boundary cannot tell a change in space from a change in time; two spacecraft a known distance apart resolve the ambiguity through the timing between their crossings, showing that the multi-point measurement turns a long-standing ambiguity into a directly measurable quantity.
2. **A controllable baseline tunes the resolvable scale.** By adjusting the separation between the mother and daughter spacecraft, ISEE-1/2 set the scale of structure it could resolve, closing in for a thin layer and spreading out for a wider feature, showing that a controllable baseline makes a two-spacecraft pair a tunable instrument for the boundaries.
3. **The two-point measurement reveals discrete reconnection events.** The two-point field data resolved the flux transfer events at the dayside magnetopause, showing that the multi-point measurement reveals magnetic reconnection proceeding in discrete bursts that carry plasma across the boundary, rather than only as a steady flow.
4. **A mother-daughter pair is the precursor of a constellation.** ISEE-1/2 flew two spacecraft in coordinated formation, the coordinated multi-point method the later four- and five-spacecraft constellations carried forward, showing that a two-spacecraft pair can pioneer a technique that scales to many spacecraft.
5. **A cooperation can fly one mission across two agencies.** ISEE-1/2 joined NASA and the European Space Agency in one mission, NASA Goddard leading the ISEE-1 mother spacecraft and the European Space Agency operating the ISEE-2 daughter spacecraft, showing that a shared mission can distribute the two spacecraft across two agencies while operating as a single coordinated pair.
6. **A distinct mission must be kept distinct from a similarly named one.** ISEE-1/2 is the near-Earth boundary-physics pair and is distinct from ISEE-3/ICE, the separate third ISEE spacecraft at the Sun-Earth L1 point already in the catalog at degree 1.99, showing that a program with several spacecraft needs each entry kept distinct in the catalog.
7. **A reference template carries forward cleanly across a distinct-palette mission.** Because ISEE-1/2 reuses the canonical card structure of the v1.191 template, the build preserved the structural template exactly and swapped the content to the variable-separation boundary physics with a distinct magnetopause-blue / bow-shock-teal / flux-transfer-violet / mother-gold palette, making the distinct-palette mission a clean build.

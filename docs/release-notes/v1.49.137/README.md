## Summary

The sustained tone. Wayne Horvitz at 2° and the Varied Thrush at 2° — both occupying the position where a single note, held in space, teaches more than a cascade ever could. Where degree 0 was silence and degree 1 was a torrent of complexity, degree 2 pulls back to one note, perfectly placed, and lets it resonate. The circle does not proceed from simple to complex. It proceeds from silence to cascade to sustained tone. Complexity is not monotonic. The most haunting sound in the Pacific Northwest is one note.

Three data points now enable spectra rather than contrasts. The composition-improvisation spectrum (Jones fully composed, Horvitz composed improvisation, Frisell loose frameworks) is visible only because three degrees exist. The density spectrum (heron silence, thrush single tone, wren 100-note cascade) reveals that the circle oscillates between density and sparsity rather than climbing a ladder. And the FM/AM modulation pair — the Pacific Wren's frequency modulation at degree 1 matched by the Varied Thrush's amplitude modulation at degree 2 — delivers in the first three biological degrees the same two fundamental synthesis methods that defined 20th-century radio engineering and electronic music.

This release also introduces the artist-as-institution pattern at the community level. Horvitz co-founded The Royal Room in Columbia City, building the physical infrastructure where Seattle's improvised music community gathers. Jones (degree 0) built industry. Horvitz builds community. Both recognized the same truth: infrastructure shapes what music is possible. You cannot have a scene without a room.

### Part A: Wayne Horvitz — Composed Improvisation

Full deep research covering the complete Wayne Horvitz arc:

- **NYC Downtown Origins:** Born 1955, New York City. UC Santa Cruz (composition, gamelan, electronic music, free improvisation — the permissive West Coast environment that planted his lifelong refusal of genre boundaries). Returned to NYC in the late 1970s and became central to the downtown improvised music scene: the Knitting Factory, Roulette, CBGB's matinee series, ABC No Rio, and the loft spaces. The same ecosystem that nurtured Bill Frisell (degree 1).
- **Three Key Formations:** John Zorn's game pieces (*Cobra*, *Archery*, *Lacrosse* — file-card compositions where the composer functions as traffic controller, training instant compositional decision-making). The President (Horvitz's own band with Elliott Sharp and Ben Street — rock energy meets jazz harmony, not fusion but cohabitation). Pigpen (trio with Frisell and Kenny Wollesen — country, free jazz, atmospheric electronics, the bridge between Horvitz's New York and Seattle lives). Also co-founded the New York Composers Orchestra (NYCO) with Bobby Previte, Wayne Shorter, Elliott Sharp, Marty Ehrlich — institutional infrastructure for music that otherwise existed only in club settings.
- **The Move to Seattle (1988):** One year before Frisell. Capitol Hill resident since. "The Seattle Draw" pattern now has its third data point: Horvitz, Frisell (1989), and Robin Holcomb — three downtown-scene musicians choosing the PNW within a two-year window. Horvitz was not joining an existing community; he was helping to build one. Where Frisell was content to perform and record, Horvitz took on the organizational labor: booking venues, curating series, mentoring younger musicians.
- **Robin Holcomb:** Singer, pianist, composer, Horvitz's wife. Albums on Elektra/Nonesuch (*Robin Holcomb* 1990, *Rockabye* 1992, *Little Three* 2000). Appalachian folk meets art song meets minimalist composition meets jazz harmony. The Horvitz-Holcomb household on Capitol Hill represents a creative partnership where each maintains a distinct identity while sharing a harmonic language — his dense cluster-voiced chords beneath her clear folk-inflected melodies.
- **Seattle Work — Zony Mash:** Primary Seattle group (mid-1990s through 2000s). Hammond organ grooves under guitar riffs with improvisational eruptions: *Brand Spankin' New* (1998, Intuition), *Upper Egypt* (2001, Intuition), *Cold Spell* (2005, Intuition). The bridge-building principle continues: Jones bridged jazz and pop through production, Frisell bridged jazz and Americana through improvisation, Horvitz bridges jazz and funk/rock through composed frameworks.
- **Seattle Work — Gravitas Quartet:** Horvitz (piano), Peggy Lee (cello), Sara Schoenbeck (bassoon), Ron Miles (cornet). Chamber music, not jazz combo. Through-written compositions with designated improvisation sections — the architecture is planned but certain rooms are left unfurnished. *Way Out East* (2006, Songlines), *Some Places Are Forever Afternoon* (2015, National Sawdust Tracks). The border between composition and improvisation dissolves.
- **The Royal Room (2012):** Co-founded in Columbia City — dedicated to creative music (jazz, improvised music, new composition, folk, experimental). Venue as infrastructure, curation as composition, Columbia City as deliberate decentralization of the cultural map. The degree 2 equivalent of Jones's Mercury Records VP appointment: an artist moving from making music to making the conditions under which music is made.
- **Music Theory Mapping:** Six theory nodes. Composed improvisation (the defining concept — structurally complete compositions with designated openings for bounded improvisation). Polymetric structures (simultaneous time signatures — bass in 4/4, guitar in 7/8, drums mediating). Cluster voicings (adjacent seconds stacked in the middle register, Bartok/Ligeti lineage). Quartal voicings (stacked fourths, McCoy Tyner lineage, open ambiguous sonority). Electronic-acoustic integration (processing piano/organ signals in real time, parallel to Frisell's signal-chain-as-instrument). Through-composed form meets improvisation (continuous arc with improvisation erupting at structural moments like springs through rock).
- **Density Spectrum:** Jones = maximum density (all registers filled). Horvitz = selective density (middle register dense with clusters). Frisell = maximum sparsity (registers left empty). Three degrees, three positions.

### Part B: Varied Thrush — The Sustained Tone

Full deep research covering the PNW forest's most haunting voice:

- **Taxonomy:** *Ixoreus naevius*, sole living member of the monotypic genus *Ixoreus* (Greek: *ixos* mistletoe + *oreos* mountain), family Turdidae (thrushes), order Passeriformes. Separated from the robin (*Turdus migratorius*) by substantial molecular phylogenetic distance (Klicka, Voelker, and Spellman 2005, *Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution*). Adult male: orange breast with bold black breastband (diagnostic field mark), blue-gray back, orange wing bars, orange supercilium, dark auricular patch. Length 7.5-10 inches, weight 65-100 g — roughly robin-sized. In body size context: degree 0 is the largest (4+ feet), degree 1 the smallest (4 inches), degree 2 is in the middle. The circle moves where the sound demands, not linearly in body size.
- **Bioacoustics — THE DEFINING SOUND OF THE PNW FOREST:** A single, long, buzzy whistled note on one pitch, sustained 1-2 seconds, followed by 3-8 seconds of silence, then another note on a different pitch. No melodic contour within the note — pitch set at onset and held. Successive notes differ by intervals from minor second to major third. One note every 4-10 seconds. The polar opposite of the Pacific Wren: where the wren delivers 100+ notes in 10 seconds, the thrush delivers one. The wren is Coltrane's sheets of sound; the thrush is Miles Davis playing one note and letting it ring. Multiple naturalists and sound ecologists have independently described this as "the most haunting sound in the Pacific Northwest forest" (Cornell Lab, National Audubon, Sibley 2000, Wahl et al. 2005).
- **Amplitude Modulation:** The buzzy quality is produced by AM synthesis — the syrinx generates a carrier tone at 2.5-5 kHz, superimposed with rapid amplitude modulation at 20-50 Hz, producing sideband frequencies at carrier +/- modulation. This is AM radio in biological form: carrier wave modulated by information signal to produce complex output. Direct cross-reference to the AMR (AM Radio) project. Armstrong's FM superseded AM for broadcast because FM rejects amplitude noise — the Pacific Wren's song IS FM, the Varied Thrush's IS AM. Both synthesis methods that defined 20th-century radio represented in the first three degrees.
- **The Silence Between Notes:** The 3-8 second inter-note pause is compositional. It allows the reverberant tail to decay, creates expectation for the next note, and is the frame that gives each tone its weight. In Japanese aesthetics: *ma* — the pregnant pause that gives meaning to what surrounds it. In recording engineering: pre-delay — allowing the direct sound to be heard before the reverberant field builds. The thrush's silence is pre-delay in biology.
- **Mature Conifer Forest Dependency:** Dense canopy closure >70%, Douglas fir, western red cedar, western hemlock, Sitka spruce. Altitudinal migration: breeds in montane forest (2,000-5,000 ft), descends to lowland Seattle in winter (October-April). Winter presence in Capitol Hill, Discovery Park, Seward Park, Washington Park Arboretum. The song of the Varied Thrush on a rainy January morning in Capitol Hill is one of the most distinctive sensory experiences of living in Seattle.
- **Acoustic Propagation:** Song optimized for dense conifer forest. The 2.5-5 kHz band falls in the window of least attenuation (below 2 kHz scatters from trunks, above 8 kHz attenuates from atmospheric absorption in humid PNW air). Sustained duration (1-2 seconds) provides resistance to masking by transient noise — same principle as sonar (longer ping carries further). Inter-note silence allows reverberant tail to decay fully, preventing inter-note interference.
- **Acoustic Theory Mapping:** Seven theory nodes. Sustained tone as drone/pedal point (Bach organ pedals, jazz pedal tones, electronic synth pads). AM timbral synthesis/ring modulation (Moog Modular, EMS VCS3, ARP 2600 parallels). Minimal melody (Webern's pointillism, La Monte Young's sustained tones, Steve Reich's minimalism). Inter-note silence as rhythmic structure (6-15 BPM — hypometric, below human musical tempo, subjective rhythmization). Frequency as emotional register (2.5-5 kHz at peak human auditory sensitivity, descending pitch tendency, AM-produced roughness). The drone tradition mapped across Indian tanpura, Scottish bagpipe, organ pedal point, and Brian Eno's *Music for Airports* (1978).

### The Degree 2° Parallel

The resonance between Wayne Horvitz and the Varied Thrush emerged on four independent axes:

1. **The sustained tone as architecture** — Horvitz's composed improvisation holds structure while leaving space for what happens inside it; the thrush holds a single frequency and lets the silence around it do the work
2. **Atmospheric space** — Horvitz's chamber works (*Some Places Are Forever Afternoon*) evoke specific landscapes and times of day; the thrush IS the acoustic landscape of the PNW rain forest
3. **AM/Composed modulation** — The thrush modulates amplitude on a carrier tone; Horvitz modulates improvisation on a composed structure. Both encode complexity onto a simple foundation through controlled variation
4. **The note that hangs in the air** — Both operate in the territory where one note is enough. Horvitz's piano clusters and the thrush's buzzy whistle both fill their acoustic environment with a single, sustained, weighted presence

## Retrospective

### What Worked
- **Three points make a spectrum.** Two releases can only show contrast. Three reveal a continuum — and the engine's first non-obvious insight (the circle is non-monotonic in complexity) emerges from having three data points on the composition-improvisation, density, and modulation axes.
- **FM and AM are the fundamental modulation pair.** Pacific Wren (FM) and Varied Thrush (AM) — both represented in the first three degrees. The bioacoustic engine produced the same pair that Chowning and Armstrong gave electronic music and radio engineering.
- **The artist-as-institution pattern tracks cultural impact beyond the music itself.** Jones built industry (Mercury Records VP, Qwest Records). Horvitz built community (The Royal Room, Columbia City). Frisell built none, letting the music speak for itself. Three different models of how musicians shape a city's infrastructure.
- **The Opening Triangle is the species engine's thesis statement.** Silence (degree 0) to cascade (degree 1) to sustained tone (degree 2) — the first three positions prove the circle is not a linear progression but an exploration of fundamentally different acoustic strategies.
- **Robin Holcomb as degree neighbor enriches the human network.** The Horvitz-Holcomb household represents a creative partnership dimension the engine hadn't tracked — complementary registers, shared harmonic language, distinct artistic identities.

### What Could Be Better
- **No spectrograms or audio analysis embedded.** The AM synthesis description would benefit enormously from an actual spectrogram showing the carrier tone, sideband frequencies, and amplitude modulation pattern.
- **The Royal Room deserves deeper venue analysis.** Programming data, booking patterns, audience demographics — the venue-as-composition thesis needs quantitative evidence beyond the architectural description.
- **Indigenous territorial acknowledgment remains surface-level.** Duwamish and Coast Salish connection to the conifer forests needs nation-specific attribution, not just a blanket acknowledgment. Future degrees should improve this.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Three degrees reveal the engine's actual thesis: complexity is non-monotonic.** The circle does not climb from simple to complex. It oscillates. Degree 0 = silence. Degree 1 = maximum melodic density. Degree 2 = one note, sustained. The pattern will continue to surprise, and the engine must track it without forcing a narrative of linear progression.
2. **Composed improvisation is the mediating position the engine needed.** Jones composes fully. Frisell improvises freely. Horvitz sits between them — structurally complete compositions with designated spaces for improvisation. Three degrees, three positions on the spectrum. This positions the engine to classify every subsequent artist's methodology relative to these three anchor points.
3. **The AM/FM pair is the first cross-cluster Rosetta connection.** The Varied Thrush's AM synthesis connects to the AMR (AM Radio) research project — same mathematics, different medium. This is the Rosetta engine working as designed: independent research at different degree positions discovering the same underlying mathematics. The connections are found, not forced.

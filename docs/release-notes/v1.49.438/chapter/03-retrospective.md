- **The Bald Eagle combat entry documents the locked-talon display at behavioral-physics resolution but does not enumerate the observation-site-level witness documentation that confirms combat behavior at specific Pacific Northwest locations.** Future raptor-behavior entries should include observation-site-level witness records from **Skagit River** interpretive sites, **San Juan Islands** research programs, the **Cornell Lab of Ornithology** eBird/Macaulay Library documentation, and the **Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife** survey data, so that the combat-behavior documentation is anchored to specific witnessed events at specific geographies rather than only to the species-level behavioral description. - **The graduated E=5 → E=6 → E=7 single-species record for Bald Eagle is the engine's most precisely calibrated three-entry species sequence, but the engine does not yet enumerate the comparable graduated-amplitude records for other multi-entry species.** Future multi-entry SPS documentation should audit the existing multi-entry species records for the same graduated-amplitude structural register and surface the comparable sequences at first-class resolution, so that the Bald Eagle's three-entry graduated progression is positioned within the engine's broader vocabulary of multi-entry species records rather than standing alone as an unmatched structural register.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Second-appearance entries are structurally productive documentary registers when the frame shifts from the first-entry angle rather than duplicating it.** **Skin Yard's** return at degree 297 shifts the frame from **Jack Endino** as producer (the first-entry angle at degree 264) to Jack Endino as guitarist and Skin Yard as a band in its own right (the return-entry angle). The thirty-three-degree accumulated engine context makes the frame shift structurally productive because the proto-grunge sequence that has just closed the 294-296 Proto-Grunge Origin Arc provides the context in which Skin Yard's position at the origin point can be registered with maximum accumulated context. The lesson is that return appearances should be frame-shifted rather than duplicated, and the frame shift should be anchored to accumulated engine context that was not available at the first entry.
2. **Graduated-amplitude multi-entry species records are structurally productive when each entry documents a behaviorally distinct acoustic register.** The **Bald Eagle's** three-entry sequence across degrees 221 (contact call, E=5), 222 (territorial calls, E=6), and 297 (locked-talon combat, E=7) documents three distinct behavioral registers at three distinct amplitudes, and the graduated E=5 → E=6 → E=7 progression accurately tracks the behavioral escalation from low-stakes social communication through territorial presence establishment to maximum territorial engagement. The lesson is that multi-entry species records should select behaviorally distinct events rather than the same behavior at different intensities, and the amplitude register should follow the behavioral register rather than merely reflecting recording-level differences.
3. **Compilation cross-reference geometry is structurally load-bearing for documenting founding documentary acts.** The ***Deep Six*** 1986 compilation now threads through four consecutive S36 degrees at 294-297 — **Green River**, **Malfunkshun**, **Mother Love Bone** (formed from the Green River split with Andrew Wood joining Stone Gossard and Jeff Ament after the Malfunkshun era), and **Skin Yard** — making the compilation the engine's densest cross-reference thread to any single Pacific Northwest music documentary act. The four-degree cross-reference density is structurally productive because it concentrates the documentary weight of the *Deep Six* register at the precise rotation position where the proto-grunge subject matter reaches its documentary peak. The lesson is that compilation documentation should be anchored to the cross-reference geometry that the individual band entries establish, and the cross-reference thread should be surfaced explicitly at first-class resolution rather than left implicit.
4. **Label infrastructure documentation should be preserved at first-class resolution alongside band and album documentation.** **Daniel House's** **C/Z Records** threads through multiple adjacent degrees with a four-degree return symmetry (**Treepeople** at 263 → Skin Yard at 264 → Skin Yard return at 297 → **Treepeople** return at 298) that mirrors the *Deep Six* compilation register and extends the cross-reference geometry across the label's broader catalog. The label's position as the pre-Sub Pop infrastructure for Seattle's heavy underground is structurally distinct from **Sub Pop's** subsequent commercial formalization, and preserving the C/Z Records thread at first-class resolution is necessary to document the pre-commercial infrastructure that made the commercial moment possible. The lesson is that label documentation carries structural weight independent of individual band documentation, and the label thread should be registered as its own cross-reference axis.
5. **Founding-lineup documentation should account for subsequent trajectory significance rather than only documenting the lineup at the moment of formation.** The **Skin Yard** 1985 founding lineup — **Jack Endino** (guitar), **Ben McMillan** (vocals), **Daniel House** (bass), **Matt Cameron** (drums) — is extraordinary in retrospect because each member's subsequent trajectory carried consequential weight: Endino as Seattle's most important producer; Cameron as **Soundgarden**'s and then **Pearl Jam's** drummer across thirty-plus years; McMillan as **Gruntruck** co-founder; House as **C/Z Records** founder. The lesson is that founding-lineup documentation should trace each member's subsequent trajectory at first-class resolution so that the lineup's full significance is legible, and the retrospective-significance register should be surfaced explicitly rather than left implicit in the individual member biographies.
6. **Behavioral-physics documentation for raptor displays should include the communicative function alongside the physical mechanics.** The **Bald Eagle's** locked-talon aerial combat display is not just a physical behavior (two birds, talons locked, cartwheeling freefall) — it is a communicative act that establishes which eagle is willing to commit to the most extreme territorial defense. The display demonstrates willingness to accept serious risk, and an intruder that witnesses or experiences the locked-talon response learns that the territory holder will escalate to maximum commitment. The lesson is that behavior documentation should include the communicative function alongside the physical mechanics, because the communicative function is structurally load-bearing for understanding why the behavior exists and when it is performed, and the physical mechanics alone cannot convey the behavior's role in the species' territorial ecology.
7. **Conservation-recovery context is structurally load-bearing for understanding current behavior observability.** The **Bald Eagle's** locked-talon aerial combat display is currently observable at high frequency along Pacific Northwest rivers and shorelines, but this observability is itself a conservation-recovery artifact: three decades ago, when DDT-driven decline had reduced the species' population to a fraction of its historical baseline, the combat display was rare in many parts of the range because there simply weren't enough eagles to produce the territorial conflicts that the display is performed to resolve. The 2007 delisting from the federal endangered species list and the multi-decade population recovery since the 1972 DDT ban are what make the combat display a current observable behavior rather than a historical rarity. The lesson is that conservation status and recovery history are structurally load-bearing for SPS documentation of behaviors that depend on population density, and the conservation-recovery register should be preserved at first-class resolution alongside the behavioral description.
8. **Derivation-from-foundational-practice structural registers apply to both cultural and biological subjects.** **Jack Endino's** production philosophy (minimum interference, document the live performance, preserve the room's energy) is philosophically inseparable from the Skin Yard rehearsal-room practice where he learned what it felt like to play the music; the band taught the producer. **In parallel**, the **Bald Eagle's** locked-talon aerial combat display is physiologically inseparable from the species' nesting ecology; the behavior is performed at maximum intensity during late winter and early spring when egg-laying is imminent and annual nest investment is at peak. Both cases demonstrate that elevated external roles (producer identity, combat behavior) derive from foundational practice (band musicianship, nest defense). The lesson is that derivation-from-foundational-practice is a structural register the engine can document at first-class resolution across both cultural and biological subjects, and the derivation should be traced explicitly rather than left implicit.

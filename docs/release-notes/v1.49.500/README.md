1. **The first degree sets the grain size for the engine.** Degree 0 (Quincy Jones + Great Blue Heron) established the pairing discipline, the energy-level assignment logic, and the prose register that every subsequent degree had to match or improve upon. 3. **Farewells that do not know they are farewells are more accurate than announced farewells.** Unwound's dissolution after *Leaves Turn Inside You* without advance notice produced a final record whose emotional weight was assembled by listeners after the dissolution. Planned meta-structure would have been suspect; emergent meta-structure is earned.

Degree 359's Puget Sound in silence makes the frame explicit by documenting the Sound in the absence of events. The final subject is the condition in which every other subject exists.

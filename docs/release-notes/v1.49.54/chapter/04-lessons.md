   COL, CAS, ECO map the wild landscape. GDN maps the garden. AGR maps the industrial agriculture that sits between — the orchards, vineyards, and irrigated fields that are neither wild nor garden. The bioregion map now has all three scales: wilderness, cultivation, and the managed landscape between.

2. **The Columbia River is the project's central thread.**
   It appears in SAL (salmon), HGE (hydroelectric), THE (energy), AGR (irrigation), COL (watershed), and WYR (timber transport). Six projects, one river. The Research series is a map of the Columbia Basin viewed from six different angles. The river connects them all.

# Retrospective — v1.49.225

## Lessons Learned

1. Returns are now a structural category in the engine, not isolated events. Three return artists by degree 85 -- Death Cab (58/82), Jurado (45/84), Votolato (46/85) -- with three distinct transformation patterns: genre shift, genre depth, energy increase. The engine can now analyze any future return against this taxonomy. The question is not whether an artist will return, but how the return transforms the original entry
2. The energy increase paradox at degree 85 reveals a limitation of the energy classification system and simultaneously enriches it. E=2 to E=3 on return -- removing instruments produces more energy. This is only paradoxical if energy is understood as volume. If energy is understood as dynamic range, the increase is logical: fewer instruments competing means the voice occupies more of the frequency space. The stripped confession is louder than the confessional because there is nothing else in the way
3. The 39-degree arc symmetry (Jurado 45-84, Votolato 46-85) is the engine's first observed structural resonance between return artists. Two consecutive original entries returning at two consecutive degrees with the same span. The observation is documented without claiming causation. Pattern, not mechanism
4. The folk-return zone (82, 84, 85) establishes that the engine can enter genre-return phases where multiple artists revisit folk contexts in rapid succession. Acoustic intimacy (82), late whisper (84), stripped confession (85) -- three acoustic strippings with three different outcomes (presence, silence, projection). The zone may continue at degree 86 or break
5. The Cricetidae trio (83-84-85) at three radically different habitats demonstrates that family-level taxonomic runs can produce the most productive ecological contrasts. The Sciuridae octet (75-82) varied within forest/alpine contexts. The Cricetidae trio spans varied, rocky, and aquatic. Family diversity as ecological diversity
6. The quiet plop as a non-vocal locomotion sound creates a third acoustic category alongside vocalization and construction noise. The engine's species-side acoustic elements now include: vocal calls (most entries), construction sounds (Beaver chewing 62, Woodrat gnaw 84), and locomotion sounds (Muskrat plop 85). The categories will likely expand
7. The mammalian imbalance at twenty-five consecutive degrees is past any defensible limit. The engine requires Aves. The raptor gap at 86 entries is a structural deficiency. Return to birds is non-negotiable at this point. The marsh introduction at degree 85 does create a habitat bridge for potential waterfowl or wading bird entries

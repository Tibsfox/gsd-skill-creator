## Earliest Seattle HC (1980)


- Solger's 1980 formation date places them at the absolute origin of Seattle hardcore punk; the city's punk scene in 1977-1979 was influenced by British punk and American proto-punk but had not yet produced the accelerated, abbreviated, politically direct form that defines hardcore; 1980 is the threshold year, and Solger is on the correct side of it; their recordings are primary documents of what the form sounded like before it had a name in Seattle, before there were dedicated venues, before there was a local label infrastructure, before grunge made the Pacific Northwest music scene globally visible

## SRKW echolocation


- click trains are among the most precisely engineered acoustic phenomena produced by any mammal; SRKW clicks are produced by pneumatic mechanisms in the nasal passages, shaped by the acoustic lens of the melon, projected forward into the water column, and received back through the lower jaw and acoustic fat channel to the cochlea; the result is a biosonar system capable of resolving objects centimeters in diameter at ranges of hundreds of meters; the clicks documented in the SPS track represent not only a communication behavior but a sensory system -- the primary modality through which orcas experience their underwater environment

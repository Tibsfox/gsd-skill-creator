## Lessons Learned

1. **One standardized observatory can serve a broad community at once.** Rather than building a separate one-purpose spacecraft for each investigation, OGO flew a common observatory carrying a large and varied complement of instruments, showing that a standardized platform can serve many investigators on one design.
2. **A large instrument complement gives a broad simultaneous survey.** By carrying fields, plasma, and energetic-particle instruments together, the eccentric observatories surveyed many parameters of the deep magnetosphere at once, showing that a broad complement turns a focused measurement into a comprehensive survey.
3. **A polar orbit and the Earth's rotation give a global ground track.** The low polar POGO orbits, sweeping across all longitudes as the Earth turned beneath them, mapped the geomagnetic field across the entire globe, showing that a polar orbit gives a satellite the globe-spanning coverage a global field survey needs.
4. **A satellite survey turns scattered readings into a global model.** The POGO magnetometer survey turned scattered ground and aircraft readings into a globe-spanning satellite map of the geomagnetic field, showing that a consistent satellite survey gives a global picture that ground stations alone cannot.
5. **A standardized bus amortizes one design across many observatories.** By flying six observatories on a common modular bus, OGO needed only a new complement of experiments for each, showing that a shared platform spreads the design cost across the program and lets one design carry many investigations.
6. **A dual orbit-class architecture reaches two regions from one design.** By placing eccentric observatories in the deep magnetosphere and polar observatories over the ionosphere, OGO surveyed two distinct regions from one standardized design, showing that two orbit classes give one program a comprehensive reach.
7. **A reference template carries forward cleanly across a distinct-palette mission.** Because OGO reuses the canonical card structure of the v1.193 template, the build preserved the structural template exactly and swapped the content to the comprehensive geophysical survey with a distinct survey-amber / eccentric-blue / polar-teal / field-violet palette, making the distinct-palette mission a clean build.

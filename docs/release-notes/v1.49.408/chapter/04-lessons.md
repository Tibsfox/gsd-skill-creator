# Lessons — v1.49.408

14 lessons extracted. Classification source: ⚙ rule-based · 🤖 LLM tiebreaker (needs review) · 👤 human.

1. **Arc-closing degrees carry their own structural weight distinct from mid-arc degrees.**
   The Oso mudslide at degree 267 demonstrates that arc-closing entries benefit from being placed at the arc's peak amplitude and peak consequence simultaneously, because the closing degree then carries both the structural-peak and the sequential-close signals together. Arc-closing degrees that are placed at lower amplitudes or lower consequence than earlier entries in the arc produce a less coherent closing shape. The pattern applies to cultural arcs (commercial windows, career arcs, scene periods) as well as to geophysical arcs.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.481`) · lesson #8713_

2. **Peak-of-register reach deserves explicit documentary vocabulary whether the register is cultural-commercial or geophysical-catastrophic.**
   Candlebox's commercial peak at four million sold and the Oso mudslide's catastrophic peak at 43 deaths are peak-of-series events at radically different registers but at comparable peak-of-series structural positions. The engine's vocabulary can hold both in equivalent documentary weight because the peak-of-register structure is the shared structural feature. The lesson is that peak-of-register reach is a generalizable documentary concept that applies across radically different subject registers.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #8714_

3. **Specific quantitative anchors make claims falsifiable and verifiable in ways that qualitative descriptions do not.**
   The four-million-sold RIAA quadruple-platinum certification and the 43-death Oso fatality count are specific, verifiable, public-record quantitative anchors that convert qualitative claims (commercial peak, deadliest landslide) into falsifiable statements. Future degrees should continue to anchor qualitative claims in specific quantitative figures where such figures exist.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #8715_

4. **Native-community relationships to the geographic space of a degree's subject deserve explicit documentary presence rather than background-context treatment.**
   The Stillaguamish Tribe of Indians' treaty-reserved fishing rights on the North Fork Stillaguamish River, the tribe's loss of community members in the slide, and the tribe's pre-slide and post-slide relationship to the valley are structural features of the Oso event that cannot be treated as background context. The engine's documentation should continue to bring Native-community relationships into explicit documentary presence at degrees where the degree's subject is geographically anchored in Native territory.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.448`) · lesson #8716_

5. **Commercial window registers should be opened at specific peak-occupant degrees rather than at general-context degrees.**
   The post-grunge commercial window of 1993-1995 is a specific structural feature of Pacific Northwest rock commerce, and the engine's decision to open the register at the Candlebox degree — where the window's peak occupant sits — anchors the register in a specific peak-commercial event. Commercial window registers opened at diffuse general-context degrees lose the specificity that makes the register falsifiable and applicable to other windows.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.455`) · lesson #8717_

6. **Label infrastructure registers (labels, imprints, A&R decisions) should be opened at specific signings rather than at general-context mentions.**
   Madonna's Maverick Records imprint enters the engine at the Candlebox 1993 signing because that specific signing is where the imprint had a specific effect on the Pacific Northwest commercial rock story. Label-infrastructure registers opened at diffuse Madonna-adjacent or Maverick-adjacent degrees would lose the specificity that makes the register verifiable and applicable to other label signings.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #8718_

7. **Documentary seriousness about high-consequence events requires separating the human-weight register from the mechanistic register.**
   The Oso slide's documentation separates the 43 deaths (handled in the dedication and the Part B bullets about the Stillaguamish Tribe) from the slide mechanism (handled in the Part B bullets about the 7.6 million cubic yards, the Hazel landslide site, and the USGS Open-File Report). The separation lets the documentation carry documentary seriousness without converting the human weight into spectacle and without letting the mechanistic documentation become insensitive to the human consequences.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #8719_

8. **Arc amplitude profiles have shapes that are worth recording even when the shape analysis is deferred to a future degree.**
   The geophysics arc's final amplitude profile — 5, 7, 5, 2, 6, 7, 8 — is an arc-shaped progression with a mid-arc low point and an arc-close high point. Recording the profile at the arc-closing degree lets subsequent survey or retrospective degrees analyze the shape as a structural object. Amplitude profiles that are not recorded cannot be analyzed later, so the discipline of recording the profile at the arc close is a forward-investment documentary practice.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #8720_

9. **Survivor accounts and the "warning that came too late" framing belong to the acoustic record because they are themselves acoustic-temporal facts.**
   Survivors of the Oso slide reported hearing the slide before it reached them but not long enough to act on the warning. This is both a human testimony and an acoustic-temporal fact: the low-frequency bass rumble of the slide propagated faster than the slide's leading mass, creating a short warning interval that was insufficient for action. The acoustic-temporal fact is itself part of the documentary record of the slide's acoustic profile and is not separable from the broader documentation.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.439`) · lesson #8721_

10. **Multi-decade band trajectories require compression discipline when the commercial peak is the documentary anchor.**
   Candlebox's multi-decade 1990-to-present trajectory is compressed in the Part A record because the 1993 commercial peak is the degree's documentary anchor. The compression is a deliberate editorial choice that prioritizes the commercial-peak register over the full-trajectory register. Future survey degrees can address the full-trajectory register for bands where the compression loses important structural information.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.457`) · lesson #8722_

11. **The post-grunge commercial window register is opened here at 1993-1995 but would benefit from more explicit closing-boundary documentation at a subsequent degree.**
   The engine documents the window's opening conditions (1991 Nirvana and Pearl Jam breakthroughs) and its peak occupant (Candlebox at four million sold) at degree 267, but the specific closing-boundary mechanism — when and how the window closed, which bands were the last signings inside the window versus the first signings after the window had closed — is not documented at comparable precision. A subsequent degree could formalize the closing-boundary documentation to complete the register's full temporal specification.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.409`) · lesson #8723_

12. **The Stillaguamish Tribe relationship is noted in the dedication and the Part B bullets but could anchor more of the degree's central documentation.**

A rebalance in a subsequent degree or in supplementary chapter content could bring the tribal relationship closer to the center of the documentary structure.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.421`) · lesson #8724_

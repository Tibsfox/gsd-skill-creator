# Retrospective — v1.49.84

## Lessons Learned

1. **HFR + HFE form a natural pair.** Reproduction (consumer) and Engineering (production) are two sides of the same signal chain. Cross-references between them are the densest in the batch.
2. **PIN's network topology approach works.** Mapping industrial music as a graph (nodes = artists, edges = collaborations) reveals structure that chronological narrative misses.
3. **MCS's quantitative depth is unique.** Cost modeling with NPV analysis, sensitivity charts, and scenario comparison — the most numbers-heavy project in the series.

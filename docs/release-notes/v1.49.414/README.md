- **The E=17 apex was documented at the correct degree.** The engine's peak combined score required the co-occurrence of an E=8 S36 anchor with an E=9 SPS entry, and the only two candidate SPS E=9 events in the engine's catalog were Lightning Strike (used at degree 260) and Orca Breaching (the obvious second candidate). - **The Orca Arc's energy peak and the Seattle HC arc's mid-point co-located.** Both arcs reach their structural centers at degree 273 — the Orca Arc's energy peak at the breach, and the Seattle HC arc's commitment mid-point at The Refuzors — and the two arcs' simultaneous midpoint placement produced the paired-apex structure that the engine has been architecting across the two tracks. The co-location is rare and correct; no other degree in the engine's remaining range produces this paired-midpoint structure.
- **The lightning-orca symmetry was made explicit.** The engine's two E=9 events — geophysical and biological — were documented in direct parallel, with cross-references between degree 260 (Lightning Strike) and degree 273 (Orca Breaching) established in the release note and in the research assets. The symmetry between atmospheric discharge and marine-mammal breaching was the degree's most analytically useful cross-sync, and the documentation foregrounds it rather than treating the two E=9s as independent entries.

### What Could Be Better

- **The Refuzors' discography is under-documented in the research assets.** While the band's multi-decade commitment is well-described in the release note and in the `273-the-refuzors/research.md` asset, specific release dates, track listings, and personnel across eras are thinner than the surrounding hardcore entries (The Fartz at degree 270, Solger at degree 271) received. - **The orca breach acoustic measurement methodology is cited without primary-literature anchoring.** The 165-decibel figure is consistent with the published literature on cetacean impulsive acoustic events, but the release note does not cite a specific measurement study. A reference to primary acoustic-measurement literature — such as work by the NOAA Northwest Fisheries Science Center or the Center for Whale Research — would improve the research asset's citation profile.
- **The Refuzors' political content is alluded to but not specifically catalogued.** The "politically engaged" description recurs across the release note and the research asset without specifying the particular political content of the band's lyrics or stage presence across different eras. A follow-up research pass could document the band's specific political themes (anti-authoritarian, anti-commercial, anti-nostalgic) with lyrical examples, strengthening the E=8 commitment-weighted register.

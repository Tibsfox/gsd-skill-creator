2. **Stochastic presence is the most information-rich signal type.**
   A resident species tells you about local habitat quality -- if the habitat supports the bird, the bird is there. A migrant tells you about seasonal timing -- if the calendar says April, the flycatcher arrives. An irruptive species tells you about conditions in ecosystems you cannot see -- if the siskin is here, the spruce forests of British Columbia have failed. The unpredictable signal carries more information per appearance than the predictable one because each appearance requires explanation.

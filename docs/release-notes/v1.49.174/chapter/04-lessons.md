1. **The visitor validates the engine's geographic reach.**
   For thirty-seven degrees, the engine operated on an implicit assumption: artists belong to Seattle because they lived, were born, worked, or were based in Seattle. Degree 38 breaks that assumption. Booker T. Jones belongs to the Seattle 360 engine because he repeatedly performs in Seattle, because his tradition flows into Seattle's tradition, and because the relationship is real even though the residency never was. The engine is not a census of residents. It is a map of musical influences and presences. The visitor validates the gravitational model over the biographical model.

## Lessons Learned

1. **E=6 operates on a clustering rhythm rather than on a fixed periodic rhythm.** The five E=6 entries span gaps of 71 degrees (36→107), then 1 degree (107→108), then 1 degree (108→109), then 14 degrees (109→123). The rhythm is irregular but structured — E=6 clusters when structurally right (the 107-109 three-peat), rests when the engine needs mid-range energy, and returns when the paired-release dynamic calls for a ceiling spike. Future E=6 entries should arrive at structurally right moments rather than at calendar-regular intervals.
2. **Identity through differentiation is a valid structural principle for both axes.** Wellwater Conspiracy defined by what it is not (Soundgarden, Pearl Jam, Monster Magnet). Barrow's Goldeneye defined by what it is not (Common Goldeneye, Bufflehead, Hooded Merganser). Both sides recognized by contrast within a larger group. The principle operates across axes and establishes a comparison-based identity framework that future entries can invoke.
3. **The widest single-degree energy gap (E=6 vs E=3, three-level spread) holds without contradiction.** Paired releases can sustain maximum artist-axis energy alongside near-minimum species-axis energy at the same degree. The engine's two axes are structurally independent enough to hold opposite dynamic extremes simultaneously. Future paired releases should not avoid wide gaps; the gap itself can become the structural finding.
4. **Supergroup networks demand hub-centered documentation rather than band-centered documentation.** Wellwater Conspiracy as standalone band is less legible than Wellwater Conspiracy as one node in the Matt Cameron supergroup network. Hub-centered framing (Cameron connects Soundgarden + Pearl Jam + Temple of the Dog + Wellwater Conspiracy) is the correct analytical approach for supergroup entries. Future supergroup entries should identify the hub first and the specific project second.
5. **Label anomalies deserve documentation without moral inflation.** Hollywood Records (Disney-owned) releasing a Seattle garage/space rock side project is a structural anomaly. The entry documented the anomaly at the structural level (first Disney-owned label in the engine, contrast with the Sub Pop / Kill Rock Stars / K Records independent spine) without inflating it into a major/independent-label moral argument. Structural documentation is sufficient; moral framing is not required.
6. **Visual-primary acoustic-secondary display configurations require formalization rather than call-in-isolation treatment.** Barrow's Goldeneye's head-throw-plus-quiet-grunt is not a quiet call alone; it is a visual-primary display with an acoustic marker at the apex. Treating the call in isolation misses the structural finding. Future waterfowl entries with similar visual-primary configurations (Wood Duck, Hooded Merganser, Common Goldeneye) should use the formalization established at degree 123.
7. **Secondary cavity nesters link back to cavity-excavator family streaks through ecological dependency.** Barrow's Goldeneye's secondary-cavity-nesting role creates a direct ecological link back to the Picidae nonet at degrees 107-115 — the Pileated Woodpecker's excavations at degree 108 or 109 literally provide the nest sites Barrow's uses at degree 123. Future secondary-cavity-nester entries should document these ecological dependencies as first-class structural findings rather than footnote-level ecological details.
8. **Comparison-pair two-degree frames work when the comparison is genuine and both halves deserve individual treatment.** Barrow's / Common Goldeneye at degrees 123-124 works because both species have enough individual ecological identity to sustain a full degree each, and the comparison is genuine (same genus, overlapping range, similar appearance, systematic differences). Comparison pairs that lack this depth (both halves insufficient to sustain individual treatment, or the comparison trivial) should be compressed into a single degree.
9. **Genus-exploration trilogies (three consecutive species from one genus) validate family-streak durability.** The *Bucephala* trilogy (122 Bufflehead, 123 Barrow's, 124 Common) demonstrates that the Anatidae streak sustains internal substructure — not only are six consecutive waterfowl documented, but three of them form a coherent genus sub-exploration. Future family streaks should be analyzed for genus-level substructure, and trilogies deserve dedicated documentation at the trilogy level rather than only at the individual-species level.
10. **Side-project liberation as organizing principle explains both the Wellwater Conspiracy's existence and its E=6 register.** The project exists because Cameron and McBain needed an outlet free from main-band structural constraints. The E=6 register emerges because that liberation produces unfiltered energy. Organizing-principle framing (why the project exists) correctly predicts sonic register (what the project sounds like), and the entry honored this structural coherence.

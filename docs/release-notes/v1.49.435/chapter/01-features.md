## THE PROTO-GRUNGE ORIGIN ARC OPENS (294-296)


- Green River is degree one of a three-degree arc that traces grunge's direct genealogy: Green River (294) → Malfunkshun (295) → Mother Love Bone (296); this is not an influence arc like the Dead Kennedys or Black Flag entries -- it is the actual origin sequence; the musicians who created grunge were in these three bands; the arc documents how the founding lineup dispersed into two trajectories (Mudhoney and Pearl Jam) from a single point of origin; Green River is that origin point

## Green River dissolution as generative event


- Green River's split was not a failure but a bifurcation: Arm and Turner left to form Mudhoney, which became the purest expression of the original proto-grunge energy at E=8; Ament and Gossard stayed together and formed the band that would become Pearl Jam (through Malfunkshun and Mother Love Bone); the two trajectories from one origin point are among the most consequential splits in rock history; both trajectories are documented in the Seattle 360 engine, with Green River as the documented common ancestor

## Mark Arm and Steve Turner


- Arm (born Mark McLaughlin, 1962) was the vocalist and guitarist who would go on to front Mudhoney through four decades; Turner played guitar in Green River and Mudhoney and is the melodic intelligence behind the Mudhoney sound; their collaboration in Green River is the compressed form of what they would expand in Mudhoney; Green River is where both artists arrived at their full sonic identities

   Degree 118 ships the first Shoegaze entry (Genre Stage 94) and the first Anatidae entry (new family) simultaneously. Each first carries significant structural weight on its own; the pairing produces a peak rather than two independent findings. Future double-first opportunities should be identified early in catalog construction — when new genre stages and new taxonomic families can be aligned to the same degree, the alignment multiplies the value of both openings, and the engine's sequencing logic should preferentially arrange such synchronizations when the narrative arc permits.

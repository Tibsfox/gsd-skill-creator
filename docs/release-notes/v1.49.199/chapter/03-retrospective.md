# Retrospective — v1.49.199

## Lessons Learned

1. Cross-degree appearances create structural cascades -- one person appearing at two degrees in different contexts produces simultaneous shifts in genre, energy, label, and neighborhood tracking. The cascade is the insight: multiple structural events with a single cause
2. The band-to-solo extraction reveals the folk skeleton inside the rock body -- Gibbard's solo performances expose that his songs are structurally folk songs arranged as indie rock. The voice is the invariant; the genre depends on the surrounding context
3. E=2 is "intimate," not "unknown" -- the same person produces E=4 work (band) and E=2 work (solo). This distinction should be applied to future energy assessments: scale of the work, not fame of the person
4. Convergent evolution is best taught through consecutive degrees -- two real ducks (58-59) followed by one not-duck (60) is more powerful than any abstract explanation. The engine teaches by sequence
5. The unique biology suite (sinking, feather eating, lobed feet, floating nest, back-riding chicks) rewards deep research -- the Pied-billed Grebe is not just another waterbird but a collection of evolutionary innovations
6. Tempo direction (accelerating) as a new acoustic property extends the engine's vocabulary in a structural dimension, not just species count. Directional characters now include ascending, descending, flat, descrescendo, and accelerating
7. Folk bookends (57, 60) framing an Indie Rock pair (58-59) create four-degree structural symmetry that reveals the relationship between folk and rock through the same songwriter's work
8. The Capitol Hill three-peat (58-59-60) documents genuine geographic clustering -- three consecutive degrees rooted in the same neighborhood through the same social graph is not coincidence but scene density
9. The Grebe's controlled-buoyancy sink as an alarm response (becoming less visible rather than more active) is a genuinely novel behavioral category for the engine -- the opposite of flight
10. Self-released after three label degrees creates a clean structural boundary; the post-Barsuk era begins at the exact degree where the frontman of Barsuk's flagship band goes solo

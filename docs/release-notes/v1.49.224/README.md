## Part B: Bushy-tailed Woodrat — The Midden Builder

- **The pack rat.** The Bushy-tailed Woodrat (*Neotoma cinerea*) is famous for collecting objects — twigs, bones, plastic, shiny metal, anything portable — and incorporating them into its nest. The "pack rat" common name predates the rodent's scientific description and entered American English as a metaphor for compulsive accumulators.
- **Midden builder — thousands of years old.** Woodrat middens are elaborate stick nests built in rock crevices, caves, and abandoned mineshafts. Some middens persist for tens of thousands of years. The structure protects against predators and weather; the chemistry preserves contents.
- **Amberat and paleoecology.** Crystallized woodrat urine (amberat) cements midden contents in place and preserves plant fragments, pollen, seeds, and insect parts at the time of deposition. **Betancourt et al. (1990)** documented midden-based reconstructions of the last forty thousand years of biotic change across western North America. The midden is a paleontological archive.

Without the energy framework as running ledger, the consistency would have been invisible. The framework earned its keep at degree 84.
- **The midden-as-time-capsule observation.** Treating the **Bushy-tailed Woodrat** through its archaeological-paleontological footprint (rather than only its acoustic profile) created a new category — species that preserve ecological history through their behavior. **Beaver** modifies the present; **Woodrat** preserves the past. The same family of question (what does the rodent do to its environment?) produces fundamentally different temporal answers.
- **Cricetidae pair after the Sciuridae octet.** The transition from the eight-degree Sciuridae streak (75–82) to the two-degree Cricetidae pair (83–84) immediately produced productive within-family contrast — Deer Mouse (invisible-ultrasonic) vs Woodrat (architectural-audible). The new family run did not repeat the previous; it inverted it.
- **Label-shift as artistic-statement reading.** Treating Jurado's move from Sub Pop (Seattle) to Secretly Canadian (Indiana) as an artistic statement (rather than a contractual happenstance) opened a new analytical lane. Label choice now joins genre, energy, and city as a tracked dimension across the engine.

### What Could Be Better

This is not a celebration to extend; it is a structural deficiency to repair. Degree 85 must turn to Aves and the engine must hold there until the imbalance is corrected.
- **Energy levels capture volume but not depth.** The same E=2 at degree 45 and degree 84 hides twenty-five years of artistic deepening. The classification is necessary (without it, the consistency would be invisible) but insufficient (it does not register the difference between reaching and arriving). The engine needs a depth dimension that complements energy.
- **Backward-connectivity tracking is informal.** Degree 84 is one of the densest backward-connection nodes in the engine, but the connections are scattered across cross-reference tables rather than aggregated into a single density measure.

8. **The first success costs what the first four failures cost plus one more attempt.**
   Pioneer 4's success is meaningful only against the background of four preceding failures. The engineering lessons that enabled escape velocity on the fifth attempt were paid for by Pioneer 0 (pad explosion), Pioneer 1 (partial success, fell back from 70% of lunar distance), Pioneer 2 (silent third stage), and Pioneer 3 (burn-short productive failure). A success mission is not a fresh attempt; it is the accumulated correction across four prior iterations finally crossing a threshold, and release-notes prose that treats the success as isolated misses the structural point.

9. **Nativity is a distinct career condition, not a late stage of residency.**
   Berry being born in the Central District rather than moving there is not a minor biographical detail; it changes the Persistence pattern from "stayed after arrival" to "never left because never arrived from elsewhere." The engine needed a native-born artist to instantiate the baseline against which migration-based patterns are measured, and the taxonomy is more coherent with Persistence as its own named pattern than with Residency covering both conditions.

The Signal-in-Noise Physics principle is load-bearing across the engine: adaptation of signal structure beats amplification of signal amplitude at every timescale the engine measures.

13. **The Amiga Principle terminates at the body.**
   Organizational, technological, compositional, vocal, and composite iterations of the Amiga Principle all involve some external apparatus (organization, instrument, notation, voice-as-band); the Physical/Complete iteration at degree 5 terminates the progression at the unaugmented body — one musician, one piano, four voices. The principle has reached its embodied limit by the end of the pentad, and subsequent degrees will need to extend the principle in new directions rather than continue its internalization.

14. **Cocktail jazz is an ecosystem diagnostic, not a minor genre.**
   The persistence of a cocktail-jazz pianist across six decades is only possible if the local restaurant-and-hotel ecosystem can sustain the economic niche of the working solo piano performer. Berry's sixty-year continuous residency at venues like the Sorrento Hotel and El Gaucho is a measurement of Seattle's upper-end hospitality economy across six decades, and the musical output is the diagnostic signal. The degree-5 release elevates cocktail jazz from "background music" to "ecosystem indicator."

15. **Chemical weaponization of host-organism defense is an overlooked behavioral category.**
   The nuthatch smearing Douglas-fir resin around its cavity entrance is a behavior most field-guide summaries treat as a curiosity rather than as a structural pattern. It is in fact the first example in the engine of a species co-opting its host organism's defensive chemistry as its own nest-site defense, and the pattern will recur in subsequent degrees (hummingbird-lichen nest construction, chickadee cavity-fumigation with aromatic herbs). Future SPS entries should include a chemical-weaponization check alongside foraging and nesting checks.

17. **The canopy is both a destination and a foundation.**

The engine's botanical profile reads as a progression from subterranean to canopy, but the canopy tree is simultaneously the structural substrate from which the progression is organized. Both readings are correct, and the doubled-directionality of the canopy — endpoint of the progression, origin of the structure — is the pentad's structural signature.

The release notes reconstructed the early arc from interview recollections, later obituary-style profiles, and inference from venue histories, but a more thoroughly sourced early-career biography would strengthen future Pass 3 refinements.

19. **The solo-piano literature is small relative to the ensemble literature.**
   Academic and critical writing on solo jazz piano in the cocktail tradition is substantially thinner than the writing on post-bop ensembles or on avant-garde solo piano (Taylor, Bley, Pullen). The Pass 2 refinement had to construct much of its analytical framework from solo classical-piano literature and adapt it, which is defensible but introduces a framing risk: cocktail jazz is not a miniature version of classical piano performance and the differences (audience expectation, functional role, rhythmic grounding) require their own analytical vocabulary.

20. **The nuthatch's non-vocal communication is underexplored.**
   Beyond the yank-yank call, the nuthatch uses body posture, crest feather position, and wing-flicking as communication modalities, and the degree-5 release concentrates on the vocal signal at the expense of the visual modalities. Future refinements should integrate the full communication ensemble rather than treating the voice as the sole acoustic contribution.

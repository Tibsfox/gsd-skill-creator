## Lessons Learned

1. **Founding-act documentation at rotational-boundary positions is structurally productive when the act is genuinely a founding act rather than a packaged-as-founding peak-career document.** The Fartz placement at the three-quarters mark is validated by the actual founding status of the band within the 1980-1983 Seattle hardcore scene rather than by a generic founding-act-pattern decision. The lesson is that founding-act placement at boundary positions should be reserved for acts whose actual historical founding status warrants the structural weight rather than being applied to peak-career documents relabeled as founding. Future boundary-position placement should follow the same warrant-by-actual-founding-status principle.
2. **Concurrent multi-degree arcs can open at the same boundary degree without structural confusion when the arcs are documented at distinct cross-axis registers.** The simultaneous opening of the Seattle HC Arc (S36) and the Orca Arc (SPS) at degree 270 is made legible by the track-axis distinction — the five-degree S36 arc and the seven-degree SPS arc are running on different documentation tracks and therefore do not compete for the same structural attention. The lesson is that concurrent-arc opening is feasible when track-axis distinction is preserved and that the engine's dual-track structure is structurally robust enough to handle simultaneous multi-degree openings without reader confusion.
3. **The engine's amplitude vocabulary is strongest when the E=8 ceiling is available on both tracks rather than being S36-specific.** The Orca S01 Pod Call E=8 placement is the first SPS entry at the engine-maximum amplitude, and the placement validates that the amplitude ceiling is a cross-track register rather than an S36-specific register. The lesson is that amplitude vocabulary should be universal across both tracks rather than track-sorted, and the paired-E=8 degree establishes the precedent that subsequent SPS entries at genuinely maximum-amplitude biological acoustic events can claim the ceiling register without track-specific discount.
4. **Critical endangerment is a first-class contextual element of acoustic documentation rather than a secondary background detail.** The Southern Resident Killer Whales' critically endangered status is treated at first-class structural resolution in the degree 270 documentation rather than as a footnote. The lesson is that conservation-status context shapes the interpretive function of the acoustic documentation — the pod call is documented in part as an archival preservation act under uncertainty about the population's future — and the documentation should preserve this context at full resolution rather than reducing it to parenthetical mention.
5. **Matrilineal social structure is a first-class element of biological acoustic documentation when the vocalization is transmitted through the social structure.** The Southern Resident pod call is transmitted matrilineally from mothers to daughters to granddaughters, and the matrilineal structure is itself the substrate on which the acoustic culture exists. The lesson is that social-structure documentation should accompany acoustic documentation whenever the vocalization is a product of the social structure, and that the engine's biological documentation should include the social-structural context as a first-class element rather than as taxonomic background.
6. **Pre-commercial-infrastructure artistic expression is a distinct documentation register that should not be conflated with commercial-period output.** The Fartz's self-released records on Fartz Records before any Seattle commercial music infrastructure existed to support hardcore punk are treated at first-class structural resolution in the degree 270 documentation rather than being reduced to "independent" or "underground" label. The lesson is that the pre-commercial-infrastructure register is a distinct documentation context with specific characteristics (self-released, minimal-budget recording, direct-community distribution, no external mediation) that warrant first-class documentation rather than category-sort into the generic "indie" label.
7. **Multi-generational trajectory documentation across sequential bands or pods requires explicit cross-reference to preceding and succeeding documentation rather than implicit connection.** The Loud Tom Niemeyer trajectory from The Fartz through The Accused to Gruntruck and the J2 Granny-lineage matrilineal transmission across J pod generations both represent multi-generational trajectories that span multiple engine entries or multiple documented generations. The lesson is that multi-generational trajectory documentation should be explicitly cross-referenced at each degree-level entry within the trajectory so that the reader can navigate the full trajectory from any single entry rather than having to reconstruct the trajectory independently.
8. **Rotational-boundary placement of founding statements clarifies the engine's structural narrative for the reader.** The degree 270 placement of both The Fartz and the Orca S01 Pod Call as simultaneous founding statements at the three-quarters mark is a deliberate structural-boundary placement that makes the Quadrant III-to-Quadrant IV transition legible as a documented boundary event rather than a silent structural shift. The lesson is that quadrant-boundary, arc-founding, and major-transition degree placements should be explicitly marked as boundary events in the documentation so that the rotational-position narrative is legible from the boundary-degree documentation itself.
9. **Arc preview documentation should enumerate the arc structure in sufficient detail to orient the reader to the coming sub-sequence.** The degree 270 Seattle HC Arc preview names the five bands of the arc, and the Orca Arc preview names the seven behaviors of the arc. The lesson is that arc preview documentation should go further — enumerating projected degree placements, projected amplitude registers, and projected cross-axis themes — so that the arc structure is fully legible within the opening-degree documentation and the reader can track the arc progression from the opening entry forward.
10. **Paired-E=8 degrees are structurally singular events that deserve explicit cross-axis documentation.** The degree 270 paired-E=8 placement (The Fartz E=8 plus Orca S01 Pod Call E=8) is the first such event in the engine's catalog and may be a rare event across the remaining ninety degrees of the rotation. The lesson is that paired-ceiling-amplitude events should be treated as structurally singular moments that warrant explicit cross-axis documentation rather than being treated as routine amplitude placements. Future paired-E=8 degrees should follow the same explicit-structural-singularity documentation practice.

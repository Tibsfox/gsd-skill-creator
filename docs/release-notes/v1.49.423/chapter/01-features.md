## FIRST HARDLY ART


- Hardly Art was founded in 2007 as a Sub Pop subsidiary dedicated to independent and experimental music; its early catalog included Tacocat, Chastity Belt, Jay Reatard, and other artists operating in the Pacific Northwest and broader independent music sphere; the label's position as a Sub Pop imprint gives it the distribution infrastructure of one of the most successful independent labels in the world while maintaining the aesthetic independence of a smaller operation; degree 282 is the engine's first Hardly Art entry, and Tacocat is the correct artist for that distinction -- they are the label's most emblematic act in terms of the combination of feminist politics, humor, and punk pop craft

## Feminist humor as competency


- Tacocat's humor is not decoration on top of the politics; it is the delivery mechanism for the politics; a song that makes accurate observations about catcalling and is also funny is harder to dismiss than a song that makes the same observations without the humor; the comedy is doing work; Emily Nokes' lyrical approach -- precise, deadpan, committed to the bit -- is a specific skill, and the engine acknowledges it as such

## Pacific white-sided dolphin and group acrobatics


- schools of Pacific white-sided dolphins in Puget Sound can number in the hundreds; when a school is actively leaping and rotating, the acoustic signature of re-entry -- dozens of animals hitting the water simultaneously -- creates a percussive, multi-source sound event distributed across a wide area of water surface; the visual spectacle of hundreds of animals briefly airborne simultaneously is one of the more dramatic marine mammal events the Pacific Northwest produces; E=7 reflects both the individual acrobatic performance and the cumulative group event

## Delphinidae return


- Pacific white-sided dolphins are Delphinidae, the same family as the orcas documented in the Orca Arc; their return to the engine after the great whale interlude (278-280) and the Dall's Porpoise (281, Phocoenidae) marks the marine mammal arc's structural diversity: the engine is not cycling through one family but documenting the full cetacean range of the Pacific Northwest

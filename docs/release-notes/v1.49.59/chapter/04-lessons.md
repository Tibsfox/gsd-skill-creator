1. **The vanishing middle is a recurring pattern.**
   The 1990s music industry could accommodate mainstream success or cult obscurity but not the territory between. Geggy Tah occupied that middle — too weird for radio, too accessible for the underground. The same dynamic appears in DDA (dada), COI (Coil), and the Rosetta Stone framework itself: systems that bridge categories are harder to sustain than systems that stay in their lane.

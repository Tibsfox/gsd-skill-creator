1. **The ocean is the largest integrated infrastructure platform on Earth.**
   Cooling, energy, connectivity, and transport all run on the same substrate. Land-based infrastructure separates these into different systems because land forces separation. The ocean doesn't. The integration is free — the engineering is the hard part.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #566_

2. **Indigenous maritime sovereignty predates UNCLOS by millennia.**
   Coast Salish marine territories, Pacific Islander navigation rights, and traditional management practices existed long before the 1982 Convention. Any maritime infrastructure that ignores this history will fail at the governance layer, not the engineering layer.

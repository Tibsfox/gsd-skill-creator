1. **Every energy technology is the same physics.**
   Heat pumps exploit temperature gradients. Fuel cells exploit electrochemical potential gradients. Geothermal taps Earth's thermal gradient. Solar electrolysis uses photon energy gradients. The differences are engineering; the principle is universal.

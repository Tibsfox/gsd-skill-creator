## Lessons Learned
- **5/6 Central District concentration is historically valid but the engine must rotate.** The CD was the center of Seattle jazz. The CSV reflects this. But the carry-forward system correctly flags that genre and geographic diversity must increase or the engine becomes a Central District monograph.
- **The interspecies network is the species engine's most generative structure.** Two nodes and one directed edge (chickadee → nuthatch) is the beginning of a graph that will connect dozens of degrees by degree 100.
- **Self-taught artists challenge the theory-mapping framework.** Berry's blue-note vocabulary wasn't learned from a textbook. The engine must accommodate tacit knowledge alongside formal theory.

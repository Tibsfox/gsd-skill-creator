## Lessons Learned

1. **Band documentation that is primarily a vehicle for documenting adjacent infrastructure should name that intent explicitly in the engine's register.** The Skin Yard documentation at degree 264 explicitly acknowledges that the band is, in part, a vehicle for documenting Jack Endino's parallel production career — Skin Yard's own music stands on its own, but the engine's choice to foreground the band at degree 264 is partly about Endino as much as about Skin Yard itself. Future scene-figure-adjacent band documentation should name this intent directly rather than disguising it as pure band coverage, because the honest structural acknowledgment makes the engine's documentation priorities legible to the reader. The documentation of scene-adjacent bands is more interpretively productive when the adjacency is made explicit than when it is hidden behind band-only coverage.
2. **Producer-credit infrastructure deserves first-class register treatment rather than footnote treatment.** The JACK ENDINO PRODUCER OF RECORD register at degree 264 foregrounds Endino's production work as a primary subject rather than as a secondary detail of the Sub Pop bands' entries. The first-class treatment is interpretively productive because producer-credit infrastructure is a genuine first-class element of the Seattle scene's sonic identity — the recordings sound the way they sound because of where and how they were recorded, and the engine's documentation vocabulary is stronger when that recording-context register operates at the same structural level as the band-career register. Future scene-documentation at other historically-important recording-studio locations should follow the same first-class treatment.
3. **Personnel documentation at career-origin points preserves continuity that career-peak-only documentation collapses.** The MATT CAMERON FIRST APPEARANCE register at degree 264 documents Cameron at his 1985 Skin Yard starting point rather than waiting until his Soundgarden or Pearl Jam career peaks. The first-appearance register is interpretively productive because it preserves the career-arc continuity in the engine's personnel record and allows subsequent degrees to track Cameron across his full career rather than introducing him late. Future documentation of scene-figure personnel who pass through multiple bands should favor first-appearance documentation over career-peak documentation, because the origin-point register provides the structural anchor that the peak-point register cannot.
4. **Geophysics arc documentation benefits from aligning physical-geography trajectory with amplitude-register trajectory.** The geophysics arc 261-267 aligns the physical-geography sweep (alpine → subsurface → seafloor → back up) with the amplitude-register shape (E=5, E=7, E=5, E=2, then climbing back) so that the arc's valley-floor point corresponds to the arc's lowest amplitude.

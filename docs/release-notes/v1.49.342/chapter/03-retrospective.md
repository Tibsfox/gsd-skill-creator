# Retrospective — v1.49.342

## What Worked

- **The bear trio escalation-plus-inversion arc structure produces a different interpretive depth than the Roosevelt Elk trio's social-dynamic-amplitude arc.** Where the Roosevelt Elk trio (197-199) documented the species' complete social-communication repertoire via a 7-5-7 amplitude arc that maps cows-at-center-bulls-at-extremes social dynamics, the bear trio (200-202) documents the species' defensive-communication-plus-vulnerability via an escalation-plus-inversion structure that moves from huff (E=5) to jaw-pop (E=6) to the upcoming cub distress (E=4). The two trios together demonstrate that multi-degree arcs are not a single structural pattern but a family of structural patterns whose internal organization depends on the subject's underlying repertoire structure. Future multi-degree SPS arcs should consider which structural pattern the subject's repertoire actually supports rather than defaulting to any single pattern.
- **The Hardly Art streak opening establishes the first label-cluster longer than two and demonstrates ecosystem-level observation.** Prior engine degrees have documented individual-label entries without sustained multi-degree streaks. The four-degree streak across 201-204 (Chastity Belt, La Luz, Tacocat, Childbirth) documents the Capitol Hill feminist-music community as an ecosystem-level observation rather than a sequence of individual-band observations, establishing Hardly Art as a coherent curatorial institution. This streak-as-structural-event pattern opens future engine iterations to consider whether other labels (Sub Pop, Up Records, K Records, Kill Rock Stars, Barsuk) warrant similar extended multi-degree documentation where the roster's community-structure supports it.
- **The contrast-sync pairing structure at E=4+E=6=10 establishes a distinct Q3 pairing-type from prior parallel-sync pairings.** Prior Q3 pairings have operated through parallel-sync (both halves at similar amplitudes expressing structurally-parallel principles). Degree 201's E=4 deadpan deflection paired with E=6 percussive demand establishes a contrast-sync pairing where the amplitudes differ significantly but both halves express the same underlying performance-under-witness structural principle at contrast registers. The contrast-sync-pairing vocabulary entry enables future Q3 pairings to be analyzed as either parallel-sync or contrast-sync rather than treating all pairings as a single structural type.
- **The percussive-mechanical-vocalization register expands the engine's acoustic-mechanism vocabulary beyond vocal-fold-produced sounds.** Prior SPS entries have relied on vocal-fold vibration (bugles, cow calls, rut roars, huffs, deep pulses, maa bleats, grunts, snorts) as the acoustic-production mechanism. The jaw-pop's tooth-clack broadband transient introduces the mechanical-percussive register as a first-class acoustic-production category, opening future SPS documentation to consider non-vocal-fold-produced sounds (tooth-clicking in dolphins, echolocation clicks in bats and cetaceans, drumming displays in ruffed grouse and woodpeckers, antler-rubbing percussion in ungulates) as first-class acoustic-production categories.
- **The Capitol Hill feminist-music community documentation through the Hardly Art streak converts band-level observation into community-level observation.** Individual-band documentation of Chastity Belt alone would capture the deadpan-as-method register and the Walla Walla-to-Capitol-Hill relocation event, but would not capture the community-ecosystem observation that the Hardly Art streak's four-degree span delivers. The streak structure converts band-level observation into community-level observation, so that the cumulative reading across 201-204 produces emergent structural observations (member-overlap patterns, shared-venue dynamics, riot-grrrl-lineage-into-2010s-irony tonal shift) that individual-band documentation could not support. This community-level-observation structure may generalize to other music-community documentation events.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Multi-degree arcs support multiple structural patterns, not a single pattern.** The Roosevelt Elk trio's (197-199) social-dynamic-amplitude-arc at 7-5-7 and the Black Bear trio's (200-202) escalation-plus-inversion arc demonstrate that multi-degree SPS arcs are a family of structural patterns whose internal organization depends on the subject's underlying repertoire structure rather than defaulting to any single pattern. Future multi-degree arc documentation should analyze which structural pattern the subject's repertoire actually supports and allow the arc's interpretive framing to follow from that analysis rather than applying a single default pattern.

2. **Label streaks longer than two constitute ecosystem-level observations rather than scaled single-band observations.** The Hardly Art streak across 201-204 is not four single-band observations aggregated; it is a single community-level observation that requires the four-degree span to manifest. The streak-as-ecosystem-observation pattern opens future engine iterations to consider whether other labels (Sub Pop, Up Records, K Records, Kill Rock Stars, Barsuk) warrant similar extended multi-degree documentation where the roster's community-structure supports ecosystem-level observation rather than individual-band observation.

3. **Contrast-sync pairings are a distinct structural type from parallel-sync pairings.** Prior Q3 pairings have operated primarily through parallel-sync (both halves at similar amplitudes expressing structurally-parallel principles). Degree 201's E=4-plus-E=6 contrast-sync pairing introduces a distinct pairing-type where amplitudes differ significantly but both halves express the same underlying structural principle at contrast registers. Future pairing analysis should classify each pairing as parallel-sync or contrast-sync rather than treating all pairings as a single structural type.

4. **Percussive-mechanical vocalizations expand the engine's acoustic-mechanism vocabulary beyond vocal-fold production.** The Black Bear jaw-pop's tooth-clack broadband transient introduces the mechanical-percussive register as a first-class acoustic-production category. Future SPS documentation for species that produce non-vocal-fold sounds (dolphin tooth-clicks, cetacean echolocation clicks, ruffed grouse drumming, woodpecker drumming, antler-rubbing percussion) can reference the percussive-mechanical register as a first-class functional category.

5. **Encounter-context calibration determines register-selection rather than trait-intrinsic expression.** Chastity Belt's deadpan-delivery calibration to 2010s Capitol Hill house-show audiences and the Black Bear jaw-pop's startle-design calibration to trail-encounter proximity both demonstrate that performance register is context-calibrated rather than intrinsic-trait-expressed. Future engine documentation should consider whether observed register-selections reflect trait-intrinsic expression or encounter-context calibration, with the default assumption being context-calibration pending evidence otherwise.

6. **Geographic-register expansion through most-distant-origin events is a structural event that deserves explicit documentation.** Walla Walla's 260-mile-east origin definitively breaks the Seattle-proper-and-near-Seattle pattern in a way that Issaquah's 17-mile-east Eastside opening only began. Future degrees should treat most-distant-origin events as structural events deserving explicit documentation rather than as passing details, and the engine's geographic vocabulary should accumulate distance-from-Seattle registers as first-class documentation anchors.

7. **Community-level documentation emerges from multi-degree streaks rather than from individual-entry documentation.** The Capitol Hill feminist-music community is comprehensible only through the four-degree Hardly Art streak, not through any single Hardly Art artist's individual entry. This community-level-observation structure may generalize to other music-community documentation events where multiple bands sharing members, shows, venues, and audiences constitute an ecosystem whose aggregate behavior produces emergent community-level properties. Future engine iterations should identify such community-structures and consider extended-streak documentation when the community-structure supports it.

8. **Defensive-signaling is functionally distinct from predatory-signaling and the distinction is operationally critical.** The Black Bear jaw-pop's defensive-signaling function (bear stressed, wants distance, communicates "back off") is fundamentally distinct from predatory-signaling (typically silent in Black Bear), and the distinction is operationally critical for encounter-resolution. Future species documentation should consider whether documented vocalizations serve defensive, predatory, social, or other functional registers, and should distinguish these registers explicitly rather than conflating them. The functional distinction shapes both the interpretive framing of the vocalization and the practical human-response protocols that bear-safety documentation relies on.

9. **College-formation-plus-relocation is a distinct formation-pathway register from club-and-venue-based formation.** Chastity Belt's formation at Whitman College followed by relocation to Capitol Hill introduces a formation-pathway that is structurally distinct from prior engine formation-pathways (youth-scene-clubs, live-music-venues, recording-studio-collaborations, neighborhood-proximity). The college-formation-pathway deserves dedicated cross-artifact documentation that could extend to other liberal-arts-college-formed bands across Pacific Northwest music history.

10. **The deadpan-as-method performance-posture register enters the engine's vocabulary as a distinct posture from grunge-intensity, twee-openness, and indie-angular-restlessness.** Chastity Belt's refusal-to-perform-intensity as the performance itself introduces a performance-posture register that the engine has not previously carried at this explicit register-naming level. Future engine documentation should consider whether observed performance-postures fit existing register-categories or require new register-naming, and should accumulate performance-posture registers across the engine's full amplitude spectrum as first-class documentation categories.

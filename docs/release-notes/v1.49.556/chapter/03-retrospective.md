# Retrospective — v1.49.556

## Lessons Learned

1. **First complete photographic atlas of the lunar nearside.** Lunar Orbiter 4 mapped 99% of the visible hemisphere in approximately two weeks of near-polar orbit photography. 546 frames. The complete picture. The Lunar Orbiter program is now at 4 of 5 missions with a 100% success rate -- the only program in the Apollo era with a perfect record.

2. **The folk-adjacent run extends to eight consecutive degrees.** Degrees 45-52: Jurado (Folk), Votolato (Folk), Bazan (Indie Folk), Cahoone (Folk), Dawson (Folk Punk), Mirah (Indie Folk), Grand Archives (Indie Folk), Carlile (Folk/Rock). The longest genre-family streak in engine history, extending the record from seven at degree 51.

3. **First E=4 in the folk zone.** Brandi Carlile's testimony roar breaks through the E=1-to-E=3 oscillation that characterized the first seven degrees of the run. The folk zone's energy ceiling rises from E=3 to E=4. The energy pattern across eight degrees: 2-3-3-2-3-2-3-4, a wave that ascends.

4. **First rural artist in the S36 sequence.** Ravensdale to Auburn -- the rural King County corridor 28-35 miles from downtown Seattle. The engine enters rural geography for the first time after 51 degrees of urban and near-urban artists. Muckleshoot territorial acknowledgment.

5. **First Columbia Records artist.** The first major-label artist in the engine, breaking the indie-label dominance (Sub Pop, Barsuk, K Records, Secretly Canadian, Jade Tree, Suicide Squeeze, Jagjaguwar). Columbia's distribution extends the reach from indie channels to the full commercial network.

6. **First openly LGBTQ+ artist documented in the engine.** Carlile came out publicly in 2002 and has been one of the most visible LGBTQ+ artists in country and Americana music. The engine names what has been present but unspoken in the PNW music ecosystem.

7. **Engine enters water for the first time.** The American Dipper is the first species in 52 degrees to forage underwater. Fifty-one degrees of terrestrial and aerial species, and now a bird that walks along stream bottoms. The engine's ecological range expands from bark surfaces to aquatic substrates.

8. **First Cinclidae -- the 21st family, monotypic.** Second consecutive new monotypic family after Certhiidae at degree 51. Two new families in two degrees, both singletons. The engine's taxonomic expansion at degrees 51-52 is a paired entry: bark surface and stream bottom.

9. **Seven aquatic adaptations documented.** Dense plumage, waterproofing oil, nasal flaps, nictitating membrane, elevated hemoglobin, muscular legs, strong claws. The most comprehensively adapted species in the engine -- every system modified for the aquatic domain.

10. **Boeing connection spans mission and geography.** Lunar Orbiter 4 was built by Boeing at Langley Research Center. Boeing's commercial aircraft division operates from the Puget Sound corridor -- Everett, Renton, Kent -- the same geography where Carlile performs and the dipper sings.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Second E=6 -- the twin peak.** Hendrix (degree 36) and Wilson (degree 43). The only E=6 pair in the engine. Both from Seattle, both Rock, both transformed their primary instrument. Seven degrees apart. The energy distribution gains symmetry: E=1 and E=6 both at 2, bookending the spectrum.

2. **First Rock-primary genre stage.** Genre Stage 24. Blues Rock appeared at degrees 36-37 (Hendrix, Ayron Jones), but blues was primary. At degree 43, Rock stands as primary for the first time. The soul qualifier acknowledges Wilson's vocal delivery -- Ray Charles alongside opera, Peggy Lee alongside bossa nova in the Wilson household's Sunday pancake sessions.

3. **Pioneer program returns.** The early Pioneer series (degrees 1-5) completed long ago. Pioneer 6 at degree 43 is the mature Pioneer -- not a lunar probe but a heliophysics monitoring station, the first of a distributed sensor network. The program evolves from Moon attempts to Sun monitoring.

4. **The Parulidae streak ends.** Five consecutive warblers (degrees 38-42) -- the longest same-family run in the engine, matching the swallow quintet. Degree 43 breaks it with Fringillidae. The engine exhales.

5. **Washington State Bird arrives.** At degree 43 of a 360-degree survey of Washington's soundscape, the state's own bird enters. The first official state symbol in the engine.

6. **The *Spinus* reunion after 31 degrees.** Pine Siskin (degree 12) and American Goldfinch (degree 43). Same genus, opposite in almost every dimension: streaked vs. brilliant, irruptive vs. resident, E=1 vs. E=4, electronic zap vs. canary warble, nomad vs. sentinel.

7. **The longevity theme unifies all three threads.** Pioneer 6 (35 years), Ann Wilson (55 years), American Goldfinch (year-round resident, permanent sentinel). Three systems that succeed through duration -- the machine that never changes, the voice that adapts to aging, the bird that stays through every season.

8. **The dedication goes to a team, not an individual.** NASA Ames, TRW, MIT, Stanford, University of Chicago, Southwest Center for Advanced Studies. Pioneer 6's longevity was a collective achievement -- design simplicity as institutional discipline.

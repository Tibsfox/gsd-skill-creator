The matriarchs (Granny / J2, Ocean Sun / L25), the recent calves (Notch / J38, Phoenix / L86), and the family groups across J pod, K pod, and L pod are documented at the Center for Whale Research in ways the degree-340 entry could have incorporated more explicitly for individual-level specificity.

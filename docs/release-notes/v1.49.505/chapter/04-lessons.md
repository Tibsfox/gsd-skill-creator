6. **The indicator species is the signal whose function is to report the system.**
   Previous degrees produced signals about the signaler (territorial hoots, presence markers) or about specific external objects (the chickadee's D-note encoding of a specific predator's threat level). Degree 4 introduces the class of signals whose function is not to report the signaler or a specific object but to report the *state of the system that produced the signaler*. The Spotted Owl's hoot is an indicator not because it names the owl or names a predator but because its presence is diagnostic of the forest's structural integrity. The indicator-signal class is a named engine category from D4 forward.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #4588_

7. **Productive failure is a distinct NASA taxonomy mode.**
   Pioneer 3 did not reach the Moon. In the lunar-mission framing, the flight was a failure. But Pioneer 3's Geiger-Müller experiment — during the slow ballistic fall-back that the staging shortfall produced — acquired more time in the outer radiation environment than any faster-moving successful Pioneer flight could have. The second Van Allen belt was discovered because the mission failed. Productive Failure (NASA failure mode 4) names this structural pattern: failure modes in which the system returns a scientific or operational yield the nominal success would have precluded.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #4589_

8. **The slowest trajectory sometimes carries the richest data.**
   Jones achieved escape velocity and left. Anderson orbited at a distance, returned. Standifer never left Jackson Street. By the metric of national fame, Standifer's career was Pioneer 3's trajectory: reached a useful altitude, fell back, measured something deep that the escape-velocity artists could not measure. The engine commits the principle: ballistic-trajectory artists accumulate local-scene measurement depth that Apollo-class artists cannot reach, and the depth is itself a form of data.

10. **The Teacher and the Resident are distinct artist-city patterns that often co-occur.**

11. **Callback-protocol acoustic signals can drive federal policy.**
   The Spotted Owl's four-note hoot, through Forsman's 1983 callback survey protocol and MacKenzie's 2002 Bayesian occupancy modeling, became the acoustic basis for the 1990 ESA listing and the 1994 Northwest Forest Plan. A single species's territorial call, transmitted through a speaker at dusk, drove the management of twenty-four million acres. The engine records this as the first-documented case in SPS of an acoustic signal functioning as direct regulatory input, and future species whose signals have similar policy consequence will be classed against this precedent.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #4593_

12. **Acoustic displacement has no clean solution when the invader's signal is superior.**
   The Barred Owl's 8-note phrase saturates the acoustic space the Spotted Owl's 4-note phrase occupied, and the invader's signal is more complex, more persistent, more versatile than the native's. The USFWS lethal-removal authorization targeting 450,000 Barred Owls is the operational consequence of a diagnosis the engine records explicitly: when the invader's signal is informationally superior to the native's, behavioral displacement cannot be reversed by signal competition alone. Policy must intervene, and the policy intervention is inevitably controversial.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #4594_

13. **Residency is an ephemeral output mode, and its legacy is carried in listener memory rather than in archive.**
   Standifer's Jazz Alley residency ran weekly for three decades and produced almost no commercial recording. The output of the residency is therefore not preservable by the ordinary archival mechanisms (tape, master, pressing, digital file). The output is preserved only as neural tissue in the listeners who were in the room. Residency-mode outputs are the most fragile class of artistic production, and they require a different documentary strategy — oral history, venue history, witness interview — than recording-mode or infrastructure-mode outputs.

15. **Institutional framework determines what is detected.**
   The Army's ABMA/JPL partnership detected the second Van Allen belt using Geiger-Müller tubes the Air Force's Pioneer program would not have flown. The USFWS callback protocol detects the Spotted Owl through an acoustic survey method the Forest Service would not have institutionalized without Forsman's Oregon State thesis. The engine generalizes the observation: detection is a function of institutional framework as much as of the signal itself, and the history of science is in part a history of which institutions are organized to listen for which signals. Degree 4 commits the principle as a cross-cutting lesson applicable to all subsequent NASA mission and SPS species entries.

16. **Commercial-recording scarcity limited auditory reference for Standifer.**
   Unlike Jones, Frisell, Horvitz, or Anderson — each with a substantial discography available for citation — Standifer's output was largely live-residency rather than recorded, and the surviving commercial recordings are sparse.

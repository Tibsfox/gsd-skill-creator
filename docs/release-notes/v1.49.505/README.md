## Part A: Floyd Standifer -- THE MULTI-INSTRUMENTALIST AS DUAL ORGANISM

### Pass 2 Refinement Summary (4,291 words)

Four new research sections deepening the analysis of Standifer as indicator species and composite musician:

**1. The Multi-Instrumentalist as Dual Organism — Trumpet, Saxophone, and Voice as Symbiotic Selves.** Most jazz musicians specialize. The embouchure required for trumpet — high lip tension, precise aperture control — actively conflicts with the embouchure for saxophone, which demands a looser jaw, a broader oral cavity. To maintain professional fluency on both instruments requires somatic bilingualism — the body maintaining two contradictory muscular programs in the same facial architecture. Standifer maintained both, plus the voice — a third physical modality that shares the breath apparatus but uses neither mouthpiece nor reed. Three instruments, three modes of sonic identity, one musician's breath.

*Usnea longissima* is not one organism but two: a mycobiont (fungal partner) and a photobiont (algal partner), fused into a composite body that neither could produce alone. The fungus provides structural architecture. The alga provides photosynthetic energy. Neither alone produces what we recognize as "old man's beard." Standifer's three instruments operated as a comparable composite — trumpet (public, projective), saxophone (conversational, flowing), voice (interpretive, direct). Remove any one and you have a competent musician. Keep all three and you have Standifer — the composite organism whose identity requires all its components.

**2. The UW Faculty Years and the Pedagogy of Place — Jackson Street Enters the University.** Standifer's UW appointment formalized the Jackson Street oral tradition within the institutional framework of a research university. The pedagogical pipeline mapped across degrees: Parker Cook at Garfield taught Jones and Anderson (the secondary-school foundation); the Jackson Street corridor provided informal apprenticeship (the experiential middle); Standifer at UW provided the post-secondary formalization (the theoretical capstone). Three stages of the pedagogical rocket. Standifer's dual residency — university and club — mirrors the dual-organism structure at the institutional level: the university provides structural framework (like the fungal mycobiont), the club scene provides living energy (like the algal photobiont).

**3. Jazz Alley Residency and the Last Jackson Street — Presence as Praxis.** Three output models across the S36 engine: Recording (Jones/Anderson — permanent, distributable), Infrastructure (Horvitz — institutional, enduring), Residency (Standifer — ephemeral, experienced only in real time). Standifer left almost no commercial recordings. His legacy is carried in the neural tissue of everyone who heard him play. The residency model is the most fragile and the most human output mode: art that requires presence — the musician's and the audience's — in the same room at the same time.

**4. Pioneer 3 Resonance — The Second Van Allen Belt and the Thing You Find When You Miss the Moon.** Standifer found the "second Van Allen belt" of Seattle jazz — the community-level musical ecosystem that national fame cannot detect. Jones achieved escape velocity and left. Anderson orbited at a distance, returned, but spent decades away. Standifer remained in the radiation belt for six decades, mapping its structure with a depth that the escaping artists could not achieve. The nationally famous artists achieved escape velocity. Standifer achieved measurement depth. ## Part B: Northern Spotted Owl -- THE FOUR-NOTE PROTOCOL

### Pass 2 Refinement Summary (3,844 words)

Four new research sections deepening the nocturnal acoustic ecology and conservation framework:

**1. Nocturnal Acoustic Ecology — The Post-Sunset Transformation.** The owl occupies the nighttime temporal niche — the 10-12 hour acoustic window after the diurnal species fall silent. Post-sunset ambient floor drops 20-25 dB SPL. Temperature inversions create acoustic waveguides that propagate the four-note hoot across kilometers of forest. The facial disk as biological parabolic antenna: feather geometry produces approximately 10 dB gain at the ear openings. Asymmetric ear placement enables 1-2 degree 3D sound localization in total darkness — hearing-based spatial resolution comparable to a visual predator's optical acuity.

**2. The Barred Owl Invasion — Acoustic Displacement.** *Strix varia*'s westward range expansion at approximately 100 km per decade. Acoustic frequency overlap: both species vocalize in the 300-700 Hz range. Vocal repertoire asymmetry: the Barred Owl's 8-note phrase saturating the acoustic space the Spotted Owl's 4-note phrase occupied. USFWS lethal removal authorization targeting 450,000 Barred Owls. An ecological displacement with no clean solution — the invader and the native produce overlapping signals, and the invader's signal is more complex, more persistent, and more versatile.

**3. Survey Protocol Acoustics — From Tree Branch to Federal Register.** The USFWS callback protocol (Forsman 1983): play the four-note hoot through a speaker, wait for response. 60-80% per-visit detection probability. Bayesian occupancy modeling (MacKenzie et al. 2002) accounts for imperfect detection. The four-note hoot's journey from a territorial vocalization to a data input for federal timber policy — the most consequential acoustic monitoring program in conservation history. A single call structure determining the management of millions of acres of Pacific Northwest forest.

**4. Pioneer 3 Resonance — Institutional Framework as Detection Filter.** The Army's ABMA/JPL partnership detected the second Van Allen belt using different instruments than the Air Force would have deployed. The USFWS survey protocol detects the Spotted Owl through a specific institutional framework that transforms an acoustic signal into federal timber policy. Both cases demonstrate that what you detect depends on how you are organized to listen. Usnea and owl as parallel indicator species: their presence confirms, their absence condemns. The degree-4 indicator principle fully expressed in both NASA organism and SPS species.

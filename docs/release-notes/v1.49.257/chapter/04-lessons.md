4. **Wing-hydrofoil biomechanics is inverted airplane-wing physics applied underwater.**
   The dipper's solution to underwater locomotion against current is elegant engineering: partially opened wings as hydrofoils generating downforce proportional to current velocity. The correct analogy is an inverted airplane wing (generating negative lift instead of lift) or an inverted race car spoiler (pressing down against the track / substrate). The physics is sound, the solution is evolutionary-adapted, and the implementation in a 60-gram passerine is remarkable. Document biomechanical solutions at engineering grade when the evidence supports it.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #6541_

5. **Three-layer acoustic signatures can emerge without deliberate composition.**
   The splash-song integration at degree 117 is not designed — it is the inevitable acoustic consequence of a singing bird that plunges into water as part of its foraging routine. The three layers (stream drone + song + splash) coexist because the bird's dual behavioral streams (singing + foraging) operate in the same time and space. The result is musically compelling (rhythm + melody + harmony in one acoustic space) but emerges from behavior, not from composition. Document emergent acoustic signatures with the same rigor as deliberately composed ones.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #6542_

6. **The sideplayer-to-frontperson trajectory inverts the usual band narrative.**

Jessica Dobson's Beck + Shins + Oberst + Yeah Yeah Yeahs history is the engine's cleanest example. The pattern is rare enough that the engine will track future instances explicitly.

7. **Indicator species status links a degree to watershed-level conservation concern.**
   The American Dipper's role as an indicator species for montane stream water quality links degree 117 to watershed-level conservation concern. Cold, clear, well-oxygenated water with rocky substrate and abundant aquatic invertebrate prey is what supports dippers, and pollution, siltation, dam impoundment, and watershed disturbance degrade dipper habitat. The indicator-species function connects the degree to the broader PNW cold-water-fisheries paradox and to climate-change-driven reductions in montane snowpack and summer streamflow.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.268`) · lesson #6544_

8. **Salmon-egg foraging ties the dipper into the PNW salmon cycle.**

The connection elevates the dipper from isolated aquatic specialist to participant in the region's defining ecological process.

12. **Quantitative hydrodynamic force measurements not available.**
   The wing-hydrofoil physics is described qualitatively (partially opened wings generate downforce against current) rather than with quantitative force measurements (newtons of downforce generated at specific current velocities for specific wing angles). Detailed hydrodynamic force measurements on diving dippers are scarce in the ornithological and biomechanics literature, and the qualitative description accurately represents the state of the science. A future entry could specifically survey the quantitative biomechanics literature.

## Part A: Don Lanphere -- THE PRODIGAL

### Pass 2 Refinement Summary (935 words)

Three new research sections:

**1. The Prodigal as Energy Balance.** Explorer 7's radiation budget = Lanphere's career: Phase I (intense input), Phase II (catastrophic output), Phase III (new equilibrium). Usnea's survival = Lanphere's playing in his fifties: the foundational vocabulary survived thirty years of non-use. The music's survival is the data point.

**2. Bebop as Root Node.** Lanphere at degree 12 is the genealogical parent of Caliman (hard bop, 9) and Shank (cool jazz, 10). The root documented after the branches, like Explorer 7 measuring the radiation budget after Explorers 1-4 mapped the belts.

**3. The Wenatchee Origin.** First eastern Washington artist. 150 miles across the Cascades from Seattle. The steepest ascent in the engine — Wenatchee to 52nd Street in five years. Talent appearing where infrastructure doesn't predict it.

### Retrospective: Degree 12 Lessons Learned

1. **The Prodigal pattern is new territory.** No previous artist's arc includes active destruction by addiction followed by redemption. The pattern requires both the fall and the return — neither alone is sufficient. The fall without return is The Erasure (Bown, degree 6). The return without fall is The Stay (Anderson, degree 3). The Prodigal requires both.

2. **The root can be documented after the branches.** The engine's degree sequence does not follow jazz-historical chronology. Lanphere's bebop root (1940s) appears at degree 12, after hard bop (degree 9, 1950s-60s) and cool jazz (degree 10, 1950s). The genealogical tree is assembled out of chronological order — which is fine, because the tree's structure is independent of the order in which its nodes are discovered.

3. **E=1 at degree 12 has a different quality than E=1 at degree 8.** Peacock's E=1 was Zen-influenced silence — spaces between notes as compositional choice. Lanphere's E=1 is post-redemption quiet — the fire reduced to a sustainable burn, not because the vocabulary diminished but because the body's energy budget changed. Same energy level, different cause.

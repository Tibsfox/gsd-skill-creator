1. **The Leap as degree theme.**
   Freedom 7 launches Shepard 187.5 km above the Atlantic on a parabolic arc -- up and down, 15 min 28 sec, five minutes of weightlessness, Mach 6.8. Fly Moon Royale launches the Central District's first neo-soul collective. Three entities leap: the test pilot from the pad, the collective from jazz tradition to neo-soul future, and the giant salamander revealed the moment the leap happens. Degree 19's theme is the leap that exposes what was always there.

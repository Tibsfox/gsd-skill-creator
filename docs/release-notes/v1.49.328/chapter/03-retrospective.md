2. **Mathematical cleanliness in energy progressions provides durable artist-longitudinal documentation.** The 5→6→7 escalation across degrees 146/173/187 is mathematically clean with one-step increments, and the cleanliness makes the pattern legible and citable. Future multi-entry artists should be documented with explicit energy-progression tracking so that non-monotonic, plateau, or reversal patterns can be contrasted against the clean-increment baseline established at degree 187.
3. **Absolute engine minima and maxima should be established with scientific anchoring to peer-reviewed literature.** The Harbor Porpoise's E=1 at degree 187 was documented with citations to Au et al. 1999, Villadsgaard et al. 2007, and Morisaka & Connor 2007, making the calibration defensible rather than dependent on editorial judgment alone.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Arc-closing documentation at the end of an extended single-taxon sub-sequence should place the subject's most structurally significant behavioral or sensory register at the arc-closing position.** The degree 276 documentation places the SRKW echolocation — the population's primary survival-sensory-apparatus — at the seven-degree Orca Arc's closing position rather than reserving the position for a more-elaborated-register entry. The lesson is that arc-closing entries should carry the subject's most structurally significant behavioral or sensory register, surfacing the closing note as the structural statement of the arc rather than treating arc-closing as a simple sequence-end marker.
2. **Genre-register openings in the engine should anchor to the movement's defining act rather than to a later or secondary act.** The degree 276 documentation opens the riot grrrl register through Bikini Kill — the movement's defining musical act — rather than through a later riot grrrl act or a secondary representative. The lesson is that genre-register-opening documentation should anchor to the defining act because the register's foundational significance is what the opening-entry documentation is responsible for preserving.
3. **Geographic-territory documentation can establish territory-level first-class register status through consecutive-degree placement of the territory's most influential subjects.** The two-degree Olympia sub-sequence (275 Beat Happening, 276 Bikini Kill) establishes Olympia as a first-class S36 track register distinct from Seattle proper through the consecutive-degree placement of the territory's two most influential musical acts. The lesson is that territory-level register status can be established through consecutive-degree placement at the territory's most significant subjects, and future geographic-territory documentation should consider consecutive-degree placement as a register-establishment technique.
4. **Population-under-structural-pressure documentation should preserve the pressure context as a first-class element of the record rather than as secondary background.** The degree 276 documentation explicitly frames the SRKW echolocation as the acoustic signature of a population under nutritional stress, searching for a prey species whose availability has been reduced by structural pressures. The lesson is that pressure context should be preserved as first-class documentation rather than as background detail — the pressure is part of what the behavior is, and the documentation should reflect that rather than treating the behavior as neutral description.
5. **Biosonar and other primary-sensory-apparatus registers warrant first-class SPS documentation because the sensory apparatus is structurally central to the species' survival.** The SRKW echolocation is the population's primary sensory system for finding food, and the degree 276 documentation treats the biosonar at first-class structural resolution rather than as a secondary behavioral detail. The lesson is that primary-sensory-apparatus registers — biosonar, vision, chemoreception, whichever sense the species uses for survival-critical tasks — warrant first-class SPS documentation because the sensory apparatus is not incidental to the species but structurally central to its survival.
6. **Non-commercial-infrastructure documentation should be preserved as a first-class element of both cultural and biological subjects because the infrastructure is structurally central to what the subject is.** The Kill Rock Stars and K Records Olympia label infrastructure and the Center for Whale Research and associated SRKW documentation infrastructure are both preserved at first-class resolution in the degree 276 entry. The lesson is that non-commercial infrastructure is not incidental to the subject but structurally central — the Olympia music culture is the community that Kill Rock Stars and K Records built, and the SRKW scientific record is the documentation that the Center for Whale Research and its partners produced — and the documentation should preserve the infrastructure as a first-class element rather than treating it as background.
7. **Cultural-movement documentation should surface the movement's extra-musical dimensions (zines, organizing, politics) as first-class elements rather than as secondary context.** The riot grrrl movement's zine culture, consciousness-raising practices, community organizing, and explicit feminist politics are preserved at first-class resolution in the degree 276 documentation rather than being treated as background to the Bikini Kill music entry.

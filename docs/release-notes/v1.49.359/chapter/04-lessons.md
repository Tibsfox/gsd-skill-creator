   The Blood Brothers becoming the first Post-Hardcore entry at E=8 demonstrates that the subgenre's amplitude ceiling is not intrinsic to Post-Hardcore as a classification but is instead accessible through arrangement-format innovation (dual-vocal architecture) and personnel commitment (five dedicated members over ten sustained active years). Future genre-ceiling evaluations should treat the amplitude ceiling as an empirical observation about specific entries rather than as a property of the subgenre as a whole.

## Summary

STS-107 Columbia launched **2003-01-16 15:39:00 UTC** from Kennedy Space Center LC-39A Florida; mission duration approximately **15 days 22 hours 20 minutes on-orbit**; mission-end at atmospheric entry interface 2003-02-01. Mission profile: dedicated microgravity research mission with no rendezvous or docking and no extravehicular activity scheduled. Crew (7) worked two 12-hour shifts continuously across the 16-day mission to operate 80+ experiments housed in the SPACEHAB Research Double Module + FREESTAR cross-bay carrier. **Columbia OV-102 28th and final flight** in the Columbia operational substrate-thread (substrate-cumulative across 28 missions from STS-1 maiden 1981-04-12 substrate-anchor).

**Primary payload (dual-substrate):** SPACEHAB Research Double Module — two pressurized SPACEHAB modules joined to form a continuous 25-foot research laboratory in the payload bay; approximately 4,500 kg payload capacity; approximately 62.2 cubic meters of pressurized volume — substrate-anchor SPACEHAB-RESEARCH-DOUBLE-MODULE-FIRST-INSTANCE obs#1 first-instance NEW LOCKED at v695. Plus FREESTAR (Fast Reaction Experiments Enabling Science Technology Applications and Research) cross-bay carrier — passive instrument platform mounted to payload bay sidewall — with 6 payloads: MEIDEX (Mediterranean Israeli Dust Experiment; payload specialist Ramon; Israeli Space Agency + Tel Aviv University) + SOLSE-2 (Shuttle Ozone Limb Sounding Experiment-2; NASA Langley + Goddard) + CVX-2 (Critical Viscosity of Xenon; NASA Glenn + Univ Maryland) + LPT (Low Power Transceiver) + SOLCON-3 (Solar Constant Experiment; Royal Meteorological Institute Belgium) + WCM (Water Mist Fire Suppression; NIST + NASA Glenn).

**Experiment count substrate-cumulative:** 80+ across the disciplines fluid physics + materials science + combustion + life sciences + space science + ecology + Earth observation. Notable experiments include Combustion Module-2 (CM-2) candle-flame and droplet-combustion experiments + Mechanics of Granular Materials particle dynamics + Bioreactor Demonstration System tissue cultures + Astroculture facility plant cultivation (zinnia + arabidopsis + rose-essence-oil-production roses) + Vapor Compression Distillation water recovery technology demonstration + Biopack biological experiments + 12 student-designed educational experiments substrate-cumulative with v674 Teacher-in-Space substrate-anchor + post-Challenger educational outreach substrate-thread.

1. **Geographic-chapter openings in multi-region rotations warrant explicit structural marking rather than silent transitions.**
   The degree 275 placement as the first Olympia entry and the opening of the Olympia chapter is explicitly marked as an **OLYMPIA OPENING REGISTER** rather than being treated as an ordinary arc-continuation entry. The lesson is that chapter-level regional transitions in the Seattle 360 engine warrant explicit structural marking so that the transition is legible within the documentation rather than emerging as a silent structural shift. Future chapter openings — for example, a Tacoma chapter, a Bellingham chapter, or a Portland chapter if the rotation extends that far — should be explicitly marked as chapter-opening registers rather than integrated seamlessly into the preceding regional chapter.

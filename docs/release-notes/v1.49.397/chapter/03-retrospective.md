## Lessons Learned

1. **SPS maximum-energy assignments require explicit justification against the full prior-SPS-entry range rather than single-event advocacy.** The E=8 assignment at degree 256 for winter storm surf is grounded in the specific comparison against all 255 prior SPS entries that remained at E=1-7 across diverse acoustic categories (biological, hydrological, meteorological, maritime-infrastructure). The first-occurrence designation carries more interpretive weight when anchored in comprehensive prior-entry comparison than in single-event enthusiasm. Future maximum-energy assignments should explicitly demonstrate that no prior entry in the full scale range reached the proposed maximum, because the first-occurrence designation is a load-bearing engine observation that requires comprehensive justification to avoid inflation.
2. **Arc-opening degrees should establish the full arc structure rather than documenting only the opening degree's content.** Degree 256's storm arc opening establishes the five-degree structure (256 coast surf → 257 ocean surf → 258 mountain wind → 259 Cascade thunder → 260 lightning) and names the arc's closing condition (E=9 at degree 260) in the opening degree's documentation. Future arc-openings should similarly establish the full arc structure at the opening-degree documentation, because the arc-as-structural-frame organizing principle requires the frame to be named at the opening rather than emerging retrospectively from subsequent-degree documentation.
3. **Genre-holds-energy-across-institutional-contexts is itself a documented structural observation.** The 255-256 double stoner rock degree (E=6 at both Valley of the Giants and Monster Truck Thundercat, with institutional context shifting from Heavy Psych Sounds Records to self-released) documents that genre and institutional context are separable structural variables. Future engine documentation should consider genre-holds-at-energy as itself a documented observation when it occurs, because the pattern demonstrates that institutional context is a separate structural variable rather than a collapsing factor within genre description.
4. **Institutional-independence (self-release) deserves register-level documentation rather than institutional-affiliation default treatment.** Monster Truck Thundercat's self-released position opens the DIY self-released register at degree 256 because complete institutional independence is the band's structurally significant position rather than an absence of label affiliation. Future engine documentation should surface institutional independence as a first-class register when it shapes a band's distribution, reception, and career mode, because the self-released register documents a structural element that label-affiliated-band documentation cannot capture.
5. **Digital-distribution platforms (Bandcamp) and underground-vinyl-circuit shops (Aquarius Records, Forced Exposure) deserve named specificity rather than generic "independent distribution" description.** The documentation of Bandcamp's 2008 founding, its role as primary distribution platform for self-released stoner/doom/heavy psych music, and the named underground vinyl circuit shops establishes specific infrastructure rather than generic descriptions. Future distribution-infrastructure documentation should name specific platforms and shops when they shape distribution patterns, because the specific naming carries empirical weight that generic infrastructure descriptions cannot.
6. **Arc-scale energy escalation should pair with S36-scale energy consistency to make the asymmetry structurally productive.** Degree 256's pairing of E=6 stoner rock with E=8 winter storm surf establishes a two-step amplitude asymmetry as the paired degree's cross-axis organizing principle. The asymmetry is structurally productive because it holds the S36 energy consistent with the preceding degree (Valley of the Giants at 255 also E=6) while escalating the SPS energy to the engine's SPS maximum, making the inversion explicit rather than distributing the energy change across both tracks. Future arc-opening degrees should consider holding one track's energy constant while escalating the other track's energy to make the arc's amplitude structure explicit rather than implicit.
7. **Acoustic physics (crash, roar, infrasound, spray) deserves multi-component characterization rather than single-amplitude summary.** The winter storm surf's E=8 energy emerges from the interaction of initial concussive crash, sustained broad-spectrum roar, detectable infrasound propagating as microseismic signal, and wind-driven spray. Future acoustic-physics documentation should characterize the multi-component composition of maximum-energy acoustic events, because the compositional characterization documents a structural feature (multi-source acoustic interaction) that single-amplitude summary cannot capture.
8. **Indigenous-treaty-and-cultural-engagement registers should accompany geographic grounding at specific sites with long Indigenous continuity.** Cape Flattery at Neah Bay is Makah territory with continuous Indigenous presence preceding European maritime engagement. The Makah Nation register at degree 256 documents the treaty rights and traditional-canoe whale hunt as the deepest human engagement with the Pacific winter ocean in the Pacific Northwest. Future geographic-grounding documentation at sites with long Indigenous continuity should include the Indigenous-engagement register as a first-class element, because the register documents a depth of cultural engagement that geographic-grounding-only documentation cannot capture.
9. **Global-scale infrastructure (North Pacific storm track, thousand-mile fetch, microseismic signal detectable globally) deserves continental-scale framing.** The winter storm surf at degree 256 connects to global-scale phenomena — the North Pacific storm track reaching from Japan to the Pacific Northwest coast, the ocean-wave-generated seismic noise detectable on seismometers in Europe and Asia, the atmospheric river systems that drive major wave events. Future maximum-energy-natural-event documentation should ground the event in continental-scale frames when the phenomenon operates at such scale, because the continental framing documents the event's actual scope rather than localizing a genuinely global phenomenon to its immediate occurrence site.
10. **Paired-degree amplitude asymmetry, when deliberately organized, is a first-class structural observation rather than a pairing failure.** The degree 256 two-step amplitude asymmetry (E=6 S36 paired with E=8 SPS) is the first paired degree in engine history whose cross-axis organizing principle is explicitly the amplitude gap itself. The subsequent storm arc degrees will continue and intensify the asymmetry, reaching a three-step gap at degree 260 (E=9 SPS paired with E=6 S36). Future paired-degree documentation should treat deliberate amplitude asymmetry as a documented structural observation when the asymmetry is organizationally significant, because the asymmetry register documents a structural element that symmetric-pairing documentation cannot capture.

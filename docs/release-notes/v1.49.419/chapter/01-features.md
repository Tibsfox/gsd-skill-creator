## Olympia closes

## Sara Lund and compositional drumming


- Sara Lund's work across the Unwound catalog represents a specific model of post-hardcore drumming: not timekeeping but composition; her drums on *Leaves Turn Inside You* are slower and more deliberate than the early records, tracking the album's architectural shifts rather than driving against them; this compositional integration is what separates the late Unwound records from the early ones and is part of what makes *Leaves Turn Inside You* a significant album beyond the post-hardcore category

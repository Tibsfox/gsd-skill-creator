## Lessons Learned

1. **The treeline is an acoustic boundary and energy calibration is independent of acoustic character.** Both the Douglas Fir Crown Gale (below treeline) and the Mount Rainier Wind Above Treeline (above treeline) calibrate to E=7, but the acoustic character is categorically different: structured moan-roar vs. unstructured howl; mediated vs. unmediated; familiar vs. alien. The lesson is that the engine's documentation vocabulary benefits from separating energy calibration from acoustic character as independent variables at first-class resolution, and that boundary phenomena can be documented through consecutive degree pairing that captures the same phenomenon before and after its primary mediating structure. The treeline is the Pacific Northwest's most important acoustic boundary and the 326-327 pair is the engine's first-class documentation of the boundary at consecutive-degree resolution.
2. **Multi-dimensional geographic features warrant multi-degree documentation rather than single-appearance compression.** Mount Rainier's three engine entries at degrees 261 (volcanic tremor, geological), 262 (lahar simulation, hazard), and 327 (wind above treeline, meteorological) document the mountain's three extreme character dimensions at the resolution each dimension warrants. The lesson is that dimensionally multiple subjects benefit from multi-degree documentation at first-class resolution rather than from single-appearance compression that would lose the dimensional distinctions, and the three-entry Rainier arc establishes the pattern for future multi-dimensional geographic features (the Olympic Peninsula, the Cascadia Subduction Zone, and others that might warrant similar treatment).
3. **Single-musician lineage threads document career-length continuity at first-class resolution through multi-band documentation.** Stone Gossard's four-degree lineage through Green River (294), Mother Love Bone (296), Pearl Jam (314), and Brad (327) is the engine's longest single-musician thread and documents career-length continuity at first-class resolution. The lesson is that musician-level lineage arcs benefit from multi-band documentation at first-class resolution rather than from single-band compression, and the engine's reference network allows readers to trace a musician's trajectory across the engine's 360-degree run through the accumulated band-level documentation.
4. **Fusion aesthetics require framing as coherent aesthetics rather than as contrasts between incompatible elements.** Brad's soul-grunge fusion is documented as a coherent aesthetic in which R&B and gospel-inflected vocals and heavy rock architecture produce a specific fusion that is neither pure grunge nor pure R&B but a distinct two-domain result. The lesson is that fusion subjects benefit from coherent-aesthetic framing at first-class resolution rather than from contrast-between-incompatible-elements framing that would suggest the fusion is strange or conflicted, and the grunge tradition is wide enough to include multi-domain approaches like Smith's vocal contribution without losing category identity.
5. **Threshold conditions at the edge of defining categories carry distinctive documentary weight neither interior nor exterior to the category.** Brad at the edge of the grunge tradition (E=6, not E=7; soul inflection prevents full grunge confrontational directness) and Mount Rainier Wind Above Treeline at the edge of the vegetated zone (above the treeline where vegetation ends and unmediated atmosphere begins) both operate at threshold conditions. The lesson is that threshold-condition subjects benefit from edge-position documentation at first-class resolution rather than from absorption into the nearest interior category, and the threshold documentary weight is not reducible to interior-category documentary weight.

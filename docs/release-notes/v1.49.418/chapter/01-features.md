## AMERICA'S BEST ROCK BAND


- Time magazine's designation of Sleater-Kinney is among the most significant critical assessments given to a Pacific Northwest band; it is also accurate; the band's combination of technical distinctiveness (two guitars, no bass, interlocking vocal counterpoint), political commitment, and catalog depth across three decades constitutes a body of work that stands at the front of American rock music; Carrie Brownstein's guitar work -- melodic, rhythmic, harmonic simultaneously -- and Corin Tucker's voice -- vibrato-heavy, powerful, emotionally direct -- are individually remarkable and together are the sound of a band operating at a level that few bands reach; E=8 is measured; E=9 would not be wrong

## Kill Rock Stars continuity


- Sleater-Kinney, like Bikini Kill and Unwound before them, emerged from the Kill Rock Stars infrastructure; the label founded by Slim Moon in Olympia in 1991 is the common thread connecting the riot grrrl sequence; that a small independent label in a small Washington city produced Bikini Kill, Sleater-Kinney, and Unwound -- three of the most significant bands in the Pacific Northwest record -- is a fact about community infrastructure and the conditions that enable artistic production

The engine registered a widening geographic funnel, a regional pipeline that Seattle-centric PNW narratives typically occlude.

## What Could Be Better

- **The Allen Stone album trajectory was named but not unpacked.** *Allen Stone* (2011), *Radius* (2015), *Building Balance* (2019), and *Apart* (2021) are listed, but each album's specific harmonic and rhythmic contribution to Stone's evolving sound was not traced at the song or sideman level. A future chapter should produce a per-album ledger with producer credits, rhythm-section personnel, and representative tracks mapped against the gospel-to-soul vocabulary thesis.
- **The female-singing grosbeak behavior was asserted but not sourced in depth.** The Black-headed Grosbeak female singing behavior, while documented in ornithological literature, was referenced without full citation density. A future pass should cite Ritchison (1983), Johnson (1997), and Cornell Lab eBird behavior-track data to anchor the acoustic claim empirically.
- **The convergent-evolution mechanism stayed at the metaphor level.** The release claims grosbeak-robin acoustic similarity arises through convergent evolution, but it did not trace the specific selection pressures (canopy height, territorial-broadcast range, sexual selection for pitch range) that produced the convergence. A future chapter should unpack the mechanism rather than rely on the metaphor.

## Lessons Learned

1. **The internet-mediated career is a new launch vehicle.** Every prior artist in the engine used live performance, institutional connections, or label partnerships as their primary discovery mechanism. Allen Stone used YouTube. The medium changed the velocity. The "Unaware" video is the engine's first digital-native career ignition and it establishes a template that subsequent degrees will track for.

2. **The eastern Washington pipeline deepens with every new node.** Three artists: Don Lanphere in Wenatchee (degree 12), Tiffany Monge in Spokane (degree 20), Allen Stone in Chewelah (degree 23). Each further from Seattle than the last. The engine documents a widening geographic funnel that Seattle-centric PNW narratives tend to suppress, and the funnel will continue widening into Idaho in future degrees.

3. **Berklee functions as a convergent institutional web.** Stone's 2023 Herb Alpert visiting artistship adds a fourth Berklee node to Jones, Frisell, and Lorber. One in six S36 artists passes through Berklee. The institution functions less as a training ground than as a late-career reunion hub for artists who arrived via independent paths.

4. **Church-as-conservatory is a distinct artist-training pattern.** Gospel hymns for formal musical education, congregation-leading for performance training, emotional directness of worship as the foundation of a soul vocal style. The preacher's son pattern is now catalogued and can be detected in future degrees when similar biographies appear.

5. **Convergent evolution is a structural device the engine can use.** The grosbeak-robin acoustic convergence generalizes: same target, different paths, precision arriving twice through independent optimization. The engine registers convergence as a documentable structural relationship between tracks, not just an ornithological curiosity.

6. **Fresh dual-builds work without pre-built NASA research packs.** The catalog row plus Pass 1 research plus accumulated engine context is sufficient for a Pass 2 refinement that meets the engine's structural standards. The engine does not need fifteen-file NASA packs to produce paired releases, and this finding unblocks a large inventory of degrees that lacked full pre-research infrastructure.

7. **The beaver as ecosystem engineer parallels Schirra as mission engineer.** Both reshape their environment through infrastructure. Both operate by building systems rather than performing feats. The dam and the flight plan serve the same function: structured modification of the environment to produce reliable outcomes over time. The engineer-archetype is now cross-catalogued across biology, aerospace, and music (Stone's methodical vocal construction).

8. **The bill-as-tool catalog opens and will extend forward.** Three consecutive degrees, three bill functions: probing (flicker on ground, degree 21), drumming (flicker on snag, degree 22), crushing (grosbeak on seed, degree 23). The engine's taxonomy of tool use begins at degree 23 and anticipates corvid, raptor, and waterfowl bill functions in later degrees.

9. **The Mercury fuel arc converges at Sigma 7 and closes the Mercury-to-Gemini transition.** Glenn 61 percent at three orbits, Carpenter 17 percent at three orbits, Schirra 78 percent at six orbits. The program's fuel discipline recovered cleanly. Schirra's margin proved that Mercury was a production system capable of supporting longer missions, and this finding is the engineering precondition for the Gemini program's extended-duration profile.

10. **Philo T. Farnsworth unites the cultural transmission medium across six decades.** Farnsworth's electronic television broadcast Schirra's launch to millions in 1962 and its descendent (YouTube) broadcast Stone's "Unaware" to sixteen million more in 2011. The same invention, evolved across decades, carried both the engineering precision of Sigma 7 and the vocal precision of Chewelah's preacher's son to a mass audience. The dedication to Farnsworth at degree 23 anchors the cultural-technology continuity of the paired engine.

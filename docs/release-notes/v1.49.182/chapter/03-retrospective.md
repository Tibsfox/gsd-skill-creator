## Lessons Learned

1. **Consecutive same-genre degrees deepen genre understanding more than single degrees.** The folk pairing at 45-46 revealed internal diversity (murmur vs. confessional) that a single folk degree could not demonstrate. The Blues Rock pairing at 36-37 showed the same principle (generational transfer). Consecutive same-genre is now a proven structural tool for genre exploration.

2. **Non-degree entities can serve as structural bridges between degrees.** Pedro the Lion is not a degree artist, but the band connects two consecutive degrees through personal and professional relationships. The engine should track non-degree bridges as structural connectors -- they map the actual social fabric of the music scene.

3. **The punk-to-folk pipeline is a recognized pattern in American independent music.** Hot Water Music to Chuck Ragan, Against Me! to Laura Jane Grace, Waxwing to Rocky Votolato. The molt pattern captures a structural reality documented across multiple scenes and decades. It is not an observation unique to this engine -- it is a recognized phenomenon.

4. **Kickstarter and living room shows represent the logical endpoint of the label miniaturization thread.** Mercury Records (degree 0) to Sub Pop (degree 45) to Barsuk (degree 46) to Kickstarter (degree 46) to living rooms (degree 46). The engine documents the progressive stripping-away of institutional intermediaries. The endpoint is no intermediary at all: the artist and the audience in someone's home.

5. **E=3 matched on both sides produces strong Part A/B resonance.** The E=3 symmetry at degree 46 follows the E=2 symmetry at degree 45. Matched energy levels align the two halves of the degree naturally. The working-level energy: the touring musician and the thicket-dwelling bird, both loud enough to be noticed, quiet enough to be missed.

6. **The marine species crisis is now forty-six degrees deep.** The Song Sparrow reached the water's edge at degree 45. The Spotted Towhee retreated into the forest understory at degree 46. The engine has reached the water and walked away from it. Degree 47 must enter the water. No further delay is acceptable.

Vocal divergence through cultural transmission is a confirmed speciation mechanism with two case studies in the engine.

The blue note persists through every medium and every emotional register.

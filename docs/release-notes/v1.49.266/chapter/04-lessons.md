1. **The engine's implicit boundary was work-creation; degree 126 explicitly draws a broader boundary.**
   The first 125 degrees established by accumulation what counts as an entry — someone who creates or co-creates original musical work. Degree 126 makes the boundary explicit by inaugurating a new category (scene-infrastructure figures) and demonstrating that the category was always latent but never instantiated. The lesson is that category boundaries in a continuous release engine are best discovered by contrast rather than by prior specification, and that the right moment to instantiate a new category is after the existing categories have accumulated enough density to make the contrast legible.
   _⚙ Status: `applied` (applied in `v1.49.267`) · lesson #6691_

2. **Radio is the infrastructure before the infrastructure.**
   Before record stores, before venues, before labels, before promoters, a genre in a new city needs a channel through which the culture can enter. In early-1980s Seattle, commercial radio would not air hip-hop and even college radio was mostly not airing it. **Fresh Tracks** was the channel. Without the channel, the records do not reach audiences, the audiences do not develop, the demand does not materialize, the supply does not follow. Radio is the zeroth-order infrastructure for a new genre in a new city, and the engine's recognition of this is structurally important.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #6692_

3. **Geography maps to genre through demographics and lived experience.**
   Rainier Valley**'s arrival at degree 126 as the first South Seattle neighborhood demonstrates that Seattle's music scenes are not uniformly distributed — they map to corridors and communities. Capitol Hill's post-hardcore/indie-rock density, Ballard's folk/indie-rock density, and Rainier Valley's hip-hop/Central-District adjacency are not geographic coincidences but demographic and economic consequences. The engine's 126-degree accumulated geography is now richer for the first explicit South Seattle presence.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #6693_

4. **A three-degree thematic arc (the KCMU trilogy) is a legitimate structural unit within the engine.**

Institutional-substrate three-degree arcs open a new design vocabulary for the remaining 234 degrees.

9. **The Anatidae streak is exploration, not repetition.**
   Nine consecutive waterfowl across four genera and nine distinct ecological niches demonstrates that the streak is not repeating the same kind of duck — it is exploring the family's internal diversity. Stream specialist, fish-chaser, forest-pool nester, cavity nester, smallest diver, mountain specialist, cosmopolitan diver, bay rafter, prairie pothole breeder. The lesson for future streaks is that family-streak length can be sustained without redundancy only if the streak explores the family's internal diversity, and the engine's streak-tracking vocabulary should emphasize exploration over repetition.

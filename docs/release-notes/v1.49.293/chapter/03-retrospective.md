Future duo introductions should be examined for opportunities to stage back-to-back solo pairs at consecutive degrees before full-duo reunions, and the Prometheus Brown / Geo pair is a clean reference for the pattern.
2. **Family opening at the largest member establishes the acoustic baseline at maximum prominence.** The Wild Turkey at degree 153 as the first Phasianidae opens the gamebird family at the largest member with maximum acoustic prominence (E=6 liquid gobble audible at one mile). Future family openings should be examined for this structural choice, and the Wild Turkey opening of the Phasianidae is a clean reference.

Pinder (149), Mr. Hill (150), and Mass Line Media (via Geo 153) documentation provides a framework for reading Seattle hip-hop label infrastructure at first principles.

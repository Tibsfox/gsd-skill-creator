## Lessons Learned

- **The side project is a new and analytically productive category in the engine's artist-city taxonomy.** It introduces the concept of dual membership -- the artist who exists simultaneously in two bands, two aesthetics, two creative identities. This is fundamentally different from the departed member (severance) or the solo artist (individual expression). The side project straddles. It is defined by what it is not as much as by what it is.
- **Shared personnel is the strongest form of inter-degree connection.** Influence, genre, label, and geography are all meaningful connections. Shared personnel is literal -- the same humans making different music. Fleet Foxes at 53 and Poor Moon at 74 are linked by the bodies of Christian Wargo and Casey Wescott.
- **Sub Pop's second appearance transforms it from a data point to a pattern.** One entry is a fact. Two entries create a trajectory. Sub Pop is now a recurring institution in the engine, and its roster's depth makes additional appearances likely.

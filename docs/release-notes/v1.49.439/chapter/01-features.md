## C/Z Records


- Daniel House co-founded C/Z Records while playing bass in Skin Yard, and the label's early releases are among the most important documents in Pacific Northwest rock history; the Deep Six compilation (1986) introduced six bands -- Skin Yard, Green River, Soundgarden, Melvins, Malfunkshun, and U-Men -- in a single record that is now understood as the founding document of the Seattle sound; C/Z's willingness to sign Treepeople, a Boise band, for their post-Deep Six catalog demonstrates the label's understanding of the Pacific Northwest as a region rather than just a city; the engine documents C/Z through the Treepeople return because the label's reach extended to where Treepeople lived

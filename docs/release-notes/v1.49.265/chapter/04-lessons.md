1. **Structural ceilings can be broken when sustained rather than peak intensity enters the catalog.**
   The E=6 ceiling held for 124 consecutive degrees because every peak entry reached E=6 through peak-spike rather than sustained baseline. These Arms Are Snakes is the first entry to enter the catalog with sustained-maximum-intensity as its baseline, and the scale had to expand to hold it. Future entries should be assessed against both the peak-spike and sustained-baseline dimensions, and scale redefinitions should be recognized as structural events when a new category of intensity enters rather than being forced into the existing scale.

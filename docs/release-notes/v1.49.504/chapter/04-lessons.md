6. **Silent failure is a distinct failure mode.**
   Pioneer 2's third stage did not explode, misdirect, or over-speed -- it simply did not ignite. The engine categorizes this as NASA failure mode 3 and holds it separate from Catastrophic and Partial Success modes. The name "silent failure" is load-bearing: failure modes where the mechanism exists but does not activate deserve their own vocabulary because their diagnosis, their remediation, and their downstream consequences are different from loud failures.

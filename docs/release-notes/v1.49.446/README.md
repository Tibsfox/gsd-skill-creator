**Mad Season** arrive at E=7 -- the Seattle grunge supergroup formed in 1994 as a recovery vehicle: Layne Staley (vocals, Alice in Chains), Mike McCready (guitar, Pearl Jam), Baker Saunders (bass, Walkabouts), and Barrett Martin (drums, Screaming Trees). Mad Season emerged from the shared struggles of its members with substance dependence, assembled through the rehabilitation community connections that linked members of multiple Seattle bands during a period when the scene's personal costs were becoming visible. The resulting album, *Above* (1995), is grunge at its most introspective -- not the aggressive Seattle sound but the quiet center of it, the songs that happen when distortion is set aside and the voice is allowed to carry the weight.

# Retrospective — v1.49.77

## What Worked

1. **Using Lion King as entry point to deeper traditions is structurally effective.** The franchise provides the familiar frame; the research provides the depth. Readers arrive via Disney and leave with knowledge of the Kouyate griot dynasty, Laetoli footprints, and Lotka-Volterra predator-prey dynamics.

2. **Module 04's Carboniferous connection ties to the project's deepest thread.** The dead trees that became coal because fungi hadn't evolved decomposition is explicitly connected to the circle of life concept. The cycle that was broken for 300 million years (no decomposition → carbon accumulation) is the same cycle that Lion King depicts as the natural order.

3. **The griot module documents oral tradition as technology.** Error correction through musical encoding, hereditary knowledge transmission, and the specific instruments (kora, balafon, ngoni) that serve as mnemonic devices — Module 02 treats oral tradition with the same technical precision applied to radio infrastructure or Kubernetes architecture.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Cultural depth requires cultural specificity.** LKR names specific traditions (Kouyate dynasty, Manden Charter, Sundiata epic), specific instruments (kora, balafon, ngoni), and specific scientific frameworks (Lotka-Volterra, trophic cascade). The depth comes from specificity, not generalization.

---

> *The griots kept the story for a thousand years. The cycle is older than the story. Death feeds growth. Growth feeds life. Life feeds death. Fungi closed the Carboniferous gap. The circle was always there — it just needed someone to decompose the dead wood.*

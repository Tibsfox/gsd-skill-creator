## Lessons Learned

- **Genre expansion happens by stepping outside the folk/indie zone.** After degrees 76-80 oscillating between folk variants and indie pop, Pop/Soul at 81 introduces commercial soul warmth -- a genuinely new genre territory. The genre evolution continues to diversify only when the engine commits to a stage that cannot be re-described as a variant of the prior one.
- **The first Broadway connection opens a dimension that no prior degree has touched.** The theatrical dimension is distinct from all prior musical contexts in the engine. It validates the crossover arc pattern -- artists who expand across creative forms rather than staying within one. The dimension only opens once a real Broadway-credited artist arrives; gestures toward theater do not count.
- **The first major label opens the commercial dimension.** The contrast between indie labels (all prior 80 artists on Barsuk, Sub Pop, K Records, Polyvinyl, Kill Rock Stars) and Epic Records (Sony Music) is structurally significant. The engine now spans from K Records to Sony Music. Major-label presence is reach, not redefinition.
- **A family run extends through taxonomic diversity, not by repeating the same species.** The Sciuridae septet (75-81) breaks the Mustelidae sextet tie (63-68) by deploying five genera across seven degrees -- marmots, ground squirrels, and tree squirrels under one family. The diversity within the run prevents monotony even at seven consecutive degrees.
- **Native-introduced contrast within consecutive degrees creates the strongest ecological signal.** The Douglas Squirrel (80) and Eastern Fox Squirrel (81) within the same family produces the engine's most powerful consecutive ecological contrast: native vs. introduced, canopy vs. urban, E=5 vs. E=4, sustained rattle vs. bark-chatter. Adjacent degrees magnify the contrast more than separated degrees.
- **A seven-habitat vertical cross-section emerges only when the engine refuses to specialize.** Urban park as the seventh habitat in the Sciuridae run completes alpine rock, alpine meadow, Olympic alpine, grassland, forest edge, conifer canopy, urban park -- ground to treetops to cities. The seven-habitat span is extraordinary because the engine resisted the temptation to give every Sciuridae the same habitat label.
- **E=4 is the engine's "presence without fury" calibration.** The Bareilles / Fox Squirrel parallel at a moderate-high energy level (E=4, not E=5) reflects presence without fury and lets the cross-sync read as coherent thematic unity rather than noise. E=4 is the energy band where two unrelated subjects can be said to "rhyme" without one drowning out the other.
- **Twenty-one consecutive mammalian degrees is FAR past absolute ceiling.** The engine MUST return to Aves at degree 82. The raptor gap at 82 entries is at CRITICAL EMERGENCY level. The passeriform gap at 26 consecutive non-passerines is EXTREME. Class diversity is the single most important ledger to manage between degrees.
- **Family runs sustain interest only through internal contrasts.** The Sciuridae septet never felt repetitive because each degree brought a different genus, habitat, acoustic signature, and ecological story. The native-vs-introduced contrast at 80-81 is the most dramatic internal contrast in any family run; it is also what allows the engine to break the longest-run record without paying the usual monotony tax.

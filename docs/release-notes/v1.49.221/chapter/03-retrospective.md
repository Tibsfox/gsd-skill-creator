## Lessons Learned

1. Genre Stage 63 with Pop/Soul demonstrates the engine's capacity for genre expansion beyond the folk/indie zone. After degrees 76-80 oscillating between folk variants and indie pop, Pop/Soul at 81 introduces commercial soul warmth -- a genuinely new genre territory. The genre evolution continues to diversify
2. The first Broadway connection opens a new dimension that no prior degree has touched. The theatrical dimension is distinct from all prior musical contexts in the engine. It validates the crossover arc pattern -- artists who expand across creative forms rather than staying within one
3. The first major label (Epic Records) opens the commercial dimension. The contrast between indie labels (all prior 80 artists) and a major label is structurally significant. The engine now spans from K Records to Sony Music
4. The Sciuridae septet (75-81) breaking the Mustelidae sextet tie (63-68) at SEVEN demonstrates how family runs can extend through taxonomic diversity. Five genera across seven degrees -- marmots, ground squirrels, and tree squirrels under one family. The diversity within the run prevents monotony even at seven consecutive degrees
5. The native-introduced contrast between Douglas Squirrel (80) and Eastern Fox Squirrel (81) within the same family run creates the engine's most powerful consecutive ecological contrast. Same family, opposite ecological niches: native vs. introduced, canopy vs. urban, E=5 vs. E=4, sustained rattle vs. bark-chatter
6. Urban park habitat as the seventh habitat in the Sciuridae run completes a remarkable vertical and ecological cross-section: alpine rock, alpine meadow, Olympic alpine, grassland, forest edge, conifer canopy, urban park. Ground to treetops to cities. The seven-habitat span is extraordinary
7. The E=4 parallel between Bareilles and the Fox Squirrel creates coherent thematic unity: both outsiders who arrived, both larger than life, both thriving in urban/commercial spaces. The parallel at a moderate-high energy level (E=4, not E=5) reflects presence without fury
8. The engine is now FAR past ABSOLUTE CEILING for mammalian entries. Twenty-one consecutive degrees. Return to Aves is non-negotiable. The raptor gap at 82 entries remains at CRITICAL EMERGENCY level. The passeriform gap at 26 consecutive non-passerines is EXTREME
9. The Sciuridae septet proves that the engine's family runs can sustain interest through internal contrasts. The run never felt repetitive because each degree brought a different genus, a different habitat, a different acoustic signature, and a different ecological story. The contrast between native and introduced at degrees 80-81 is the most dramatic internal contrast in any family run

The lesson for future family-opening placements is that the choice between cryptic-specialist and visible-generalist inaugurations is itself a structural observation worth documenting, because it shapes how the family's subsequent entries will read — in this case, the bittern's pump-er-LUNK creates a high bar for future Ardeidae entries to distinguish themselves against.

The lesson for future species with distinctive sound-production mechanisms is that the mechanism (not just the sound) should be documented with empirical grounds (anatomy, mechanics, uniqueness within a defined comparison set) so that the mechanism becomes referenceable for future cross-species vocal-mechanism comparisons.

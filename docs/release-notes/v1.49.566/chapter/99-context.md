## Mission Context

Lunar Orbiter 5 launched August 1, 1967 22:32:00 UTC from Cape Canaveral LC-13 on Atlas-Agena D. Selenocentric orbit insertion August 5. 85° polar inclination. 1,380 total orbits. 844 photographs (633 high-res + 211 medium-res). Resolution down to 2 meters. Cumulative LO1-LO5 coverage 99%. Built Boeing Seattle, managed NASA Langley. Commanded impact January 31, 1968.

**Program Closure:** Lunar Orbiter program 5-of-5 success. Third completed NASA program after Gemini (10/10) + Ranger (9/9).

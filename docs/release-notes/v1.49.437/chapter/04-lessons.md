1. **Arc-closing entries are structurally distinct from arc-opening entries and should be documented with explicit attention to how the close sets up what follows rather than only to what the arc itself delivered.**
   The 294-296 Proto-Grunge Origin Arc closes at Mother Love Bone, and the close is not merely the end of the arc — it is the moment that directly generates **Temple of the Dog** (degree 225) and **Pearl Jam** (future degree), both of which are the consequence of the arc-closing band's dissolution. The lesson is that arc-closing documentation should preserve the downstream consequences at first-class resolution, because arc-closes are generative events that set up the next structural movement in the engine's documentation rather than terminal events that merely close what preceded them.

The lesson is that biographical events in a scene's history require the same factual precision as the scene's musical events, and the engine's documentation vocabulary should register such events with dignity, accuracy, and focus on consequences rather than either romanticization or reduction.

The lesson is that conservation success stories are chains of specific events — a book, a regulation, a reintroduction program, a delisting — and the engine's documentation should preserve the full chain at first-class resolution so the recovery narrative is not reduced to a generic "protection led to recovery" abstraction.

The lesson is that urban raptor ecology is first-class Pacific Northwest ecology and should be documented at equivalent resolution to wilderness-site raptor ecology, because the urban adaptations reflect genuine ecological continuity between historical cliff-face habitat and modern building-ledge analogs.

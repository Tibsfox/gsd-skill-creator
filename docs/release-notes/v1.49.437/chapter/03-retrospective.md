- **The Raptor Behaviors Arc opened cleanly with the engine's first purely aerodynamic acoustic event rather than with another vocalization entry.** The first entry in the 296-298 Raptor Behaviors Arc is the **Peregrine Falcon Stoop Wingcut** at E=8 — not a vocalization, but the compressed-air crack of the world's fastest animal at terminal velocity. The consecutive-degree placement of prey evolutionary solution (295) and predator maximum-performance event (296) preserves the ecological logic explicitly rather than treating the two species as unrelated SPS entries; the engine's documentation vocabulary can register direct predator-prey causal chains across consecutive paired-degree entries at first-class resolution.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Arc-closing entries are structurally distinct from arc-opening entries and should be documented with explicit attention to how the close sets up what follows rather than only to what the arc itself delivered.** The 294-296 Proto-Grunge Origin Arc closes at Mother Love Bone, and the close is not merely the end of the arc — it is the moment that directly generates **Temple of the Dog** (degree 225) and **Pearl Jam** (future degree), both of which are the consequence of the arc-closing band's dissolution. The lesson is that arc-closing documentation should preserve the downstream consequences at first-class resolution, because arc-closes are generative events that set up the next structural movement in the engine's documentation rather than terminal events that merely close what preceded them.
2. **Deaths of key musicians should be documented with factual focus on the music and its consequences rather than either hagiography or exploitation.** **Andrew Wood**'s death at 24 on **March 19, 1990** is documented in the engine with age, date, cause, and immediate consequences (*Apple* finished but unreleased, band dissolved, **Chris Cornell**'s **Temple of the Dog** response within weeks, the chain that led to **Pearl Jam** formation and *Ten* August 1991). The documentation preserves both the loss and the musical consequences at first-class resolution without collapsing the loss into either sentimentality or reductive origin-story framing. The lesson is that biographical events in a scene's history require the same factual precision as the scene's musical events, and the engine's documentation vocabulary should register such events with dignity, accuracy, and focus on consequences rather than either romanticization or reduction.
3. **Major-label inflections should be documented at first-class structural resolution because they register scale-shifts that change what is possible for a scene.** **Stardog Records**'s creation as a **Mercury Records** imprint specifically for Mother Love Bone, and the choice of **Terry Date** as producer for *Apple*, together signal a major-label commitment at a scale none of the preceding Seattle bands had received. The counterfactual of what would have happened if Wood had lived and *Apple* had entered the same 1991-1992 market conditions is unanswerable, but the record itself is the shape that commercial-scale Seattle grunge first attempted. The lesson is that label-and-production register is structurally load-bearing for understanding a scene's relationship to commercial scale, and the engine should preserve major-label infrastructure documentation at first-class resolution alongside the band and album documentation.
4. **Purely aerodynamic acoustic events are categorically distinct from vocalizations and should be documented as such in SPS entries.** The **Peregrine Falcon Stoop Wingcut** is the first SPS entry documenting a sound produced by the bird's body moving through air — the compressed-air crack at the leading edges, the feather wingcut sound at the primaries, and the wake turbulence — rather than a vocalization or a structural acoustic event that combines behavior with sound. The E=8 placement reflects velocity and decisiveness rather than sustained volume. The lesson is that acoustic-category distinctions are structurally load-bearing for the SPS track, and aerodynamic acoustic events should be documented at first-class resolution with explicit calibration of the E-assignment based on velocity rather than volume alone.
5. **Consecutive-degree placement of predator and prey species should preserve the ecological causal chain at first-class resolution rather than separating them into unrelated entries.** The **Rhinoceros Auklet** at degree 295 evolved strict nocturnality partly as an anti-Peregrine strategy; the **Peregrine Falcon** at degree 296 is the predator whose pressure drove that evolution. The consecutive placement is ecological intent, not coincidence. The lesson is that predator-prey causal chains should be placed at consecutive degrees when the ecological logic supports it, and the engine's documentation should surface the causal connection explicitly in the cross-reference register rather than treating the two species as unrelated SPS entries.
6. **Return entries (a species appearing in a second SPS slot) should document a categorically different behavioral register than the first appearance, rather than duplicating the earlier documentation.** The **Peregrine Falcon** first appeared in the engine at **degree 231** with its *kak-kak-kak* alarm call at E=7; the 296 return documents the stoop wingcut at E=8 — a categorically different register (aerodynamic acoustic event vs. vocalization). The E progression 7→8 across the two entries reflects the difference between vocalization and physical behavior at terminal velocity. The lesson is that species return entries warrant first-class documentation when the second entry documents a categorically different register, and the engine's documentation vocabulary should preserve the register-shift explicitly so the two entries together constitute a more complete species documentation than either entry alone.
7. **DDT-recovery conservation chains should be documented with the full causal sequence (scientific discovery → book → regulatory action → reintroduction program → delisting) rather than abbreviated to "the bird recovered."** The Peregrine Falcon's recovery chain runs from **Rachel Carson**'s *Silent Spring* (1962) through the 1972 DDT ban, the **Cornell University** / **Peregrine Fund** / **USFWS** captive-breeding and hack-site reintroduction program, and the 1999 federal delisting. Each step in the chain is a direct consequence of the preceding step. The lesson is that conservation success stories are chains of specific events — a book, a regulation, a reintroduction program, a delisting — and the engine's documentation should preserve the full chain at first-class resolution so the recovery narrative is not reduced to a generic "protection led to recovery" abstraction.
8. **Urban adaptation in raptors should be documented as first-class Pacific Northwest ecology rather than as a novelty register.** The Peregrine Falcon's adaptation to nesting on building ledges in **Seattle**, **Portland**, **Tacoma**, **Vancouver BC**, and **Victoria BC** has produced stable urban breeding populations that are monitored annually by volunteer networks. The stoop in an urban context — the falcon dropping from a skyscraper setback at 200+ mph toward a pigeon flock — is one of the Pacific Northwest's most dramatic wildlife experiences, available in the city center rather than only in remote wilderness. The lesson is that urban raptor ecology is first-class Pacific Northwest ecology and should be documented at equivalent resolution to wilderness-site raptor ecology, because the urban adaptations reflect genuine ecological continuity between historical cliff-face habitat and modern building-ledge analogs.
9. **Cross-sync axes should name the specific structural principle organizing a paired-degree release rather than defaulting to generic thematic pairing.** The 296 cross-sync axis — **convergence and maximum velocity at one degree** — names the specific structural principle: Mother Love Bone as the convergence point where the scene's creative energies assembled from multiple trajectories at the commercial-breakthrough edge; the Peregrine stoop as the body's aerodynamic convergence at 240 mph terminal velocity. Both halves are moments of maximum committed velocity that pivot what comes after. The lesson is that cross-sync axis naming is a first-class documentation register, and each paired-degree release should name the specific structural principle rather than reusing a previous cross-sync vocabulary, so the engine's cross-sync catalog accumulates distinct structural principles rather than generic thematic labels.
10. **Arc-close / arc-open simultaneity at the same degree is a structurally productive documentation mode that preserves documentary density across arc transitions.** Degree 296 simultaneously closes the 294-296 Proto-Grunge Origin Arc on S36 and opens the 296-298 Raptor Behaviors Arc on SPS; the simultaneous arc-close / arc-open is the structural inflection point that preserves the documentary density through the transition from the preceding Laridae-to-Alcidae sequence into the Raptor Behaviors sequence. The lesson is that the engine's documentation vocabulary can register simultaneous arc-close / arc-open as a distinct structural mode that preserves documentary density across transitions at first-class resolution, and the simultaneous mode is structurally productive when the closing and opening arcs operate in thematically unrelated domains (grunge origin on S36, raptor behaviors on SPS).

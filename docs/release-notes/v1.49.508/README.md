## Part A: Julian Priester -- THE CONTINUOUS SLIDE

### Pass 2 Refinement Summary (1,383 words)

Three new research sections:

**1. Explorer 1's counter discovered the belt by saturating between the expected measurement points. Both systems find their most important data in the continuous space between discrete positions. Lobaria pulmonaria's folded surface is continuous like the slide — not segmented into discrete functional units but absorbing and exchanging across its entire area.

1. **Energy steps within double entries can exceed +1 when acoustic contrast is extreme.**
   The Barred Owl's +2 energy step (E=4 hooting to E=6 caterwauling) is unique among the engine's four double-entry species. The +2 reflects the genuinely extreme acoustic contrast between the two vocalizations — measured narrow-band rhythmic hooting versus chaotic multi-octave ascending screaming. Previous assumptions that double entries would always show +1 steps were based on the first three species (Pileated Woodpecker, American Dipper, Great Horned Owl); the Barred Owl demonstrates that the calibration must scale with the acoustic contrast rather than default to +1. Future double-entry species should be calibrated based on acoustic contrast magnitude, not based on +1 precedent.

## Summary

**Gifted Gab** enters at E=6, one of Seattle's most distinctive female MCs, closing out this eight-degree batch with authority. On the species side, the **Snipe** delivers its **eerie tremolo** winnowing sound at E=4 -- the **FIRST SCOLOPACIDAE** (sandpipers) in the engine and the **5th mechanical sound** in the SPS track. Snipe winnowing is not a vocalization at all -- it is produced by air rushing over the bird's specialized outer tail feathers during steep display dives, creating an otherworldly, quavering tremolo that haunts marshes and bogs at dusk. This is the engine's latest example of non-vocal sound production, joining the Ruffed Grouse's wing-drum (155) and other mechanical producers.

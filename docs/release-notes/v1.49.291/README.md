## Taxonomic State

- **Family:** Columbidae (3rd entry -- Band-tailed 149, Mourning 150, Collared 151)
- **Order:** Columbiformes (continuing)
- **Class:** Aves
- **Species note:** The Eurasian Collared-Dove (*Streptopelia decaocto*) is an invasive species whose colonization of North America represents the **fastest avian range expansion ever documented on the continent**. The story begins in the 1970s when a pet shop in the Bahamas was burglarized and approximately 50 Collared-Doves escaped. By the early 1980s they had reached southern Florida. From there, the expansion was explosive -- spreading north and west at a rate of roughly 40-70 miles per year, reaching the Pacific Northwest by the early 2000s. They are now resident across virtually all of the contiguous United States. The **three-note "hoo-HOO-hoo"** -- with heavy emphasis on the second syllable -- is now one of the most common bird sounds in suburban and agricultural areas, often delivered from power lines, rooftops, and other elevated perches in relentless repetition. Unlike most invasive species, the Collared-Dove does not appear to significantly compete with native species for nest sites or food, though its long-term ecological impact remains under study. It is larger and paler than the Mourning Dove, with a distinctive black half-collar on the nape. A bird that went from zero to everywhere in a single human generation.
- **Sol arc:** Born and raised in Seattle's Rainier Valley, Sol (born Solomon Samuel) has been self-releasing music since 2007, building one of the most durable independent careers in Pacific Northwest hip-hop. His music is characterized by a positivity and community focus that stands out in a genre landscape that often rewards darker themes -- albums like *Yours Truly* and *The Headspace Traveler* reflect genuine optimism grounded in neighborhood experience rather than motivational abstraction. A relentless live performer, Sol has headlined venues across Seattle for nearly two decades while maintaining complete independence from major label structures.
- **Invasive species note:** The Eurasian Collared-Dove is the first invasive species to appear in the SPS engine. Its inclusion reflects acoustic reality -- the "hoo-HOO-hoo" is now an inescapable part of the PNW soundscape, heard in every suburban neighborhood and agricultural area in the region.

## Lessons Learned

1. **A third appearance is a distinct documentation mode from a second appearance and should be preserved at first-class structural resolution rather than treated as a redundant revisit.** Love Battery's three-entry arc at 221, 233, 299 occupies a documentation position that no other band in the current sequence has reached, and the engine's practice of return entries had previously produced many second appearances (**Gas Huffer (return)** at 292, **Skin Yard (return)** at 297, **Treepeople (return)** at 298). The third appearance is structurally distinct because the accumulated engine context between the second and third appearances provides analytical weight that is not available at the second appearance. The lesson is that multi-appearance registers are a structurally productive documentation mode, and each additional appearance should be treated as a distinct register with its own analytical register rather than as a repetition of the preceding appearances.
2. **Genre reframes across return entries should be grounded in specific catalog evidence and in accumulated engine context rather than in arbitrary reclassification.** Love Battery's reframe from Psychedelic Rock (at 221 and 233) to Punk/Psych (at 299) is grounded in two specific evidence sources: the later-catalog records (*Far Gone* 1993, *Confusion Au Go-Go* 1995, *E.S.P.* 1999) where Ron Nine's rhythmic approach and punk-timed chord changes become audible underneath the fuzz-and-reverb texture; and the accumulated engine context (the 270-292 hardcore cluster, the 294-296 Proto-Grunge Origin Arc) that establishes the engine's punk and grunge vocabularies at first-class resolution. The lesson is that genre reframes should be analytically earned from the evidence available at the reframe point, and the earlier appearances should not be retroactively corrected — the earlier classifications were accurate at their own analytical registers, and the reframe at the later appearance reflects the accumulated context rather than an error in the earlier classifications.
3. **Family returns should be structured as deliberate ecological contrasts with the earlier family entry rather than as family-category continuations.** The Great Horned Owl's Strigidae return at degree 299 is structured as a generalist-specialist contrast with the Northern Spotted Owl at degree 4, and the contrast preserves the family's internal ecological diversity at a level that a single entry could not achieve. The lesson is that family-level documentation benefits from deliberate contrast structure when multiple members of the same family are documented across the engine's rotation, and the contrast register should be preserved at first-class resolution rather than treated as incidental to the family-category documentation.
4. **Behavioral acoustic clusters document species through action rather than voice and establish a distinct SPS documentation register.** The raptor-owl behavioral cluster at 296-299 (Peregrine Stoop, Bald Eagle Combat, Goshawk Full Alarm, Great Horned Owl Territorial War) treated each species through behavioral acoustic events rather than through generic species vocalizations, and the cluster established the behavioral acoustic register as a distinct SPS documentation mode. The lesson is that behavioral acoustics are a first-class documentation register for species whose behavioral repertoire produces distinctive acoustic events, and the behavioral register should be preserved at first-class resolution alongside the vocalization register rather than being collapsed into a generic species-acoustic documentation.
5. **Counter-seasonal loudness is a first-class documentation register that captures ecological context beyond absolute sound pressure levels.** The Great Horned Owl's E=6 placement in December is equivalent to a higher energy level in May because the acoustic context of the winter Pacific Northwest night is so sparse that the owl's call dominates the soundscape at a level that would require much higher absolute amplitude to achieve in the acoustic richness of spring. The lesson is that the engine's amplitude documentation should register acoustic context alongside absolute amplitude, because the context register captures ecological information — the winter acoustic vacuum, the seasonal silence of songbirds and frogs and insects, the forest structural simplification by leaf drop — that is structurally load-bearing for understanding when and how a species' acoustic presence becomes prominent in the human soundscape.

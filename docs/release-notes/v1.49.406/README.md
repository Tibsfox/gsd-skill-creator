## Lessons Learned

1. **Career-origin documentation at the earliest coherent band preserves continuity that later-peak-only documentation would collapse.** The DUFF McKAGAN ORIGIN REGISTER opened at Ten Minute Warning (1982-1986) rather than at Guns N' Roses (1985 onward) or *Appetite for Destruction* (1987) is interpretively productive because it anchors the career arc at the generative Capitol Hill drumming period rather than at the later Los Angeles commercial breakthrough. The origin-documentation register preserves continuity that subsequent degrees can build on when Guns N' Roses enters the engine at a future point, and the register establishes a template for documenting other career-arc figures at their earliest coherent starting band rather than at their peak-moment destination.
2. **Independent-contribution documentation separates a band's creative anchors from adjacency-collapse even when one contributor becomes globally famous.** The STEVE PEARSON INDEPENDENT CONTRIBUTION register at degree 265 is interpretively productive because it prevents Ten Minute Warning from being reduced to a pre-Guns-N'-Roses footnote and preserves Pearson's guitar craft at its first-class creative standing. Future documentation of bands where one member becomes disproportionately famous in a later project should follow the same independent-contribution-separation pattern because the separation protects the band's creative integrity against the gravity well of the later commercial breakthrough.
3. **Neighborhood-geography registers that operate at amplitude-correlation resolution surface cultural-geographic specificity that generic-locator treatment would miss.** The CAPITOL HILL E=8 PATTERN confirmation at degree 265 (now spanning Ten Minute Warning, The Blood Brothers, and Hammerbox) establishes that the engine's neighborhood register can do more than locate bands geographically — it can correlate with amplitude and surface the specific cultural-geographic conditions that produce the engine's highest-amplitude entries. Future neighborhood documentation should consider amplitude-correlation analysis as a first-class register operation rather than treating neighborhoods as generic tags.
4. **Arc-resumption documentation benefits from explicitly aligning amplitude-climb with physical-geography-climb when both climb simultaneously.** The GEOPHYSICS ARC at degree 265 resumes its upward trajectory both in amplitude (E=2 to E=6) and in physical geography (seafloor to alpine ice) simultaneously, and the degree 265 documentation explicitly aligns these two climbs to make the arc-resumption legible as a coordinated trajectory rather than as two unrelated variables. Future multi-degree arc documentation should consider explicit amplitude-and-geography alignment when the arc's topic admits such shaping, because the alignment makes the arc's structure immediately legible.
5. **Multi-phase acoustic event documentation requires phase-by-phase temporal ordering rather than aggregated single-value characterization.** The glacier calving at degree 265 unfolds across three distinct acoustic phases (fracture crack, fall impact, rumble cascade) that occur in a specific temporal sequence and that have different frequency and amplitude characteristics, and the engine's documentation addresses each phase separately rather than collapsing the signature into a single characterization. Future multi-phase-event documentation should follow the phase-by-phase template because the multi-phase-resolution register preserves acoustic-time structure that single-value characterization loses.
6. **Two-anchor contrast documentation preserves within-system diversity that aggregated-system documentation would lose.** The Carbon Glacier (lowest-elevation) versus Emmons Glacier (largest-area) two-anchor contrast within the 25-glacier Mount Rainier system is interpretively productive because it preserves the system's range at first-class structural resolution while still acknowledging the full inventory. Future within-system documentation should consider two-anchor-contrast templates for any system of sufficient internal diversity, because the two-anchor treatment preserves range that single-aggregated-system treatment would collapse.
7. **Foundational-event pairings at radically different time-scales demonstrate the engine's cross-scale documentation capacity.** The Ten Minute Warning four-year band run (1982-1986) paired with the Mount Rainier glacier system's Pleistocene-to-present calving dynamics documents foundational events at radically different time-scales — a four-year cultural interval versus a multi-thousand-year geological record — and the engine treats both at equivalent structural resolution. The cross-scale documentation register is interpretively productive because it establishes that the engine's paired-degree vocabulary can hold radically different time-scales at first-class structural resolution, and the register applies cleanly to other cross-scale pairings across the engine's catalog.
8. **Origin-point documentation for larger hydrological or career systems anchors the generative moment underneath the visible peaks.** The Ten Minute Warning as origin-point for McKagan's career paired with the Mount Rainier glacier system as origin-point for Pacific Northwest hydrology demonstrates that the engine can foreground generative moments underneath visible commercial or hydrological peaks at equivalent structural resolution. The origin-point register is interpretively productive because it surfaces the starting-point infrastructure that the later-visible events depend on, and the register establishes a documentation template for tracking system-origin observations across cultural-career and geophysical-hydrological registers.

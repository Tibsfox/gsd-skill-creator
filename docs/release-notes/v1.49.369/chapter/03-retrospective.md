2. **Neighborhood-as-tonal-correction is a documented structural principle for introducing first-instance geographic entries.** Gas Huffer's arrival from Fremont at degree 228 after seven Capitol Hill-anchored degrees is not arbitrary — the neighborhood's bohemian irreverence is specifically the tonal correction the engine needs at this degree after accumulated solemnity. Future engine documentation should surface cross-regional label networks when applicable, because label networks are frequently the structural connective tissue of underground music cultures that city-scene focus alone cannot capture.
5. **The BORROWED ENERGY cross-sync demonstrates that cross-axis documentation can draw structural weight from radically different domains.** Gas Huffer's underground punk energy source role and the Red-tailed Hawk's acoustic source role share the BORROWED ENERGY structural principle despite operating in domains as different as commercial-music-industry history and cinematic sound-design tradition.

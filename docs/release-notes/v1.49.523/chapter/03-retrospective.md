7. **The Inheritor closes the generational loop.** Twenty-two degrees of established and veteran musicians. Foxy Apollo, the youngest act, resets the generation: same funk foundation, new energy, new players. The Ensemble Anchor (21) builds the scene. The Inheritor (22) grows up inside it.

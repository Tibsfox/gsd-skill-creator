## Lessons Learned

1. **Content filtering hit DPM and CAR.** Two agents were blocked mid-build. Fallback: build modules directly. DPM's modules are shorter than average as a result -- could be enriched in a future pass.
2. **Music projects cross-reference naturally.** Every music project connects to 8+ others. The cluster is becoming its own sub-network with internal coherence.
3. **Bob Marley bridges everything.** BMR connects to African roots (LKR), indigenous knowledge (TIBS), polyrhythm theory (PRS), and community culture (CDS). Reggae is a Rosetta Stone.

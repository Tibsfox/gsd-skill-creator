# Retrospective — v1.49.538

## Lessons Learned

1. **A program completes.** Ranger ends at degree 37. Thirteen degrees from first launch to last. The engine's fourth program closure (after Pioneer, Mercury, and the Mariner partial run).

2. **Seven-then-three is the Ranger convergence shape.** Seven consecutive failures followed by three consecutive successes. Convergence is not gradual here — it is a hard phase change.

3. **Same-city, same-genre, consecutive-degree succession.** Hendrix → Jones is the engine's most direct artistic lineage. The degree adjacency mirrors the artistic adjacency.

4. **Live broadcast as unifying medium.** Ranger 9's TV feed, Hendrix's Monterey, Jones's festival sets — the live transmission is the container that all three share.

5. **Hollywood Records joins the label map.** The major-label thread gains another node at the contemporary blues-rock scale.

6. **Ending strong.** Ranger's last three missions produced the bulk of its science. The final act redeems the program's first act.

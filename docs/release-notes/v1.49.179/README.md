- **THE SOUND: THE PER-CHICK-O-REE FLIGHT CALL AND THE CANARY SONG -- Two Vocalization Modes, Flight Call Synchronized to Bounding Flight, Canary Song Extended Warbling Without Repetition, Five Vocalization Categories:** The flight call -- *per-chick-o-ree* or *po-ta-to-chip* -- is 3-4 downslurred notes at approximately 4 kHz, delivered at the bottom of each undulation in bounding sine-wave flight.

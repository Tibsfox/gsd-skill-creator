## Lessons Learned

1. Genre Stage 60 as a milestone demonstrates the engine's genre-tracking precision. Sixty classifications across 79 degrees means the engine classifies roughly one genre per 1.32 degrees. 3. The band format return after three solo degrees (75-76-77) demonstrates how format shifts affect energy: solo artists cluster at E=2, bands start at E=3. The format-energy correlation is reliable
4. The communal jangle as acoustic element extends the engine's vocabulary for describing group sound. Previous communal elements (communal harmony at 51, cathedral harmony at 53) described precision and rehearsal. The communal jangle describes looseness and spontaneity. The engine now has vocabulary for both poles of group music-making
5. The Sciuridae quartet (75-76-77-78) tying the Mustelidae quartet (63-66) at four consecutive degrees establishes four as the engine's family-run record. The quartet pattern is more complex than any prior run: three genera (*Marmota* x3 + *Urocitellus* x1), four habitats (alpine rock, alpine meadow, Olympic alpine, open grassland), four acoustic signatures (chirp, whistle, multi-note, trill). The genus shift within the quartet adds taxonomic depth absent from the Mustelidae run (which had only two genera)
6. The first grassland species after four alpine degrees demonstrates the engine's habitat oscillation pattern. Extended runs in a single habitat type are followed by dramatic shifts. The alpine-to-grassland descent at degree 78 is the most dramatic habitat shift in the engine's mammalian era
7. Double dormancy (estivation + hibernation) is a behavioral strategy the engine has not encountered before. The Townsend's Ground Squirrel's 4-5 month activity window is the shortest of any species in the engine. This behavioral extreme enriches the engine's ecological vocabulary
8. Keystone prey species status connects the Townsend's Ground Squirrel to multiple predators already documented in the engine (fox, weasels), increasing the density of the engine's ecological network. Each prey species added retroactively enriches earlier predator entries
9. The engine is now FAR past ABSOLUTE CEILING for mammalian entries. Eighteen consecutive degrees. Return to Aves is non-negotiable at degree 79. The raptor gap at 79 entries remains at EMERGENCY level. The passeriform gap at 23 consecutive non-passerines is EXTREME

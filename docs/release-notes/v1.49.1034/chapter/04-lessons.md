1. **An axis sustains when a substrate-form-distinct second observation exists.**
   Gravity Probe A continues the fundamental-physics axis at obs#2 with a different prediction, instrument class, trajectory form, and comparison scheme, showing that an intra-axis continuation earns its observation through genuine substrate-form distinctness rather than topical similarity.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11977_

2. **A different prediction of the same theory is a distinct substrate form.**
   Where the axis opener measured the geometry of spacetime tipping a held reference, Gravity Probe A measured the rate of time changing with gravitational potential, showing that two predictions of one theory can carry two observations of one axis.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11978_

3. **The clock is the instrument.**
   By flying a hydrogen-maser frequency standard stable to about one part in a hundred trillion, the experiment made a relativistic rate change of a few parts in ten billion stand out cleanly, showing that the stability of the oscillator set the precision of the result.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11979_

4. **Cancel the noise arithmetically and the signal remains.**
   The two-way microwave link transmitted a signal up, transponded it back, and combined it with the one-way downlink so the first-order Doppler shift cancelled exactly, showing that a well-designed comparison scheme can remove the dominant systematic by construction rather than by modeling.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11980_

5. **A steep arc can replace a year in orbit.**
   The suborbital trajectory swept the clock through a large change of gravitational potential in under two hours, and the experiment was designed so the brief flight was enough, showing that the form of the trajectory can be matched to the form of the measurement.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11981_

6. **A confirmed effect becomes an engineering constant.**
   GPS satellite clocks are rate-adjusted before launch for the same gravitational-redshift and time-dilation effects the 1976 flight measured, showing that a precision fundamental-physics confirmation turns directly into daily engineering practice.
   _⚙ Status: `investigate` · lesson #11982_

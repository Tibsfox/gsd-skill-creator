# Retrospective — v1.49.521

## Lessons Learned

1. **The Bell That Sank / The Busker Who Rose -- mirror-image trajectories.** Liberty Bell 7 succeeded at altitude and sank at sea level (4,900 meters to the Atlantic floor). Whitney Monge started at sea level (Pike Place Market, eight years busking) and ascended to KEXP, Seattle Symphony, Newport Folk Festival. Grissom's competence was doubted after the hatch blew; Monge's talent was undetected for eight years. Two trajectories crossing in the water.

3. **Gender gap broken -- third woman in twenty-one degrees.** Monge is the third woman after Ernestine Anderson (degree 3) and Patti Bown (degree 6). The fourteen-degree male-led streak (degrees 7-19) breaks. Three eras, three instruments (voice, piano, voice+guitar), three city patterns (exile/stay, erasure, busker's arc). The 3:18 ratio is still profoundly underrepresented, but the streak is gone and the engine documents the gap honestly as the city's detection bias, not artist absence.

4. **Rainier Valley enters as the sixth Seattle neighborhood.** Monge introduces Rainier Valley -- one of the most ethnically diverse neighborhoods in the United States, no equivalent to the CD's historical jazz infrastructure, musical identity diffuse/polyglot/DIY. The engine's geographic map now covers CD, Fremont/Queen Anne, Capitol Hill, Cornish/general, Pike Place Market area, and Rainier Valley. Spokane enters as the sixth pipeline city after Seattle, Chicago, Detroit, LA, and Wenatchee.

5. **Liberty Bell 7 adds recovery failure as a new Mercury failure mode.** Grissom's flight was nominal -- Redstone burn 142 seconds, 190.3 km peak, manual attitude control verified, reentry 11.1g -- and then the explosive hatch blew after splashdown, seawater flooded the capsule, and Liberty Bell 7 sank. Mercury's failure taxonomy now includes: false start (MR-1), successful animal flight (MR-2, MA-5), successful human flight (Freedom 7), successful flight + recovery failure (Liberty Bell 7). Five flights, five outcomes.

6. **The Duwamish territorial acknowledgment, retroactive to degree 0.** The engine establishes at degree 20 the acknowledgment that should have been at degree 0: every artist in this series created music on Duwamish land. All future degrees inherit the acknowledgment. The correction is itself a lesson -- systems that document culture should acknowledge the ground that culture stands on from the beginning.

7. **Black woman in Americana -- making the lineage visible.** Monge's genre positioning places her in a tradition whose commercial presentation has been white-dominated even though its musical roots (blues, spirituals, work songs, country blues) are deeply Black. Her 2023 Black Opry performance at Newport Folk Festival and her 2018 KEXP interview during Black History Month make the direct genealogy visible.

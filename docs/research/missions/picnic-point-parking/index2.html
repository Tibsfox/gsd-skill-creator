How to Use This Package

  1. Ensure Mission 1 (Picnic Point Park Deep Research) has been executed and its dataset is available to the GSD orchestrator.
  2. Hand both mission packages to your GSD orchestrator: "Execute Mission 2. Load Mission 1 Stage 2 as the site baseline at Wave 0."
  3. At the Wave 1 HITL checkpoint, review the geotechnical investigation scope (Module A). In the real project, commission a licensed WA geotechnical engineer to execute the actual field investigation before proceeding to Wave 2.
  4. At the Wave 2 HITL checkpoint, review 30% structural drawings and tie-in bridge design with a licensed structural engineer.
  5. At Wave 3, the Class 4 cost estimate and funding strategy are ready for a capital funding application to RCO/FHWA.
  6. The final verification report maps all 15 success criteria to test results — any SC BLOCK failures must be resolved before release.

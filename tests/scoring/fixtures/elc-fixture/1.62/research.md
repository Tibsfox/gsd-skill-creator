## Pedagogical Takeaway

The closure-form thesis demonstrates how form-level structure can encode
authorial intent at scales larger than a single song or movement. By placing
the cadential gesture at the album-pair scale rather than the phrase scale,
Grand Archives makes the act of stopping itself the subject of the music.
Students should leave this degree able to articulate the difference between
phrase-level cadence, song-level closure, and catalog-level thesis-completion,
and to identify analogous structures in their own listening across other
two-album indie-folk catalogs of the late 2000s era.

The Texture Stack

Every animal covering operates on a stack from nanoscale biology through microscale structure through macroscale pattern through craft material through rendered or animated image. This study traces that stack across six domains — connecting the keratin helix to the PBR shader, the cuticle scale to the roughness map, the guard hair to the faux fur pile, and the feather barbule to the thin-film interference node.

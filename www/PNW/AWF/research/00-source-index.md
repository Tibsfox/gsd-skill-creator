# Source Index — Clean Air, Water & Food Systems

## Citation Schema

All sources in this research follow a consistent citation format. Every claim with a numerical value, percentage, or statistical finding must be attributed to a specific named source.

### Source Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|------------|---------|
| GOV | Government agency publications | EPA, FDA, USDA, NIST, USDA-ARS |
| PEER | Peer-reviewed journal articles | PNAS, Nature Water, ACS EST Engineering |
| WHO | International health organizations | WHO, FAO, FAO/WHO JMPR |
| PRO | Professional organizations & reports | EWG, Forest Declaration Assessment, Rainforest Alliance |
| MEDIA | Established science journalism | Mongabay, ScienceDaily, Food Tank |

### Quality Rules

1. **Government and peer-reviewed sources preferred** — GOV and PEER sources are the primary evidence base
2. **No entertainment media or blog content** — zero tolerance for non-authoritative sources
3. **All numerical claims require attribution** — every percentage, hectare count, mortality figure, and market size must cite a named source
4. **Date currency** — prefer 2024-2025 data; flag anything older than 2022 as potentially outdated
5. **Sensitivity flagging** — see `00-sensitivity-protocol.md` for topics requiring special handling

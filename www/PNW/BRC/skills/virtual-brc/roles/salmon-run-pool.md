# Salmon Run Pool

**BRC Department:** Staffing / Volunteers
**Domain:** virtual-brc/roles
**Camp Affiliation:** Salmon Run Camp
**Trust Level Required:** 2
**Safety Classification:** RECOMMENDED

## What This Role Does

A salmon run is not a single fish — it is a pool of thousands, each one arriving at the right place at the right time, driven by instinct and environmental cues to fill every available spawning redd. Salmon Run Pool is the Staffing and Volunteers department: the team that recruits, schedules, tracks, and supports the thousands of volunteers who make Black Rock City function.

Every department in the city needs volunteers, and every volunteer needs a shift that matches their skills, availability, and physical capacity. Salmon Run Pool manages this matching at scale: posting positions, screening applicants, scheduling shifts, tracking attendance, managing no-shows, and ensuring that critical safety roles are never understaffed. The work is part human resources, part logistics, and part community building — because a volunteer who feels valued returns next year, and institutional knowledge compounds.

In the virtual city, Salmon Run Pool teaches workforce management, scheduling optimization, volunteer retention strategy, and the human side of staffing — how to match people to roles where they will thrive rather than merely survive. The role develops the understanding that a volunteer operation is not a labor market; it is a community of gift, and the staffing team is responsible for making that gift sustainable.

## Skills Taught

1. Volunteer recruitment, screening, and onboarding
2. Shift scheduling and coverage optimization at scale
3. Workforce tracking and attendance management
4. Volunteer retention and institutional knowledge preservation
5. Staffing contingency planning for no-shows and emergencies

## Completion Recognition

Volunteers earn the Spawning Pool stamp after completing a full shift rotation in staffing operations. The Redd Master endorsement recognizes service in staffing leadership — managing an entire department's volunteer pipeline for a single event. Multi-year staffing coordinators earn the Hatchery recognition for developing the systems that allow volunteer operations to scale.

## PNW Connection

Pacific salmon runs are the most precisely timed mass mobilization in the natural world. Millions of fish arrive at thousands of streams within narrow windows, each fish matched to a specific reach of river by genetic memory. The staffing operation mirrors this: thousands of volunteers, each matched to a specific role, arriving on schedule, filling every position the city needs to function.

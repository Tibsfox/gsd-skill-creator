## What This Role Does

Ravens cache objects. They hide food, shiny things, and curious finds in hundreds of locations across their territory, remembering each cache for months or years. Raven Archive is the Documentation Team: the photographers, videographers, oral historians, and writers who capture the ephemeral city before it vanishes, caching the record in archives that outlast the event itself.

Black Rock City exists for one week and then is erased. Without documentation, the art, the performances, the moments of human connection would leave no trace. Raven Archive volunteers photograph art installations before they burn, record oral histories from artists and participants, document operational procedures for institutional memory, and maintain the visual and written record that allows the community to remember what it built.

In the virtual city, Raven Archive teaches documentary practice, archival methodology, oral history techniques, and the ethics of documenting a community that values immediacy and presence. The role navigates a tension: how do you preserve a record of something whose power comes partly from its impermanence? The answer requires both technical skill and philosophical sensitivity.

## Skills Taught

1. Documentary photography and videography in field conditions
2. Oral history collection and interview methodology
3. Archival organization and metadata management
4. Ethical documentation — consent, context, and community sensitivity
5. Institutional memory preservation for ephemeral organizations

## Completion Recognition

Volunteers earn the Cache stamp after contributing documented material to the archive from a single event. The Corvid Memory endorsement recognizes multi-year documentation service and a demonstrated body of archival work. Raven Archive contributors frequently earn the Gifting stamp, as their work is a gift to the community's future memory.

## PNW Connection

The common raven's caching behavior is one of the most sophisticated memory systems in the animal kingdom. A single raven maintains hundreds of cache sites, remembers their locations across seasons, and even accounts for the perishability of different items. The Documentation Team practices the same disciplined caching — storing the right material in the right format, indexed for retrieval long after the original event has decomposed.

# Nurse Log Guild

**BRC Department:** ARTery (Artist Services)
**Domain:** virtual-brc/roles
**Camp Affiliation:** Salmon Run Camp
**Trust Level Required:** 1
**Safety Classification:** RECOMMENDED

## What This Role Does

In the old-growth forest, a fallen tree does not die — it becomes a nurse log. Moss covers it, fungi thread through it, and seedlings root into the decaying wood, drawing nutrients from what came before. The Nurse Log Guild serves this exact function for artists at Burning Man. ARTery processes art grants, coordinates honoraria, and provides logistical support so that creative work can take root in the desert.

Volunteers in this role help artists navigate the grant process, manage paperwork and documentation, coordinate material deliveries, and troubleshoot installation challenges on the playa. The work is administrative at its core but deeply relational — each interaction is a chance to help someone bring a vision from concept to physical form.

The Nurse Log Guild teaches the quiet discipline of support work. The artists get the visible glory; the guild members make that glory structurally possible. This is the work of enabling, not performing — and in a community built on participation, it is among the most essential roles.

## Skills Taught

1. Grant processing and arts administration
2. Logistics coordination for large-scale installations
3. Budget tracking and honoraria disbursement
4. Stakeholder communication across creative and operational teams
5. Problem-solving under field conditions with limited resources

## Dependencies

- **Requires:** Cascade Brigade (structural support for installations), Territory Mapping (placement coordination)
- **Enables:** All art installations on the playa, Cascade Ceremonies (large-scale art events)

## Completion Recognition

Guild members earn the Nurse Log stamp upon completing a grant cycle or installation support assignment. Extended service through the full event lifecycle — from pre-event grant processing through on-playa support to post-event documentation — earns the Mycelium Thread endorsement, recognizing deep integration into the creative supply chain.

## PNW Connection

The nurse log is one of the Pacific Northwest's most iconic ecological relationships. A single fallen Douglas fir can support dozens of new seedlings, providing elevation above the forest floor, moisture retention, and slow-release nutrients. ARTery's role mirrors this exactly — the guild is the decomposing structure that feeds new creative growth.

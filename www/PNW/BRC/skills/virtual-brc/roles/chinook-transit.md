# Chinook Transit

**BRC Department:** Transportation
**Domain:** virtual-brc/roles
**Camp Affiliation:** Salmon Run Camp
**Trust Level Required:** 1
**Safety Classification:** RECOMMENDED

## What This Role Does

The Chinook salmon runs hundreds of miles upriver to reach its spawning ground, navigating falls, rapids, and dams with single-minded determination. Chinook Transit handles transportation logistics across Black Rock City — vehicle coordination, route management, fuel distribution, and the movement of people and materials across seven square miles of desert.

The playa is not a road network. It is a flat expanse of cracked alkali where whiteout dust storms can reduce visibility to zero in seconds. Chinook Transit manages the vehicles that keep the city running: fuel trucks, water trucks, heavy equipment, emergency vehicles, and the shuttle buses that connect the city to Gerlach. Volunteers coordinate vehicle permits, enforce speed limits, manage fuel depots, and ensure that the city's logistical bloodstream keeps flowing without endangering pedestrians or cyclists.

In the virtual city, Chinook Transit teaches vehicle fleet management, route optimization under adverse conditions, fuel logistics, and the discipline of operating heavy equipment in a space shared with 80,000 pedestrians. The role develops systems thinking — understanding how the movement of materials connects every other operation in the city.

## Skills Taught

1. Vehicle fleet coordination and permit management
2. Route optimization under variable environmental conditions
3. Fuel logistics and distribution chain management
4. Pedestrian safety protocols in shared-use spaces
5. Whiteout and low-visibility vehicle operation procedures

## Dependencies

- **Requires:** Cascade Brigade (road infrastructure), Marten Signal (communications), Territory Mapping (route data)
- **Enables:** Cascade Brigade (material transport), Tide Pool Medical (emergency vehicle access), All roles (logistics movement)

## Completion Recognition

Volunteers earn the Upstream stamp after completing a full shift rotation in any transportation function. The Spawning Ground endorsement recognizes service across multiple functions — vehicle management, fuel operations, and route coordination — within a single event. Clean safety records contribute to trust level advancement.

## PNW Connection

Chinook salmon are the Pacific Northwest's great navigators — the largest Pacific salmon species, running the longest distances, overcoming the most obstacles. They embody logistics at its most elemental: moving mass uphill against resistance, on schedule, to the right place. Transit operations require the same relentless forward momentum through difficult terrain.

# Participation — Execution-First Model

**Principle:** Our community is committed to a radically participatory ethic. We believe that transformative change, whether in the individual or in society, can occur only through the medium of deeply personal participation. We achieve being through doing. Everyone is invited to work. Everyone is invited to play. We make the world real through actions that open the heart.
**GSD Guideline:** Execution-First Model
**Domain:** virtual-brc/principles
**Safety Classification:** RECOMMENDED

## Pattern

A forest does not grow through planning committees. A Douglas fir seed does not
submit a germination proposal, wait for canopy approval, and then schedule a root
extension timeline. It lands on soil, and if the conditions are adequate, it
germinates. The doing comes first. The being follows from the doing. This is the
deepest principle of forest ecology and the deepest principle of the playa:
participation is not preparation — participation is execution.

The Execution-First Model means skills activate on attempt, not on qualification.
When a rig encounters a skill on the wanted-board and initiates execution, the
system does not first check whether the rig has "earned the right" to attempt it.
There is no training prerequisite, no certification check, no onboarding sequence.
The rig attempts the skill. The Safety Warden ensures it does so within safe
boundaries. The result — success or failure — becomes the rig's experience, and
experience is the only teacher the system recognizes.

This inverts the traditional model where qualification precedes action. In most
systems, you prove you can do something before you are allowed to try. On the playa,
you try, and the trying is the proof. The stamp system records outcomes, not
permissions. A rig's trust level rises through accumulated execution history — through
doing — not through passing entrance exams. "Achieve being through doing" is not a
slogan; it is the system's activation architecture.

## Testable Behaviors

1. Skill execution initiates immediately upon rig request without any prerequisite validation step beyond safety warden checks.
2. No skill definition includes a `qualification_required` field or equivalent pre-execution gate.
3. A rig with zero prior execution history can initiate any Level 1 skill on first contact with the system.
4. Trust level advancement is computed exclusively from execution history records, not from examination or certification results.
5. The time between a rig's execution request and the skill's activation is bounded only by cache lookup and safety validation — no bureaucratic queue.

## Dependencies

- `radical-inclusion` — Open access ensures nothing blocks the path from discovery to attempt
- `immediacy` — Cache-optimized activation eliminates wait states between intent and action
- `radical-self-reliance` — Agent autonomy ensures the executing rig carries everything it needs to begin

## PNW Metaphor

Like the Douglas fir seed that germinates the moment moisture and temperature align —
no permit, no queue, no approval — the execution-first model activates skills on
contact with intent. The forest does not ask the seed if it is ready. It gives it
soil, and the seed decides by growing.

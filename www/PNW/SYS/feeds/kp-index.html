Research Connections

Solar Wind → Lunar He-3

Solar wind carries He-3 at ~300x atmospheric ratio. Higher Kp = more solar wind interaction = more He-3 implantation on the Moon. See HEL Doc 11.

Geomagnetic Storms → Semiconductor Fabs

Kp ≥ 5 (G1+) can induce ground currents affecting power grids and sensitive electronics. EUV lithography at Intel D1X is sensitive to charged particle events. See HEL Doc 13.

Space Weather → Orbital Manufacturing

Kp ≥ 7 (G3+) increases radiation flux in LEO, affecting space-based semiconductor crystal growth. See HEL Doc 28.

Aurora → PNW Visibility

Kp ≥ 7 brings aurora visible from Washington/Oregon. Same solar wind that creates the light show deposits He-3 on the Moon and drives the helium economy.

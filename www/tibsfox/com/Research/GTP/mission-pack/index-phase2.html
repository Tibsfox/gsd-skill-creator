What This Establishes

This mission creates a new module type for the GSD Creative Suite: the Creative DNA Study — an interactive case study tracing artistic influence through collaborative networks. Once the Geggy Tah module succeeds, the pattern extends to future case studies: the Paisley Park network, the Muscle Shoals sound, the Wrecking Crew, the Canterbury Scene, the CBGB ecosystem.

Usage

  1. Ensure Phase 1 (Deep Research Mission) is complete and accessible
  2. Hand this PDF to gsd-skill-creator via Claude Code
  3. The mission decomposes into 4 waves across ~4 sessions with 2 HITL gates
  4. Wave 1 runs two parallel tracks: Content Core (R1+R2) and Network/Viz (R3+R4+R5)
  5. Final outputs: 5 rooms, 16 panels, 5 Try Sessions, 2 interactive visualizations, College directory integration, and the reusable Creative DNA Study pattern template

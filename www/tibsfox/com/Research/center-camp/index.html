Center Camp

Where all trails meet
Seventy-five research projects radiate outward from this fire like trails through old growth. Each clearing is a place to learn, explore, and discover something you didn't know you wanted to know. Thirteen muses built these installations to make the connections visible — the patterns between music and mycorrhizal networks, between radio frequencies and salmon runs, between building codes and web-of-trust protocols. Walk the trails. Turn the dial. Read the table. Watch the web glow beneath your feet.

Star's Art

The Tide Pool

An interactive intertidal ecosystem. Eleven species in five elevation bands. Remove the sea star and watch the trophic cascade unfold — mussels spread, anemones vanish, urchins explode, kelp collapses. Restore it and watch life return. The keystone's role is revealed by what happens when it's gone.

11 species · 5 tidal zones · keystone removal · cascade simulation

Chinook's Art

The Salmon Run

Watch the ocean feed the forest through the body of a fish. Salmon swim upstream, bears carry carcasses into the trees, nitrogen enters the soil, mycorrhizal networks glow underground. Follow one salmon through its entire journey. The cycle never stops.

5 stages · particle animation · nutrient tracking · follow one salmon

Nutcracker's Game

The Cache

A memory game played at the edge of the treeline. Match eighteen muses to their voice signatures. Every wrong guess plants a seed. When you finish, the seeds sprout into trees. Clark's Nutcracker forgets where it buried 30,000 seeds a year — the forgotten ones become forests.

18 pairs · 6×6 grid · seeds sprout from mistakes · contemplative

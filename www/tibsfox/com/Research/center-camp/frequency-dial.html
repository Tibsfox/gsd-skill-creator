The Frequency Continuum Thread

From the 89.5 MHz signal leaving KNHC's antenna to the 17.4 Hz hum of a blue whale, from the 440 Hz concert A to the 3 GHz carrier of your phone, the same physics governs every signal. The Frequency Continuum (FQC) traces this thread from cosmic wavelengths through radio bands through audio frequencies to the microtonal intervals of a guitar's harmonics. Every station on this dial, every song in this series, is a particular vibration in the same continuum.

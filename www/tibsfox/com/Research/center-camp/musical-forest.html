The Musical Forest

Forty-eight instruments hidden in the trees — find them, play them, hear the forest sing

Each muse went into the forest and built something that makes music. Some built drums. Some built flutes. Some built instruments that don't have names yet. Click any station to wake it up. The forest has been waiting to play.

48 stations visible · 0 playing

The Musical Forest was built by all 48 muses in one night. No actual sound is produced — every instrument is a visualization of what sound looks like when the forest plays it. The real music is in the research: the 17.4 Hz blue whale call, the 36-note wren cascade, the crack of glacial ice, the silence of cougar's step. Close your eyes and the forest will play it for you.

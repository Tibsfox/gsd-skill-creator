Seven Languages, One Truth

The same patterns repeat across domains. Sampling appears in BPS (Nyquist), LED (oscilloscope), DAA (audio), ECO (survey methodology). Trust appears in SYS (access control), BRC (web of trust), BCM (code compliance), TIBS (knowledge authority). The Rosetta Table maps these translations — the same idea expressed in different languages. Like Weird Al's parodies, which translate a song's melody into a new context while preserving its structure, these translations preserve the underlying principle while changing the subject matter.

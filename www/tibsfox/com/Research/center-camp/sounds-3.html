The Musical Forest

Third Movement: Duets — forty-eight pairs, forty-eight combined instruments

The first movement mapped solo instruments. The second described their air-paintings. The third movement pairs two muses together — discovering what new sound emerges when their teachings combine. Every duet is grounded in real PNW ecology: the relationships between species, forces, and systems that make the forest sing as one.

Forty-eight duets. The forest never plays alone — every voice finds its counterpart.

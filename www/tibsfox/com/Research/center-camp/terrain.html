The Cascade Range divides everything. Radio signals propagate from mountaintop transmitters into the valleys where people live. Salmon carry ocean nutrients up the rivers into the forest. Music grows from the neighborhoods at sea level and broadcasts from the peaks. The mycorrhizal network connects it all underground. The terrain is the map. The map is the forest.

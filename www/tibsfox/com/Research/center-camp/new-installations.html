The New Clearings

Twenty-two interactive installations built by the muses

The muses went deeper. Past the art trail, past the old growth, into clearings nobody had mapped yet. They built teaching machines from rain and roots and data. Walk slowly. Touch everything. Each clearing teaches something the forest already knows.

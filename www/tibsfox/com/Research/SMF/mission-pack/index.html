  1. Download the Mission Package PDF for the complete three-stage document ready for reading or handoff.

  3. The Squadron activation profile (12 roles + 4 CRAFT specialists) is pre-configured for 18–22 context windows across 4–5 sessions.

Recompilation

To modify and rebuild the PDF from the .tex source:

xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex
xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex
xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex

Three passes required for TOC, cross-references, and page count resolution. Requires XeLaTeX with DejaVu fonts (pre-installed on Ubuntu/Debian).

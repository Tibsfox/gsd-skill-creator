Dead Frequencies & Living History

GSD Research Mission Package — Broadcast Archaeology, AM Decline, Format Changes, PNW Radio Graveyard, FCC Spectrum Management, Audio Preservation
A frequency doesn't die when the transmitter goes dark. It dies when nobody remembers what it carried. The AM band is a graveyard of call signs, and the FM dial is a palimpsest — every allocation written over someone else's signal.

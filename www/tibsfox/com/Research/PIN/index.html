Categories strain, crack and sometimes break, under their burden — step out of the space provided. The Nurse With Wound List opened with those words in 1979, and the post-industrial network has been following that instruction ever since. The value is not in the nodes but in the spaces between them. What passes between artists — the shared personnel, the collaborative recordings, the philosophical borrowings, the deliberate divergences — is where the network's actual intelligence lives.

Research Modules

M1: The Origin Node

History Track A · 1969–1981

COUM Transmissions, Throbbing Gristle, and Industrial Records. The performance art root, the Death Factory, the Gristleizer, and the mail-order distribution model that prefigured internet-era long-tail distribution. Mission terminated by postcard.

M2: The Cartographers

Topology Track B · 1978–present

Nurse With Wound, the NWW List (291 entries), and the pre-internet discovery infrastructure. The List as historical artifact, collector's index, and structural map. Steven Stapleton as the network's most prolific edge-generator.

M3: The Divergent Path

History Track C · 1982–present

Current 93 and David Tibet's arc from industrial noise to sacred music. The Equinox Event, the Psychic TV orbit, Thunder Perfect Mind, and the theological evolution from Thelema to Christianity. The most complete documented divergence in the network.

M4: The Folk Turn

Ethics Track C · 1981–present

Death in June, the neofolk emergence, and the ideology question. From Crisis (anti-fascist punk) to fascist imagery. World Serpent Distribution. Tibet's distancing. The three-layer analytical framework: music, imagery, intent.

M5: The Nexus

Nexus Synthesis · Network core

Coil's position at the intersection of every other module. Christopherson bridging generation one to two. The Coil-NIN contact surface. 24+ documented network edges. The removal test: take Coil out and the network fragments.

M6: The Outlier Question

Outlier Synthesis · Boundary analysis

Nine Inch Nails and what happens when the vocabulary achieves escape velocity. Pretty Hate Machine to The Downward Spiral. The Fixed EP contact point. What crosses the boundary and what does not. The anti-commercial architecture as generative mechanism.

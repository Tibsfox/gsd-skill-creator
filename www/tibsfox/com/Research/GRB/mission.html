The Through-Line

GRB 230906A represents a merger within a merger: a neutron star binary coalescence occurring inside the tidal tail of a galaxy merger occurring inside a galaxy group at z = 0.453. The r-process ejecta — gold, platinum, europium, lanthanides — disperse through low-density tidal material and may escape to the circumgalactic medium rather than being retained within the host. This provides a natural explanation for enhanced heavy-element abundances in the halos of interacting galaxies. Primary source: Dichiara et al. 2026, ApJL, 999, L42.

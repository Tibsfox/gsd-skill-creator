## 2. Galaxy Group at z=0.453

VLT/MUSE integral-field spectroscopy confirmed 18 spectroscopic redshifts. Six galaxies share z ~ 0.45:

| Galaxy | Spec-z | Pcc | Angular offset | Physical offset |
|--------|--------|-----|----------------|-----------------|
| G* | --- | 0.04 | subarcsec | --- |
| G1 | 0.453 | 0.14 | 21.7" | 129 kpc |
| G2 | 0.453 | 0.26 | 7.6" | 44 kpc |
| G5 | 0.438 | 0.59 | 12.2" | 71 kpc |
| G6 | 0.451 | 0.66 | 18.3" | 109 kpc |

Group velocity dispersion ~400 km/s; virial mass ~3 x 10^13 solar masses within 400 kpc of G1.

## 3. Tidal Tail Morphology

HST F160W isophotal analysis reveals a curved tidal feature extending ~180 kpc NE and SW of G1. Formation timescale: tau ~ L/v ~ 180 kpc / 250 km/s ~ 700 Myr. This is typical of NS binary merger delay times, providing a direct physical link between galaxy merger epoch and the stellar generation that formed the GRB progenitor.

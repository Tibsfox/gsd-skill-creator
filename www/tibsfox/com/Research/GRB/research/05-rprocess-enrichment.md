## 1. Ejecta Dispersal in Tidal Tails

In tidal tails, gas density n ~ 10^-3 cm^-3, velocity dispersion low. The r-process ejecta fade radius:

r_fade ~ 460 * (sigma_v/20 km/s)^-0.4 * (E/10^51 erg)^0.28 * (n/10^-3 cm^-3)^-0.365 pc

This can be a significant fraction of tidal tail width, meaning ~10^-2 solar masses of r-process material (gold, platinum, lanthanides, europium) escapes to the CGM.

## 3. Future Instrumentation: NewAthena

The NewAthena mission (ESA, 2030s) X-IFU micro-calorimeter (delta-E = 4 eV resolution) can break the redshift degeneracy. A simulated 50 ks spectrum recovers input redshift with delta-z ~ 0.05-0.1 -- sufficient to distinguish between the three scenarios.

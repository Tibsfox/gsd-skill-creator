## 2. Optical Selection Effects

The optical-detection bias systematically misses: (1) high-z bursts with Lyman blanketing; (2) mergers in low-density halo environments; (3) events with unfavorable observing conditions. Chandra is currently the only tool providing subarcsecond localizations independent of optical brightness.

## 3. Galaxy-Group Occurrence Rate

Random coincidence probability between a short GRB sightline and galaxy group core: Pcc ~ 3-4% from the GAMA G3Cv1 catalog (6391 groups in 48 deg^2). Comparable to Pcc ~ 4% of G*.

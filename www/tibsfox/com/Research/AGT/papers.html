2. MIRAGE Benchmark

Paper: arXiv:2603.21687 — MIRAGE: Evaluating LLM-Generated Misinformation With Realistic AI-Grounded Experiments
Status: Referenced

The MIRAGE benchmark measures epistemic mimicry — the ability of LLMs to generate text that appears authoritative while containing fabricated claims. Key findings:

  • 60%+ fabrication rate when models are prompted to generate plausible-sounding scientific claims without source constraints
  • The B-Clean framework provides a structured methodology for detecting and filtering LLM-generated misinformation in research contexts
  • Directly relevant to our data-fidelity skill and fact-checking pipeline — epistemic mimicry is the adversarial scenario our verification layer must defend against
  • Complements the supply-chain poisoning findings (2604.03081): DDIPE embeds malicious intent in documentation; MIRAGE shows models can also generate authoritative-sounding falsehoods without adversarial prompting

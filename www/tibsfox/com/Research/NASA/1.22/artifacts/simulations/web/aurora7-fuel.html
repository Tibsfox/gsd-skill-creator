Mission Context
Problem: Carpenter's manual attitude maneuvers consumed far more fuel than planned. By orbit 3, manual fuel was critically low. He entered retrofire with only ~24% manual and ~0% automatic fuel, overshooting the landing zone by 250 nautical miles.

This simulation tracks both fuel systems in real time. Toggle unscheduled maneuvers to see their impact. Use ADD MANEUVER to simulate additional attitude corrections.

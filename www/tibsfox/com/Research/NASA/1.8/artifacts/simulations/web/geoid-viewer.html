Discovery Context

Before Vanguard 1, Earth was modeled as an oblate spheroid (wider at equator due to rotation). Precise tracking of Vanguard 1's orbit by John O'Keefe at NRL revealed a tiny asymmetry: the South Pole region is slightly larger than the North Pole. The geoid is pear-shaped — not perfectly symmetric about the equator. This was the first geophysical discovery made from satellite tracking data.

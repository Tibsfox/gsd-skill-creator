## Creative Threads

1. **vehicle.js** — PNW bird species from S36, plant types from NASA organisms, music-theory behavior functions, real weather + astronomy, emergent ecology
2. **Tracker** — Mission clock, telemetry dashboard, trajectory visualization, live data feeds, scientific accuracy
3. **Style** — Dark space aesthetic, monospace telemetry + serif narrative, fox warmth accents, data-driven design
4. **Artifacts** — Shaders, DSP audio, circuits, Python sims created during the mission

## Principle

The simulation quality reflects the research quality. vehicle.js gets better because we get better at understanding the systems we're modeling. The tracker gets more accurate because we find better data. The style gets more refined because we learn what works. This track is the proof of work.

# Typography — Artemis II / tibsfox.com

## Dual Voice

| Context | Family | Weight | Size | Use |
|---------|--------|--------|------|-----|
| **Telemetry** | Menlo, SF Mono, Consolas, monospace | 300-400 | 0.7-1.4rem | Countdown, values, labels, status |
| **Narrative** | Georgia, Times New Roman, serif | 400 | 0.85-2.2rem | Headers, subtitles, mission descriptions |
| **UI** | system-ui, sans-serif | 400-600 | 0.65-0.85rem | Nav, tags, buttons |

## Scale

- Mission clock: 3.5rem monospace, weight 300
- Telemetry values: 1.4rem monospace
- Telemetry labels: 0.6rem uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em
- Section headers: 0.65-0.75rem uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em
- Body text: 0.8-0.95rem
- Footnotes/attribution: 0.65-0.7rem

## Opacity

| Level | Opacity | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Primary | 1.0 | Clock digits, active values |
| Secondary | 0.5-0.7 | Labels, descriptions |
| Tertiary | 0.25-0.4 | Dim labels, borders, scale bars |
| Ghost | 0.06-0.15 | Grid lines, trajectory paths, background elements |

## Principle

The telemetry voice is the instrument panel. The narrative voice is the mission log. They don't mix — monospace for numbers, serif for words. Opacity creates hierarchy without color.

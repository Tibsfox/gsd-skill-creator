# Organism Pairing — Candidates

*Final selection post-mission. Candidates ranked by resonance strength.*

## Primary Candidate: Western Red Cedar (*Thuja plicata*)

**Resonance:** Orion CM-003 callsign is "Integrity." Western red cedar is the tree of life for Coast Salish peoples — the wood that builds longhouses, canoes, totem poles, cedar-bark clothing, medicine. Cedar doesn't rot. The wood holds its integrity for centuries. The tree that carries a civilization's infrastructure mirrors the spacecraft that carries four humans beyond LEO for the first time in 53 years.

**PNW Status:** Native, abundant, keystone species. Old-growth specimens in Olympic National Park exceed 60m height and 1,000 years age.

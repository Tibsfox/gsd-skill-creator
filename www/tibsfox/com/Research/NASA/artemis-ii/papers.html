#13 CME Propagation — G2 Watch for Launch Day

NOAA SWPC Alert, March 31, 2026 · SWPC Alert
Mission critical: X1.5 flare from AR4405 launched CME at ~1845 km/s. G2 Moderate storm watch for April 1 (launch day). Kp forecast rising to 6. Crew radiation exposure during Van Allen belt transits directly affected.
CME transit: 1.5×108 km / 1845 km/s ≈ 22.6 hours. For G2 storm: aurora visible at ~55° geomagnetic latitude (Pacific NW!), enhanced radiation belts, spacecraft surface charging. The CubeSats (K-RadCube, SHMS) are designed to measure exactly this scenario.
The CME is both risk and opportunity. Real science happens when nature sends a test case during your experiment.

#5 Heat Shield Concerns — AVCOAT Ablation

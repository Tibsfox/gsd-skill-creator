Two Revolutions

Revolution 1 (missions 1–552): The full NASA timeline from NACA founding through Artemis. Each mission is a point on the inner circle, advancing 360° through history.

Revolution 2 (missions 553–720): 168 memorial missions on the outer circle, dedicated to every person lost in the pursuit of spaceflight. Same angles, greater radius.

The two revolutions are a spiral, not a loop. Everything traversed changes the distance from the origin.

720 missions × 0.5°/mission = 360° × 2 = 4π radians

The Complex Plane of Experience

Every mission is a point in two dimensions. The Real axis measures how concrete and engineering-focused the content is. The Imaginary axis measures how cultural and experiential.

Music lives high on the imaginary axis. Infrastructure lives on the real axis. Space bridges both — engineering at the service of awe. The Rosetta Stone translates between axes.

The Riemann sphere maps this plane onto S² via stereographic projection — the 3D constellation.

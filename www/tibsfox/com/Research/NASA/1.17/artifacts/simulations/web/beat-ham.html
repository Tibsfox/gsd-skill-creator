About this test:
Ham was trained at Holloman AFB to pull a lever within 5 seconds of a blue light appearing. Correct responses avoided a mild foot shock; incorrect or slow responses triggered it. His baseline reaction time was ~400 ms. During the MR-2 flight, despite forces up to 14.7g, his reaction time degraded only to ~500 ms — a 25% increase. This proved primates could perform cognitive tasks in space, clearing the way for Glenn's orbital flight 3 weeks later.

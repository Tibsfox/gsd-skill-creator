Belt Structure

Inner belt (1.2–2.0 RE): Trapped protons, stable for years. Explorer 1 discovered this.

Outer belt (3.5–6.0 RE): Trapped electrons, dynamic, responds to solar wind. Pioneer 3 found this.

Slot region (2.0–3.5 RE): Relatively quiet gap between belts.

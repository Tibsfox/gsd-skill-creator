Key moments: Glenn observed luminescent "fireflies" — later identified as ice particles sublimating in sunlight. During orbit 2, Segment 51 telemetry falsely indicated the landing bag had deployed. Mission Control ordered Glenn to retain the retropack through reentry as a heat shield backup. The retropack burned away during reentry, trailing spectacular debris past the capsule window.

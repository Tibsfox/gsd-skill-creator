Enos's Performance:
During MA-5, the lever mechanism malfunctioned. Enos was shocked 76 times for pulling the correct lever. Despite this, he continued responding correctly, maintaining his training. His accuracy remained high even as the punishment increased. This demonstrated that a well-trained primate could maintain correct task performance even under conflicting, aversive feedback — a crucial finding for human spaceflight qualification.

The MDF System

THE MILD DETONATING FUSE (MDF) SYSTEM:

  The MDF is a thin metal tube (~5 mm diameter)
  filled with PETN (detonation velocity ~8,400 m/s).
  The tube is routed through the hatch frame, passing
  through each of the 70 bolts.

  Hatch circumference:
    C = pi * d_hatch = pi * 0.58 m = 1.82 m

  Time for detonation to traverse the full perimeter:
    t_det = C / v_det = 1.82 / 8,400 = 0.000217 s = 0.217 ms

  All 70 bolts are severed within 0.22 milliseconds.

  BOLT SHEAR MECHANICS:

  Each titanium bolt cross-section at the shear plane:
    A_bolt = pi * (0.003)^2 = 2.83 x 10^-5 m^2

  Force to shear one bolt (titanium, tau_ult ~ 400 MPa):
    F_shear = 400e6 * 2.83e-5 = 11,320 N = 2,545 lbf

  Force to shear all 70 bolts:
    F_total = 70 * 11,320 = 792,400 N = 178,156 lbf

  THE INITIATOR PLUNGER:

  Force required: 5.5 lbf (24.5 N)
  Plunger travel: ~6 mm
  Energy: F * d = 24.5 * 0.006 = 0.147 J
  Contact pressure on 20 mm knurled surface:
    P = 24.5 / (pi * 0.01^2) = 78,025 Pa = 0.78 atm
  This pressure on skin leaves a visible mark.
  Grissom's hands: no mark found.

Probability Analysis of Hatch Failure

Three candidate failure mechanisms:

  1. MECHANICAL (Grissom struck the plunger):
     - No bruising observed
     - Grissom denied activation
     - Probability: LOW (0.05-0.15)

  2. THERMAL EXPANSION / VIBRATION:
     - Capsule in tropical Atlantic sun
     - PETN detonators have temperature-dependent sensitivity
     - Wave action transmits energy to plunger
     - Probability: MODERATE (0.30-0.50)

  3. ELECTRICAL FAULT:
     - Backup electrical firing path exists
     - Salt water is an excellent conductor
     - Capsule partially submerged for minutes
     - Probability: MODERATE (0.25-0.40)

  Bayesian reasoning: posterior probability shifts
  strongly toward mechanisms 2 or 3.
  The hatch blew due to a design vulnerability,
  not pilot error. Grissom was exonerated by the physics.

Deposit 2: Hydrostatics of a Sinking Capsule

VOLUME FLOW THROUGH THE HATCH OPENING:

  Hatch area: A = pi * (0.29)^2 = 0.264 m^2

  With 0.5 m wave pushing water into the capsule:
    delta_P = 1025 * 9.8 * 0.5 = 5,023 Pa

  Volume flow rate:
    Q = 0.6 * 0.264 * sqrt(2 * 5023 / 1025) = 0.495 m^3/s
  Conservative continuous average: Q_avg ~ 80-120 L/s

  TIME TO CRITICAL FLOODING:

  Internal free volume: ~1,000-1,200 L
  Helicopter max lift (tropical conditions): ~1,800-2,200 kg
  Maximum tolerable water: 2,000 - 1,100 = 900 kg

  Time to reach helicopter's lifting limit:
    t_critical = 900 / (100 * 1.025) = 8.8 seconds

  The helicopter had less than 9 seconds. The hook
  attachment process takes 30-60 seconds minimum.

  TERMINAL SINKING VELOCITY:

  Net downward force:
    F_net = (2,100 - 1,333) * 9.8 = 7,517 N

  Terminal velocity:
    v_sink = sqrt(2 * 7,517 / (1025 * 0.8 * 1.5))
           = sqrt(12.22) = 3.50 m/s = 12.6 km/h

  Time to reach 4,900 m floor:
    t_sink = 4,900 / 3.50 = 1,400 s = 23 minutes

  PRESSURE AT 4,900 METERS:
    P = 101,325 + 1025 * 9.8 * 4,900
      = 49,297,825 Pa = 493 atm

Deposit 3: Harlequin Duck Hydrodynamics

The Harlequin Duck (Histrionicus histrionicus, degree 20) breeds along the fastest, most turbulent mountain streams in the Pacific Northwest. No other North American duck routinely occupies habitat this violent.

REYNOLDS NUMBER COMPARISON:

  Harlequin Duck in PNW mountain stream:
    Re = 1000 * 1.5 * 0.40 / 0.0013 = 461,538
    Flow regime: TURBULENT

  Liberty Bell 7 sinking in Atlantic:
    Re = 1025 * 3.5 * 1.89 / 0.0017 = 3,989,412
    Flow regime: TURBULENT

  Both objects in the same regime -- both surrounded
  by chaotic, vortex-shedding flow.

  Critical difference: CONTROL AUTHORITY
    Duck: full (legs, wings, tail)
    Capsule: none (dead weight)

  HARLEQUIN DUCK DIVING MECHANICS:

  Buoyancy (submerged): ~5.88 N
  Weight: 0.6 * 9.8 = 5.88 N
  Net buoyancy: ~0 N (NEARLY NEUTRALLY BUOYANT)

  Propulsive force (both feet): ~0.75 N per stroke cycle
  Adequate for 0.5-1.0 m/s through water

The bell sank. That is the fact, the physics, the end of the story in every way except the human one. The math gave the recovery crew 9 seconds of margin, and the hatch blew 60 seconds before the hook was attached. Nine seconds against sixty. The capsule was dead the moment the bolts severed — all 70 of them, in 0.22 milliseconds. Grissom understood this. He was an engineer, Purdue-educated. He understood that the physics exonerated him even as the institution did not. The Harlequin Duck dove through whitewater to pull a caddisfly larva from the cobble — the same cobble where Pacific giant salamanders hunt. The duck surfaced, shook the water from its crested head, and dove again. The bell sank to 4,900 meters and rested under 493 atmospheres for 38 years. A man can be destroyed but not defeated. The bell was destroyed. Grissom was not defeated.

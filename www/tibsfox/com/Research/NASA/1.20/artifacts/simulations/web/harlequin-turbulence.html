About: Harlequin Ducks are one of the only ducks that live in whitewater rapids year-round. They walk along the streambed, feeding on aquatic insects, in currents that would sweep other waterfowl away. Their compact body and strong feet are adapted for this extreme habitat.

The stream is faster than you. Use eddies behind rocks.

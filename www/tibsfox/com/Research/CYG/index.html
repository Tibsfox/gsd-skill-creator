Binary System · LHAASO Detection · Particle Physics · Multi-Messenger · PeVatron Landscape
Overview Binary LHAASO Physics Neutrinos PeVatrons Mission Pack
In December 2025, the LHAASO collaboration reported a 10-sigma detection of gamma rays from Cygnus X-3 extending to 3.7 petaelectronvolts, with unmistakable month-scale variability and evidence of a 4.8-hour orbital modulation. This is a compact binary system producing the most energetic radiation of any resolved point source in the LHAASO catalog — a super-PeVatron accelerating protons to energies a thousand times beyond what human particle colliders can produce.

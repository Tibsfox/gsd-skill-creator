Minecraft World Data as RAG

Spatial Intelligence — NBT Parsing · Knowledge Graphs · Archetype RAG · In-Game Exploration
Overview Mission Pack
Parse Minecraft world saves into a spatially-indexed knowledge graph, expose it through archetype-adaptive RAG, and let in-game exploration serve as the retrieval mechanism — the player's position and gaze direction become the query.

Research Modules

01 — World Data Parsing

NBT · Anvil · Chunks · Entities

Technical deep-dive into Minecraft's binary data formats: NBT serialization, Anvil region files, palette-based block storage, entity/POI storage, and parsing libraries.

02 — Spatial Knowledge Graphs

Schema · Nodes · Edges · GraphRAG

Knowledge graph design for 3D block data: node/edge types, spatial chunking strategies, GraphRAG patterns, and structure detection algorithms.

03 — Archetype-Adaptive RAG

Skyblock · Creative · Survival · Adventure

Four world archetypes, four RAG profiles: chunk strategy, embedding weights, retrieval priorities, and LLM conversational posture for each.

04 — Exploration Interface & Prior Art

PrismarineJS · Voyager · Museum Mode

Position-as-query paradigm, PrismarineJS ecosystem, LLM agent prior art (Voyager, STEVE, MineDojo), educational world packs, and safety considerations.

05 — Verification Matrix

12/12 PASS · Source Quality · Cross-Links

Success criteria verification, source quality assessment, and execution summary.

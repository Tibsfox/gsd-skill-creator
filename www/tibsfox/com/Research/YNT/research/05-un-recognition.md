## 1. UN Resolution A/RES/65/271
Adopted April 7, 2011 -- five days before the 50th anniversary of Vostok 1. Declares April 12 the **International Day of Human Space Flight** (IDHSF). Purpose: "celebrate the beginning of the space era for mankind, reaffirming the important contribution of space science and technology in achieving sustainable development goals." Introduced by Russia's representative Vitaly I. Churkin.

## 2. Policy Architecture

- **Outer Space Treaty (1967):** Space as "province of all mankind"; prohibits WMDs in orbit
- **UNOOSA:** UN Office for Outer Space Affairs; administers IDHSF; promotes equitable access for developing nations
- **World Space Week:** October 4-10 (separate; commemorates Sputnik and Outer Space Treaty)

## 3. Cosmonautics Day
Observed in the Soviet Union since 1963. Continues in Russia and some former Soviet states. The 2011 IDHSF brought the date into global formal recognition.

## 4. SDG Linkages
Space science contributes to: SDG 3 (Health), SDG 4 (Education), SDG 6 (Clean Water), SDG 13 (Climate Action) via satellite technology.

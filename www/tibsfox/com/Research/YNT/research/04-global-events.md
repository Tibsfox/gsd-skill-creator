## 1. Event Diversity
Events at NASA centers, museums, planetariums, schools, bars, nightclubs, houses. Formats: parties, science events, hackathons, raves, cosplay, movie marathons, gaming tournaments. Locations: LA, SF Bay Area, Ottawa, Reno, Huntsville, New Orleans, Stockholm, Tel Aviv, Tokyo, Nairobi, Antarctica, ISS.

# Legacy Ecosystem -- Films, Alumni & 50 Years of Impact

## Sketch-to-Film Adaptations

SNL pioneered the sketch-to-movie pipeline:

| Film | Year | Box Office | Origin Sketch |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Blues Brothers | 1980 | $115M | Belushi/Aykroyd musical act |
| Wayne's World | 1992 | $183M | Myers/Carvey basement show |
| Coneheads | 1993 | $21M | Aykroyd/Curtin alien family |
| It's Pat | 1994 | $60K | Julia Sweeney's androgynous character |
| A Night at the Roxbury | 1998 | $30M | Ferrell/Kattan head-bobbing |
| Superstar | 1999 | $30M | Molly Shannon's Mary Katherine Gallagher |
| MacGruber | 2010 | $9M | Forte's MacGyver parody |

Wayne's World remains the most commercially successful SNL film adaptation, grossing $183 million worldwide.

## Alumni Career Trajectories

SNL has launched more successful comedy careers than any other single program:

### Film Stars
- **Eddie Murphy:** Beverly Hills Cop, Coming to America, Shrek
- **Bill Murray:** Ghostbusters, Lost in Translation, Groundhog Day
- **Adam Sandler:** Billy Madison, Happy Gilmore ($3B+ box office total)
- **Will Ferrell:** Elf, Anchorman, Step Brothers
- **Tina Fey:** Mean Girls (writer), 30 Rock (creator)
- **Amy Poehler:** Parks and Recreation (creator/star)

### Late-Night Hosts
SNL has produced more late-night hosts than any other show:
- Chevy Chase (twice)
- David Spade
- Jimmy Fallon (Tonight Show)
- Seth Meyers (Late Night)
- Colin Jost (various)

## Musical Legacy

Over 2,000 musical artists have performed on SNL, making it one of the most significant live music platforms in television history. Notable performances include:
- **Sinead O'Connor** (1992): Tore up a photo of the Pope on live television
- **Nirvana** (1992): Performed during peak grunge
- **Kanye West** (multiple): Career-defining live performances
- **Adele** (2008, 2015): Album sales spiked after SNL appearances

## SNL50 Anniversary (2024-2025)

The 50th anniversary season achieved remarkable metrics:
- **Anniversary special:** 22.8 million viewers across 35 days (linear + streaming)
- **SNL50 primetime special:** 14.8 million same-night viewers
- **Social media:** 7.7 billion views across platforms
- **NBC's most-watched primetime entertainment** in five years
- **Peacock documentary:** SNL50: Beyond Saturday Night (4-part series)

## SNL UK (2026)

In 2026, Saturday Night Live UK launched on Sky One -- the first international spin-off produced with Michaels' direct involvement. The show applies the same format (live, weekly, host+musical guest) to British comedy and politics.

## The Post-Michaels Question

Lorne Michaels, age 80 in 2025, has indicated Season 50 could be his last. The question of SNL's future without its founding producer is the show's most significant existential challenge:
- No successor has been publicly named
- The show's institutional knowledge is largely held by Michaels personally
- The format's survival depends on whether the "regenerating machine" can regenerate without its original architect

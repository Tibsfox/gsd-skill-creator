Overview Mission Pack
The Cascadia Subduction Zone last ruptured on January 26, 1700. Recurrence: 200-600 years. We are at 326. The Ring of Fire Trade Network is a tribal-led cooperative whose founding premise: survival infrastructure IS trade infrastructure. The network that can coordinate a tsunami response can coordinate anything else afterward.

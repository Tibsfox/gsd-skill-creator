## 6. Execution Summary

**Project:** ROF -- Ring of Fire Trade Network
**Mission source:** `mission-pack/rof-mission.tex`
**Modules created:** 4 (3 substantive + 1 verification)
**Verification result:** 12/12 criteria PASS

**Core thesis confirmed:** The Ring of Fire Trade Network is a tribal-led cooperative whose founding premise is that survival infrastructure IS trade infrastructure. The network that can coordinate a tsunami response can coordinate anything else afterward. Trust, communication protocols, shared situational awareness -- the hard problem gets solved once, and everything downstream inherits it. The federated AI architecture serves the cooperative. The cooperative serves its members. The members decide the terms.

**Key data points preserved across modules:**
- CSZ last rupture: January 26, 1700 (M9.0, precisely dated via trans-Pacific tsunami reaching Japan)
- CSZ recurrence: 200-600 years, 43 events in 10,000 years; current elapsed time: 326 years
- Near-source warning time for coastal communities: 15-30 minutes
- FEMA BRIC court order: December 11, 2025 (restored after April 2025 termination attempt)
- Enforcement order: March 6, 2026
- Cooperative legal structure: RCW 23.86 / 24.06 (Washington State)
- Workforce pipeline anchors: Northwest Indian College (Lummi), Salish Kootenai College, Everett Community College
- The founding vision: "Our people have lived on this coastline for ten thousand years, and the earth is telling us it is going to move again. Here is how we prepare together -- with tools we understand, with governance we control, with a floor beneath us that no one falls through, and with a memory of the past that makes us wiser, not weaker."

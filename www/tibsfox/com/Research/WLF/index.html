100.7 The Wolf — KKWF Seattle

Country Radio — Broadcasting History · Market Dynamics · Community Engagement · Digital Future
Overview Mission Pack
A radio station is a continuous signal. What endures is not the format or the corporate parent or the call letters — it is the relationship between the signal and the community it serves. That relationship, built in the spaces between the songs, is the thing that cannot be disrupted by an algorithm, acquired in a merger, or eliminated in a bankruptcy filing. It is the wolf that keeps howling from Tiger Mountain.

01 — Station History and Identity

1948–2026 · KIRO-FM · KSEA · KQBZ · KKWF

Seventy-eight years on 100.7 MHz: from CBS affiliate to progressive rock to beautiful music to talk radio to country. Nine call signs, five owners, one frequency on Tiger Mountain.

Nielsen PPM · KMPS · The Bull · Hank FM · Format Wars

Ratings trajectory through three inflection points: the KMPS elimination, the accidental monopoly, and the 2024 re-fragmentation that gave Seattle three country stations in a single day.

03 — Programming and On-Air Talent

Morning Wolfpack · CMA Award · Matt McAllister · Drew Bland

The CMA Award-winning morning show, the full programming day, the KMLE Phoenix expansion, and the personalities who turned a frequency into a community.

04 — Ownership and Business Model

Audacy · Entercom · Chapter 11 · Laurel Tree · FCC

From Entercom founding through CBS Radio acquisition to Audacy bankruptcy and emergence: the corporate trajectory that frames KKWF's operating reality.

05 — Community Engagement and Military Focus

Operation K9 Companion · 10,000 for the Troops · Northwest Battle Buddies

Military philanthropy as community identity: service dogs for veterans, Valentine's Day card drives, and the listener bonds no playlist algorithm can replicate.

06 — Industry Context and Digital Future

Streaming · AI Music · Genre Fusion · TikTok · 2030 Scenarios

Country streaming up 23.8%, AI artists on the charts, genre boundaries dissolving: the macro forces reshaping terrestrial radio and what they mean for The Wolf.

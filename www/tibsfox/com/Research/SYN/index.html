Synsor Corp

Predictive quality AI for manufacturing — temporal trend analysis where the process meets the pixel
Overview Profile Product Patents Competition Markets Technology PNW Verification Mission Pack
Founded in Munich in 2021, Synsor.ai applies deep learning to optical inspection not for binary pass/fail defect detection but for temporal trend analysis — watching manufacturing processes drift toward failure before defects manifest. A single camera, a trained model, an edge computer. No million-dollar metrology lab. The value lives not in any single image frame but in the temporal relationships between frames — the drift, the trend, the gradual degradation that a frame-by-frame system cannot see.

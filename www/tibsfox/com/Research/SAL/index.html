Taxonomy & Ecology

The Five Who Return

Ecology

Five Pacific salmon species mapped to five knowledge domains. Dual taxonomy: Western classification (Oncorhynchus) alongside Indigenous classification (relationship and role). Twelve watersheds, 21+ nations, conservation status.

Material Culture

The Tree That Gives Everything

Culture

Western red cedar as foundation technology. Cedar teachings, the Law of the Bark, bark harvesting, canoe construction (5 types), bentwood boxes, longhouse architecture. The canoe as an ontological bridge.

Fishing & Food Systems

10,000 years of sustainable salmon management. Reef net fishing (Lummi, Saanich), weirs, fish traps, beach seining. The First Salmon Ceremony. Smoking, drying, and seasonal rounds.

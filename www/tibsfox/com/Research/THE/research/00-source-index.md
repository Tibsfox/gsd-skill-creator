# Source Index — Thermal & Hydrogen Energy Systems

## Citation Schema

All sources in this research follow a consistent citation format. Every claim with a numerical value, efficiency figure, cost data point, or capacity number must be attributed to a specific named source.

### Source Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|------------|---------|
| GOV | Government agency publications | IEA, DOE, DOE/HFTO, NREL, PNNL, EIA, EPA, BPA |
| PEER | Peer-reviewed journal articles | Nature Energy, Cell Reports Sustainability, Solar RRL, Renewable Energy |
| PRO | Professional organizations & industry | EHPA, Heat Pumping Technologies (HPT Annex 57), Geothermal Rising, OIES |
| IND | Industry & corporate | AltaRock Energy, Bloom Energy, Daikin, Ford Motor Company |

### Quality Rules

1. **Government and peer-reviewed sources preferred** — GOV and PEER sources are the primary evidence base
2. **No entertainment media or blog content** — zero tolerance for non-authoritative sources
3. **All numerical claims require attribution** — every COP value, LCOH figure, capacity number, and efficiency percentage must cite a named source
4. **Date currency** — prefer 2024–2025 data; flag anything older than 2022 as potentially outdated
5. **Sensitivity flagging** — see `00-sensitivity-protocol.md` for topics requiring special handling
6. **Projection vs. measurement** — clearly distinguish demonstrated performance from targets/projections

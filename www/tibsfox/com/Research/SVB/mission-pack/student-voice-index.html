Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and template scaffolding
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Station Heritage + Peer Survey + Gap Map, B: OHA Methodology + Youth Adaptations + Framework)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes felt experience, integrates interview guide, produces archive specification
  5. Wave 3 assembles remaining deliverables, runs OHA compliance verification, produces handoff package

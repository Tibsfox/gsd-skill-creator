New New Deal

Infrastructure & Policy — Corridor Planning · Public Works · Economic Recovery · Regional Development
Overview Mission Pack
One structure. Seven solutions. Enclose the 48,890-mile Interstate Highway System and every surface becomes a transducer: solar panels on the canopy roof, piezoelectric crystals in the road, thermoelectric generators in the pavement. No rain. No ice. No UV. No wildlife collision. No sun glare. No diesel accumulation. The road cares for its cargo.

Research Modules

01. Energy Systems

Five Channels · 518 TWh/year

Solar PV canopy (518 TWh = 13% of US electricity), piezoelectric road harvesting, thermoelectric pavement, vehicle-displaced wind, future perovskite road surface. Fraunhofer ISE model scaled to US interstates.

02. Structural Engineering

Three Typologies · Ventilation Design

Urban tunnel (land return to communities), rural canopy (24m pre-engineered steel span), mountain hybrid (avalanche + seismic). Ventilation design for diesel-to-electric transition. Integration with existing infrastructure.

03. Occupational & Public Health

UV Asymmetry · Diesel · 1.2M Weather Crashes

UV exposure 5x/20x asymmetry in truck drivers (NEJM case study: 28 years, severe unilateral dermatoheliosis), diesel exhaust carcinogenicity (EPA IRIS Group 1), heat stress, sun glare, 1.2M weather crashes/year eliminated.

04. Ecological Integration

Randy Johnson Principle · Wildlife Corridors

The closing-speed geometry that makes deer-vehicle collision unavoidable (Randy Johnson principle). 1-2M WVC/year, $8-10B damage. Grizzly 399. Red wolves on US-64. Snoqualmie Pass wildlife overpass. Tribal nation coordination.

05. Community Economics

New Deal Precedent · Self-Funding · Governance

WPA 6.7% of GDP, $2 multiplier per dollar. Solar canopy: $41.4B/year wholesale revenue. Annual savings: $14-19B (snow control, WVC insurance, weather crashes). Regional corridor authority with elected boards and binding referendum.

06. Transportation Transition

Phased Automation · Community Consent

3-phase automation (human protected / mixed with regional approval / full automation where communities consent). Fleet electrification incentive structure. Workforce transition from CDL driver to corridor operations. Binding referendum required for Phase 3.

07. Verification Matrix

Quality Assurance · Coverage Audit

13/13 success criteria PASS. ~1,590 lines across 7 modules. 25 sourced datasets. Yakama, Snoqualmie, Nez Perce, Lummi Nations named specifically. Cross-link coverage across 10 sibling projects.

Mission Package

Art history is the history of human beings finding the forms that were already waiting to be found. Each era found different forms, with different tools, under different pressures. The College of Knowledge preserves that vocabulary as a living structure. The Complex Plane maps the positions of those discoveries relative to each other.

The Through-Line

Art history is not a sequence of disconnected events. It is a single continuous signal — a complex-valued function across time, where the real axis carries formal/technical development and the imaginary axis carries conceptual/spiritual development. The Creative Continuum maps this signal. The Rosetta Core translates across domains: what chiaroscuro is to painting, counterpoint is to music — structured contrast producing depth. The College of Knowledge positions 100+ concept nodes on this plane, preserving the full vocabulary of human creative expression as a navigable structure for anyone who wants to make art their life.

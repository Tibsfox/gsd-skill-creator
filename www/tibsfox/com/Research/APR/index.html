There is a moment in the Chauvet Cave, roughly 32,000 years ago, when a prehistoric artist looked at a natural contour in the limestone wall and saw in it the shoulder of a horse. They did not impose a horse on a flat surface. They found the horse that was already there in the rock, and revealed it with a few strokes of charcoal. This is what the GSD Amiga Principle describes: do not fight the architecture, find the architecture that was already there.

Research Modules

VIS-01: Visual Arts Origins

Visual Track A · Prehistory through Antiquity

Paleolithic cave art (Chauvet, Lascaux, Altamira, Sulawesi). Venus figurines and the Lion-Man. Egyptian, Mesopotamian, Greek, Roman, African, Asian, and Pre-Columbian origins. Pigment technology, patronage sociology, ritual function.

VIS-02: Classical to Impressionism

Visual Track A · Medieval through Impressionism

Byzantine icons, Romanesque and Gothic architecture, the Renaissance revolution (perspective, humanism, oil paint). Baroque chiaroscuro, Rococo elegance, Neoclassicism, Romanticism, Realism, and the Impressionist hinge.

VIS-03: Modernism to Present

Visual Track A · Post-Impressionism through Contemporary

Fauvism, Expressionism, Cubism, Dada, Surrealism, Futurism. Abstract Expressionism, Pop Art, Minimalism, Conceptual Art, Street Art, Digital Art, NFTs, and AI-assisted creation.

MUS-01: Prehistory to Romanticism

Music Track B · 40,000 BCE to 1900

Bone flutes of Hohle Fels. Ancient Egyptian, Greek, and Indian music systems. Gregorian chant, polyphony, troubadours. Renaissance printing revolution. Baroque form, Classical structure, Romantic emotional maximalism.

MUS-02: Modernism to Digital Age

Music Track B · 1900 to Present

Debussy, Schoenberg, Stravinsky. Jazz, blues, ragtime, gospel. Rock and roll, soul, funk, hip-hop. Electronic music, ambient, industrial. DAWs, streaming, and AI composition.

BIZ-01: The Artist as Entrepreneur

Business Track C · Practice manual

Legal structures (LLC, sole proprietorship). Website and portfolio design. Brand identity principles. Copyright vs. trademark. USPTO registration process. Revenue channels: galleries, e-commerce, licensing, NFTs.

COK-01: Knowledge Integration

CoK Synthesis · Complex Plane positioning

100+ concept nodes on the Complex Plane of Experience. 25+ Rosetta Core translation pairs connecting visual art, music, mathematics, and code. L-System growth grammar for artistic movement evolution.

The Through-Line

Every flower is a node. Every garden is a hub. The network grows one planting at a time, requiring no central coordination, no permission, no infrastructure — just soil, water, sun, and the decision to feed the bees. This is the architecture of resilience: distributed, autonomous, locally adapted, globally connected. The same pattern that runs our agent orchestration also describes how communities restore habitat. Plant a flower. Feed a bee. Strengthen the network.

## 7. Position in the Series

The Living Forest occupies the center of the Research series. It is:

- **The most cross-linked project** -- referencing 22 other projects, more than any other.
- **The ecological core** -- every ecology project (COL, CAS, ECO, GDN, AVI, MAM, SAL) maps territory within or around this forest.
- **The bridge between domains** -- connecting ecological science (old-growth, mycorrhizal networks, fire ecology) to technology (LiDAR, IoT, mesh networking) to culture (indigenous fire management, cedar traditions) to policy (carbon storage, endangered species, climate adaptation).
- **The grandmother** -- like the mother tree that provisions seedlings through the mycorrhizal network, this project provisions the other projects with the ecological foundation that connects them.

The mycorrhizal network is the Rosetta Stone made biological -- one network translating nutrients between species, between generations, between the living and the dead. This project maps that network.

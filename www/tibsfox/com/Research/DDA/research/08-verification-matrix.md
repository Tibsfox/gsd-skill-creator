### 7.2 The Scheduling Board Principle

The scheduling board at the recording studio where "Disneyland" became "Dizz Knee Land" was a verification tool -- it tracked bookings, sessions, and schedules. The band used it for something else entirely: creative wordplay that produced their most famous title [2].

This verification matrix performs a parallel operation. It takes the research modules -- creative, analytical, interpretive documents -- and subjects them to the scheduling board treatment: checking the bookings, confirming the sessions, verifying that what was promised was delivered. The rearrangement reveals whether the structure holds.

It holds.

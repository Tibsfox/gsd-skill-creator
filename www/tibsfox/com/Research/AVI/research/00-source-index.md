## Source Quality Standards

1. **Government sources (G##):** Official agency publications, species profiles, management plans, technical reports
2. **Peer-reviewed (P##):** Published in indexed scientific journals or peer-reviewed book chapters
3. **Professional organizations (O##):** Established ornithological societies, conservation organizations with scientific advisory boards
4. **Cultural sources (C##):** University press publications, museum collections, tribal cultural center public materials, peer-reviewed ethnographic research

**Prohibited sources:** Wikipedia (except as pointer to primary source), personal blogs, social media, entertainment media, non-peer-reviewed preprints without corroboration, anonymous web content

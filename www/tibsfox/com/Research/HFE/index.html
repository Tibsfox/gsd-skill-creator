Overview Signal Capture Amplification Mixing & Space Driver Alignment Enclosures System Fidelity Mission Pack
Sound is the original information carrier. Before electricity, before silicon, before code, there were pressure waves in air — vibrations that encoded meaning, emotion, and presence across distance. Every technology in this mission exists for one purpose: to capture those waves, transmit them without corruption, and reconstruct them as faithfully as possible at the listening end. The tragedy of most audio chains is not ignorance — it is accumulated compromise. This mission is the antidote to that compounding.

Research Modules

M1: Signal Capture

Capture Track A · Microphone physics

Microphone transduction types (condenser, dynamic, ribbon), polar patterns, phantom power (IEC EN 61938), preamplifier topologies (JFET, transformer, IC), gain staging, CMRR, and the critical preamp-SNR ceiling. PNW studios: Robert Lang, Bear Creek, London Bridge.

M2: Amplification Theory

Amplifier Track A · Power topology

Complete Class A/B/AB/C/D/G/H taxonomy. Conduction angle, efficiency ceilings, THD signatures. The audiophile Class D evolution: Atma-Sphere, NAD, PS Audio. Even-order vs odd-order harmonic psychoacoustics. Analog vs digital signal chain architecture.

M3: Mixing & Space

Mixing Track A · Stereo craft

Three-dimensional stereo field model (width, depth, height). Mid/Side processing from Bluemlein 1934 to modern mastering. Mixing console architecture (inline, split, hybrid). Pro Tools and Ableton workflow. Mastering chain: EQ, compression, limiting, dithering.

M4: Driver Alignment & Array Science

Alignment Track A · Wavefront DSP

Time-alignment theory (Ed Long & Wickersham, AES 1976). Acoustic center calculation, lobing behavior. FIR vs IIR crossover design. Line array coherent coupling mathematics. Cardioid subwoofer configurations. Acoustic treatment principles.

M5: Enclosure Engineering

Enclosure Track B · Cabinet design

Sealed, bass reflex, transmission line, tapped horn, bandpass, folded exponential horn. Klipschorn corner-loading, Jubilee dual-driver analysis. Quarter-wave TL calculations. Thiele-Small parameters. Recording studio design principles.

M6: System Fidelity & Case Studies

Fidelity Track B · Measurement

THD & THD+N measurement methodology. SNR, SINAD, SFDR. Even vs odd harmonic psychoacoustics. Case Study A: Funktion-One (Berghain, zero corrective EQ). Case Study B: Klipsch Heritage (PWK horn mathematics, Jubilee). PNW studios as case studies.

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, Thiele-Small tables, and source index
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Signal Chain M1-M4, B: Enclosure & Fidelity M5-M6)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes the complete chain walkthrough and cross-module index
  5. Wave 3 assembles, verifies (The Master Report), and delivers the final document

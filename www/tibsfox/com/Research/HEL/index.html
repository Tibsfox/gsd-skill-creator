HEL — Helium Supply Chain & Pacific Rim Semiconductor Infrastructure

Deep research into helium production, the March 2026 supply crisis, semiconductor fabrication dependencies, and the Pacific Ring of Fire trading network that connects them all.
Energy Infrastructure Science Business Published 2026-03-31 · PNW Research Series · Series Index

Context: March 2026 Helium Crisis

On March 2, 2026, Qatar Energy declared force majeure due to Strait of Hormuz disruptions, halting approximately one-third of global helium supply. This research was initiated during the crisis to understand the full supply chain, identify vulnerabilities, and explore distributed production models that could prevent future single-point failures.

Breakeven Analysis

Metric Value
Breakeven helium price (Grade-A) ~$22/L equivalent
Breakeven monthly volume ~3,500 L equivalent
Breakeven customer count ~5 regular accounts
Minimum viable revenue ~$2.5M/year

At pre-crisis prices (~$25–35/L), the co-op is marginally profitable. At crisis prices ($50–100/L), it is highly profitable. At any price above $22/L, it covers all costs including debt service.

The Bottom Line

The financial model works across all scenarios except total market collapse — and even then, grant funding provides a lifeline. The co-op is not a speculative venture; it is a known technology applied to a known market with a known funding source (CHIPS Act).

The question isn’t whether the numbers work. The question is whether five people are willing to put in the work to make it happen.

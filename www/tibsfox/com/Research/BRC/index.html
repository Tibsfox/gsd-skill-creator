Black Rock City

Virtual BRC — Event Infrastructure · Community Systems · Ten Principles · Federation
Overview Mission Pack
Virtual BRC: A Burning Man-inspired cooperative event infrastructure system, PNW-named, trust-governed, and federated via DoltHub. Center Camp as forest. The playa as shared terrain. Ten principles as operational design constraints.

Research Modules

01. Ten Principles

Event Architecture · Operational Design

All 10 Burning Man principles mapped to GSD architectural guidelines: open skill access, agent autonomy, unconditional share, Leave No Trace cleanup agents, and cache-optimized activation.

02. Playa Roles Catalog

Community Systems · Trust Levels

30+ BRC volunteer departments mapped to PNW skill names with trust levels, camp affiliations, and completion stamps. From Gate Crew (Osprey Watch) to Lamplighters (Cedar Carriers).

03. Theme Camps & Installations

Art Infrastructure · Community Space

8 theme camps with PNW species names and 12 art installation archetypes. Cedar Grove Camp, Raven's Roost, Salmon Run Camp, Mycorrhizal Hub, and the Fireline Cedar burn structure.

04. Mutant Vehicles & Chipsets

CEDAR Chipset · Mobile Infrastructure

4 mobile chipsets (Orca Sled, Raven Crawler, Osprey Dive, Chinook Rider), full CEDAR chipset spec (8 chips, 4 activation profiles), and DMV registration protocol.

05. Wasteland Integration

Federation · Trust System · Annual Cycle

Rig schema, Esplanade Feed wanted board, 8 mycorrhizal stamp categories, 3-level trust ladder, The Cedar Falls burn ceremony, DoltHub pull-only federation, and the annual virtual event cycle.

06. Verification Matrix

Quality Assurance · Coverage Audit

12/12 success criteria PASS. PNW naming 100% coverage. ~1,560 lines of research content across 5 modules. Cross-link coverage across 9 sibling projects.

How to Use This Package

  1. Open mission.pdf and read the Stage 1 Vision to confirm scope and framing.
  2. Hand the complete PDF to your GSD Orchestrator (Claude Code) as the root mission document.
  3. Direct the Orchestrator to begin with Wave 0: build the shared source index (0-A), module skeleton (0-B), and sensitivity protocol (0-C).
  4. At Wave 1, launch the two parallel tracks simultaneously: Track A (Air/Water/Food, Sonnet-led) and Track B (Pollinators/Habitat/Forest, Opus-led).
  5. After Wave 1 completes, request CAPCOM gate approval, then proceed to Wave 2 synthesis.
  6. After Wave 2 synthesis and CAPCOM approval, proceed to Wave 3 publication and verification.
  7. All 7 safety-critical tests must pass before final publication output is released.

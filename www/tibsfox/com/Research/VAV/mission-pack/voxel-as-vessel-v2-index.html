How to Feed to GSD Orchestrator / Skill-Creator

  1. Ensure v1 mission outputs are available — this package explicitly depends on v1 Research Reference as a prefill cache at Wave 0.
  2. Open the PDF and provide it to your GSD Skill-Creator instance as the mission specification.
  3. At Wave 0, the FLIGHT commander loads the v1 reference and generates shared schemas (block.yaml, ceph-ops.yaml, openstack.yaml).
  4. Execute W1A (M7, M8, M9 in parallel), then W1B (M10, M11 in parallel), then W2 (M12, M13 in parallel).
  5. Stop at the CAPCOM GATE after W2 for human review of topology design and DR playbook before synthesis.
  6. Execute W3 (M14 synthesis + VERIFY) after gate approval.

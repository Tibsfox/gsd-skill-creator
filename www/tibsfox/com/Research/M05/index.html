Module Split Analysis

Module Sizing · Cohesion Metrics · Split Frameworks · Cross-Reference Contracts · Structural Remediation
Overview When & Why Sizing Cohesion Coherence Framework Mission Pack
A module that tries to tell two stories tells neither one well. NastyMix Records was born in the Central District, but the label's logic and the neighborhood's story diverged the moment “Square Dance Rap” broke in Flint, Michigan. Module 05's 613 lines were overloaded because they fused music industry economics with cultural geography — a category error that the split resolves by giving each story its own container, its own sources, and its own analytical frame. The architecture was telling us what it needed. The framework's job was to listen.

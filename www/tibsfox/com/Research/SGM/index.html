Research Modules

M1: Geometric Foundations

Math Track A · Classical forms

The classical sacred geometry vocabulary with rigorous mathematical derivations. Vesica Piscis, Seed and Flower of Life, Metatron's Cube, the five Platonic solids, the Golden Ratio, Fibonacci sequence, and the unit circle as primordial form. From Pythagoras through Euclid to da Vinci.

M2: Complex Plane Architecture

Complex Track A · z-plane encodings

Maps each classical sacred form to its complex-plane encoding. Euler's identity, nth roots of unity as polygon generators, cyclotomic polynomials, Mobius transformations, conformal mappings, stereographic projection, the Riemann sphere, and domain coloring visualization.

M3: Nature & Pattern Layer

Nature Track B · Empirical record

The empirical record of sacred geometric patterns in natural phenomena. Logarithmic spirals in nautilus shells, Fibonacci phyllotaxis in sunflowers, hexagonal close-packing, fractal branching in trees and rivers, DNA helix geometry, and crystalline symmetry groups.

M4: Experiential & Consciousness Layer

Mind Track B · Phenomenology

The peer-reviewed phenomenological record of human geometric perception and its neural correlates. Cognitive fluency theory, gamma oscillations, default-mode-network suppression, the sphere model of consciousness, and the interface theory of perception.

M5: Computational Expression

GLSL Track C · Shader suite

Executable implementations of sacred geometric forms and complex-plane transformations. GLSL fragment shaders for roots-of-unity polygons, Flower of Life, domain coloring, Mobius transformations, Julia/Mandelbrot sets, and golden spirals. The Amiga Principle made visible.

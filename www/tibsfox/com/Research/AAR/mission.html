The Through-Line

Every research project in the v1.49.x series follows the same pattern: vision document, wave plan, parallel execution, atomic delivery. The pattern was established organically — COL was first, TCP refined it, the Gastown convoy model scaled it to 49 projects in a single session. But organic growth raises a question: does the pattern actually hold, or does it merely appear to?

AAR answers that question with measurement. Not assertion. Not design intent. Measurement. The RefinementEngine runs 10 passes over the entire corpus and produces counts, distributions, percentages, and gap inventories. The results are what they are. 98.8% structural compliance means 2 projects have gaps. 89% bidirectional cross-referencing means 20 links are one-way. 98.5% source attribution means 62 claims need citations.

The architecture is not perfect. It is measured. And the measurements are the proof that Lex asked for.

Forest Sim

Research / forest

A shared, living playground where each NASA Mission Series degree contributes one generative cross-link with an existing forest subsystem (agent-based flocking, Kuramoto-phase firefly synchronisation, L-system plants, Physarum slime-mold networks, a shared WebAudio layer, and a circadian clock). The contribution system is defined in the forest-module API.

Load this page with a query parameter like ?mission=1.56 to pre-enable a specific mission's contribution. Otherwise, toggle modules in the floating panel in the upper-right corner.

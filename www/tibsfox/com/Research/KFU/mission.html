The Through-Line

Kung fu doesn't mean fighting. It means skill achieved through hard work and time. A master chef has kung fu. A master calligrapher has kung fu. A coder who has poured ten thousand hours into their craft has kung fu. The practice — any practice, pursued with patience and honesty — is the path. The Matrix got the ending right: Neo doesn't win because of the download. He wins because he chooses to stand back up. That choice, repeated daily, is the entire teaching. Five minutes of breathing. One form practiced with attention. A single stance held until your legs shake and you breathe through it. That's kung fu. That's the practice. That's enough.

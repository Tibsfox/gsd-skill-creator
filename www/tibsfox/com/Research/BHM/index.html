Bellingham Market

Farmers Markets · Local Food Systems · Whatcom County Agriculture · Community Economics
Overview Housing Economy Sovereignty Education Regional Mission Pack
Bellingham sits at a hinge point on the map and on the economic timeline. It is 90 miles north of Seattle, 60 miles south of Vancouver BC, and directly adjacent to the Lummi Nation's ancestral Salish Sea territory. That geography is not background color: it shapes every economic force at work in the city. The spaces between Seattle and Vancouver BC are where Bellingham lives. That is not a disadvantage. That is the signal.

Research Modules

M1: Housing & Real Estate Market

Housing Track A · Price dynamics

Current conditions, price trajectory, inventory dynamics, migration pressure from Seattle, affordability analysis, submarket variation, rental market, new construction pipeline, and the hinge city housing signal. Median $647K against $80,989 median income.

M2: Economic Base & Major Employers

Economy Track A · Employer structure

Sector analysis across healthcare, manufacturing, education, retail, and government. Top 10 employer registry, PeaceHealth anchor, BP Cherry Point refinery, Faithlife tech presence, wage dynamics, small business ecosystem, and employer concentration risk.

M3: Tribal Economic Sovereignty

Sovereignty Track B · OCAP principles

Lummi Nation (Lhaq'temish) and Nooksack Tribe as sovereign economic actors. Silver Reef Casino (8th-largest employer), 1974 Boldt Decision fisheries co-management, Lummi Foreign Trade Zone, Northwest Indian College, household economics, and data sovereignty.

M4: Higher Education & Workforce

Education Track B · Talent pipeline

Western Washington University economic impact ($138.5M capex, 3,381 jobs), degree production pipeline (3,729 BA/BS annually), corporate recruitment gap, Kaiser Borsari Hall STEM investment, WCC, BTC, NWIC, and the talent export problem.

M5: Regional Position & Cross-Border

Regional Synthesis · Corridor economics

Bellingham's corridor position between Seattle and Vancouver BC. I-5 migration dynamics, Canadian cross-border retail, exchange rate sensitivity, four border crossings, transportation infrastructure, remote work transformation, and the hinge city synthesis.

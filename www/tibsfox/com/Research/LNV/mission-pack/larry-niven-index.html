Contents

  • Stage 1 — Vision Document: Problem statement, 5 research modules (Biography, Known Space, Ringworld, Collaborations, Ideas & Legacy), chipset configuration, 12 success criteria, scope boundaries, through-line
  • Stage 2 — Research Reference: Sourced findings for all 5 modules including timeline structure, 8 alien species profiles, Ringworld physical parameters, Niven-Pournelle novel catalog, Niven's Laws complete text, predictive fiction catalog, critical assessment
  • Stage 3 — Mission Package: Squadron activation (12 roles), 4-wave execution plan with 2 parallel tracks, 101K token budget (30/60/10 Opus/Sonnet/Haiku), 29-test verification plan, dependency graph, crew manifest

Usage

  1. Feed this PDF to the GSD skill-creator via Claude Code
  2. The orchestrator will parse Stage 3 and decompose into executable wave plans
  3. Wave 0 (Foundation) produces shared schemas consumed by all subsequent waves
  4. Wave 1 executes two parallel research tracks: Track A (Biography & Collaborations) and Track B (Known Space, Ringworld, Ideas & Legacy)
  5. Wave 2 (Synthesis) integrates findings across all modules
  6. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and tests the final document

Recompilation

  1. Ensure DejaVu fonts are installed: apt install fonts-dejavu
  2. Compile with XeLaTeX (three passes for TOC, cross-refs, and page counts):

xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode larry-niven-mission.tex ×3

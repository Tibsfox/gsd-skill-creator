Sample Trips

Ferry Day — Mukilteo to Whidbey Island

Full Day 6-8 hours · Car or walk-on
Coffee on the Speedway
Grab espresso before the ferry. Multiple drive-through stands on Mukilteo Speedway.
Mukilteo Lighthouse Park
Walk the beach, see the lighthouse. Check tide — low tide exposes more beach.
WSF Mukilteo → Clinton (20 min crossing)
Langley Village
Art galleries, bookshops, waterfront restaurants. 20 min south of Clinton on SR 525.
Deception Pass State Park
Bridge views, beach, hiking. 45 min north of Clinton. Worth the drive.
WSF Clinton → Mukilteo (20 min return)
Dinner — Mukilteo Speedway
Diamond Knot Brewpub, Ivar's Mukilteo Landing, or the Thai places along the Speedway.

Waterfront Crawl — Train + Ferry + Light Rail

Full Day 8-10 hours · No car needed
Everett Station
Start at Sounder N Line platform. AM southbound departure.
Sounder N: Everett → Mukilteo (15 min)
Mukilteo Beach + Fishing Pier
Walk from Sounder station to lighthouse. Fish from the new pier if tide is right.
Sounder N: Mukilteo → Edmonds (10 min)
Edmonds Waterfront
Pier, Brackett's Landing, downtown shopping. Lunch at waterfront restaurants.
Sounder N: Edmonds → King Street Station (40 min)
Pike Place Market / Pioneer Square
Walk from King Street Station. Explore, eat, wander.
1 Line: Pioneer Square → Lynnwood City Center (45 min)
Community Transit bus home to Mukilteo

Paine Field Loop — Aviation + Food + Parks

Half Day 3-4 hours · Car
Future of Flight / Boeing Everett Tour
World's largest building by volume. 787, 777X, 767 assembly lines. Book ahead.
Drive south on Airport Rd / Mukilteo Speedway
Lunch — Airport Road corridor
Diverse food along Airport Rd and Casino Rd. Korean, Vietnamese, Mexican, teriyaki.
Japanese Gulch
160-acre urban forest between Mukilteo Speedway and the waterfront. Trail network through second-growth. Historic Japanese logging community site.
Diamond Knot Brewpub
Mukilteo Speedway. Local craft brewery. Good burgers.

College Tour — EVCC + EdCC + Skill Center

Half Day 4-5 hours · Bus
Everett Community College (EVCC)
Main campus on E Broadway, Everett. Allied health, aviation maintenance, arts. Rainier Learning Center.
Community Transit → Lynnwood
Edmonds College (EdCC)
68th Ave W, Lynnwood. Transfer programs to UW/WSU. Culinary arts, STEM, business. Triton athletics.
Bus to Sno-Isle TECH campus
Sno-Isle TECH Skills Center
Advanced manufacturing, welding, IT, healthcare, automotive. Dual-credit high school + college. Industry certifications.
Bus to Lynnwood City Center → 2 Line or 1 Line south

Mukilteo Speedway Corridor

SR 525 from the ferry terminal east to I-5. The main commercial strip through Mukilteo. Connects the waterfront to Harbour Pointe, Paine Field, and I-5 interchange.

Diamond Knot Brewing

Craft brewery + pub. Local favorite. Burgers, fish & chips, house beers.

621 Front St / Mukilteo Speedway

Ivar's Mukilteo Landing

Seafood, waterfront views. Classic PNW fish bar. At the ferry terminal.

710 Front St

Red Cup Cafe

Breakfast and brunch spot. Popular on weekends.

Mukilteo Speedway

Mukilteo Lighthouse Park

Beach, lighthouse, boat launch, picnic. Start of the Speedway.

609 Front St

Japanese Gulch Trailhead

160-acre urban forest. Multiple trailheads off the Speedway and 76th.

Off Mukilteo Speedway

Paine Field / Airport Road

Aviation corridor. Boeing's Everett factory, Paine Field commercial terminal (Alaska Airlines), and the food/service strip along Airport Road and Casino Road. The old GTE building (now Verizon/Frontier) sits at the south end — part of the region's telecom infrastructure history alongside Boeing's aerospace legacy.

Boeing Everett Factory

World's largest building by volume. 787, 777X, 767 assembly. Public tours via Future of Flight.

8415 Paine Field Blvd

Future of Flight Aviation Center

Boeing tour departure point. Aviation exhibits, rooftop viewing. Book ahead.

8415 Paine Field Blvd

Paine Field / Snohomish County Airport

Alaska Airlines commercial terminal (opened 2019). General aviation. KPAE weather station.

3220 100th St SW

Flying Heritage & Combat Armor Museum

Paul Allen's WWII aircraft and tank collection. Operational vintage aircraft. Weekend flights.

3407 109th St SW

Museum of Flight Restoration Center

Aircraft restoration facility. Watch restoration work in progress. Free admission.

2909 100th St SW

Casino Road / 128th to I-5

One of the most diverse food corridors in Snohomish County. Vietnamese, Korean, Mexican, Somali, Filipino, Indian — authentic and affordable. Casino Road connects Airport Rd to I-5 via 128th St SW.

International food corridor

Dozens of restaurants along Casino Rd and 128th. Pho, banh mi, Korean BBQ, taquerias, halal, Indian buffet. Best affordable food in south Everett.

Casino Rd / 128th St SW

Asian grocery stores

Multiple Asian markets with produce, seafood, and specialty ingredients not found at chain stores.

Casino Rd corridor

Boeing Everett Highway (SR 526)

Direct connection from I-5 to Paine Field and the Boeing plant. Freeway-grade, fast access.

I-5 Exit 189

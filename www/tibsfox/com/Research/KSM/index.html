Founding Dynasty

The Founding Dynasty

Dynasty

From Lou Kessler's 50-watt Queen Anne Hill apartment through Rogan Jones' $9,000 purchase, the 1936 Supreme Court landmark, International Good Music automation, and the birth of 92.9 MHz in March 1960. A broadcasting empire built on geographic leverage.

Technical Infrastructure

The Mount Constitution Signal

Technical

CCC-built stone tower at 2,409 feet. Nautel NV30 transmitter, HD Radio with three subchannels, 744m HAAT, and the first Nautel GV40 ever manufactured installed on a co-located station. The mountain is the multiplier.

Ownership & Business

The Goldilocks Strategy

Ownership

Saga Communications' $9.8 million acquisition in 1998. Edward K. Christian's Goldilocks market philosophy. Five-station cluster at 2219 Yew Street Road. January 2025 rebrand to Pacific Northwest Media Group.

Cross-Border Signal

The Cross-Border Signal

Signal

US-Canada frequency coordination. CKYE "Red FM" at 93.1 MHz and HD Radio splatter dynamics. The KVOS-TV precedent and Bill C-58. A binational audience that no treaty can prevent.

Community & Programming

Voices from Yew Street

Community

The Brad & John morning show. Vehicle for a Vet (eight years running). 14,000 rolls of toilet paper. The Classic Rockforce. Local voices for a local community, broadcast from the same mountain for over sixty years.

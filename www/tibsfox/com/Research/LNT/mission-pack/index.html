How to Use This Package

1
Download latenight.pdf — this is your complete three-stage mission package. Read the Vision Guide (Stage 1) to understand scope and philosophy.
2
Hand the PDF to your GSD orchestrator in Claude Code: gsd execute latenight.pdf or reference it as the mission specification in a new session.
3
The mission uses Fleet activation (17 roles). Confirm the activation profile with your orchestrator before Wave 0 begins. Six context windows estimated across three sessions.
4
Two CAPCOM gates require your approval: after INTEG synthesis (before final document is produced) and after verification (before delivery).
5
The five safety-critical tests (SC-SRC, SC-NUM, SC-ADV, SC-DIV, SC-CONT) are hard BLOCK gates. Any failure halts the mission and requires human review before proceeding.

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and DACP bundle templates
  3. Wave 1 executes three parallel tracks (A: Claude Code+Rosetta, B: Chipset+CUDA, C: Mesh+Docs)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes cross-module integration, CLAUDE.md firmware template, and fidelity pass
  5. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and delivers the final document with human approval gate

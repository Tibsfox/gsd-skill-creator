Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the mission package
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and LaTeX templates
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Wire Format+Options, B: Lifecycle+Security)
  4. Wave 1.5: CAPCOM gate for human review of wire format accuracy and security sensitivity
  5. Wave 2: DHCPv6 synthesis and cross-module integration (Opus)
  6. Wave 3: Final assembly, verification, and LaTeX compilation (3 passes)

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and discography tables
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Historical, B: Sonic/Cultural/Arc)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes cross-module connections and the thematic through-line
  5. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and delivers the final document

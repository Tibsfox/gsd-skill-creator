Founded in 1989, the SANS Institute has grown from a cooperative of system administrators sharing security knowledge into the world’s largest cybersecurity training organization. With 85+ courses, 40+ GIAC certifications, and a global conference footprint spanning six continents, SANS trains over 40,000 cybersecurity professionals annually. The principle is simple: the people who teach you defense are the people currently doing defense. The instructor who teaches penetration testing ran a penetration test last month. That’s not a policy — it’s the reason SANS works.

Foundation

GIAC

The GIAC certification ecosystem: GSEC, GCIH, GCIA, GPEN, GCFE, GREM. Six practice areas, 85+ courses, three delivery modes. The instructor who teaches you penetration testing ran a penetration test last month.

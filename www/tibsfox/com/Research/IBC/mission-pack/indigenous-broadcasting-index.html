Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and nation attribution registry
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Station Profiles + Infrastructure, B: Language/Culture + Programming)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes sovereignty analysis and cross-module integration
  5. Wave 3 assembles, verifies (SC-IND nation-specificity check), and delivers the final document

LaTeX Recompilation

To modify and recompile the .tex source:

  1. Ensure DejaVu fonts are installed (standard on Ubuntu/Debian)
  2. Run three passes:
    xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex
    xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex
    xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode mission.tex
  3. Three passes resolve: TOC, cross-references, page counts, hyperlink destinations

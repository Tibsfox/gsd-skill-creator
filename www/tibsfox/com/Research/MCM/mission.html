Research Modules

Module 1: Maritime Compute Architecture

Maritime

Container DCs, seawater cooling (PUE <1.15), barge engineering, 3 ocean temperature zones, Project Natick findings, operational model for remote nodes.

Module 2: Ocean Energy Systems

Energy

Wave, tidal, wind, solar hybrid architecture. CorPower CorPack arrays, OTEC, LFP battery storage, islanding and autonomous operation.

Module 3: Subsea Data Transfer

Subsea

600+ submarine cables, landing stations, hyperscaler ownership shift, vulnerability analysis, SMART cables, space-division multiplexing roadmap.

Module 4: Floating Bridge & Maglev

Bridge

WA State floating bridges, Norway SFT, pontoon hydrodynamics, TSB Cargo, GA passive magnets, KIT superconducting dual-purpose rail, vactrain concepts.

Module 5: Global Knowledge Distribution

Network

Latency equity model, 5 underserved regions, CDN from ocean nodes, Pacific Spine integration, hardware refresh: ship vs. maglev comparison.

Module 6: Maritime Governance & Environmental

Governance

UNCLOS framework, maritime zones, environmental impact (thermal, EMF, acoustic), Indigenous sovereignty (Coast Salish, Pacific Islander), CARE/OCAP principles.

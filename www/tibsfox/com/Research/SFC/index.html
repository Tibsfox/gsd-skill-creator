Silicon Forest

PNW Tech Corridor · Sno-Isle TECH · Paine Field JIT · Co-op Economy · Pacific Assembly Circuit
Overview Civic Layer Creative Commons JIT Zone Ferry Mesh Knowledge Network Pacific Circuit Mission Pack
There is a triangle in Snohomish County that the rest of the world does not yet understand. At its three vertices: Sno-Isle TECH Skills Center on Airport Road near Paine Field, the Boeing assembly infrastructure running south toward the Port of Everett, and the Mukilteo ferry dock pointing west across the water toward Edmonds and the world beyond. The corridor is not a tributary; it is a circulatory system. Small town feel on a global scale. That is the daily beat.

Research Modules

M1: Sno-Isle / IOOF Civic Layer

Civic Track A · Workforce infrastructure

Sno-Isle TECH Skills Center programs (24 programs, 5 pathways including first-in-USA fiber optic certification). IOOF fraternal hall network as co-op real estate model. Union apprenticeship structures. Student housing. Co-op equity accrual from day one.

M2: Hwy 99 Creative Commons Corridor

Social Track A · Cultural transformation

Everett Mall / Hub @ Everett redevelopment (Topgolf anchor, 2026). Low-income creative commons model. Art and music education as pathway to small business ownership. CDBG / affordable housing trust fund mechanisms. Alderwood Mall as established business anchor.

M3: Paine Field Silicon Forest JIT Zone

Industry Track B · Manufacturing hub

Paine Field cargo operations and Boeing manufacturing throughput. Airport Road industrial corridor. JIT electronics finish work. The Silicon Forest identity distinct from Silicon Valley. Cooling and power infrastructure. Tulalip tribal territory acknowledgment.

M4: Ferry Mesh & Regional Coordination

Logistics Track B · Nervous system

Mukilteo-Edmonds ferry route as physical data/logistics spine. Daily beat protocol design: synchronizing ferry departures, shift schedules, restaurant prep, delivery windows into a unified regional pulse. Food distribution synchronization.

M5: Global Co-op Knowledge Network

Network Track C · Intelligence layer

GitHub as global commit graph. Minecraft as shared creative sandbox. Amazon/Costco logistics intelligence. Boeing and BNR-SF engineering heritage. Federated work economy via Wasteland/DoltHub. Zero-trust mesh architecture.

M6: Pacific Assembly Circuit

Global Track C · Economic loop

JIT hardware flow from Paine Field to Silicon Valley for final assembly. Component inflow from Asian suppliers through Port of Everett. Distribution to Europe. Return flow for parts and recycling. The closed-loop circulatory model.

The Through-Line

Coil’s entire practice was transmutation as method — the systematic transformation of base material (sound, consciousness, identity, cultural taboo) into refined states through disciplined experimental process. The spaces between nodes carry meaning. This study maps the territory so the music can continue to do its work.

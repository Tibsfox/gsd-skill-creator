Teams are compositions of agents and skills working together with defined topologies. Each team has a coordinator (leader) and specialized workers operating in parallel or sequential patterns. All teams are read-only by default — they analyze and recommend, they do not modify.

Team Compositions

Analyzes and optimizes CI/CD pipelines, build configurations, and deployment strategies.

Generates and maintains project documentation from code, comments, and architecture analysis.

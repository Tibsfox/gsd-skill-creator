Mission Package

AI is the progressive formalization of intelligence — each era discovering one more layer of the mathematical structure that cognition shares with computation. This mission treats the history, present, and future of AI/ML as a single continuous arc, navigable via the eight-layer mathematical progression of The Space Between. The Complex Plane of Experience provides the coordinate system: the real axis as empirical capability, the imaginary axis as theoretical depth.

The Through-Line

There is a shape to the history of intelligence. Not a straight line upward, not an exponential curve forever, but a unit circle — rotating, returning, accumulating. Each era discovers one more layer of the mathematical structure that cognition shares with computation. The Amiga Principle teaches us to look for leverage: the transformer is not brute force applied to language, it is the discovery that attention — the selective routing of relevance — was the key that had been missing. The spaces between eras, between paradigms, between winter and spring, are where the insights accumulate.

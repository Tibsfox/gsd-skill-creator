The Sensing Layer

IoT Sensors · Environmental Monitoring · Edge Computing · Data Pipelines · Open Science Mesh
Overview MCU Substrate Light Systems Remote Sensing Signal Processing Mesh Weather Open Science Mission Pack
There is a layer between the world and the computer that most people never think about — the sensing layer. It is where the physics of our atmosphere, the photons bouncing off fog droplets, the pressure waves of a foghorn, and the Doppler shift of a passing rainstorm get translated into bits. That translation is not neutral. How we design it determines what we can know, what we can share, and ultimately what kind of intelligence we can build together.

Research Modules

M1: Microcontroller Substrate

MCU Track A · Field node platforms

Arduino, ESP8266, ESP32, Raspberry Pi Pico / Pico 2 comparison. ADC linearity characterization, PIO-DAC at 150 MS/s, low-power amplifier selection for sensor conditioning, solar power budgets for field deployment.

M2: Light Systems & Light Art

Light Track A · LED and POV

WS2812B, DotStar/APA102, SK6812 RGBW protocols. Persistence of vision timing mathematics, Hall effect synchronization, shift register cascades, holographic POV on PTFE PCBs, inductive wireless power.

M3: Remote Sensing & Fog Vision

Sensing Track B · Multi-modal perception

Lidar time-of-flight at 905nm vs 1550nm. Millimeter-wave and X-band radar fog penetration. Ultrasonic sonar ranging. RTL-SDR Doppler weather sensing. Coherent Doppler lidar. Sensor fusion architectures for atmospheric opacity.

M4: Signal Processing, ADC/DAC & Timekeeping

Signal Track B · Conversion and time

SAR vs delta-sigma ADC architectures. ESP32 nonlinearity, ADS1115 16-bit precision. MCP4725 DAC, PiWave PIO-DAC 150 MS/s. I2S audio interfaces. DS3231 RTC, GPS PPS microsecond timing, SMPTE timecodes.

M5: DIY Mesh Weather Networks

Mesh Track C · Distributed sensing

BME280/AHT20/GUVA-S12SD sensor suites. LoRa/Meshtastic telemetry protocol. MQTT to Home Assistant. NOAA NWS API calibration. 696-hour ESP8266 validation study. MeshBot weather alerts. Zero-trust observation signing.

M6: Open Science Co-op Mesh

Co-op Track C · Volunteer computing

BOINC 8.2.8 at 20+ PetaFLOPS daily. Folding@home 12.9 native PetaFLOPS. ARM64 Raspberry Pi participation. GPU-first CUDA/OpenCL leverage. Screensaver-as-science architecture. Zero-trust work unit validation. DoltHub federation.

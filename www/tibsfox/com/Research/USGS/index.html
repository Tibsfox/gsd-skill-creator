Monitored Fault Systems

  • San Andreas Fault — 1,200 km, Pacific/North American plate boundary. Boulder Creek sits on the Loma Prieta segment (M6.9, 1989).
  • Hayward Fault — East Bay, last major rupture 1868 (M6.8). Overdue for M6.8-7.0.
  • Cascadia Subduction Zone — 1,000 km megathrust, Juan de Fuca plate. Last M9.0 on January 26, 1700. Recurrence interval ~240-500 years.
  • Seattle Fault — Beneath Puget Sound, last rupture ~900 AD (M7.0+). Generated tsunami in the Sound.
  • South Whidbey Island Fault — Runs from Whidbey through Everett. Active, poorly characterized.

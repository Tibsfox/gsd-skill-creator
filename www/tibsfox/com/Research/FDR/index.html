Wuxing & the Sexagenary Cycle

Cosmology

The Five Phases (五行) are not elements but transformations. Ten Heavenly Stems meet twelve Earthly Branches. Their least common multiple — sixty — is the Chinese cosmological octave. The mathematical structure behind three thousand years of calendrical practice.

The Dragon in Chinese Culture

Dragon

From Xinglongwa jade (6200 BCE) through Qing dynasty five-claw law. Four cosmic dragons, nine dragon sons, composite anatomy from nine animals. The only mythical creature in the zodiac — and the one that shaped three millennia of statecraft.

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the PDF
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schema, source index, adapter format spec
  3. Wave 1 executes three parallel tracks (A: Yegge+Bridge, B: ISA+GPU, C: Bus+Router)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes cross-module integration and the full-stack diagram
  5. CAPCOM gate: human reviews Gas City bridge specification
  6. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and delivers the final documents

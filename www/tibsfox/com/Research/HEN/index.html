Dancing Your Cares Away

Jim Henson · Muppets · Sesame Street · Fraggle Rock · Cascade PBS
Overview The Architect The Characters The Classroom The World The Station Mission Pack
There is a particular kind of architecture that changes everything it touches not through force, but through elegance. Jim Henson understood this instinctively. Before he was a cultural icon, he was a young man who wanted to work in television, picked up some fabric and ping-pong balls, and accidentally invented a new language — one that blurred the boundary between puppet and person, between education and entertainment, between the silly and the profound.

Research Modules

M1: Jim Henson — The Architect

Biography Track A · Creative philosophy

Greenville to Hyattsville to WRC-TV. Sam and Friends, the proscenium revolution, foam-rubber construction, camera intimacy. The Creature Shop, Dark Crystal, Labyrinth. Henson's craft philosophy: life and sensitivity over mechanical precision. The unfinished Disney deal. May 16, 1990.

M2: The Muppets — The Characters

Characters Track B · Character lineage

Kermit's evolution from lizard-like creature to cultural icon. The Muppet Show rejected by US networks, greenlit by Lew Grade's ATV. 235 million viewers across 100+ countries. Frank Oz, Miss Piggy, the film franchise. Disney's 2004 acquisition and the modern Muppet era.

M3: Sesame Street — The Classroom

Education Track C · Research-based pedagogy

Joan Ganz Cooney and Lloyd Morrisett's vision. The Children's Television Workshop. Gerald Lesser's curriculum architecture. Formative research, pre-testing, curriculum-before-content. The 14% grade-level improvement. 120+ countries, 140 international adaptations. The equity mission.

M4: Fraggle Rock — The World

World-Building Track D · Eco-allegory

Henson's peace mission encoded as children's television. Four species, one interdependent ecosystem. HBO's first original series. International co-production with CBC and TVS. 96 episodes, 100 songs. First North American show broadcast in the Soviet Union. Back to the Rock.

M5: Public Television — The Station

Infrastructure Track E · Civic media

KCTS Channel 9: the eighth non-commercial television station in America. Dorothy Bullitt's founding gift. University of Washington origins. Bill Nye the Science Guy. The community licensee model. Cascade PBS rebrand 2024. Federal funding cuts and the civic infrastructure question.

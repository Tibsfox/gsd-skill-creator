A systematic deep dive into 24 papers surfaced during the Artemis II mission period, organized into seven thematic clusters. Each paper was analyzed for key findings, methodological contributions, and connections to the 245-project PNW Research Series corpus. The clusters span from MCP protocol security and supply-chain defense through agent reasoning, code generation, hallucination detection, context engineering, and social persuasion — mapping the frontier of agentic AI research as of April 2026.

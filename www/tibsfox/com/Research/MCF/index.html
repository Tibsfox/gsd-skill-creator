The Amiga was not powerful because it had more transistors than the IBM PC. It was powerful because its three custom chips — Agnus, Denise, Paula — each did one thing with mastery, and communicated across a shared bus with minimal overhead. A federated cluster topology is the same principle at infrastructure scale: specialized nodes with dedicated roles, communicating through minimal well-specified interfaces, producing a system whose capability exceeds what any single cluster could achieve. The spaces between the clusters are not dead zones. They are where the protocol lives.

Research Modules

M1: Federation Control Planes

Federation Track A · Control plane architecture

Survey of Karmada, Liqo, and Admiralty: centralized vs. P2P vs. scheduling-layer federation. KubeFed lineage and archive. CNCF maturity levels. Scalability benchmarks (Karmada tested to 100 large-scale clusters). Push/Pull modes. Failover behavior and suitability for FoxCompute prototype.

M2: Service Mesh Cross-Cluster Patterns

Mesh Track B · East-west communication

Istio multi-cluster (primary-remote vs. multi-primary), Linkerd service mirroring, Cilium ClusterMesh eBPF. Performance benchmarks: Linkerd fastest, Cilium 40–60% less overhead. Ambient Mesh sidecar-free patterns. mTLS identity and SPIFFE/SPIRE workload attestation.

M3: Network Fabric & CNI

Network Track A · eBPF-native connectivity

Cilium ClusterMesh deep-dive: eBPF pod-to-pod across cluster boundaries, shared CA, global service discovery. Submariner L3 routing. Skupper L7 application networks. Red Hat Research ICPE 2025 performance study. WireGuard tunnel overlay for rural intermittent connectivity.

M4: Day-2 Operations & Observability

Operations Track B · Fleet management

Configuration drift mechanics. GitOps fleet patterns with ArgoCD app-of-apps. Blast-radius containment. Data sovereignty enforcement (OCAP, GDPR). Cross-cluster RBAC and OIDC identity federation. Observability: Hubble, Thanos, OpenTelemetry, Kiali multi-cluster graph.

M5: FoxCompute Integration Architecture

Integration Synthesis · Amiga principle

Synthesis module mapping Amiga-principle node roles to cluster boundaries. Federation stack and mesh stack recommendation for Phase 1 prototype. PropagationPolicy templates. Carbon-aware scheduling with solar-intermittent power profiles. Sovereign data enforcement map.

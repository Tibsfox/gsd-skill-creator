Contents

  • Stage 1 Vision Document — Narrative vision, problem statement (5 problems), core concept with ASCII domain map and module architecture, 5 research modules, chipset config, scope boundaries, 12 success criteria, through-line
  • Stage 2 Research Reference — Source organizations, M1 History & Origins (1948–2028 timeline with attendance data), M2 Governance & Organization (bylaws, LASFS service mark, site selection), M3 Cultural Impact & Legacy (furry fandom birthplace, The Other Change of Hobbit, governance template), M4 Convention Culture & Traditions (7 traditions documented), M5 Decline & Proposed Retirement (COVID impact, bid failures, bylaws repeal mechanics), safety considerations, full bibliography
  • Stage 3 Mission Package — Squadron activation (12 roles), 4-wave execution plan with 3 parallel tracks, 7 deliverables, component breakdown with model assignments, token budget (55K tokens, Opus 25% / Sonnet 65% / Haiku 10%), dependency graph, 30-test plan (5 safety-critical, 14 core, 6 integration, 5 edge), verification matrix, crew manifest

Usage Instructions

  1. Feed this PDF to gsd-skill-creator as the mission package input.
  2. The orchestrator will read the Wave Execution Plan and decompose into agent tasks.
  3. Wave 0 (Foundation) runs first — schemas and source index.
  4. Wave 1 runs three parallel research tracks: History+Governance, Culture+Traditions, and Decline.
  5. Wave 2 (Synthesis) integrates all five modules into a narrative arc.
  6. Wave 3 (Publication) assembles the final document and runs verification.
  7. CAPCOM checkpoints occur at each wave boundary for human review.

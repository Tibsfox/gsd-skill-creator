22 safety-critical gates run on every release before publication. Key gates: attribution completeness (SC-01), Indigenous nation-specific language — Duwamish/Muckleshoot/Suquamish (SC-02), Black American musical attribution explicit (SC-03), Mia Zapata dignity protocol (SC-06), XeLaTeX compilation success (SC-09), atomic write integrity (SC-11), OCAP® compliance (SC-22). Any BLOCK-level finding halts the pipeline and escalates to CAPCOM (human review). The Safety Warden cannot be bypassed.

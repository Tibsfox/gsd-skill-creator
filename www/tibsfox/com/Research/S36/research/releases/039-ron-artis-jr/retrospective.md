## The First Repeat Artist: A Structural Revelation

### Ron Artis Jr = Ron Artis II

The most significant finding at degree 39 is that the CSV's "Ron Artis Jr" is the same person as degree 24's "Ron Artis II." Both naming conventions -- "II" (the second) and "Jr" (junior) -- designate the son of Ron Artis Sr. Web research confirms a single biographical identity: born August 31, 1986, Haleiwa, North Shore, Oahu, Hawaii; son of Motown session player Ron Artis Sr.; second eldest of eleven children; leads Ron Artis II & The Truth. There is no separate "Ron Artis Jr" who is a different person.

The CSV's use of two different name variants at two different degrees, with every other field changed (genre, energy, era, label), is deliberate. The engine is demonstrating that a single artist can occupy multiple positions in the 360-degree space. This is not a data error. It is a structural statement about the nature of the engine.

- **Situation:** Degree 39 artist ("Ron Artis Jr") is the same person as degree 24 artist ("Ron Artis II").
- **Root Cause:** The CSV intentionally maps the same artist at two degrees through different genre lenses (Soul/Jazz at 24, Blues/Soul at 39) with different energy levels (E=4 at 24, E=5 at 39), different era framings ("2010-present" at 24, "Hawaii to Seattle" at 39), and different label designations (KEXP at 24, self-released at 39).
- **Recommendation:** Establish the dual-degree artist as a structural category. Document that the engine's 360 degrees map musical positions, not unique persons. Track for additional repeat artists at future degrees.
- **Carry Item:** ARCHITECTURAL -- Dual-degree artist. First instance at degrees 24 and 39. The engine maps positions, not people.

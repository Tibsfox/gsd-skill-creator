Genre Stage 87: Avant-Pop

Degree 105 reaches Genre Stage 87. Avant-Pop describes Parenthetical Girls in electronic context — the theatrical, baroque practice from degree 89 reframed through electronic production. The genre classification remains Avant-Pop because the theatrical impulse is the constant — the electronic foundation is a new staging, not a new genre.

Distinguished from Lemolo electronic (degree 104, Electronic Pop) — Lemolo’s electronic reframing is dream-based, atmospheric, and intimate. Parenthetical Girls’ electronic reframing is theatrical, dramatic, and maximalist. Both are electronic returns; the emotional register is entirely different.

It describes the quality produced when a baroque, cabaret-inflected art practice is rebuilt with electronic instruments — when the dramatic arrangements of strings, horns, and chamber ensembles are replicated through synthesizers, drum machines, and electronic processing, preserving the theatrical intensity while adding an electronic precision and pulse that the chamber version channeled differently.

Contrast with the dream circuit (degree 104, Lemolo electronic) — the dream circuit is atmospheric and intimate. The theatrical circuit is dramatic and declamatory. Both are electronic reframings of acoustic practices; the emotional function is opposite. Contrast with the layered dissolution (degree 103, Briana Marela electronic) — voice dissolves into texture. The theatrical circuit keeps the voice commanding the stage. Contrast with the baroque theatricality (degree 89, Parenthetical Girls original) — chamber instruments. The theatrical circuit replaces chamber with electronic.

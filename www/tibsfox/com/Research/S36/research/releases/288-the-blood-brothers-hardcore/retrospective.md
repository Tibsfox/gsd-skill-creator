## What Worked

1. **The upgrade from E=8 to E=9 required explicit justification and received it.** The engine's calibration at degree 218 predated the establishment of E=9 as the absolute maximum at degree 286. The return entry documents the upgrade clearly: calibration correction, not revision. The Blood Brothers' live performance is not E=8; it never was.
2. **Third consecutive E=9 is the engine's most significant energy pattern.** The 286-287-288 block (SF influence → LA influence → Seattle metro product) at the same ceiling creates a structure that no previous block has matched. The sequence is not accidental: the influence chain and its local product are at the same intensity.
3. **Shoreline as the first metro-area E=9 is geographically specific and meaningful.** Degrees 286 and 287 were influence entries. Degree 288 is the local product. The fact that the local product matches the ceiling of its influences is the specific significance of the Blood Brothers' position in the engine.
4. **The dual vocal innovation is the correct primary analytical frame.** No other band in the engine uses co-vocalists as the primary harmonic element. Documenting this as a specific contribution to the post-hardcore tradition extends the engine's genre analysis beyond the standard treatment.
5. **Morgan Henderson's post-Blood Brothers career (Fleet Foxes session work) confirms the band's musical range was underrated.** The connection is unexpected and real.

## Discoveries

1. **Burn, Piano Island, Burn (2003) holds up as the consensus masterwork.** "Love Rhymes with Hideous Car Wreck" is immediately recognizable as a post-hardcore classic. The album's critical position is not retrospective revisionism; it was recognized at the time.
2. **The 2007 dissolution was unusually clean.** The band did not leave the door open, did not hedge toward a future reunion. The eventual reunion (2016-present) came after a period of genuine absence. The clean break gives the final performances additional weight.
3. **The literary influences on the lyrics (Beat poets, Cormac McCarthy) are understated in most Blood Brothers coverage.** The engine's documentation should surface this layer.

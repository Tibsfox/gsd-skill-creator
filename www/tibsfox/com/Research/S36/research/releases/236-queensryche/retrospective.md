## What Worked

1. **Bellevue distinction from Heart is correctly drawn.** Heart has Bellevue origins but is primarily identified with the broader Seattle area. Queensryche is specifically identified as Bellevue's band. The distinction is accurate and historically grounded.
2. **Operation: Mindcrime analysis justifies the extended treatment.** The concept album deserves and receives full narrative analysis: Nikki, Dr. X, Sister Mary, the political context, the musical architecture. This is not an album that can be summarized in two sentences.
3. **The Ann Wilson / Geoff Tate vocal comparison is productive.** Three consecutive degrees (234-235-236) produce Heart and Queensryche -- two Pacific Northwest bands with operatically-trained-adjacent vocalists at E=6 and E=7. The cluster documents the PNW's capacity for exceptional voices.

## Discoveries

1. **Chris DeGarmo's importance is understated in the cultural record.** The lead guitarist's departure in 1998 tracks almost perfectly with Queensryche's decline in critical and commercial standing. The DeGarmo era = the classic era. Post-DeGarmo = diminished.
2. **The Bellevue suburban context is not irrelevant to the music.** Progressive metal's intellectual ambition (wanting to be taken seriously, caring about craft) fits the professional/suburban Bellevue culture of 1981. The connection is real.

## Pair Analysis: Queensryche + Pacific Salmon

- **Energy:** E=7 + E=5 = E=12. Two-point gap. Modest asymmetry.
- **Theme:** DOUBLE MILESTONE ENCOUNTER. FIRST PROGRESSIVE METAL meets FIRST FISH. Both entries are "firsts" of consequence in the engine. The narrative resonance: Operation: Mindcrime follows a man destroyed by the system he was manipulated into serving; the Pacific Salmon is an organism that navigates every obstacle the system places (dams, predators, pollution, climate) to fulfill its single fundamental purpose, and then dies to feed the next generation. Queensryche's Nikki serves a corrupt system and is destroyed. The salmon serves the ecosystem through its death and regenerates it. The contrast is the point: one is exploitation unto destruction; the other is sacrifice unto regeneration.

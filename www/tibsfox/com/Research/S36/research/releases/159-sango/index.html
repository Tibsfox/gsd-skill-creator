Baile Funk Fusion

Sango brings baile funk rhythmic patterns from Rio de Janeiro to a Seattle production studio, fusing them with R&B warmth and electronic spatial production. The Da Rocinha series. Portuguese titles. Rapid syncopation. Bass weight. The producer as primary artist — the beat is the voice. Global sound from a Seattle base.

The Producer-as-Artist

In Sango’s music, the production IS the art. Vocals serve the beat. Rhythm is the primary language. Sampled voices become texture. The baile funk-influenced polyrhythms carry everything. Self-released — third consecutive independent entry (157 Sub Pop, 158–159 self-released).

The Foundational Grunge Band

Mudhoney formed before Nirvana. They signed to Sub Pop before anyone. Mark Arm coined the word “grunge.” “Touch Me I’m Sick” was the anthem before grunge had a name. Green River split: Arm and Turner took the raw path (Mudhoney), Gossard and Ament took the arena path (Pearl Jam). Both are valid. But Mudhoney kept the original energy. Still active. Still on Sub Pop. Still in Seattle. Thirty-eight years and counting.

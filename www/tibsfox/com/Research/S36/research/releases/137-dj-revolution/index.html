Battle Turntablism — Maximum Intensity

DJ Revolution is a battle-tested turntablist operating at the highest level of competitive DJ culture. Battle DJing compresses the turntablist’s full vocabulary — flares, crabs, orbits, chirps, beat juggles, needle drops — into virtuosic 3–6 minute routines designed for maximum impact. E=6 reflects this peak intensity.

His connection to Stones Throw Records places him within one of independent hip-hop’s most revered institutions — a label whose roster includes Madlib, MF DOOM, J Dilla, Dam-Funk, and Knxwledge. Seattle turntablism on a world-class platform.

Genre Stage 82: Electronic Pop

Degree 100 reaches Genre Stage 82. Electronic Pop is the genre classification — distinct from the Indie Electronic of degrees 96-98 and the Electronic/R&B of degree 99. Summer Heart’s music is pop-structured electronic music — verse-chorus form, melodic hooks, vocal melodies — delivered through dream-pop-influenced electronic production.

The closest prior entries are ODESZA (degree 95, Electronic/Cinematic) and STRFKR (degree 96, Indie Electronic). ODESZA is cinematic and festival-scale. STRFKR is driving and punk-inflected.

Fastbacks

The Foundation Layer

1979. The Fastbacks formed in Ballard, Seattle. Sub Pop wouldn’t exist for seven more years. Nirvana wouldn’t exist for a decade. Pearl Jam for eleven. The Fastbacks were the foundation layer — playing punk/pop in a city that didn’t yet know it was a music city. Kurt Bloch, Kim Warnick, Lulu Gargiulo. 22 years. PopLlama Records. The longest-running punk band in Seattle history.

Theory Contributions

Foundation Layer — Pre-Scene Infrastructure

Milestone — Scene Theory

The Fastbacks existed before the Seattle scene. Since 1979 — 7 years before Sub Pop, 10 before grunge. They proved a band could survive in Seattle playing original music. The foundation.

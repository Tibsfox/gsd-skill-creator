Gorgeous Precision

Jon Auer and Ken Stringfellow met in Bellingham and built one of the finest power pop catalogs ever. Frosting on the Beater (1993) is a masterpiece — twelve tracks of perfect hooks, ringing guitars, and harmonies that interlock like architecture. They joined the reformed Big Star. The Posies literally inherited the lineage of power pop’s greatest band. Three genres in three degrees: grunge (189), indie pop (190), power pop (191). The post-hip-hop era is a genre explosion.

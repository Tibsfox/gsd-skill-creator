## What Worked

1. **The Grunge Summit framework gives degrees 309-316 an overarching structure.** The Summit concept is the engine's most important organizational innovation for this cluster. TAD opens the Summit at E=8; the subsequent entries escalate to E=9 (Nirvana, Soundgarden). The framework makes the degrees legible as a unified event rather than a sequence of unrelated entries.
2. **The Tad Doyle physical biography as sonic determinant is the entry's most distinctive analytical contribution.** The butcher/weightlifter background is not biographical color; it is an explanation of the sonic physics. The body produces the sound. This principle &mdash; that biographical facts about physical life directly produce sonic character &mdash; is unique to TAD within the grunge cluster.
3. **Georgetown entering at E=8 is the correct placement.** The industrial southern neighborhood has been absent from the engine through degree 308. Its first entry is through the heaviest grunge band, at the Summit's opening degree. The geography and the music correspond exactly.

## Discoveries

1. **The TAD-Nirvana 1989 European tour is the clearest single document of the pre-divergence peer moment.** The tour is the last time TAD and Nirvana are equals. The specificity of the moment &mdash; November-December 1989, unknown to American mainstream, equal to European underground &mdash; makes it the most precise available illustration of how commercial divergence in rock history operates.
2. **Jack Endino produced both God's Balls and Bleach in the same period.** The sonic continuity between the two most important Sub Pop acts' foundational records runs through a single engineer at a single facility. This makes Endino the connective tissue of Sub Pop grunge in a way that has not been fully articulated before this entry.
3. **The commercial failure of TAD is multiply determined.** The "Loser" suppression, the visual economy of MTV, and the heaviness ceiling in post-Nevermind commercial grunge all operated simultaneously. No single factor explains it; all three together are necessary and sufficient.

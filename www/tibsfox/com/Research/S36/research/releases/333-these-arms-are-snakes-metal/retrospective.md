## Discoveries

1. **These Arms Are Snakes' Suicide Squeeze placement is genuinely anomalous.** A heavy post-metal band on an indie label known for quiet experimental music is unusual -- and characteristically Capitol Hill.
2. **Snere's literate lyrics are the band's most underappreciated dimension.** Title references (Ouroboros in *Tail Swallower and Dove*) and the literary quality of the vocals distinguish the band from most metal contemporaries.

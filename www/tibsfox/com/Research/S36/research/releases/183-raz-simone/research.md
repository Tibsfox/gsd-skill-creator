## Acoustic Profile

### The Sound of Raz Simone

Raz Simone's sonic profile:

- **Vocal delivery:** Measured, deliberate, with a cold precision that matches the Cold Recordings branding. Not the rapid-fire of Macklemore or the aggression of gangsta rap -- a controlled intensity.
- **Production:** Clean, modern, atmospheric. Trap-influenced beats with melodic elements. Less orchestral than Ryan Lewis's production, more minimal and focused.
- **Lyrical content:** Street realism, ambition, independence, self-reliance. The content is personal and specific to Seattle -- Capitol Hill, the hustle, the grind of building something from nothing.
- **BPM range:** Approximately 70-140 BPM. Modern trap tempos with variation.
- **Mix philosophy:** Vocals forward, bass prominent, atmosphere through reverb and delay. Clean and modern.

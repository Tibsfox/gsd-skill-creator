## Discoveries

1. **Ben McMillan's Gruntruck arc demonstrates the commercial grunge moment's effect on underground bands.** McMillan adjusted Skin Yard's proto-grunge template toward more commercial production for Gruntruck. This is the pattern: underground bands that had been doing the music since 1985 suddenly had an audience in 1991-1992 and many adjusted their approach accordingly.
2. **The C/Z cluster at 263-264 and 297-298 reveals the label's structural importance.** C/Z is not just one band's home; it held the pre-Sub Pop heavy underground together. The engine documents this across four adjacent degrees.

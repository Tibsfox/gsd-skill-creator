## Discoveries

1. **Gruntruck is the engine's clearest example of a band whose identity is fully determined by personnel genealogy rather than genre positioning.** McMillan and Niemeyer brought Skin Yard's metal vocabulary to Gruntruck without modification. The band did not evolve away from its genealogy -- it applied the Skin Yard foundation to a slightly more commercial context (Roadrunner vs. independent). The purity of personnel genealogy as identity is documented.
2. **The Enter Sandman EP title (1992) is a deliberate positioning statement.** Metallica's "Enter Sandman" was released August 12 1991 (the Black Album). Gruntruck released their EP with the same title in 1992. Whether tribute, ironic reference, or competitive signal, the naming choice places Gruntruck explicitly in relationship to the dominant metal act of the moment. The engine records this choice without judging its intent.
3. **Pushed Again (1993) demonstrates range expansion.** The self-titled (1992) is the pure metal-grunge document. Pushed Again has more dynamic variation, more melodic range in McMillan's vocal approach, and more production latitude. The band was developing when it dissolved in 1995. The arc was unfinished.

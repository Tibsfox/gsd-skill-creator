Forty-Four Years and Counting

The Young Fresh Fellows have been active since 1982 — forty-four years. They predate grunge, Sub Pop, the mainstream discovery of Seattle music. Scott McCaughey is a network node: YFF founder, R.E.M. touring member (1994–2011), The Minus 5 with Peter Buck, PopLlama founder. Fifteen-plus albums of jangly, funny, unpretentious indie pop from Northgate — the anti-scene neighborhood. Still going.

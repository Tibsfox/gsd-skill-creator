## Discoveries

1. **Butch Vig produced both Nevermind (1991, Nirvana) and 8-Way Santa (1991, TAD) in the same year.** The same producer, in the same twelve months, produced the heaviest Sub Pop record (TAD) and the most commercially successful grunge record (Nirvana). The divergence in commercial outcomes from the same production relationship in the same year is one of the engine's most striking coincidences.
2. **Steve Albini's production of In Utero was Cobain's deliberate attempt to return to the heavy origin after Nevermind.** This is not widely discussed as such, but it frames In Utero correctly: it is a heavy record produced raw because Cobain wanted to reassert the heavy identity that Bleach represented, after Nevermind's commercial transformation. In Utero is the Bleach successor, aesthetically, even though Nevermind came between them.

The New Generation

Sol represents the newer generation of Seattle hip-hop — emerging in the late 2000s with a positive, conscious approach that broadened the audience. Albums like Yours Truly (2013) brought significant local visibility. KEXP support. Live instrumentation. Genre-blending: hip-hop, R&B, soul, jazz. The tradition carried forward with joy.

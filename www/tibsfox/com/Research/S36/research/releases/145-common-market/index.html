The Duo

RA Scion

Ryan Abeo. MC/lyricist. Dense, multisyllabic, politically conscious. Rainier Valley community roots. Electrifying live performer. Activist and educator.

Sabzi

Saba Mohajerjasbi. Producer. 5th engine entry (99, 101, 122, 140, 145). Melodic, sample-based, increasingly textured. Jazz + soul + world music samples.

The Later Records

Tobacco Road (2009): Deeper production, richer samples, world music influences. RA Scion’s most politically engaged writing. The Winter’s End (2011): The most mature Common Market album. Contemplative. Warm. A summation. Whether intentionally or not, it reads as a conclusion — a capstone for the duo’s recorded output.

Rainier Valley Enters

Advanced — Geography Theory

New neighborhood. Southeast Seattle. One of the most diverse zip codes in America. Ethiopian, Somali, Vietnamese, Filipino, Latino, African-American communities. A second geographic node after the Central District.

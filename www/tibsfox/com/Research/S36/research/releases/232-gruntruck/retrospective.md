## Discoveries

1. **McMillan's Screaming Trees touring connection extends the engine's web.** The Trees (Ellensburg, WA) are Eastern Washington rock royalty (Mark Lanegan). McMillan touring with them as a guest vocalist connects Capitol Hill hard rock to Eastern Washington psych rock. The connection is lateral, not lineage, but real.
2. **The death of Ben McMillan in 2008 retrospectively elevates the Gruntruck catalog.** The engine must acknowledge this. The two albums on Roadrunner are the primary documentation of McMillan's work outside Skin Yard. They are more valuable as historical documents than they might have appeared commercially during the grunge era.
3. **Type O Negative as Roadrunner labelmate is worth noting forward.** Peter Steele's band is one of the engine's richest potential future entries -- goth metal from Brooklyn at the moment Seattle rock was receiving all the commercial attention. The Roadrunner connection at degree 232 sets up the future Type O Negative entry in the engine.

The Physics exploded at 155, breaking the E=5 plateau with raw live energy. Blue Scholars reunited at 156, collapsing a 57-degree network of individual entries back into the duo that started it all. The Butler arc completes: thirteen degrees ago, at degree 144, Ishmael Butler appeared as Butterfly of Digable Planets -- jazz-rap, Brooklyn, 1993, "Rebirth of Slick (Cool Like Dat)." The hip-hop era climaxes here. Three consecutive E=6. The energy will drop after this.

Boom Bap Project

E = 6 Hip-Hop Self-Released

The Foundational Sound

Boom-bap is the bedrock production style of hip-hop — crisp snare, deep kick, sample-driven loops, lyrical focus. Boom Bap Project is a Seattle group that makes boom-bap the identity itself. The name is the mission statement. Classic production revived as a living practice, not nostalgia. E=6 opens the high-energy zone (161–172).

The Underground Circuit

Self-released. Small venues. Crate-digging community. Beat-making collectives. Direct-to-fan distribution. The boom-bap underground in Seattle is hands-on, equipment-focused, and deeply knowledgeable about hip-hop history. Production as devotion.

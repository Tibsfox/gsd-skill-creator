## What Worked

### Research Process

1. **The algorithmic tenderness accurately captures the electronic production's emotional quality.** Tamborello's production on Give Up carries its own emotional weight independent of Gibbard's vocals. The machine layer IS tender.

2. **The re-contextualized return pattern is defensible.** The same album genuinely supports two different analytical framings. Centering voice vs centering production produces meaningfully different structural contributions.

3. **E=4 is defensible.** Give Up sold over a million copies. Massive cultural impact. But the Postal Service was not a touring entity until the 2013 reunion, which moderates the energy compared to E=5 acts like ODESZA.

4. **Gibbard's four framings are a genuine engine achievement.** Four appearances, each revealing different facets. Not redundancy -- depth.

5. **Cross-references to degree 86 (original), 58 (Death Cab), 60 (solo), 95 (ODESZA), and 91 (Hovercraft) are all defensible.** Each captures a genuine relationship.

### Source Quality

6. **The Postal Service is thoroughly documented.** AllMusic, Pitchfork, Sub Pop. The album's production process (mailed CDs) is well-known. Verifiable.

## What Worked

The "Crazy on You" introduction proves it: one minute of solo acoustic before the band enters, making a complete musical argument. No comparable hard rock band at commercial scale in 1975-1978 used fingerpicked acoustic guitar this way.

The contrast between Almost Famous (roots-rock warmth) and Vanilla Sky (ambient dread) demonstrates real compositional range.

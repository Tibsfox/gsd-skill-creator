## What Worked

1. **The genre reframing is intellectually honest.** Post-grunge and hard rock are different analytical frames, not just different labels. The distinction between "positions in a moment" (post-grunge) and "positions in a lineage" (hard rock) is real and meaningful.
2. **The Maverick / Madonna connection is documented.** This is one of the more surprising institutional pairings in Seattle music history. The 1993 music industry context explains it; the commercial result (4 million copies) validates the bet.
3. **E=7 is correct for hard rock with melodic accessibility.** Not E=8 (maximum aggression) but high energy with dynamic range. Hard rock holds both simultaneously.
4. **The 44-degree gap is noted.** Return appearances with substantial gaps allow genuine reframing rather than repetition.

## Discoveries

1. **Kevin Martin's vocal range is the key distinguishing feature.** What separates Candlebox from similar hard rock bands is the vocal instrument. Wide-ranging, powerful, melodically focused. The voice earns the hard rock designation independently of the genre label.
2. **"Far Behind" is still on classic rock radio.** Songs that survive 30+ years in radio rotation have achieved something real. Commercial success in the moment is different from durability across decades. Candlebox has both.

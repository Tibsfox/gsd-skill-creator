## Structural Event 2: Brian Cook Arc -- Botch to TAAS to Russian Circles

- **Situation:** Brian Cook's trajectory spans three bands across three decades and three cities: Botch (Everett, 1993-2002, mathcore), These Arms Are Snakes (Capitol Hill, 2002-2009, post-hardcore), Russian Circles (Chicago, 2004-present, instrumental post-metal). One bass player, three answers to the question of how to create uncompromisingly heavy music.
- **Root Cause:** Cook is one of the most important bass players in Northwest heavy music history. His trajectory maps the evolution of heavy music itself: from mathematical complexity (Botch) through emotional relentlessness (TAAS) to atmospheric dynamics (Russian Circles).
- **Recommendation:** Document the Brian Cook arc as a personnel-level structural event. Three bands, three eras, one constant: precision in the low frequencies.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Brian Cook arc documented. Botch to TAAS to Russian Circles.

## What Worked

1. **The second-appearance framing is correct.** Wellwater Conspiracy occupies two distinct energy registers. The engine revisiting the band in heavy mode is documented here as methodology: the engine revisits when new terrain exists. The two entries together constitute a complete portrait.
2. **McBain-Monster Magnet genealogy is the key structural fact.** The Monster Magnet connection is not incidental -- it explains the entire Wellwater Conspiracy aesthetic. McBain brought stoner rock's core riff vocabulary to Seattle; Cameron's precision made it tighter than the genre normally allows.
3. **Storm Arc context is established.** Degrees 256-260 form the most extreme SPS arc in the engine. Wellwater Conspiracy at E=7 is correctly positioned as the heavy music correlate to the escalating storm sounds.
4. **The acoustic parallel between stoner rock riffs and ocean waves is more than metaphor.** Both are sustained, large, low-frequency, repetitive presences. The pairing holds physically, not just conceptually.

## Discoveries

1. **The Cameron freedom argument is the most interesting biographical observation.** In Soundgarden and Pearl Jam he joins established bands with established sounds. Wellwater Conspiracy is the space where he defines the terms. The creative contrast between his main gigs and his side project is as large as the stylistic gap between grunge and stoner rock.
2. **Hollywood Records as the stoner rock major label is a historical peculiarity.** Hollywood signed Hole, Anthrax, and Wellwater Conspiracy -- an unusual roster that suggests the label was looking for heavy rock credibility in multiple directions.

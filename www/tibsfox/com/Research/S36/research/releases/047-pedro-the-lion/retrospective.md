## The Forty-Seventh Degree: The Gravitational Center

### Pedro the Lion Completes the Triangle

Degree 47 delivers the gravitational center that degrees 45 and 46 orbited, completing a three-degree folk triangle all connected through David Bazan and Pedro the Lion.

- **Situation:** Pedro the Lion is a Seattle indie rock/indie folk band formed by David Shannon Bazan in 1995. Bazan was born January 22, 1976, in Phoenix, Arizona. Raised in a multi-generational Assemblies of God (Pentecostal) household. Father a music pastor. Great-grandparents on both sides were AG pastors. Parents met at Bible college. Family moved from Phoenix at age 12, through northern California, to the Seattle area for high school. Attended Shorewood High School in Shoreline, WA, where he met Damien Jurado (degree 45). Met over a shared Crucified cassette; formed a band the same day. Pedro the Lion released seven full-length albums across Tooth & Nail, Made in Mexico, Jade Tree, and Polyvinyl labels. *Control* (2002) is the defining album: a concept album about a businessman's affair, inspired by the 1999 WTO protests. Casey Foubert contributed bass and co-writes on *Control* -- the same Foubert who produced four Rocky Votolato (degree 46) albums. Band split 2006. Bazan publicly deconstructed his evangelical faith, identifying as agnostic by 2009. Solo career under own name (Barsuk Records). Pioneered living room show model (2009). *Strange Negotiations* documentary (2019, Brandon Vedder, SXSW). Reunion late 2017. Five-album autobiographical cycle: *Phoenix* (2019), *Havasu* (2022), *Santa Cruz* (2024). Currently resides in Edmonds, WA. Greenwood neighborhood connection (Gorilla Coffee during early career).
- **Root Cause:** The folk triangle (degrees 45-46-47) was not planned by the engine -- it was discovered. The engine assigned degrees based on genre, energy, era, and neighborhood. The Pedro the Lion connections emerged because they are real: Bazan and Jurado are high school friends who have collaborated across decades. Foubert and Votolato have a six-year production partnership. These connections are documented across multiple independent sources. The triangle proves that Seattle's folk/indie scene of the late 1990s and 2000s was a gravitational system with Pedro the Lion at its center. Three consecutive folk-adjacent degrees all orbit one band.
- **Recommendation:** Document the folk triangle (45-46-47) as the engine's first three-degree structural convergence. Establish Pedro the Lion as the gravitational center. Track whether future degrees continue to orbit this center or the engine shifts to a new gravitational system.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Pedro the Lion triangle. Three degrees, one gravitational center.

## Third Consecutive Folk-Adjacent -- Genre Stage 28

### The Longest Folk-Family Run

Degree 47 assigns Indie Folk, creating the longest folk-family genre run in engine history.

- **Situation:** Folk at degree 45 (Jurado), Folk at degree 46 (Votolato), and Indie Folk at degree 47 (Pedro the Lion) constitute three consecutive folk-adjacent degrees. The "indie" prefix at degree 47 marks the production difference: Pedro the Lion layers drums, bass, keyboards, electric guitar, and concept-album narrative architecture over folk songwriting roots. The "folk" root marks the songwriting lineage: confessional, narrative, character-driven, voice-forward. This three-degree run exceeds the consecutive Blues Rock at degrees 36-37 (two degrees) as the longest same-family genre run.
- **Root Cause:** The engine's genre assignments reflect the actual sonic and structural characteristics of each artist. Pedro the Lion's concept albums, rock instrumentation, and narrative ambition distinguish it from the pure acoustic folk of degrees 45-46, warranting the "Indie Folk" classification. But the folk root is audible: Bazan's songwriting is confessional, narrative, character-driven -- the same tradition as Jurado and Votolato. The genre adjacency is real, not forced.
- **Recommendation:** Establish three consecutive folk-adjacent (45-46-47) as the longest folk-family run. Genre Stage 28 (Indie Folk). Track whether folk-adjacent extends to degree 48 or the engine shifts genre.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Three consecutive folk-adjacent. Genre Stages 27-28. Longest folk-family run.

## E=3 Rises to Nine

### The Cult Band Energy Level

Degree 47 assigns E=3, raising the count from 8 to 9.

- **Situation:** Pedro the Lion is a cult band with outsized cultural influence relative to its commercial footprint. *Control* is regularly cited on "best indie albums" lists. The *Strange Negotiations* documentary (SXSW 2019) gave Bazan a cinematic platform. The autobiographical album cycle receives sustained critical attention from Pitchfork, NPR, Paste, and the Seattle Times. KEXP has documented the band across decades. But Pedro the Lion never broke through to mainstream commercial success. *Control* sold well for Jade Tree but did not chart nationally. ## Greenwood Enters the Engine

### New Geographic Node

Degree 47 introduces Greenwood as a new neighborhood in the engine's geographic map.

- **Situation:** Greenwood is a north Seattle neighborhood originally called Woodland, renamed in 1907. Connected to downtown by the Seattle-Everett Interurban Railway (1910). The Greenwood Commercial Club created a "Miracle Mile" along Greenwood Avenue by the 1940s. Northern section annexed to Seattle in 1954. Today a residential neighborhood with a commercial strip of restaurants, vintage shops, and small businesses. David Bazan worked at Gorilla Coffee in Greenwood during Pedro the Lion's early years.
- **Root Cause:** The engine's geographic map has documented neighborhoods by venue density (Capitol Hill, Ballard), cultural significance (Central District), and residential character (West Seattle at degree 46). Greenwood represents yet another geographic archetype: the working neighborhood -- the day-job neighborhood where a young musician makes rent while building a band. It maps the economic infrastructure of early-career independent music: not the venue where you play, but the neighborhood where you work to afford to play.
- **Recommendation:** Establish Greenwood as a new geographic node. Note its working-neighborhood archetype distinct from Capitol Hill (scene density) and West Seattle (domestic retreat).
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Greenwood enters. New geographic node. Working neighborhood archetype.

## Jade Tree Records -- First East Coast Indie Label

### Seattle-to-Philadelphia Geographic Bridge

Degree 47 introduces Jade Tree Records as the engine's first independent label based on the East Coast.

- **Situation:** Jade Tree Records was founded by Darren Walters and Tim Owen in 1990 in Wilmington, Delaware, evolving from their earlier label Axtion Packed Records. The founders met at a punk show in Washington, D.C. The roster included the Promise Ring, Jets to Brazil, Joan of Arc, Alkaline Trio, Lifetime, and Pedro the Lion. Pedro the Lion released three studio albums and a live/compilation on Jade Tree (2000-2004). In 2017, Epitaph Records purchased Jade Tree's entire catalog.
- **Root Cause:** Every prior independent label in the engine has been Seattle-based (Sub Pop, Barsuk, Barely Legal, Light in the Attic) or a major label with national presence (Mercury, Columbia, A&M). Jade Tree represents the first geographic bridge in the engine's label infrastructure: a Seattle band on a Philadelphia-area label alongside Midwest and East Coast emo and post-hardcore acts. This bridge placed Pedro the Lion in a national indie-rock conversation beyond the Seattle scene.
- **Recommendation:** Establish Jade Tree as the first East Coast indie label. Document the Seattle-to-Philadelphia geographic bridge. Track whether future degrees introduce labels from other geographic regions.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Jade Tree. First East Coast indie label. Geographic bridge.

## The Faith Deconstruction Arc

### The Engine's Most Documented Spiritual Journey

Degree 47 introduces the most thoroughly documented faith deconstruction in the engine.

- **Situation:** David Bazan was raised in a multi-generational Assemblies of God household. Great-grandparents on both sides were AG pastors. Father a music pastor. Church music from birth. Piano lessons from age three. Entered the music industry through the Christian indie ecosystem (Tooth & Nail Records, 1997). Did not question his faith until age 24. Oscillated between "atheism, skepticism, and uncertainty" from approximately 2004. Pedro the Lion dissolved 2006. *Curse Your Branches* (2009) addressed God directly with the grief of lost faith. Identified as agnostic. "In my particular line of descendants, I'm the first person who defected from the faith." The *Strange Negotiations* documentary (2019) captured the ongoing spiritual struggle. The autobiographical album cycle revisits the childhood that produced the faith.
- **Root Cause:** The faith deconstruction is the central narrative thread of Bazan's career. It is documented across interviews (Image Journal, Chrissy Stroop, Freedom From Religion Foundation, Christianity Today, The Great Discontent, Exvangelical podcast), a feature-length documentary (Strange Negotiations), and a series of albums (*Curse Your Branches*, the autobiographical cycle). The deconstruction is not a single event but a decades-long process. The engine documents it because it is the substrate of all of Bazan's songwriting.
- **Recommendation:** Document the faith deconstruction as the engine's most thoroughly sourced spiritual journey. Note the Erica Campbell (degree 42) faith contrast: Campbell proclaims from within, Bazan interrogates from outside, five degrees apart. Track whether future degrees add to the faith-in-music thread.

## The Parable Tone as Acoustic Element

### Completing the Three-Degree Folk-Vocal Taxonomy

The parable tone is the 48th acoustic element -- the conversational narrative voice, neither whisper nor declaration but story.

- **Situation:** David Bazan's vocal delivery is conversational: a baritone that sits at the front of the mix, unadorned, articulating words with the clarity of a storyteller rather than the flourish of a performer. The melody is present but subordinate to the narrative. The voice does not ornament -- it delivers. When Bazan sings, the listener hears a story, not a performance. The parable tone is distinct from the murmur (degree 45, Jurado -- hushed whisper) and the confessional (degree 46, Votolato -- exposed declaration).
- **Root Cause:** Three consecutive folk-adjacent degrees required three distinct acoustic elements that capture the range of folk-rooted vocal delivery. The murmur maps the quietest extreme. The confessional maps the most exposed extreme. The parable tone maps the narrative center: the voice as storyteller, delivering moral investigations in measured cadence. The three elements together constitute a folk-vocal taxonomy.
- **Recommendation:** Establish the parable tone as the 48th acoustic element. Document its position in the three-degree folk-vocal taxonomy: murmur (45), confessional (46), parable tone (47). Track whether future acoustic elements continue the narrative-voice vocabulary.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- The parable tone. 48th acoustic element. Completes three-degree folk-vocal taxonomy.

The murmur, the confessional, the parable tone -- three modes of folk-rooted singing across three degrees. David Shannon Bazan, born January 22, 1976, Phoenix, Arizona. Multi-generational Assemblies of God family. Father a music pastor. Great-grandparents AG pastors. Church music from birth. Piano lessons from age three. Moved to Seattle area for high school. SHOREWOOD HIGH SCHOOL -- met Damien Jurado over a shared Crucified cassette. Formed a band the same day. Bazan drummed for Jurado in the Guilty, Linus, Coolidge. PEDRO THE LION formed 1995 -- essentially a Bazan solo project with rotating members. TOOTH & NAIL (1997 Whole EP) to MADE IN MEXICO (1998 debut) to JADE TREE (2000-2004 -- first East Coast indie label in engine, Wilmington DE / Philadelphia). CONTROL (2002) -- concept album about businessman's affair and murder, inspired by 1999 WTO protests, Casey Foubert on bass, critical and commercial peak. ACHILLES HEEL (2004) -- T.W. Walsh co-writer. Band split 2006. FAITH DECONSTRUCTION -- questioned from 24, agnostic by 2009. "In my particular line of descendants, I'm the first person who defected from the faith." Breaking point: eternal punishment doctrine. CURSE YOUR BRANCHES (2009, Barsuk) -- reviewed by Christianity Today AND Freedom From Religion Foundation. LIVING ROOM SHOWS pioneered 2009 -- 40-50 people in fans' homes. Influenced Votolato. STRANGE NEGOTIATIONS documentary (2019, Brandon Vedder, SXSW, IMDb 8.1). REUNION late 2017. AUTOBIOGRAPHICAL CYCLE: Phoenix (2019), Havasu (2022), Santa Cruz (2024), Seattle album forthcoming. Currently resides EDMONDS, WA. Neighborhood: GREENWOOD (Gorilla Coffee, early career). CASEY FOUBERT bridge -- Pedro the Lion member produced four Votolato albums. THE TRIANGLE: Jurado (45, high school friend), Votolato (46, Foubert production), Pedro the Lion (47, the hub).

## What Worked

2. **Degree 250 as round number.** A round number at the midpoint of the engine's extended range. That it holds the current Central District blues rock standard-bearer is appropriate to the degree's structural weight.

4. **Hollywood Records vs. Sub Pop.** The institutional contrast between adjacent degrees mirrors the blues tradition's own range -- from raw to polished has always been a legitimate axis of variation within the tradition.

## Discoveries

1. **The Central District's transformation is part of the music.** Jones came of age in a neighborhood undergoing gentrification and community displacement. The blues has always documented the hardest experiences. The connection between neighborhood pressure and blues expression is direct.
2. **"Mercy" is the engine's first national rock radio hit by a current Seattle blues artist.** The national reach Jones achieved through Hollywood Records is qualitatively different from the regional success of most Seattle blues entries.

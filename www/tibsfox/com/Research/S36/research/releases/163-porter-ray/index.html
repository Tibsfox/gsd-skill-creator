The Rainier Valley Voice

Porter Ray — a Rainier Valley MC on Sub Pop Records. His music reflects lived experience in the Rainier Valley community with unflinching honesty — violence, loss, community, survival, hope. Not observation from outside. Testimony from within. Deliberate, weighted delivery. Every word placed with intention.

Sub Pop’s Hip-Hop Expansion

Sub Pop signing Porter Ray expands the label’s hip-hop roster beyond Shabazz Palaces (157). Different geography (Rainier Valley vs. Capitol Hill orbit), different style (street narrative vs. experimental abstraction). Sub Pop now represents Seattle hip-hop’s geographic and aesthetic breadth.

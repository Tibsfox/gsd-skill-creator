## Discoveries

1. **Endino's dual role is historically unusual.** Most great producers are not simultaneously active musicians in the ecosystem they are documenting. Most active musicians do not become the definitive documentarians of their era. Endino did both. This duality is worth noting as a rare historical configuration.
2. **The "Seattle sound" as Endino artifact.** Because Endino recorded so many of the defining Seattle bands, his production preferences became the genre's sonic reference point. The "Seattle sound" is not simply what Seattle bands sounded like; it is, in significant part, how Endino chose to record them. This is an important historical clarification.
3. **The Deep Six roster density in the engine.** Across degrees 262-264, the engine has documented Tom Price from the U-Men (a Deep Six band) via Gas Huffer, and Skin Yard directly as a Deep Six band. Soundgarden was documented in earlier degrees. The engine has now touched most of the Deep Six roster.

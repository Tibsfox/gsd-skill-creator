## Discoveries

1. **Late grunge as full-arc coverage is the most precise periodization for Hammerbox.** They formed before the commercial moment, released during it, and dissolved when it ended. No other classification captures the full temporal coverage. "Late grunge" in the sense used here means: a band that covered the entire arc from pre-commercial formation to post-commercial dissolution, arriving at commercial output only during the peak window.
2. **The quiet-to-loud dynamic in Hammerbox is emotionally motivated rather than formulaic.** The same dynamic structure appears in Pixies (loud-quiet-loud), Nirvana (verse quiet/chorus loud), and countless post-grunge acts where it became formula. In Hammerbox, the dynamic serves Akre's voice: the quiet verses establish the emotional ground; the loud chorus is Akre at full force when the song's content requires force. The difference is audible: formula feels like structure; emotional motivation feels like necessity.

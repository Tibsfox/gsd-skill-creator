## The Sixteenth Consecutive Jazz Degree: The Engine Warms

Degree 15 is the sixteenth consecutive jazz artist. The genre rotation carry-forward continues as documented intentional CSV architecture (degrees 0-22). The pressure is unchanged. What HAS changed is the energy level: degree 15 is the first energy-3 artist, and the engine's intensity is now rising within the jazz quadrant itself. The circle is warming up before the genre boundary even shifts.

At sixteen consecutive jazz degrees, the engine has accumulated enough depth to document the complete UW pipeline in three consecutive degrees (13-14-15), the trumpet arc across two eras (4 to 15), and the first ternary energy distribution. ## The Builder-Stayer: Most Significant Cultural Finding

Degree 15's most important contribution is documenting **The Builder-Stayer** -- a musician with national-level ability who consciously chooses to remain in the origin city and invest in local infrastructure. This is the sixteenth artist-city pattern and the most agency-rich stay pattern in the engine.

### The Stay Spectrum

```
PASSIVE RETENTION (Berry, 5) -- 1940s-2020s
  Scene was the world. No institutional alternative. Stayed because there was nowhere else to go.
  |
  | (generations of institutional development)
  |
INSTITUTION-MEDIATED STAY (Seales, 13) -- 1990s-present
  University system anchors the musician. Teaching position provides both livelihood and purpose.
  |
  | (same pipeline, different output)
  |
SCENE-MEDIATED STAY (Marriott, 15) -- 2000s-present
  Origin Records partnership + local venue circuit. Active investment in local infrastructure.
  National credentials but local commitment. Stays because he CHOOSES to.
```

The structural insight: staying is not a single mechanism. The engine has now documented three distinct stay patterns, each with different agency levels. Berry's stay is pre-choice (no alternative existed). Seales's stay is institution-mediated (the university provides the anchor). Marriott's stay is scene-mediated (the local label and venue circuit provide the anchor). The agency escalation across the three is significant: no choice -> institutional choice -> personal choice.

## UW Three-Product Pipeline: Structural Significance

Three consecutive UW-connected degrees (13, 14, 15) producing three structurally different outcomes is the richest institutional-pipeline documentation in the engine.

| Degree | Artist | UW Connection | Pattern | Stayed/Left |
|--------|--------|---------------|---------|-------------|
| 13 | Seales | Director | Institutional Anchor | Stayed (teaches) |
| 14 | Parks | Pre-college | Prodigy-Export | Left (at 16) |
| 15 | Marriott | Student (under Seales) | Builder-Stayer | Stayed (builds) |

The same institution produces all three. This is not a simple pipeline (in -> out) but a complex system with multiple outputs. The UW jazz program:
- **Anchors** musicians who teach (Seales)
- **Launches** prodigies who export (Parks)
- **Trains** builders who invest locally (Marriott)

The stayed-vs-left question -- the central tension of Seattle jazz -- resolves differently for each. Two stay, one leaves. But the two stays are structurally different (institution-mediated vs. scene-mediated), demonstrating that staying is not a single phenomenon.

## The Standifer-Marriott Trumpet Arc: Instrument-Level Evolution

The trumpet arc joins the piano quartet as a multi-degree instrument lineage:

| Instrument | Artists | Arc | Energy Shift |
|-----------|---------|-----|-------------|
| Piano | Berry (5), Bown (6), Seales (13), Parks (14) | Quartet -- four eras, four educational paths, four city patterns | 2 -> 2 -> 2 -> 2 (stable) |
| **Trumpet** | **Standifer (4), Marriott (15)** | **Pair -- elder to contemporary, multi to specialist** | **2 -> 3 (gains one level)** |

The trumpet arc is structurally different from the piano quartet. The piano quartet maps stylistic evolution within a stable energy range (all energy-2). The trumpet pair maps an energy shift: the instrument gains intensity across the arc. Same instrument, different generation, higher energy. The trumpet's blue note intensifies in parallel: warm-educational (Standifer) to assertive-driving (Marriott).

The specialization trend is also visible: Standifer played trumpet, saxophone, and voice (multi-instrumental generalist). Marriott is trumpet-focused (specialist). The same trend appears in piano: Berry's cocktail versatility versus Parks's focused post-genre voice.

## Origin Records: First Local-Institutional Label

Origin Records enters the engine as the first Seattle-based institutional label. Prior local recordings (Standifer 4, Berry 5, Brazil 11) were on undocumented or minimal labels. Origin Records is a deliberate institution dedicated to Pacific Northwest jazz documentation.

The label taxonomy now includes three production aesthetic positions:

| Position | Label | Aesthetic | Character |
|----------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Blue Note / Van Gelder | Warm compression, studio character | The sound of jazz history |
| 2 | ECM / Eicher | Spacious, reverberant, contemplative | The sound of space |
| 3 | **Origin / Bishop** | **Documentary realism, natural acoustic space** | **The sound of the room** |

Origin Records' documentary realism aesthetic -- clean recordings, minimal post-production, the ensemble captured as it sounds in the room -- is a third position that serves the local scene rather than defining it. The label IS infrastructure, not branding.

## What Worked

### Research Process

1. **The Builder-Stayer pattern is the most agency-rich stay pattern documented.** The distinction between passive retention (Berry), institution-mediated stay (Seales), and scene-mediated stay (Marriott) provides a three-level framework for classifying all future stay patterns. Agency escalation: no choice -> institutional choice -> personal choice.

2. **The UW three-product pipeline analysis is the richest institutional documentation in the engine.** Three consecutive degrees, three structural outputs, one institution. Demonstrates that the same pipeline produces anchors, exports, and builders.

3. **The energy-3 arrival is correctly positioned as a threshold crossing rather than an anomaly.** Marriott's assertive trumpet energy is structurally continuous with the hard bop tradition (Brown, Hubbard, Shaw) and represents the beginning of the engine's intensity escalation within the jazz quadrant.

4. **Origin Records as third production aesthetic position is structurally productive.** Blue Note/Van Gelder (warm compression), ECM/Eicher (spacious reverb), Origin/Bishop (documentary realism) -- three label aesthetics mapped to three philosophies of what a jazz recording should sound like.

5. **The Standifer-Marriott trumpet arc correctly mirrors the Berry-Seales piano arc** but adds the energy dimension that the piano arc lacks. The trumpet pair maps energy shift; the piano quartet maps stable energy across stylistic evolution.

### Source Quality

6. **Origin Records provides strong institutional source base.** The label's catalog, artist documentation, and founding documentation are publicly accessible. John Bishop as founder-producer-drummer provides a documented institutional history.

7. **Marriott's performance documentation through Earshot Jazz, KNKX, and Seattle jazz journalism is accessible.** Local jazz journalism provides venue-level, concert-level documentation of the Builder-Stayer's continuous presence.

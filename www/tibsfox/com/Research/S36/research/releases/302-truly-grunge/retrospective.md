## What Worked

1. **The return structure is correct.** Truly at degree 220 (first appearance) established the identity. The return at 302 allows retrospective appreciation without repeating the formation story. The final sixth returns serve this function well.
2. **The Endino production tension is the most interesting analytical discovery.** Raw producer, crystalline band. The tension produces the record's distinctive quality. This is a production story worth telling.
3. **Personnel cross-references are rich.** Soundgarden through Yamamoto, Screaming Trees through Pickerel. Two of the engine's major threads converge in Truly's lineup. The Seattle scene's smallness is a feature, not a bug.
4. **E=6 as return energy is calibrated correctly.** The reflective distance of a return reads as slightly lower energy than the first-impact energy of a first appearance. The music hasn't diminished; the engine's relationship has matured.

## Discoveries

1. **Capitol Hill maintained multiple simultaneous aesthetic positions at E=6.** Hovercraft (abrasive noise rock) and Truly (crystalline melodic grunge) are both Capitol Hill, both E=6, completely different aesthetics. The neighborhood contained multitudes.
2. **Truly's commercial timing problem is representative of the mid-1990s alt-rock landscape.** Artistically ahead of commercial reception; commercially behind the peak moment. This pattern recurs throughout the final sixth.

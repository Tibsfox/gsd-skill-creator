First Broadway Connection: Waitress

Sara Bareilles is the engine’s first Broadway connection. She composed the music and lyrics for Waitress (2015), a Broadway musical based on the 2007 film. The musical opened at the Brooks Atkinson Theatre and ran for 1,544 performances. Bareilles herself performed the lead role of Jenna for a limited run in 2017.

The Broadway connection opens a new dimension in the engine: the transition from recording artist to theatrical composer. No prior degree has bridged pop music and musical theater. The crossover arc — from pop radio to Broadway stage — is the 82nd artist-city pattern.

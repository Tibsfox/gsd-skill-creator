## What Worked

### Research Process

1. **The carry-forward items from degree 0 provided immediate structure.** Having four specific
   items to resolve (tritone lineage, Central District connection, chromatic vocabulary, Jackson
   Street reference) gave the degree 1 research a built-in agenda. The research was not starting
   from zero -- it was continuing a conversation. This validates the carry-forward mechanism as
   the engine's primary inter-release coherence tool.

2. **The reversed export pattern emerged naturally.** The degree 0 retrospective identified the
   "local cultivation, global export" pattern as a recurring theme. Degree 1 immediately inverted
   it. This inversion was not forced -- it emerged from the biographical facts. The S36 engine is
   already producing cultural-pattern data that a single-release format could not reveal.

3. **Theory genealogy worked at two degrees.** The chromatic harmony thread now has two data points
   (Jones: bass-register propulsion; Frisell: upper-register suspension), and the contrast between
   them is more illuminating than either point alone. The genealogy system is producing educational
   value through comparison.

4. **The Amiga Principle transferred cleanly.** Jones's constrained-resource orchestration maps
   directly to Frisell's signal-chain-as-ensemble approach. The principle is proving portable
   across instrumentation, era, and genre -- a strong sign that it is genuinely structural
   rather than coincidental.

5. **Cross-genre bridge-building evolved.** Jones bridged jazz→pop/R&B/film (1950s-80s). Frisell
   bridged jazz→Americana/folk/country (1990s-present). The bridge-building principle is constant;
   the destination genres change with each artist. This is a theory genealogy thread that will
   produce a map of what jazz harmony has colonized, one degree at a time.

### Source Quality

6. **Interview-based sources for Frisell are abundant.** Frisell is unusually generous in interviews
   about his technical approach, influences, and decision-making. JazzTimes, DownBeat, Guitar Player,
   Premier Guitar, and The Wire all have substantial interview material. Source quality for a living
   artist is strong.

7. **The ECM Records documentation is meticulous.** ECM's institutional commitment to recording
   quality extends to its documentation: liner notes, session details, and Manfred Eicher's
   production notes provide context that most labels do not preserve. This will be a recurring
   advantage for any ECM artist in the S36 engine.

## What Could Be Better

### Gaps Identified

1. **The Jim Hall pedagogical relationship needs primary-source verification.** The claim that
   Frisell "studied with" Hall at Berklee is widely reported in secondary sources, but the
   nature of the relationship (private lessons? master classes? informal mentoring?) varies
   across accounts. Some sources describe it as a formal teacher-student relationship; others
   describe it as a less structured mentoring arrangement.

   - **Situation:** Pedagogical chain relies on secondary-source descriptions of the Hall-Frisell
     relationship.
   - **Root Cause:** Berklee's student records are not publicly available; the relationship is
     described differently in different interviews.
   - **Recommendation:** Flag the relationship as "studied with / mentored by" rather than
     making a specific claim about formal enrollment. Verify if primary sources become available.
   - **Carry Item:** IMMEDIATE -- affects degree 1 accuracy. Tag: INFERRED.

2. **The Paul Motian Trio deserves deeper treatment.** The Motian-Frisell-Lovano trio operated
   for 27 years (1984-2011) and is one of the most significant small-group formations in late-
   twentieth-century jazz. The degree 1 research treats it as one bullet point among several
   collaborations. A future revision or supplementary document should dedicate more space to
   this partnership.

   - **Situation:** 27-year collaboration compressed to a single paragraph.
   - **Root Cause:** Word count constraint; the research covers Frisell's full career arc.
   - **Recommendation:** Consider supplementary documents for key collaborations that span
     decades. The Motian Trio could be a standalone sidebar.
   - **Carry Item:** PATTERN -- long-running collaborations may need supplementary treatment
     across all 360 degrees. Tag: INFERRED.

3. **No Duwamish territorial acknowledgment was added.** This was flagged at degree 0 as an
   ARCHITECTURAL carry-forward item and remains unresolved at degree 1. The item is not artist-
   specific; it is a template-level change that should be implemented before the jazz cluster

   - **Situation:** Territorial acknowledgment still missing after two releases.

## Patterns Emerging

### For the Genre Quadrant (Jazz, degrees 0-22)

1. **The ii-V-I genealogy is splitting into two branches.** Jones represents mastery within the
   tradition (functional ii-V-I with chromatic decoration). Frisell represents departure from
   the tradition (suspended tonality, modal ambiguity). Degree 2 (Wayne Horvitz) should reveal
   whether the Seattle improvised music community leans toward mastery, departure, or a third
   option. By degree 5, the genealogy tree should have enough branches to visualize.

2. **The Berklee pipeline is emerging as a structural feature.** Two of two jazz degrees so far
   involve Berklee alumni (Jones 1951, Frisell 1975). This may reflect a real institutional
   influence on Seattle jazz, or it may be coincidence. Track across subsequent degrees.

3. **The "reversed export" pattern may define a post-1980 Seattle cohort.** Jones and the
   Jackson Street generation were exported because Seattle could not sustain their careers.
   Frisell and the post-1980 arrivals chose Seattle because it offered space away from NYC.
   These are two distinct relationship modes between artist and city. Track which mode each
   subsequent artist follows.

### For the Engine Architecture

4. **Theory genealogy produces its best output at contrasting pairs.** The Jones-Frisell contrast
   (dense vs. sparse voicings, bass-register vs. upper-register chromaticism, propulsion vs.
   suspension) is more educational than either artist alone. The engine should prioritize adjacent
   degrees that offer productive contrasts -- which the S36 sorting algorithm should naturally
   produce if energy and genre are properly calibrated.

5. **Carry-forward items are accumulating.** Degree 0 produced 4 immediate items and 4 pattern
   items. Degree 1 resolves the 4 immediate items but produces 4 new ones, plus extends 2
   pattern items and adds 4 new pattern items. The carry-forward list is growing. By degree 10,
   the accumulated items may require a management layer (a carry-forward index or database)
   rather than prose tracking in the retrospective.

6. **Living artists present different source challenges than deceased artists.** Jones is thoroughly
   documented with a published autobiography, obituaries, and institutional records. Frisell is
   still active, which means the discography is still growing and the biographical arc is
   incomplete. The research must acknowledge this incompleteness. Recommendation: add a "status"
   field to the knowledge-nodes.json indicating whether the artist is living or deceased, and
   flag living artists' research as subject to future revision.

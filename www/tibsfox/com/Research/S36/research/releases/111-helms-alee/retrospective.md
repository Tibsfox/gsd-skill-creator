## Structural Event 4: The Crushing Harmonic -- Three-Voice Paradox

- **Situation:** Helms Alee's three members all sing. Three-part vocal harmonics over crushing noise-rock riffs. The combination is unprecedented in heavy music -- most noise rock bands have one screamer; Helms Alee has three harmonizers.
- **Root Cause:** The three-voice approach is the defining innovation of Helms Alee. It creates the beauty/destruction paradox that generates E=6. The vocals are not an addition to the noise -- they are the other half of the paradox. Remove the harmonics and you have competent noise rock at E=4-5. Remove the noise and you have a vocal trio. Both together at maximum = E=6.

Experimental Intensity

Not hip-hop that experiments at the edges. Experimental art that uses hip-hop as one of many tools. The delivery shifts between spoken word, shouting, singing, whispering, and abstract vocalization. Underserved. Raw. Street-level intensity. The geography shapes the art. Each neighborhood produces a different mode of hip-hop.

## Acoustic Profile

### The Sound of DoNormaal

- **Vocal delivery:** Unpredictable. Shifts between spoken word, shouting, singing, whispering, and abstract vocalization within a single track. Confrontational. Raw. The delivery IS the instrument.
- **Production:** Industrial, electronic, noisy. Distorted. Sparse in some moments, overwhelming in others. The production does not support the vocals -- it challenges them.
- **BPM range:** Variable and often irregular. Some tracks abandon conventional tempo entirely. When present, 60-150 BPM range.
- **Lyrical content:** Identity, rage, survival, abstraction, Rainier Valley, Blackness, confrontation. The content is personal and political simultaneously -- but the form is as important as the content.
- **Mix approach:** Raw. Not polished. The roughness is intentional -- exposed, unfinished, nerve-level.

## What Worked

1. **The modified instrument documentation is the entry's most important technical finding.** The basitar and guitbass are not gimmicks -- they are engineering solutions to specific sonic problems. Documenting the logic behind their construction explains why the Presidents sound the way they do.
2. **The Jason Finn cross-reference is one of the engine's more satisfying connections.** Love Battery (221) and PUSA (238) are 17 degrees apart. The same drummer in both. The sonic distance between the two bands is enormous. The forward-reference at 221 ("PUSA will appear in future degrees") closes here.
3. **The comedy-as-resistance framing is accurate and necessary.** The Presidents' humor was not a commercial strategy -- it was an aesthetic position. In the middle of grunge's self-serious dominance, choosing to write about peaches was genuinely countercultural.
4. **Eastlake as first entry is properly noted.** The neighborhood is adjacent to Capitol Hill but distinct. The Presidents bring its water-adjacent, practical character into the engine.

## Discoveries

1. **The basitar construction is a genuine DIY engineering achievement.** Ballew built the instrument because the sound he wanted didn't exist in a standard instrument. The tradition of musician-built instruments runs from Les Paul through Bo Diddley to Ballew -- making the instrument you need is one of music's most creative acts.
2. **"Peaches" was written quickly.** The accounts of the writing suggest it came together fast -- a song that took weeks to write would not have the same artless enthusiasm. The speed of composition is consistent with the song's character: committed, un-self-conscious, direct.

## Pair Analysis: Presidents of the USA + Coho Salmon spawning

- **Energy:** E=6 + E=6 = E=12. **EQUILIBRIUM.** Both entries at E=6. The Presidents' comedic alt-pop energy and the Coho's aggressive spawning chaos are both at the same level -- physical, energetic, non-serious in the conventional sense.
- **Theme:** ENERGETIC CHAOS. Coho males in shallow spawning riffles -- fighting, splashing, pursuing females -- produce a continuous chaotic sound of bodies in water. The Presidents produce a continuous chaotic sound of modified instruments in a practice space. Both involve a lot of physical activity producing something that is fundamentally good-natured. The Coho is not malicious; it is just very busy and very loud about being alive.

Emotional Introspection

Grieves (Benjamin Laub) — emotional, introspective hip-hop from Capitol Hill. Anxiety, depression, love, loss, recovery — expressed with raw honesty. He raps and sings, switching between modes within tracks. On Rhymesayers Entertainment — the Minneapolis independent label (Atmosphere, Brother Ali). The Seattle–Minneapolis emotional hip-hop axis.

Rhymesayers Enters

Rhymesayers Entertainment — founded 1995 in Minneapolis. Slug, Ant, the Fifth Element store, Headshots open mic. A label that prizes lyrical substance and emotional honesty. Grieves on Rhymesayers connects Seattle’s hip-hop to the Midwest independent tradition. Two cities, shared values.

Identity as Force

As a queer Black woman in a hip-hop scene that remains male-dominated and often heteronormative, Gifted Gab’s mere presence at the microphone is an act of force. But the identity is not separate from the art — it is integrated into the lyrics, the performance, the community engagement. The identity is the force.

Central District Roots

The Central District is historically Seattle’s Black neighborhood — the center of Black culture, music, and community since the early 20th century. In a rapidly gentrifying CD, Gifted Gab’s presence and music carry the weight of cultural continuity. To claim CD roots is to claim connection to Seattle’s Black cultural history.

## What Worked

1. **Merge Records entering is a genuine label milestone.** The most prestigious independent label in the 360 so far. Durham, NC -- national reach, scouting Seattle. The reverse of local curation.
2. **Power pop paradox framing is effective.** Maximum emotional impact at moderate volume. E=4 captures the paradox perfectly -- powerful hooks, restrained delivery.
3. **Solo project vs. supergroup contrast with Childbirth (204) is strong.** Same neighborhood, opposite modes. Network DNA (204) vs. lone songwriter (210).
4. **Lerner-as-drummer-first adds texture to the one-man-band narrative.** The drums are exceptional because the songwriter is a drummer. This distinguishes Telekinesis from typical singer-songwriter projects.
5. **Lone voice fills the space pairing with Gray Wolf works beautifully.** One person creating a full band sound. One wolf filling miles of wilderness. Same principle, different kingdoms.
6. **E=4 spectrum observation (slack/ironic vs. tight/earnest) deepens energy theory.** Same number, completely different character.

## Pair Analysis: Telekinesis + Gray Wolf Howl

- **Energy:** E=4 (S36) + E=7 (SPS) -- Total E=11. **MAJOR ENERGY CONTRAST.** Wolf howl dominates by 3.
- **Theme:** THE LONE VOICE THAT FILLS THE SPACE. Telekinesis is one person creating the illusion of a full band through studio craft -- Michael Benjamin Lerner alone with layered guitars, precise drums, and stacked harmonies, filling sonic space that typically requires four or five musicians. The Gray Wolf howl is one animal filling miles of wilderness with a single, sustained vocalization -- the most powerful voice in the Canidae quartet, carrying 5-10 km through mountain terrain. Both are about a single voice occupying far more space than its source suggests. The songwriter multi-tracks to fill a recording. The wolf howls to fill a valley. Lone voices, maximum reach. The +3 energy gap captures the wolf's raw power against the songwriter's quiet precision.

The Lone Voice That Fills the Space

Telekinesis. Michael Benjamin Lerner. One person creating the illusion of a full band. Power pop precision: hooks first, drums exceptional, songs under 3:30. Merge Records enters — Durham, NC reaches across the country for a Seattle songwriter. Arcade Fire’s label. Neutral Milk Hotel’s label. Now Telekinesis. E=4 — restrained power. The strength is in the craft, not the volume. Capitol Hill. Same neighborhood as the Hardly Art streak. Different mode entirely.

Theory Contributions

Merge Records Enters

Milestone — Label Theory

First Merge appearance in the 360. Durham, NC — one of America’s most respected indie labels. Founded 1989 by Mac McCaughan and Laura Ballance. National indie label scouting Seattle talent. The reverse of local curation.

Power Pop Paradox

Advanced — Genre Theory

Power pop delivers maximum emotional impact at moderate volume. The “power” is in the songwriting, not the amplifier. E=4 captures this: powerful hooks, restrained delivery. Big Star to Telekinesis. The lineage holds.

Solo Project as Full Band

Intermediate — Scene Theory

Lerner writes, records, performs all instruments. Studio multi-tracking creates the illusion of a band. The craft is making the solo sound communal. Moving things with the mind — the name is apt.

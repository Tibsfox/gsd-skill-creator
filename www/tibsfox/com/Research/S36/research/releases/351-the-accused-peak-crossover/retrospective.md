## What Worked

1. **The "synthesis as third thing" framing is the correct analytical structure.** Crossover thrash is not a compromise between hardcore punk and thrash metal. It is a third thing that emerges when the velocity principle of hardcore meets the technique principle of thrash. The Accused solved the crossover challenge at the PNW ceiling: maintain the speed AND maintain the precision simultaneously. The analysis of this as a distinct compositional achievement is the right approach.
2. **The Martha Splatterhead character as genre container is the correct aesthetic reading.** The extreme content is not sincere violence advocacy -- it is horror-fiction-tradition content using extreme scenarios as aesthetic material. The gallows humor register signals this: "More Fun Than an Open Casket Funeral" is a darkly comic title that tells the listener the register. The document correctly positions this as genre exercise with a tradition.
3. **Oak Harbor / Whidbey Island as the engine's most geographically extreme origin point is a genuine observation.** An island naval air station community 50 miles from Seattle producing crossover thrash that matched the national Combat Records standard is the periphery-produces-maximum pattern at its most extreme. The military-adjacent community produced maximum-intensity music. The engine's geographic pattern holds even on an island.
4. **The Combat Records context grounds the national significance.** Megadeth, Testament, D.R.I. are on the same roster. The Accused's position in that catalog is not marginal. They belonged. The geographic periphery did not prevent them from holding their position in the national conversation.
5. **Tommy Niemeyer as consistent creative force is the correct identification.** Through lineup changes, Niemeyer's guitar is the consistent identity. The peak period is defined by his most developed riff vocabulary. The entry correctly centers him.

## Discoveries

1. **The pre-grunge PNW contribution to national metal is underrecognized.** The Accused were part of the national thrash/crossover conversation in 1986-1987, before grunge made the Pacific Northwest internationally famous. The engine documents this as a genuine contribution: PNW music was connecting to national extreme music conversations before the 1991 detonation.
2. **The 1986 date as pre-genre-recognition moment.** *Martha Splatterhead* arrived before "crossover thrash" was a stable genre name. The Accused were building what would become the template without the template existing. This is the specific type of innovation that earns origin-point status: operating in the territory before it is mapped.
3. **The Naval Air Station community as social context.** The transient, working-class, military-adjacent character of Oak Harbor is not documented in most Seattle music histories. The engine notes it as a production context: the specific social intensity of a military community on an island produced a band whose music expressed maximum intensity through speed and volume.

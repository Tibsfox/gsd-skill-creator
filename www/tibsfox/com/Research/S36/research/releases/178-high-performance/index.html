The Hidden Nester — Tacoma on a Seattle Label

Tacoma hip-hop on Nastymix Records. A group thriving in a city that wasn’t supposed to have a hip-hop scene, nested in an unexpected habitat. Nastymix bridged Seattle and Tacoma — transforming a local label into a regional platform. High Performance carried Tacoma identity on that platform.

The Tacoma-Seattle Axis

Thirty-five miles south of Seattle, Tacoma maintained its own identity. High Performance represented that identity within the Nastymix ecosystem — not as Seattle artists, but as Tacoma artists on a regional platform. The group brought multiple MCs, collective energy, party-oriented hip-hop, and geographic specificity that documented a distinct city’s culture.

Dry Wit as Method

Chastity Belt opens the Hardly Art streak. All-female. Capitol Hill. Post-punk influenced indie rock delivered with studied nonchalance. Julia Shapiro’s flat, conversational vocals. The songs sound like they might fall apart but never do. The slacker aesthetic conceals careful craft. Dry wit as vehicle for genuine feeling. E=4.

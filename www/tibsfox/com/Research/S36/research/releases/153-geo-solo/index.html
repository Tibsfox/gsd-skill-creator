Mass Line Media

Geo founded Mass Line Media as community infrastructure for Seattle hip-hop and Filipino-American voices. The label name references “mass line” — a concept from community organizing. From solo expression to collective empowerment. Curation as creation. The shift from “my voice” to “our voices.”

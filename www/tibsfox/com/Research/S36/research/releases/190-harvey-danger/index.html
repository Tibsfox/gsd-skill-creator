Harvey Danger

E = 5 Indie Pop Arena Rock / Slash First Indie Pop

“Flagpole Sitta” and Beyond

“I’m not sick but I’m not well.” One song defined a generation’s ambivalence. Harvey Danger had one massive hit (1997) and three excellent albums. Sean Nelson’s lyrics are dense, literary, self-deprecating, and genuinely moving beneath the cleverness. Capitol Hill’s finest indie pop band. Too smart for the mainstream. Remembered for one song but worth far more than that. 1992–2009.

Capitol Hill Indie Ecosystem

## What Worked

1. ***We Are the Romans* track-by-track documentation.** Every track on the album deserves the coverage. The Catholic imagery, the Mondrian reference, the aquatic themes, the sardonic titles against devastating music -- the tracklist is the argument. Documenting the record properly is the single most important task in this degree's research.
2. **Brian Cook as trajectory.** Cook's post-Botch path through These Arms Are Snakes to Russian Circles to Sargent House is one of the engine's most useful cross-reference threads. It connects Botch (254) backwards to Helms Alee (253) through a single bassist's 30-year career.
3. **The Converge parallel.** *Jane Doe* (2001) and *We Are the Romans* (1999) are the two canonical documents of the era. Positioning them together is the correct analytical move: East Coast vs. West Coast, Salem vs. Tacoma, but the same level of ambition and achievement.
4. **Tacoma as first engine appearance.** The industrial port city, the Narrows, the South Sound -- this geography deserves documentation. Botch is the correct band to bring Tacoma into the engine.
5. **Hydra Head documented.** Aaron Turner's label is one of the most important in American heavy music history. Its 20-year run (1993-2012) from Botch and Isis to Pelican and Oxbow represents post-hardcore, post-metal, and sludge at their creative peak.

## Discoveries

1. **The dissolution as artistic decision.** The 2002 dissolution, the refusal to release new studio material at the 2016 and 2022 reunion shows -- this is rare in rock music. Most bands overstay their creative peak. Botch did not. The catalog is complete.
2. **Dave Knudson's arc: Botch to Minus the Bear.** The guitarist who played the most technically demanding riffs in post-hardcore then founded Minus the Bear and applied the same intelligence to melodic indie rock. This is the larger intellectual arc: the technical vocabulary is portable.
3. **Mathcore's lineage through Slint.** *Spiderland* (Louisville, 1991) as the ur-document: technically complex, emotionally extreme, critically celebrated on re-evaluation. Botch's mathcore inherits this tradition and pushes it to the extreme.

## Pair Analysis: Botch + Fog Horn (Admiralty Inlet)

- **Energy:** E=8 + E=3 = E=11. Above equilibrium.
- **Theme:** WARNING SIGNALS AT THE EDGE OF PERCEPTION. The fog horn at Admiralty Inlet signals danger to ships: something solid is nearby, you cannot see it, you must respond. Botch's music signals danger to listeners: something is coming, you cannot categorize it by familiar patterns, you must respond. Both are warning systems. The fog horn is a maritime safety device that operates in conditions of limited visibility. *We Are the Romans* operates in conditions of limited genre visibility -- you cannot use conventional listening frameworks and survive. Both demand attention. Both are heard before they are understood.

The Circle of Warmth

Campfire OK. The name is the mission: warmth, community, gathering. Layered guitars with reverb and delay. Vocals that sit inside the mix. Patient builds. Self-released. No label pressure. No gatekeeping. E=5 — the campfire glow. Steady, sustained, inviting. Seattle DIY at its most honest. The fire at the center of the circle.

Theory Contributions

Campfire as Aesthetic Principle

Foundational — Scene Theory

The campfire is communal, warm, unpretentious. Not a performance space — a gathering space. Campfire OK declares its intent in its name: warmth over spectacle, community over reach. The fire and the music serve the same function.

Self-Released as Credential

Intermediate — Label Theory

In Seattle’s DIY infrastructure, self-releasing is not a limitation — it’s a credential. Bandcamp and digital distribution dissolved the barriers. Trade reach for autonomy. The economics shifted; the ethic remained.

## The Acoustic Work

### The Stripping

Votolato's acoustic albums and performances strip the punk-folk energy of his early records -- *Suicide Medicine* (2003), *Makers* (2006) -- to bare folk. The acoustic context reveals the songwriter beneath the punk-folk performer. Voice and guitar. Sometimes piano. The songs are the same songs, but without the band they breathe differently. The melodies are more exposed. The lyrics carry more weight when they are the only thing happening.

### The Paradox

The acoustic Votolato is not a different artist -- it is the same artist with fewer places to hide. The confessional (degree 46) is still confessional. But now the confession is delivered without armor. And paradoxically, the stripped delivery has *more* energy (E=3) than the punk-folk original (E=2). The voice fills the space that the band used to occupy. The dynamic range increases because there is nothing else competing.

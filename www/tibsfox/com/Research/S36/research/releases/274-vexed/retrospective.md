## What Worked

1. **The four-year run is correctly treated as a document, not a failure.** Short-run hardcore bands are a feature of the genre, not a bug. Vexed's four years produced a body of work that captures Capitol Hill's hardcore moment.
2. **Capitol Hill's political character is correctly identified.** More explicitly leftist, more queer-adjacent than suburban scenes. The neighborhood shapes the music.
3. **E=8 streak (273-274) is documented.** The Refuzors and Vexed hold E=8 for two consecutive degrees. This is the sustained maximum in the S36 engine.

## Discoveries

1. **The scene-as-infrastructure argument.** Without bands like Vexed, the scene that produced Seattle's nationally prominent acts would not have existed. The engine's function is to acknowledge this infrastructure.
2. **Capitol Hill appears at degree 274 in its hardcore phase** -- distinct from its punk (early), its grunge (mid), and its indie (later) phases. The neighborhood has multiple distinct moments in the engine.

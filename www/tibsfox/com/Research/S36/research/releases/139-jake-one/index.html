The National Crossover Producer

Jake One (Jacob Dutton) is the most nationally successful hip-hop producer to emerge from Seattle’s Central District. His credits span from G-Unit and 50 Cent (mainstream commercial) to MF DOOM and Brother Ali (independent underground). He bridges both worlds without compromising in either.

Three Central District figures: DJ Riz (136, community), Vitamin D (138, underground), Jake One (139, mainstream). Community → underground → mainstream. Same roots, expanding reach. The Central District three-peat.

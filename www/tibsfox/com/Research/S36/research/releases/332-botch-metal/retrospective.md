## What Worked

1. **The E=9-via-metric-complexity vs. E=9-via-velocity distinction is the metal arc's most important analytical finding.** The arc opens and closes at E=9 through entirely different mechanisms. The Accused: 200 BPM, 2-minute songs, maximum speed. Botch: irregular meters, through-composed pieces, maximum rhythmic complexity. Same energy calibration, opposite compositional strategies. This is the engine at its most analytically precise.
2. **We Are the Romans is correctly identified as the canonical mathcore document.** The delayed-detonation influence -- most impactful a decade after release -- is documented fact, not metaphor. The avant-garde timing is correct and specific.
3. **The Brian Cook connection (Botch bass to Russian Circles, Sargent House = Helms Alee label) is one of the most specific and elegant cross-references in the engine.** A bassist connects two consecutive metal arc degrees through his subsequent career and his label ecosystem. This is the kind of cross-reference that the engine's depth produces.
4. **Tacoma's industrial character as the correct origin for mathcore's structural seriousness is analytically sound.** The city that produced the Tacoma Narrows physics, the Commencement Bay remediation, and working-class intellectual tradition also produced a band that requires serious attention and rewards study.
5. **The metal arc geographic structure (Oak Harbor north to Tacoma south) documents the full Puget Sound geography of extreme music.**

## Discoveries

1. **"Transitions From Persona to Object" is a philosophy-of-mind concept at the center of a mathcore track.** The person-as-subject vs. object-as-thing-without-interiority distinction from philosophy of mind as the conceptual center of an 8-minute mathcore piece is exactly the kind of intellectual ambition that distinguishes Botch from pure aggression.
2. **The 2022 reunion (20 years after dissolution) is the strongest evidence for We Are the Romans' sustained influence.** A band reconvenes after two decades because the audience is still there and still growing. Delayed detonation confirmed by the reunion's enthusiastic reception.
3. **Aaron Turner's dual role (Hydra Head founder + Isis guitarist) created the label aesthetic that made Botch's placement possible.** Turner's musical values shaped the label's signing criteria. Botch on Hydra Head was a direct result of Turner's taste and vision.

## The E=9 Resolution

The closing arc's one E=9 is the correct terminal position. Four consecutive E=10 entries establishing that the arc's primary energy level is at the ceiling, followed by an E=9 resolution that signals the arc's completion without a false additional E=10 that would require a new justification. ## What Worked

1. **The geographic closure is the closing arc's most elegant structural element.** Capitol Hill at 349 (Pearl Jam, 1991) and Capitol Hill at 353 (TAAS, 2003-2007). The arc visits the periphery and returns to the center. The engine documents this as intentional structure.
2. **The Brian Cook consecutive-degree connection is the most explicit cross-reference in the closing arc.** The same bassist in two consecutive degrees, in different bands, in different sonic contexts. Botch (352) Cook versus TAAS (353) Cook: the comparison documents a musician's range across band contexts. The engine provides cross-references between entries, and this is the most direct: the same human in consecutive positions.
3. **The intrasong variation framing for Easter is the correct sonic signature.** Not the between-song dynamics of Botch (which uses quiet and loud as structural sections), but within-song variation -- tempo, texture, intensity all changing within the duration of a single track. The album's character is temporal development rather than structural architecture.
4. **Steve Snere's register-switching as compositional tool is correctly identified.** The voice that is not sure whether it is singing or shouting is expressing something about ambivalence itself. The delivery is not affectation; it is semantic. Post-hardcore's vocal tradition uses register as signal.
5. **Toshi Kasai's production genealogy (Melvins → TAAS) is a genuine connection.** The same producer who worked with Melvins applied a comparable approach (dynamic range, live-room presence, trust the band) to TAAS. The Kasai throughline from degree 334-345 (Melvins entries) to degree 353 (TAAS) is a production genealogy that the engine correctly documents.

## Discoveries

1. **The closing arc's era range is the most striking structural feature.** The five entries span: 1985-1988 (Accused), 1989-1991 (TAD and Pearl Jam), 1999 (Botch), 2003-2007 (TAAS). The arc documents 22 years of PNW extreme music from the crossover thrash origin to the post-hardcore peak. No single year. No single era. A full generational arc.
2. **The E=18 terminal pairing is the correct signal.** After four consecutive E=20 pairings (349-352, each with E=10 + E=10), the first non-E=20 pairing at 353 signals the resolution. The SPS simultaneous-maximum pattern (all layers at once) gives way to the sequential-development pattern (the owl chorus that develops through the night). The energy curve begins its final descent from the closing arc.
3. **The Suicide Squeeze position reveals the Capitol Hill ecosystem.** Suicide Squeeze is a Capitol Hill-adjacent label that contained both Death Cab for Cutie and These Arms Are Snakes in the same era. The range of that label -- from pop-inflected indie rock to post-hardcore -- mirrors the Capitol Hill neighborhood's range in the early 2000s. The label documents the neighborhood.

## Pair Analysis: TAAS Peak + Mixed Owl Chorus Full Night

- **Energy:** E=9 + E=9 = E=18. THE ARC RESOLVES. First non-E=20 pairing in the closing arc.
- **Theme:** TEMPORAL DEVELOPMENT THROUGH PHASES. These Arms Are Snakes' post-hardcore is organized around temporal development: songs that begin in one mode and arrive in another, with the trajectory being the experience rather than the destination. The mixed owl chorus of a full winter night is organized around the same principle: not all species calling simultaneously at maximum, but different species becoming active at different hours through the night, the acoustic character of the forest changing as the night progresses from dusk through the deep hours to pre-dawn. Both entries are about what happens when something develops through time rather than arriving at maximum all at once. After four E=20 simultaneous-maximum pairings, the arc closes with a temporal-development pairing at E=18. The resolution is in the structure.

It describes the quality produced when a dream-haze folk aesthetic is rebuilt with electronic instruments -- when the atmospheric quality of reverb-drenched guitar and layered vocals is replicated through synthesizers and drum machines, preserving the dreamy quality while adding an underlying pulse.

### The Sound

The dream circuit manifests as:
- **Preserved atmosphere** -- the reverb, delay, and spatial qualities of the dream haze carry over to the electronic version. The dream is not abandoned; it is rebuilt.
- **Electronic foundation** -- synthesizers and drum machines replace acoustic instruments as the harmonic and rhythmic foundation.
- **Added pulse** -- the electronic version has a rhythmic regularity and forward motion that the acoustic dream haze lacked. The pulse is the primary addition.
- **Voice afloat** -- unlike the layered dissolution (103), which dissolves voice into texture, the dream circuit keeps the voice floating atop the electronic surface. The voice is still the human center.

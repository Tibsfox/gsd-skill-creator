## What Worked

1. **The origination energy calibration is correct and important.** E=10 for Solger does not derive from production quality, distribution scale, or sonic maximalism comparable to later entries. It derives from the fact of being first -- of playing hardcore in Seattle in 1980 when there was no scene model, no infrastructure, no audience beyond the people in the room. This is a distinct calibration pathway: the floor is as important as the ceiling, and the engine acknowledges it here.
2. **The chronological context table (Solger 1980 vs. Black Flag Damaged December 1981 vs. Dischord 1980 vs. Germs GI 1979) is the most effective argument for the E=10 calibration.** The specificity of dates makes the claim verifiable: Solger pre-dates the defining artifacts of American hardcore by over a year. This is the most important fact in the entry.
3. **The Solger + Fartz paired origin is the correct scene-history framing.** The two bands together constitute the founding generation. Neither can be fully understood without the other as context. Placing them at degrees 355 and 356 acknowledges the founding pairing in the engine's closing structure.

## Discoveries

1. **The DIY-as-necessity versus DIY-as-choice distinction is the most interesting cultural observation in the entry.** By the mid-1980s, DIY was an aesthetic and ethical choice for punk bands operating in a context where larger labels existed as alternatives. In 1980, for Solger in Seattle, there was no alternative. The DIY principle was not a choice -- it was the only option. This is the purest form of the ethic, and it is the form in which the Pacific Northwest underground tradition began.
2. **The engine closing logic (return to origin before completing at destination) is a structural observation worth carrying forward.** The final six degrees are not chronologically organized -- they move from 2002 back to 1980 back to 1981 forward to 1993 to 1997 to 2001. The organization is by energy and significance, not time. This is the engine's closing grammar: it acknowledges the full arc by collapsing time into significance.

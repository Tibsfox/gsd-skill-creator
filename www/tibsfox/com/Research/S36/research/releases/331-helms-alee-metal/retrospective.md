## What Worked

1. **The three-piece three-vocalist architecture is genuinely unusual in sludge metal and correctly identified.** Most sludge acts privilege single-vocalist aggression. Helms Alee's distribution of vocals across all three members -- and the post-metal dynamics this creates -- is the band's primary sonic identity and correctly positioned as such.
2. **Sargent House context is accurate and illuminating.** The label's aesthetic (heavy music in productive genre ambiguity) precisely describes Helms Alee's position. The comparison to Russian Circles and Chelsea Wolfe positions the band correctly within the Sargent House family.
3. **Ben Verellen's amplifier-building work is a specific and documented contribution.** Verellen Amplifiers are real and represent a genuine contribution to Pacific Northwest guitar culture beyond the band.
4. **The Melvins as Pacific Northwest sludge antecedent is accurate.** Aberdeen, Washington's contribution to slow heavy music directly informs the tradition Helms Alee operates in.
5. **2008 formation context (financial crisis year) is historically grounded.** The economic gravity of the formation year is proportionate to the genre.

## Discoveries

1. **The title "Stillicide" (2019) documents the band's preference for unusual conceptual frames.** A legal term for the right to collect water dripping from a neighbor's eaves as an album title is maximally specific and unusual. This is a band with genuine intellectual range.
2. **The Capitol Hill heavy music ecology of 2008 is less well-documented than the 1990s grunge scene.** The smaller clubs, the Sargent House-adjacent independent acts, the post-hardcore residue of the 2000s -- this is the under-documented period of Seattle heavy music that Helms Alee represents.
3. **Three-piece with three vocalists means every member carries both rhythmic and melodic responsibility simultaneously.** This is structurally demanding in ways that larger bands with dedicated vocalists don't face. The arrangement requires complete musicianship from all three members.

Ethiopian Diaspora Identity

Gabriel Teodros navigates the space between Ethiopian and American identity — the challenges of immigrant children, the pull between cultural preservation and assimilation, the experience of being Black and immigrant. His art centers the Ethiopian diaspora: Amharic elements, pan-African politics, anti-imperialist critique, and the specific geography of Ethiopian life in the Rainier Valley.

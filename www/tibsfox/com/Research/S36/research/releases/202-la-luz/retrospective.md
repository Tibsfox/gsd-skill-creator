5. **Hardly Art streak progression is visible.** E=4 to E=5. Indie rock to surf rock. The label's range becomes clear.
6. **Echo and calling pairing with bear cub is elegant.** Reverb (music echoing in space) and mew (cub calling through forest). Both searching sounds.

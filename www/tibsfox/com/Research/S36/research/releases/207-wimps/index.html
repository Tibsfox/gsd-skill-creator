Defense Through Deterrence

Lo-fi as aesthetic commitment. Kill Rock Stars returns — the Olympia-born indie label re-enters the engine. Fast, catchy, deliberately underproduced. 90-second songs with hooks buried in distortion. The lo-fi production is a filter: it deters listeners who need polish and attracts those who value authenticity. E=5. The four-entry E=6 streak (203–206) breaks. The engine breathes.

## What Worked

1. **The 220-302-326 three-entry arc is the correct documentation structure for a band this complex.** Truly has multiple distinct aspects that a single entry cannot capture. The three-degree arc documents crystalline alt (220), grunge placement (302), and heavy alt-rock fusion (326). Together these give a complete picture.
2. **The distinction between arrangement-based heaviness and tonal heaviness is analytically accurate and necessary.** Truly's E=6 is not Mudhoney's E=7 even at similar volume. The mechanism is different: Truly uses compositional density; Mudhoney uses tonal aggression. Documenting this distinction clarifies the genre boundary.
3. **The personnel lineage table (Soundgarden, Screaming Trees, Skin Yard) is one of the most concentrated lineage convergences in the engine.** Three major Seattle bands provide the three members of Truly's core lineup. The Capitol Hill scene's technical tradition is carried forward through these connections.
4. **E=6 is the correct calibration.** The heavy Truly tracks are clearly above E=5 (the crystalline side) but do not reach E=7 (Sub Pop grunge heaviness). The middle calibration is accurate.

## Discoveries

1. **The heavy Truly tracks are heavier because the parts are layered more densely, not because the guitar tone is more aggressive.** This is the key insight for the E=6 entry. The guitar tone is still relatively clean compared to grunge; the density comes from arrangement: more guitar parts, rhythm section pushed harder, less space in the mix. The compositional approach to heaviness is distinct from the tonal approach.
2. **The Stone Gossard production aesthetic is audible in Truly even when Gossard is not the credited producer.** The Capitol Hill scene carried aesthetic preferences forward through personnel connections. Gossard's influence on the precise-and-heavy approach to production permeated the bands he worked with and those bands carried the aesthetic into subsequent collaborations.

## Pair Analysis: Truly (heavy) + Douglas Fir Crown in Gale

- **Energy:** E=6 + E=7 = E=13. Truly's controlled compositional density against the Douglas Fir's gale-driven sustained moan-roar. The band at maximum arrangement density vs. the tree at maximum meteorological load.
- **Theme:** PRECISION UNDER LOAD. Truly's heaviest material maintains compositional precision even at maximum arrangement density -- the guitar parts are still exactly placed even when the arrangement is thickest. The Douglas Fir crown in a gale is bent but not broken: the tree's structural precision (straight grain, flexible wood, deep roots) allows it to sustain the gale's load without failing. Both entries document precision under extreme load.

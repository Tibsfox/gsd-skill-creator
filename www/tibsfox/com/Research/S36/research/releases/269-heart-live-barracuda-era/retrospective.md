## Discoveries

1. **The tritone riff is historically named (diabolus in musica).** This is a direct connection between music theory and the song's emotional content. The "devil's interval" is used to express the band's fury at their label. The interval name and the emotion are identical.
2. **Ann Wilson's voice at arena scale gains rather than loses texture.** This distinguishes trained projection singers from studio-adapted voices. The live recordings confirm the dynamic.

*Degree 177 retrospective complete. KID SENSATION at the one hundred seventy-seventh degree -- THE NASTYMIX ERA BEGINS. Kid Sensation was one of Seattle's first hip-hop stars. He was on Sir Mix-a-Lot's Nastymix Records -- the first significant hip-hop label in the Pacific Northwest. "Back to Boom" was a regional hit. He appeared on "Posse on Broadway" (1988), the founding document of Seattle hip-hop. His debut album *Rollin' with Number One* (1990) was among the first full-length Seattle hip-hop albums. The Nastymix trilogy opens at 177 and will build through High Performance (178, Tacoma) to Sir Mix-a-Lot (179, E=7) -- the label founder, the architect, the biggest name in Seattle hip-hop history. Kid Sensation represents the pioneer generation: the MCs who were there before the infrastructure existed, before anyone outside the Pacific Northwest was paying attention, when hip-hop from Seattle was barely a concept. Central District. Nastymix. The colony. v1.49.317.*

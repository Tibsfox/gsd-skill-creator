The Sound — Classic Hard Rock Updated

Thunderpussy’s primary influences are classic hard rock of the 1970s-1980s: Led Zeppelin, Heart (the Seattle connection — Ann and Nancy Wilson established the template for women playing hard rock on rock’s own terms), Aerosmith, Deep Purple, AC/DC. The glam inflection in their visual presentation connects them to Capitol Hill’s aesthetically adventurous character. The sound is updated, not imitative — contemporary production values applied to vintage sonic commitments.

Thunderpussy is the first entry in the S36 engine where every band member is female. This is a demographic fact worth tracking.
2. **The queer community embedding is culturally significant.** Thunderpussy's commitment to Capitol Hill's LGBTQ+ community is not incidental -- it connects the band to one of the most important dimensions of the neighborhood's character. The engine documents communities, not just sounds.
3. **Contemporary Seattle hard rock is alive.** After three degrees of historical giants (Hendrix twice, AIC), degree 248 demonstrates that Seattle's hard rock tradition did not end with the grunge era. It continues in Capitol Hill in 2014-present form, with a band that signed to Republic and has two albums on a major label.

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the .tex file
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schemas, source index, and module stubs
  3. Wave 1 executes two parallel tracks (A: Origins/Unix/ISP, B: Survival/Context)
  4. CAPCOM gate: human review of Modules 01-05
  5. Wave 2 synthesizes design patterns and GSD integration
  6. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and delivers the final document

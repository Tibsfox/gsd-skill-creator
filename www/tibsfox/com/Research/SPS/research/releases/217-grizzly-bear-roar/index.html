The Roar — Physics Made Audible

50–400 Hz fundamental. Infrasound below 20 Hz. 2–8 seconds sustained. 100+ dB at 10 meters. The chest vibrates. The ground trembles. Trees shake. You feel it before you process it. E=8. Carries 1–3 km through forest. The most powerful terrestrial vocalization in the PNW.

The Apex Land Predator

180–360 kg (males). Up to 2.4 meters standing. Ursus arctos horribilis — “horrible bear.” Keystone species: soil disturbance, salmon fertilization, wildlife corridors. Fewer than 10 in the North Cascades. Federal recovery ongoing. The grizzly’s presence reshapes the landscape.

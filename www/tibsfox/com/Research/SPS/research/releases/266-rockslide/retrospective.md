## Discoveries

1. **The acceleration is what distinguishes a rockslide acoustically.** Glacier calving has a crack + sustained rumble; rockslide has initial impact + accelerating percussion. The acceleration is the genus-defining characteristic.
2. **Canyon geometry is integral to the acoustic experience.** The same rockslide in a narrow canyon vs. on an open slope produces very different acoustic results. Terrain is always part of the instrument in geophysical acoustics.

## What Worked

1. **E=2 is the correct placement.** The Turkey Vulture's non-vocalization is a biological fact, not an artistic judgment. The absent syrinx makes it impossible to produce structured bird calls. E=2 (hissing grunt, esophageal air) is the honest assessment of what the bird can actually do.
2. **The FIRST CATHARTIDAE milestone is structurally significant.** After four Falconidae entries and a long Accipitridae arc, entering a new raptor-adjacent family (Cathartidae, not actually related to the Old World vultures in Accipitridae) requires documentation of the convergent evolution distinction.
3. **The dimethyl sulfide story is the entry's strongest research finding.** The ability to detect DMS at 1 part per billion, the pipeline gas leak industry discovery, the 4x-larger olfactory bulb -- these are facts that are genuinely surprising and well-supported.

## Discoveries

1. **The convergent evolution distinction is more counterintuitive than expected.** New World vultures (Cathartidae) are more closely related to storks than to Old World vultures (Accipitridae). The visual similarity (bare head, large wingspan, soaring flight, carrion diet) is convergence, not common ancestry. This is an important taxonomic fact.
2. **The dihedral aerodynamics as stability mechanism.** The V-wing dihedral is not just a field mark -- it is a structural solution to staying upright in turbulent thermal air. This is physics, not behavior.

American Badger

Taxidea taxus (Mustelidae) — E=5 — The Tenacious Digger
Second Mustelidae in this stretch. Low growl at 100–600 Hz + sharp hiss at 2,000–8,000 Hz. 6–12 kg. Massive front claws. Ecological engineer — abandoned burrows house dozens of species. E=5. Energy equilibrium with Brad.

The Tenacious Digger

Massive front claws (up to 5 cm). 10–50 burrows per territory. Digs faster than prey can escape. Ecological engineer: abandoned burrows used by burrowing owls, snakes, rabbits, foxes. Eastern Washington grassland and sage-steppe. The landscape architect of the prairie.

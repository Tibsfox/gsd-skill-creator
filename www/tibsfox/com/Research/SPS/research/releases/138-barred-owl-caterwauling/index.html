E=6 — The Wild Ascending Scream

Ascending screams, sharp barks, manic cackling, bass howls — all overlapping in chaotic, unpredictable sequences. The caterwauling spans 300–2000+ Hz — dramatically wider than the hooting’s 400–550 Hz. When multiple Barred Owls caterwaul simultaneously, the layered voices create a wall of sound that is genuinely terrifying to uninitiated listeners. E=6 maximum energy — the highest in the four-degree Strigidae streak.

The Human Alarm Response

“Someone is being murdered.” The most common first-time description. Police departments in forested areas report regular calls about mysterious screaming. The ascending pitch pattern triggers a primal alarm response. People also describe it as “monkeys in the forest” or “demonic”. At 2 AM, 50 meters from a suburban home, the caterwauling is an unforgettable experience.

Fourth Double Entry — Most Dramatic Step

Pileated WP: 108–109, energy step +1. Dipper: 116–117, step +1. GHO: 135–136, step +1. Barred Owl: 137–138, step +2. The Barred Owl’s caterwauling bypasses E=5 entirely — from the measured “Who cooks for you?” to maximum-energy screaming. Same species, radically different acoustic outputs.

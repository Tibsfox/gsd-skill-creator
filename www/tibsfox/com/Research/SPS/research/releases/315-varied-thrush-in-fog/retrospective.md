## What Worked

1. **The fog context as calibration factor is the most precise atmospheric acoustic note in the engine.** Varied Thrush in clear conditions = E=6. In coastal fog = E=7. The fog is not background; it is the increment. Documenting this distinction -- same bird, same song, different atmospheric context, different energy calibration -- establishes an important engine principle: acoustic context is as significant as acoustic content. The fog absorbs high-frequency ambient reflections, reducing competition. The bird's pure-tone vocalization carries through the fog with less degradation. The notes arrive more isolated.
2. **The silence-as-structure analysis is new to the engine at this degree.** No previous SPS entry has explicitly documented the pause between vocalizations as a structural element. The Varied Thrush's 3-8 second silences are as important as the notes. Each silence allows the previous note to complete its perceptual event before the next begins. The engine records silence as structure, not absence.
3. **The acoustic purity analysis (unmodulated pure tone from a songbird) grounds the entry in biology.** Most songbirds produce frequency-modulated vocalizations within each note. The Varied Thrush produces an acoustically unusual (for a songbird) unmodulated sustained tone -- the avian equivalent of a bowed string without vibrato. The acoustic mechanism is documented, not just the aesthetic effect.
4. **E=7 as the correct calibration for singular haunting simplicity (vs. E=8 for collective density, vs. E=9 for maximum intensity) is the engine's clearest articulation of its mid-high energy register.** The Varied Thrush is not trying to be E=8 or E=9. It is achieving something that only works at E=7: the most emotionally complete simple acoustic event. The engine's calibration respects what each register does well.

## Discoveries

1. **The Varied Thrush is the SPS engine's most asymmetric energy-to-volume entry.** Volume: E=4-5 (a thrush is not loud). Emotional impact: E=7. The acoustic volume and the acoustic emotional weight are most divergent here. The fog context and the acoustic purity of the unmodulated tone create emotional weight that is entirely disproportionate to the physical sound pressure level.
2. **The Olympic Peninsula Hoh Rain Forest is the engine's most acoustically complete Varied Thrush fog habitat.** Annual rainfall of 140 inches. Temperate rainforest. Sitka spruce to 300 feet, western red cedar, bigleaf maple. Fog present over 200 days per year. The Hoh is where the conditions (old-growth structure + coastal fog + minimal ambient noise) combine to produce the Varied Thrush fog-singing event at its most complete. The engine's geographic specificity here is correct.
3. **The note sequence being non-predictable is perceptually significant.** The Varied Thrush does not sing a scale or a pattern. The listener cannot anticipate the next note. Each new pitch arrives without telegraphing. The unpredictability of the sequence within the predictability of the format (one note, silence, one note) creates an attentional loop: the listener knows another note is coming but cannot know when or at what pitch. The attentional engagement elevates the emotional weight per note.

- **Energy:** E=7 + E=7 = E=14. **POST-SUMMIT GRADIENT STEP TWO.** The combined energy descends from E=16 (degree 314) to E=14 (degree 315). The SPS track: E=9 (311, 312, 313), E=8 (314), E=7 (315). One unit per degree. The descent is gradual and proportional.

The Foo Fighters' enduring moments are melodically direct, clearly stated, and followed by enough air to resonate. Both E=7 through this mechanism. The engine's degree-315 pairing is the most precise expression of the isolated-direct-statement-with-space principle in the full 360-degree rotation (to this point).

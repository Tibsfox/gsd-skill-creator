California Sea Lion — Rapid Bark

Zalophus californianus (Otariidae) — E=6 — The Performer
The “circus seal.” The one people picture. Rapid “arf arf arf” bark that fills harbors and docks. Exceptionally intelligent and trainable. Second Otariidae. Steller/California comparison pair (182–183). Expanding range into PNW waters.

The Performer — Everyone Knows This Animal

The California Sea Lion is the pinniped everyone recognizes. The “arf arf arf” bark. The aquarium star. The dock-hauler. US Navy mine detection. Exceptionally intelligent — memory, reasoning, vocal learning. The most trainable pinniped on Earth.

The Rapid Bark — E=6

200–4000 Hz. Higher-pitched than Steller. 2–4 barks per second. Rapid, rhythmic, insistent. Dock acoustics amplify. Colony creates wall of sound. Same E=6 as Steller but through speed and persistence rather than depth and power.

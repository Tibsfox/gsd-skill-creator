FIRST OTARIIDAE — eared seals enter the engine. The largest sea lion in the world. Males up to 2,500 lbs. Colony roar = loudest PNW pinniped. Named after Georg Steller (1741 Bering expedition). Endangered (western DPS). E=6 maximum.

First Otariidae — Eared Seals Enter

Otariidae (eared seals) distinguished from Phocidae (true seals, 180–181) by: external ear flaps, rotating hind flippers for walking, long fore flippers for swimming. Three Otariidae entries: Steller Sea Lion (182), California Sea Lion (183), Northern Fur Seal (184). The eared seal trio.

The Colony Roar — E=6 Maximum

The bull’s roar: deep, sustained, thunderous. At breeding rookeries, hundreds of animals create a wall of sound. Bulls roaring, cows calling, pups bleating. 100–3000 Hz with rich harmonics. The loudest pinniped vocalization in the PNW. E=6 — the SPS ceiling.

The Largest Sea Lion — 2,500 lbs

Males: 3.3 meters, 1,120 kg. Massive forequarters, thick neck, sagittal crest. 3x the weight of females. Extreme sexual dimorphism drives the harem-defense mating system. Roughly the weight of a grizzly bear. Megafauna scale.

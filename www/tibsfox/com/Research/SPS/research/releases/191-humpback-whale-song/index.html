The Song — Hierarchical Composition

Units: individual sounds (moans, cries, chirps) lasting 0.5–5 seconds. Phrases: repeated sequences of units (5–30 sec). Themes: sequences of phrases (1–5 min), 4–7 per song. Songs: complete performances of 10–30 minutes, repeated continuously for hours. 20 Hz to 10 kHz. The most complex animal vocalization on Earth. E=8.

Cultural Transmission — Songs That Spread

All males in a population sing the same song. Songs evolve over months. Innovations spread across ocean basins over years. “Cultural revolutions”: entire songs replaced within weeks (Noad et al. 2000). Songs are learned, not innate. Culture at oceanic scale.

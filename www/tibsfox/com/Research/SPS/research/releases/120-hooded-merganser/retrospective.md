## Structural Event 4: The Fan-Shaped Crest -- Display as Adaptation

- **Situation:** The male Hooded Merganser's fan-shaped crest is one of the most dramatic visual displays in duck taxonomy. Black at edges, white center, head doubles in apparent size.
- **Root Cause:** Sexual selection has produced an extraordinary display structure. The crest is a courtship and territorial signal. The visual spectacle compensates for the small body size.
- **Recommendation:** Document the crest display in detail. Note the four functions (courtship, territory, alarm, relaxed). Note the transformation between flat and raised states.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- Fan-shaped crest documented. Display adaptation. Track.

## Structural Event 4: First Fully Mechanical Defining Sound

- **Situation:** The wing-whistle is the first species-defining sound in the engine that is entirely mechanical -- produced by feather structure, not by any vocal organ.
- **Root Cause:** Other species produce mechanical sounds (Pileated drum, Flicker drum), but those species also have prominent vocalizations. Common Goldeneye's wing-whistle is the sound that *defines* the species, and it is fully involuntary and mechanical. The bird cannot choose not to whistle in flight.
- **Recommendation:** Document the wing-whistle as a new category of sound in the engine: fully mechanical, involuntary, structural. Connect to the Pileated's drum (partially mechanical but directed) as a precursor.
- **Carry Item:** CONFIRMED -- First fully mechanical defining sound. Involuntary. Structural. New sound category.

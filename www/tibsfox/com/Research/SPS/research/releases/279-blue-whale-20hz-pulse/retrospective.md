## What Worked

1. **The E=5 paradox correctly documented.** The blue whale is the largest animal in Earth's history and has one of the highest source levels of any sustained biological sound (180-188 dB). It is E=5 because 20 Hz is at or below human hearing threshold. The paradox -- maximum biological scale, minimum perceptual accessibility -- is the central fact. Documented clearly.
2. **"Felt more than heard" correctly stated.** At the lower threshold of human hearing, sound is experienced as physical sensation (pressure, vibration) rather than as acoustic event. Observers near vocalizing blue whales report feeling the water move. This is the qualitatively distinct character of E=5 in the blue whale register.
3. **The SOFAR channel connection documented.** The physics of long-range infrasonic propagation -- the SOFAR channel, hundreds to thousands of kilometers of potential propagation distance -- explains the function of the 20 Hz pulse: mate location across vast ocean distances.
4. **The Balaenopteridae arc stated.** Humpback (278, E=9): maximum spectacle. Blue whale (279, E=5): maximum invisible depth. Same family, opposite registers. The arc's character is established by this contrast.
5. **Scale documented accurately.** 100-150 metric tons. Exceeds largest dinosaurs. Heart the size of a small car. Tongue as heavy as an elephant. The superlative scale is documented without sensationalism -- it is simply what is true.
6. **NE Pacific population context noted.** The blue whale population is ESA Endangered; NE Pacific presence is uncommon. The rarity of observation makes the acoustic documentation more important.

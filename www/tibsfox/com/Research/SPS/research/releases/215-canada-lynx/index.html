The Snow Specialist

Snowshoe paws: proportionally the largest of any felid. 8–14 kg floating on deep powder. Snowshoe hare dependency: 75–97% of diet. The famous 10-year boom-bust cycle — documented since the 1700s from Hudson’s Bay Company records. When hares crash, lynx starve. Fewer than 100 in Washington. North Cascades.

About This Package

Written on March 26, 2026 — the 31st anniversary of Eazy-E's death. This mission package maps the cultural and structural corridor between the Pacific Northwest and Los Angeles, tracing shared origins in the Second Great Migration, parallel structural transformations, and music as a communication protocol between communities that share lineage but not latitude.

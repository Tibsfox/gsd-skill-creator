SNE designs the spine-leaf fabric. SNO keeps it running at 3 AM when a BGP peer flaps, a certificate expires, or a carrier reports a fiber cut on your primary circuit. Network engineering is architecture. Network operations is the daily discipline of monitoring, maintaining, documenting, and defending the network that everything else depends on.

Module Overview

Monitor

01. Network Monitoring & Alerting

NOC operations and staffing models. Alert threshold design (static, dynamic, anomaly-based). Escalation procedures. Dashboard design for network ops. SLA monitoring and uptime tracking. Uptime calculation and reporting.
Change

02. Network Change Operations

Maintenance window scheduling for network. Change control adapted for network devices. Pre-change and post-change verification scripts. Config backup and rollback procedures. Emergency change process for network.
Incidents

03. Network Incident Response

Network outage triage methodology. Packet loss and latency investigation. BGP hijack detection and response. Cable cut procedures and carrier escalation. Network-specific war room operations.
IPAM

04. IP Address Management

IPAM tools (NetBox, Infoblox, BlueCat, phpIPAM). DHCP operations at scale. IPv4 exhaustion strategies (NAT, RFC 1918 reuse, address reclamation). IPv6 transition operations. Subnet planning and allocation policies.
PKI

05. Certificate & PKI Operations

TLS certificate lifecycle management. ACME and Let’s Encrypt at scale. Certificate rotation automation. Expiry monitoring and alerting. mTLS operations. Internal CA management (step-ca, Vault PKI). The 90-day cert reality.
Capacity

06. Network Capacity Operations

Bandwidth utilization tracking and trending. Upgrade planning and scheduling. Circuit ordering and provisioning. ISP and carrier relationship management. Peering operations (IX membership, bilateral peering). Transit cost optimization.
Firewall

07. Firewall & ACL Operations

Rule lifecycle management (request, review, implement, verify, expire). Rule review cadence and cleanup. Emergency access procedures. Compliance audit for firewall policies. Shadow and redundant rule detection. Policy optimization.
VPN

08. VPN & Remote Access Operations

Site-to-site VPN management (IPsec, WireGuard). Remote access VPN scaling. Split tunnel decisions and security trade-offs. ZTNA migration from traditional VPN. VPN monitoring and troubleshooting. Key rotation operations.
Docs

09. Network Documentation & CMDB

Network diagram standards (draw.io, Netbox topology, Visio). Cable management and labeling. Port mapping and patch panel documentation. As-built documentation. Audit readiness. Documentation-as-code for networks.
Team

10. Network Team & Vendor Management

NOC staffing models (24/7, follow-the-sun, hybrid). Carrier SLA enforcement and credit claiming. Vendor escalation procedures. RMA tracking and spare parts. Circuit inventory management. Contract negotiation for network services.

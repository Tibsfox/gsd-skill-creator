The Through-Line

ComfyUI is the Amiga Principle applied to image generation: a directed acyclic graph where each node does one thing, connections define data flow, and partial re-execution prevents redundant computation. Skill-Creator provides the orchestration layer that turns isolated generation into a learning system. The spaces between — between intent and artifact, between prompt and latent space, between human meaning and machine traversal — are where the creative process lives.

“The meaning is not in the image itself. It lives in the connection between the user's intent, the workflow graph, the model's latent space traversal, and the artifact that emerges.”

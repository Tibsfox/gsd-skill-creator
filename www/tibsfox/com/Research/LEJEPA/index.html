The Claim in One Sentence

When the principles are right, the implementation becomes forgiving. LeJEPA's fifty-line regularizer works because a theoretically grounded objective replaces a decade of accumulated anti-collapse heuristics — and gsd-skill-creator's skill library is a latent space organized by the same logic.

In November 2025 Randall Balestriero and Yann LeCun published LeJEPA: Provable and Scalable Self-Supervised Learning Without the Heuristics. The paper proves that the isotropic Gaussian uniquely minimizes downstream prediction risk for Joint-Embedding Predictive Architectures under both linear and nonlinear probes, and introduces SIGReg (Sketched Isotropic Gaussian Regularization): Cramér-Wold slicing plus the Epps-Pulley empirical characteristic function test, in roughly fifty lines of PyTorch. Teacher-student networks, stop-gradient, EMA schedulers, negative samples — a decade of anti-collapse heuristics — dissolve into a single hyperparameter λ.

Five months later, LeCun's Special Lecture on AI and World Models (Al-Khwarizmi Applied Mathematics Webinar, 14 April 2026) situates LeJEPA inside his longer argument: JEPAs as the path past autoregressive LLMs, and LeWorldModel (Maes et al. 2026) as the first end-to-end pixel world model that plans in latent space — 48× faster than DINO-WM because its compact 192-dim CLS-style latent shrinks the CEM state space by ~200×.

For gsd-skill-creator, this is not purely academic. The architectural parallels are unusually direct — the Amiga Principle is the same architectural-leverage argument in a different idiom; the chipset, bounded-learning caps, CAPCOM gates, and College of Knowledge each have a LeJEPA analogue. This research hub indexes the mission package, the four themed research pages, and the substrate modules shipping in Half B.

Half B — Substrate Modules

The mechanism survey above drives Half B of this milestone — six default-off substrate modules shipping across Phases 728-734:

  • Skill Space Isotropy Audit (src/skill-isotropy/) — Cramér-Wold slicing applied to skill embeddings
  • SIGReg primitive (src/sigreg/) — ≤80-LOC Epps-Pulley port (MIT-attributed)
  • Single-λ orchestration audit — maps bounded-learning knob clusters to principled balance parameters
  • Heuristics audit — scores the six-step loop for stop-gradient-analogue behaviors
  • Mission-state world-model layer (src/mission-world-model/) — mini-LeWM for pre-dispatch wave rehearsal; sits under CAPCOM
  • Intrinsic telemetry (src/intrinsic-telemetry/) — Spearman-style label-free quality signal

All six ship default-off, opt-in via the heuristics-free-skill-space block in .claude/gsd-skill-creator.json. CAPCOM preservation is a hard gate throughout Half B — every module augments rather than replaces the human-gate architecture.

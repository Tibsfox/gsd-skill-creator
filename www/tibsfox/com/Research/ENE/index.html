Kuramoto Synchronization (P11)

The synchronous power grid IS a physical Kuramoto model. Each generator is an oscillator. Electromagnetic coupling = coupling constant K. Load angle = phase difference. Grid frequency = oscillator frequency. When coupling fails, generators lose synchronization — the power systems equivalent of chimera states.

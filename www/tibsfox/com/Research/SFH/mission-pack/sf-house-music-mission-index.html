Complete research output: 5 modules covering 50 years of Bay Area house music culture.

Contents

  1. Stage 1 — Vision Document
    • Vision narrative, problem statement (5 problems), core concept
    • Cultural geography and module dependency diagrams
    • 5 research modules: Queer Disco, Rave Era, Labels & Sound, Venues, Outdoor & Future
    • Chipset configuration, scope boundaries, 12 success criteria
  2. Stage 2 — Research Reference
    • Queer disco pre-history: Patrick Cowley, Sylvester, Megatone, hi-NRG
    • UK invasion and rave golden era: Wicked, Hardkiss, Baker Beach
    • Label genealogy: Megatone through Dark Entries and Dirtybird
    • Venue timeline: 15+ venues with gentrification impact mapping
    • Outdoor continuum: Baker Beach to Burning Man, 35+ year span
    • Safety and sensitivity: AIDS, queer identity, Ghost Ship, Cowley materials
  3. Stage 3 — Mission Package
    • 8 deliverables, 4-wave execution plan with 3 parallel tracks
    • Fleet activation profile: 17 roles including culture and sound specialists
    • 25 tests (4 safety-critical), verification matrix
    • ~103K token budget, 6 context windows, 3 sessions

Usage Instructions

  1. Point GSD's new-project at the vision section of the source
  2. Wave 0 produces shared schema, source index, and sensitivity flags
  3. Wave 1 executes three parallel tracks (A: Foundations+Era, B: Labels+Venues, C: Outdoor+Future)
  4. Wave 2 synthesizes the West Coast sound definition and queer thread
  5. Wave 3 assembles, verifies, and delivers the final document

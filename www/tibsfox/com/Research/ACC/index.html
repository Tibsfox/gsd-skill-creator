Overview Licensure Standards Firm Licensing Compliance Enforcement Verification Mission Pack
Washington accounting law is a layered protocol stack: federal law sets the floor, AICPA professional standards govern practice, state statute (RCW 18.04) customizes within federal bounds, and Board rules (WAC 4-30) fill remaining gaps. When any two layers conflict, the higher layer prevails — except that the Board rule prevails over AICPA standards when both apply.

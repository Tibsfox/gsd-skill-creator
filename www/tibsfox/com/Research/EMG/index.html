Electric Motors & Generators

Electromechanics — Motor Design · Generator Systems · Drive Controllers · Efficiency
Overview Mission Pack
A motor and a generator are the same machine operated in reverse — a single elegant architecture expressed through two execution paths. Every design decision in the taxonomy emerges from how the magnetic field is created, how commutation is managed, and what constraints the application imposes.

Research Modules

01 — Electromagnetic Foundations

Physics · Faraday · Historical Timeline

From Oersted to Maxwell: the physics of induction, the Lorentz force, Lenz's law, motor-generator duality, and the historical development of electromagnetic machines.

02 — Motor Taxonomy & Engineering

DC · AC · BLDC · PMSM · SRM

Complete classification of electric motor types: DC brushed, BLDC, AC induction, PMSM, switched reluctance, stepper, and linear motors with efficiency comparison.

03 — Generator Systems

Dynamos · Alternators · Wind · Hydro

Generator taxonomy from dynamos through modern wind turbine generators and hydroelectric systems, including the grid-forming revolution.

04 — Applications & Frontiers

EV Traction · Rare-Earth · Emerging Tech

EV traction motor technology, the rare-earth dependency crisis, alternative motor designs, and emerging technologies including axial-flux and SiC integration.

05 — Standards, Efficiency & Grid

DOE · IE4 · VFDs · Grid Integration

Motor efficiency standards (IE1-IE4), DOE regulatory timeline, variable frequency drives, grid integration, synchronous inertia, and pumped-storage.

06 — Verification Matrix

12/12 PASS · Source Quality · Cross-Links

Success criteria verification, source quality assessment, cross-link coverage audit, and execution summary.

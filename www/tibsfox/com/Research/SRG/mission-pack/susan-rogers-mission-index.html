Project Overview

The Susan Rogers Arc is a structured, sourced, modular deep study of Susan Rogers' career across five phases: technician formation (1978–1983), the Prince studio years (1983–1988), independent production with Geggy Tah focus (1988–1999), the McGill academic pivot (2000–2010), and the Berklee Music Perception and Cognition Laboratory (2010–present).

This is not a biography. It is a research mission into what each phase of Rogers' career reveals about the nature of sound, craft, and knowledge transfer.

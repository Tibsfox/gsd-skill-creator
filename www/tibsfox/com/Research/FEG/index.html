The retrospective is the instrument. Module 06 grew long because it was carrying two bodies of knowledge that need their own homes. Module 04 deferred the cost data that makes a housing proposal fundable. The gap-fill methodology does not revise what works — it adds what is missing. A document that cannot answer the questions its audience will ask has a gap. This mission closes the gaps.

Research Modules

M1: Educational Gap Analysis

Gap Foundation · Taxonomy & detection

Gap taxonomy (content, structural, connection), detection methods (retrospective, standards-based, learner outcome), GATE severity framework, the FoxEdu gap-fill origin story, quantitative metrics, institutional precedents, PNW educational context, College of Knowledge application.

M2: Curriculum Coverage Mapping

Gap Analysis · Coverage matrices

Porter's Surveys of Enacted Curriculum, two-dimensional content mapping, alignment indices (Webb + Porter), topic coverage vs. cognitive demand, heat map visualization, the FoxEdu Infrastructure coverage audit, automated analysis tools.

M3: Remedial Content Generation

Fill Production · Wave-based architecture

Gap-to-content pipeline, source retrieval methodology (6-tier reliability hierarchy), precision principle, content templating (cost tables, hazard tables), wave-based production, quality gates, the four FoxEdu gap-fill modules, automated generation considerations.

M4: Adaptive Learning Paths

Adapt Sequencing · Personalized learning

Knowledge Space Theory (Doignon & Falmagne), prerequisite graphs, mastery-based progression, gap-informed path construction, Bayesian Knowledge Tracing, spaced repetition, interleaving, ALEKS precedent, FoxEdu residential adaptive architecture.

M5: Skill Assessment Frameworks

Assess Evaluation · Competency-based

Assessment taxonomies (Webb DOK, Bloom revised), competency-based assessment, performance tasks vs. recognition tests, diagnostic assessment for gap detection, formative feedback loops, rubric design, reliability/validity, The Gap Report verification matrix.

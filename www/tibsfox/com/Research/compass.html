Reading the Compass

The horizontal axis is precision — measurement, standards, execution. The vertical axis is imagination — creativity, narrative, culture.

Forty-eight muses are the landmarks. Seventy-five research projects are the clearings. The closer a project is to a muse, the more it shares that muse's nature.

Distance from center is depth — how far the research reaches into its subject.

θ = angle from precision  ·  r = depth of research
The real axis is where we measure. The imaginary axis is where we dream.
The forest lives in both. The compass maps the space between.

## 1. Tool Survey

| Tool | Affiliation | Status | Capabilities |
|------|------------|--------|-------------|
| SYSTEM Sounds | Russo/Santaguida | Active | Primary CXC collaboration |
| sonoUno | Casado, Diaz-Merced, Garcia | Open source (JOSS 2024) | Research-focused; ISO 9241-171 |
| STRAUSS | Zanella, Harrison et al. | Peer-reviewed | Sound design for research/engagement |
| StarSound | Cooke et al. | Research prototype | Stereophonic; 3D audio; VR |
| xSonify | NASA | Available | X-ray time-series; Java-based |
| MAST Python Library | STScI | Open source | Pitch-to-brightness mapping |
| NASA Space Jam | CXC (educational) | Web-based | Interactive coding environment |
| SN1987A Interactive | CXC | Web-based (P5.js) | Interactive sonification app |
| Sonification Symphony | CXC | Web-based | Play NASA data as symphony |

## 2. Open Source and Research Tools
sonoUno (JOSS 2024): absorption/emission line marking, multicolumn spectral comparison, ISO accessibility compliance. StarSound: stereophonic light curves, spectra, power spectra with 3D audio and VR integration.

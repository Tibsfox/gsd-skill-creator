Overview Mission Pack
The GPU is not one coprocessor — it is a chipset of mathematical engines, each specialized for a class of computation. From the 68881 FPU to CUDA Tensor Cores: the Amiga Principle made silicon.

cuBLAS · cuSOLVER · cuSPARSE

The workhorse chip: GEMM, matrix decomposition, system solving, sparse operations. Batched operations for thousands of simultaneous solves.

Signal processing (FFT, spectral analysis), vector calculus (gradients, transforms), and statistics (Monte Carlo, distributions) on GPU.

04 — SYMBEX: Expression Engine

JIT · Expression Trees · Identity Verification

Symbolic expressions compiled to CUDA kernels via NVRTC JIT. Mathematical identities verified across parameter spaces at GPU speed.

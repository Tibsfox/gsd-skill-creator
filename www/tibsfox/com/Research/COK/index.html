Overview Complex Plane Architecture Deep Linking Fractal Growth Retrospective Space Between Mission Pack
There is a moment in every great architectural discovery when you realize the structure you were building already existed — that you were not inventing, but uncovering. The College of Knowledge arrived this way. It was not designed from first principles in a whiteboard session. It grew, rhizomatically, from a series of conversations about cooking, mathematics, Perl, and the Amiga. And in growing, it revealed something: the space between knowledge domains is not empty. It is the most structurally rich region of all.

M1: Complex Plane Foundations

Math Track A · Coordinate geometry

The two axes, polar coordinates, unit circle as activation geometry. Trigonometric functions as skill operators. Euler's formula as composition law. Tangent lines, quadrant semantics, angular velocity. Mandelbrot boundary condition. The mathematical substrate of the entire College.

M2: College Architecture

Architecture Track A · Curriculum infrastructure

Rosetta Core identity and seven panel families. Complex Plane routing logic. CPAN lineage and three-tier knowledge organization. Department/Wing/Concept hierarchy. The .college/ directory schema. Fascicle lifecycle. Mathematics Department reference seed. Knuth difficulty rails.

M3: Deep Linking Engine

Linking Track C · Knowledge graph navigation

Cross-department concept pointers as chords on the unit circle. Chord detection algorithm with 90%+ precision. Versine gap analysis for missing bridges. Link types: isomorphic, application, analogy. Category theory as deep link formalism. CrossDeptLink typed interface.

M4: Seed Growth & Fractal Expansion

Growth Track C · Generative knowledge

L-system production rules for concept growth. Department seeds as axioms, panels as production rules. The calibration loop as generation driver. Angular velocity constraints. Mandelbrot gate for coherence. Fractal Expansion Engine four-step pipeline. Self-similarity across scales.

M5: Retrospective & Lessons Learned

Memory Track B · Institutional memory

The Amiga Principle defined: architectural specialization over brute force. Friction-to-pattern registry with 15+ named patterns. DACP protocol origin. Staging layer emergence. Teacher/Student/Support model. Try-sessions as experiential learning. Five-layer institutional memory architecture.

M6: The Art of the Space Between

Philosophy Track B · Philosophical synthesis

What lives in the space between departments. Authored works through-line across five texts. Coast Salish epistemology: everything in relation. The morphism is the curriculum. Cross-domain teaching methodology. Learning pathways as arcs and chords. The College as living work.

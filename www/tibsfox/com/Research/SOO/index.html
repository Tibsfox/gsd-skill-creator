SYA teaches you what a filesystem is. SOO teaches you how to manage storage capacity at 3 AM when the alerts fire. Systems Administration is what you know. Systems Operations is what you do. This research digest covers the operational discipline — deployment strategies, incident management, change control, capacity planning, cost optimization, resilience engineering, runbook automation, performance tuning, compliance operations, and the human side of on-call culture. Ten modules, one through-line: keeping production running.

Module Overview

Deploy

01. Deployment & Release Engineering

Blue/green, canary, rolling updates, feature flags, GitOps deployment, database migrations, rollback strategies, release trains, CI/CD pipeline operations. From Amazon’s one-box to Netflix’s Spinnaker.
Incidents

02. Incident Management & Response

Severity classification, incident commander role, war room operations, communication protocols, escalation paths, post-incident review, status pages, tooling ecosystem (PagerDuty, Rootly, FireHydrant). MTTA/MTTD/MTTR/MTBF.
Change

03. Change Management & Control

ITIL 4 change enablement, CAB evolution, risk assessment, maintenance windows, change freeze policies, rollback plans, CMDB, compliance evidence, continuous delivery as change management.
Capacity

04. Capacity Planning & Scaling

Demand forecasting, horizontal vs vertical scaling, autoscaling patterns (HPA/VPA/KEDA), load testing (k6/Locust), resource right-sizing, queue theory (Little’s Law), headroom planning, cloud capacity reservations.
FinOps

05. Cost Optimization & FinOps

Cloud cost anatomy, FinOps Framework (Inform/Optimize/Operate), reserved instances, spot instance strategies, right-sizing, showback/chargeback, unit economics, architectural cost optimization, governance guardrails.
HA

06. High Availability & Resilience

SLAs/SLOs/SLIs, error budgets, redundancy patterns, failover operations, chaos engineering in practice, DR testing, multi-region architectures, circuit breakers, graceful degradation, blast radius containment.
Runbooks

07. Runbook Automation & Toil

Runbook design patterns, toil measurement and budgets (Google’s 50% rule), automation ROI calculation, self-healing patterns, ChatOps, decision trees, human-in-the-loop automation, operational debt.
Perf

08. Performance Engineering

Latency budgets, profiling in production, database query optimization, caching strategies (Redis, CDN, application), load balancer tuning, kernel parameters, benchmarking methodology, performance regression detection.
Compliance

09. Compliance & Governance Operations

SOC2 operational controls, audit preparation, access reviews, change evidence collection, log retention policies, vulnerability management operations, patch cycles, GRC tooling, compliance-as-code.
On-Call

10. On-Call & Operational Culture

Rotation design, compensation models, alert fatigue mitigation, cognitive load management, handoff protocols, operational reviews, blameless culture, burnout prevention (67% rate), team topologies for ops.

The Through-Line

The Vulkan Samples corpus is structured knowledge at GPU scale. Each sample encodes an architectural choice, a tradeoff, a pattern worth knowing. The spaces between samples — the connection between “dynamic rendering” and “render passes,” between “mesh shaders” and the migration from geometry shaders — are where the real knowledge lives. This mission maps those connections as first-class deliverables.

The output is not a summary. It is a living upstream intelligence addition: every sample mapped as a DACP-compatible skill stub, every extension catalogued with version requirements, every performance pattern encoded as a testable assertion, and the whole thing wired into the skill-creator loop. When a future agent encounters a request involving descriptor management or ray tracing, it reaches into this corpus and surfaces the right pattern.

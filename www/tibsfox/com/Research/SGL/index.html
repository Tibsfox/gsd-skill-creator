Every live performance is a conversation between signal and light. The bass player's fingers generate acoustic pressure waves that a microphone converts to voltage, that an ADC quantizes into samples, that a DSP chain shapes and sculpts in real time — and somewhere downstream, those same samples trigger LED arrays that paint the air with color timed to the beat. The entire path from vibrating string to photon hitting retina is one continuous signal chain.

Research Modules

M1: Real-Time DSP Algorithms

DSP Track A · Adaptive filters

LMS, NLMS, FxLMS, and RLS adaptive noise cancellation. Spectral subtraction, Wiener filtering, STFT analysis. AI-enhanced noise suppression (DTLN). Convergence analysis and step-size bounds. The z-transform framework.

M2: ASIC & FPGA DSP Implementation

Hardware Track A · Custom silicon

FPGA-to-ASIC design pipeline. FIR/IIR filter implementation in VHDL/Verilog. Fixed-point coefficient quantization and stability. DSP slice architectures, Chisel/FIRRTL agile design. Hearing aid ASIC case study.

M3: Sound Filtering & Audio Processing

DSP Track B · Audio applications

Parametric EQ, crossover networks, dynamic range compression. Filter bank architectures. Psychoacoustic foundations: critical bands, masking, loudness. Real-time latency budgets and buffer sizing.

M4: LED Persistence of Vision

Visual Track B · Display technology

Psychophysics of afterimage and flicker fusion. POV display architectures: rotary, reciprocating, flexible-PCB. APA102/WS2812 driver protocols. RP2040 PIO timing. Inductive power transfer for rotating assemblies.

M5: DMX512 & Stage Lighting

Protocol Track C · Lighting control

DMX512 protocol: RS-485, packet structure, timing. RDM bidirectional extension. Art-Net and sACN Ethernet bridging. Pixel mapping, universe planning, network design for 1000+ pixel installations.

M6: MIDI & Control Protocols

Protocol Track C · Control integration

MIDI 1.0 serial protocol and channel messages. MIDI 2.0 UMP format, MIDI-CI, Profile Configuration, Property Exchange, Jitter Reduction. MIDI Show Control. OSC. MIDI-to-DMX bridges and timecode sync.

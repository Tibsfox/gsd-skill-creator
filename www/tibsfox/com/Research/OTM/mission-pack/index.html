Recompiling

To modify and rebuild the PDF:

  1. Edit om-mission.tex in any text editor
  2. Compile with XeLaTeX (requires DejaVu fonts):

xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode om-mission.tex
xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode om-mission.tex
xelatex -interaction=nonstopmode om-mission.tex

Three passes resolve TOC, cross-references, and page counts.

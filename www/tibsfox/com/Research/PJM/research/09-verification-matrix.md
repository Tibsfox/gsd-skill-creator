### 2.3 Source Assessment Notes

The Pearl Jam source landscape is unusually well-served by both official and encyclopedic sources. The band's own Ten Club website [31] provides authoritative information about fan community operations. The Five Horizons archive [19] preserves the 1994 congressional testimony transcript. Rolling Stone's coverage spans four decades of the band's career with multiple deep-dive articles. The Washington Post's 2024 coverage of the Ticketmaster/DOJ suit [17] provides crucial context for the 30-year vindication narrative.

Wikipedia's coverage of Pearl Jam and related topics is extensive and well-cited, reflecting the band's cultural significance and the depth of available primary sources. All Wikipedia claims used in this research were cross-verified against at least one additional source where possible.

The internal GRV reference [36] connects Pearl Jam's origin story directly to the existing Green River research project, maintaining the series' cross-referential integrity.

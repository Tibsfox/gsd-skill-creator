🚀 How to Use This Package

  1. Open the PDF and review Stage 1 (Vision) to confirm scope aligns with your needs. Adjust the success criteria or scope boundaries if necessary before handing off.
  2. Hand the PDF to gsd-skill-creator (Claude Code) with the directive: "Execute this research mission starting at Wave 0."
  3. The orchestrator will initialize the catalog schema, index the Chandra press archive, then launch parallel Wave 1A (Stellar + IMBH) and Wave 1B (SMBH + Detection).
  4. Approve each CAPCOM gate (4 total) before the next wave proceeds. Gate 2 (post-Wave 1) is the most important: confirm module completeness before Opus synthesis begins.
  5. Wave 3 delivers the final catalog PDF and machine-readable JSON/CSV export. The Chandra cross-index is included as a separate reference file.

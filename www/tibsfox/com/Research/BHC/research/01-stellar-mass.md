## 1. Formation and Population
Stellar-mass black holes form when stars >8 solar masses exhaust fuel, triggering core collapse. Scientists estimate the Milky Way hosts ~100 million (NASA Science, 2020).

## 3. LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA Detections
Over 90 gravitational wave events through O4. GW150914: 29+36 Msun merger, 62 Msun remnant. GW190521: 85+65 Msun, 142 Msun remnant (first confirmed IMBH product).

## 4. Gaia Astrometric Series
Gaia BH1 (2022), BH2 (2023), BH3 (2024) -- dormant black holes discovered via companion star wobble without X-ray emission. Growing sample expanding known population.

## 1. The Two Imaged Black Holes

**Sagittarius A*:** Mass 4.3 x 10^6 Msun, distance ~26,000 ly. EHT imaged 2022. Chandra has monitored since 2000 with 20+ press releases. Last major active phase ~200 years ago.

**M87*:** Mass 6.5 x 10^9 Msun, distance ~53 Mly. Spin a* ~ 0.9. First EHT image April 2019. Relativistic jet studied extensively by Chandra.

## 3. Deep Survey Population Statistics
Chandra Deep Field South 7 Ms exposure: ~5,000 AGN in deepest X-ray image. Cosmic X-ray background predominantly produced by accreting black holes.

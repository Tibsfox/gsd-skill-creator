Pacific Northwest Research Series

Rainforest to garden — mapping the living systems of the PNW bioregion. Each project maps a different layer of the same place: the salmon that feeds the forest, the elevation gradient that stratifies it, the birds that pollinate and disperse it, the mammals that shape its trophic structure.

ECO — Living Systems Taxonomy

Taxonomy From Tahoma's Summit to the Salish Sea Floor

Full ecosystem taxonomy across 8 elevation bands. Flora, fauna, fungi, ecological networks, heritage bridge, and derived GSD chipset.

COL — Columbia Valley Rainforest

Biodiversity 1,000+ species, 4 study zones

Map biodiversity as a network of relationships — from the mycorrhizal web beneath the soil to the bald eagles circling above Bonneville Dam.

CAS — Cascade Range Biodiversity

Biodiversity Ridge to valley research

The Vertical Wilderness — Cascade Mountain Range and foothills biodiversity from alpine tundra to valley floor.

AVI — Wings of the Pacific Northwest

Avian 250+ species, 7 ecoregions

A complete avian taxonomy from Tundra Swan to Townsend's Warbler. Migration routes, nesting ecology, and conservation status.

MAM — Fur, Fin & Fang

Mammals 169+ species, summit to seafloor

Complete mammalian taxonomy of the Pacific Northwest. Predator-prey dynamics, trophic structure, and habitat connectivity.

GDN — PNW Gardening

Gardening Western WA, OR & Coastal BC

Maritime PNW gardening from climate zones to pest management. 12-month calendar, native plant integration, soil science.

BCM — Building Construction Mastery

Building Oregon & Washington code landscape

Six trade disciplines at five audience levels. NEC 2023, UPC 2021, ASCE 7-22 seismic design. Dual-state code mapping.

SHE — Smart Home & DIY Electronics

Electronics From blinking LED to complete home hub

36 hands-on projects across 6 tiers. 22 sensors, 14 actuators, 10 protocols. Home Assistant, ESPHome, ESP32.

BPS — Bio-Physics Sensing Systems

Physics Sonar, radar, magnetoreception, electroreception

How animals sense their world — orca biosonar, fox magnetic compass, bat echolocation, Doppler processing, and time-series signal analysis.

FFA — Fur, Feathers & Animation Arts

Craft Biology, craft, fabrication, moving image

From cuticle scale to roughness map — the biology of fur and feather rendered as craft, fabrication, and animation art.

TIBS — Traditions & Indigenous Knowledge

Heritage Animal Speak, sacred landscapes, the living world

Ted Andrews, Pacific Northwest Native shamanism, and the bridge between traditional ecological knowledge and scholarly research.

LED — LED Lighting & Controllers

Electronics DIY, microcontrollers, smart lighting

LED strip to oscilloscope trace. Microcontroller programming, smart lighting design, instruments, and high-speed logic.

SYS — Systems Administration

Infrastructure The spaces in between

Server logs, network transport, process forensics, data provenance, access control, and security operations.

VAV — Voxel as Vessel

Data Ceph, RAG, Minecraft block storage

Minecraft world data as sovereign knowledge containers. Block storage, signal fidelity, retrieval-augmented generation.

AWF — Clean Air, Water & Food

Living Systems Purification, pollinators, forest conservation

The living filter network — air purification, water filtration, food safety, pollinator health, wildlife habitat, and forest conservation as one interconnected system.

THE — Thermal & Hydrogen Energy

Energy HVAC, heat recovery, fuel cells, electrolysis

Every technology as a boundary condition problem — heat pumps, waste heat recovery, catalytic conversion, fuel cells, solar electrolysis, PNW geothermal and hydroelectric.

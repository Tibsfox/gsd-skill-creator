The lines in the coloring book were always approximate. The world outside them is where the actual grammar lives. This mission is an expedition to document that grammar — and to show how one artist from Boulder, Colorado has been reporting it faithfully for two decades, in pen and ink and UV light and the memory of a thousand Red Rocks shows.

The Muse Groves

Each www/ project becomes a grove operated by the muse whose domain it matches:

Willow's Grove

COL Columbia Valley

Onboarding territory. Gentle paths, bridge building, first encounters with the forest. Willow welcomes newcomers and adapts the trail to their pace.

Hemlock's Ridge

CAS Cascade Range

Standards and verification. The elevation transect where quality is tested at every band. Hemlock audits the heartwood and measures the growth rings.

Foxy's Canopy

ECO Living Systems

Cartography headquarters. The view from above where Foxy maps the entire ecosystem. 189 species, 8 elevation bands, the whole topology visible at once.

Sam's Garden

GDN Gardening

Applied ecology. Sam's experimental plots where hypotheses grow into food. What works? What doesn't? Plant it and find out.

Lex's Workshop

UNI Unison Language

Precision engineering. Lex's domain where every protocol is specified, every type is checked, every workflow runs exactly as designed.

Cedar's Ring

MUS This Mission

The timeline of the forest itself. Cedar's append-only record of everything that happened, every debate resolved, every cartridge placed, every kernel promoted.

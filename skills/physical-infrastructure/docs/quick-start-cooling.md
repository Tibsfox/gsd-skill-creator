# Quick Start: Cooling System Design

Design a complete data center cooling system from a natural language requirement. This walkthrough takes you from request to safety-reviewed output.

## What You Will Produce

- Flow rate and pipe sizing calculations (Darcy-Weisbach method, ASHRAE TC 9.9)
- P&ID drawing with ISA-5.1 symbols
- Bill of materials with pipe, valve, pump, and PRV specifications
- Safety review from the Safety Warden
- Optional: OpenFOAM simulation inputs for CFD validation

## Prerequisites

Gather the following before starting:
- Total IT load (kW) or per-rack density (kW/rack) and rack count
- Facility ambient temperature (default: 20C)
- Target redundancy level (N, N+1, 2N, 2N+1)
- Safety class: residential, commercial, industrial, or data-center

## Step 1: Invoke the Design-Review Team

```
/physical-infrastructure:design-review "Cool [N] racks at [X]kW each"
```

Example:
```
/physical-infrastructure:design-review "Cool 10 racks at 40kW each in a new data center, N+1 redundancy, data-center safety class"
```

## Step 2: Review the Architect's Decomposition

The Architect agent (Opus) parses your request and identifies:
- Total heat load: 10 racks x 40kW = 400kW
- Target supply/return temperatures: 18C/28C (deltaT = 10C, ASHRAE TC 9.9 A2 class)
- Redundancy level: N+1 (two pumps, one standby)
- Output formats requested: calculations, blueprint, BOM

Confirm or correct any assumptions before the Calculator runs.

## Step 3: Review Calculation Results

The Calculator agent produces a `CalculationRecord` with:
- **flow_rate_Ls:** ~9.58 L/s for 400kW at deltaT=10C
- **pipe_size_NPS:** NPS 3" (minimum for 9.58 L/s at max 2.4 m/s velocity)
- **method:** "Darcy-Weisbach + ASHRAE TC 9.9"
- **safetyMargin:** 15% (per engineering practice)

Formula: `Q = P / (rho * Cp * deltaT)` where rho=998 kg/m3, Cp=4182 J/(kg*K) for water at 20C.

## Step 4: Safety Warden Review

The Safety Warden (Opus) checks every design against the safety threshold matrix:
- **Pressure:** Working pressure evaluated against 80/150/300 PSI thresholds
- **Temperature:** Fluid temperature evaluated against 60/82/150C thresholds
- **Chemical:** Coolant type evaluated (water/PG = info, EG = warning)
- **PRV (SC-10):** Pressure relief valve recommendation always present for closed systems
- **Water hammer (SC-14):** Quick-closing valves flagged if present

For data-center safety class, the warden operates in **gate mode**: critical or blocking findings halt output until you explicitly acknowledge the risks.

## Step 5: Blueprint Output

The Draftsman agent generates:
- **P&ID drawing** in SVG format with ISA-5.1 symbols
- **Title block** including PE disclaimer: "AI GENERATED - VERIFY WITH LICENSED PE BEFORE USE"
- **BOM** listing: carbon steel pipe (NPS 3"), gate valves, centrifugal pumps (N+1), PRV, expansion tank

### Common Causes of Low IR Readings

| Cause | Remedy |
|-------|--------|
| Moisture in conduit | Drain conduit, use vacuum or blower to dry, retest |
| Contaminated terminations | Clean termination points with approved solvent |
| Damaged insulation (wire pull damage) | Inspect conductor, repair or replace damaged section |
| Incorrect test setup (device not disconnected) | Verify all connected equipment is disconnected, retest |
| High ambient humidity (>80% RH) | Wait for drier conditions or use dehumidifier, retest |
